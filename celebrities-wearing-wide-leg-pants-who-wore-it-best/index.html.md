# 11 Celebrities Wearing Wide Leg Pants  Who Wore It Best?

Take a cue from celebrities wearing wide leg pants this season. The wide leg pant is a chic alternative to all those skinny jeans and skin-tight leggings that dominate the fashion landscape. From relaxed boho-inspired looks to red carpet-worthy outfits, there’s a wide leg pant to suit all occasions. So, ditch the skinnies and take some wardrobe inspiration from these celebrities wearing wide leg pants.

## 1. Jessica Biel

![Jessica Biel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/1_jessica-biel_550x1100.jpg)

One of the celebrities wearing wide leg pants is Jessica Biel. Here she works a very feminine angle in an all lace ensemble. She teams her wide leg lace pants with a lace top, coral coloured heels, and matching coral earrings.

## 2. Nicole Richie

![Nicole Richie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/2_nicole-richie_550x1100.jpg)

Nicole Richie works her signature boho-with-a-modern-edge style in these scarf print pants. She teams them with a black muscle tee and a matching scarf print top tied around her waist. Oversized aviator sunglasses, a chunky chain necklace, black sandals, and an embellished clutch complete this cool daytime look.

## 3. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/3_jennifer-lawrence_600x900.jpg)

Jennifer Lawrence steps out in a head-to-toe couture look. She wears a Christian Dior Couture cropped lace top and asymmetrical pleated pants. She teams them with classic black pumps and aviator sunglasses. It’s a brave look and, while it might not be everyone’s cup of tea, it’s one that J. Law definitely works with attitude.

## 4. Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

![Rosie Huntington-Whiteley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/03024353_5746_399x594.jpg)

Here, Rosie Huntington-Whiteley works a casual daytime look. She teams a pair of paisley wide leg pants with a floaty cream top for a boho inspired look. Tan sandals, a stylish carry bag, and oversized sunglasses compete this relaxed take of the trend. This outfit is a great example of how to take a summery approach to wearing wide leg pants.

## 5. Victoria Beckham

![Victoria Beckham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/03024718_3021_600x901.jpg)

Victoria Beckham works a polished look in a pair of tailored wide leg pants. She teams her bright orange pants with a crisp white blouse, aviator sunglasses, and a long military-style jacket. This kind of outfit is perfect for working a more corporate look with wide leg pants.

## 6. Zosia Mamet

![Zosia Mamet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/6_zosia-mamet_550x1100.jpg)

Actress Zosia Mamet works a bold statement look in head-to-toe blue. She wears a silky blue button-up blouse with matching blue pants. Assorted rings and a pop of pink lipstick complete this colourful look.

## 7. Rooney Mara

![Rooney Mara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/03024859_3908_395x594.jpg)

Menswear inspired trends are popular at the moment. Rooney Mara works a masculine inspired look with some pinstripe men’s style pants. The pants have a relaxed fit with a wide cummerbund-style waistband. She completes the elegant eveningwear look with a dark red lipstick and toe-cap heels.

## 8. Jennifer Lopez

![Jennifer Lopez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/8_jennifer-lopez_550x1100.jpg)

Jennifer Lopez wears her wide leg Roberto Cavalli pants with a printed blouse, metallic clutch, and platform heels. Stacked bracelets, a gold necklace, and dark burgundy lips complete this glamorous look from the starlet.

## 9. Florence Welch

![Florence Welch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/9_florence-welch_550x1100.jpg)

Songstress Florence Welch mixes and matches her prints. Better known for wearing flowing, ethereal gowns, she takes a fashion risk in this Miu Miu ensemble. She wears these cropped wide leg pants with a clashing button-up shirt, platform heels, and a blue bejewelled handbag.

## 10. Shailene Woodley

![Shailene Woodley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/03025411_9008_500x750.jpg)

Here, Shailene Woodley wears sleek black and white separates. She teams a pair of basic black wide leg pants with a pleated top and strappy sandals. It’s a simple and elegant look that you could accessorise simply, as Shailene has done here, or dress up with statement-jewels and accessories.

## 11. Naya Rivera

![Naya Rivera](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/11_naya-rivera_600x900.jpg)

Naya Rivera takes a very elegant, ladylike approach in this polka dot outfit. She teams her sheer top and wide leg pants with hoop earrings, a necklace, and assorted rings for a glamorous eveningwear look.

These are just some celebrities we’ve spotted wearing wide leg pants. As you can see, there are many different ways to style them. Are you a fan of wide leg pants? Which of these celebrity looks do you like best?
