# A\-Rod & Cameron's Romantic Weekend ...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2010/12/Cameron-A-Rod_560x783.jpg)

The on-again-off-again couple - Cameron Diaz and Alex Rodriguez - now seems to be on again as evident from their weekend in Miami.

The duo have been trying to keep their personal lives private and have been eluding the cameras for months as a twosome. However, Cameron and A-Rod could not keep their hands to themselves during their romantic stroll.

Cameron’s friend Joanna Garcia who is supposed to have set them up in the first place is the reason once again why the pair were in town - to attend her wedding with the Yankees' Nick Swisher.

The two first became a thing back in July but the relationship couldn't find steady ground and the two headed for a split in October. Though at the time A-Rod wanted to break-up, it was apparently him who begged Cameron to give him another chance now.

Hollywood Life reported a source as saying "He called her and asked her to get together and at first Cameron said, ‘no’, But after a long conversation he finally convinced her to meet with him. For now, it looks like all is forgiven."

Since they've gotten back in November, the couple seems to be stronger than ever and specially with A-rod having some time off before next year's season, it looks like the two might be able to work things out.
