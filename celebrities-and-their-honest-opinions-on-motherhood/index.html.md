# 7 Celebrities and Their Honest Opinions on Motherhood ...

I think it's so interesting to read about celebrities' opinions on motherhood because it's a reminder that they go through the same struggles as anyone else. They worry about whether they're "doing it right," their post-baby body, and feeling like they're breaking down. Check out some celebrities' opinions on motherhood:

## 1. Hilary Duff

![Hilary Duff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jw/ge/koaozr8m_600x421.jpg)

YES! Hilary Duff opened up about pregnancy and body image and I think it's something all moms would appreciate. How can you feel bad about your body when you remember the tiny precious human that came from it? This is just one of many celebrities' opinions on motherhood.

## 2. Shakira

![Shakira](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pk/b8/wrovvw5j_600x407.jpg)

When it comes to motherhood, Shakira embraces the good and the ugly. I love that she's so honest about how hard it is and how that doesn't keep her from enjoying every part of it. The idea of motherhood (and whether people find it hard or easy) is different for every mother but it works if you work it, right?

## 3. Jennifer Love Hewitt

![Jennifer Love Hewitt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ub/i1/rp4z8hja_600x486.jpg)

I love how honest J. Love is! A lot of times (not just in motherhood), people come across situations that prove to be very difficult. It's totally okay to wish you had things a bit easier but don't let that feeling keep you from actually being the best that you can be. Motherhood will definitely have its days where you feel like things are a lot more than you bargained for but just keep holding on!

## 4. Tina Fey

![Tina Fey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m1/x6/b5jluty3_600x400.jpg)

Funny woman Tina Fey might seem like she's superwoman but she breaks down just like everyone else does. I love this quote because you can apply it to any aspect of your life, not just motherhood. Sometimes, things get overwhelming and it's easy to feel like you're drowning in it all. The important thing to do is acknowledge that feeling because that's what's going to help push you out of it.

## 5. Gisele Bundchen

![Gisele Bundchen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/be/ueglrlzx_600x400.jpg)

Gisele knows that motherhood means learning something new every day. It's going to be challenging most of (a lot of) the time but hey, no one was ever promised an easy life, right? Making mistakes and learning from them is the rewarding part of it all! How many lessons have you learned while being a mother?

## 6. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gv/yf/wyucr9xf_600x511.jpg)

Jessica's the mother to two beautiful baby girls, Honest and Haven. She makes it look so easy but like every other mother, she's just doing the best she can. It's important for everyone to realize that there's no perfect way to parent because everyone's circumstances are different! Try your best not to judge other mothers and continue to do all that you can do. If you ever feel like you're being judged, just remember that what other people think doesn't matter!

## 7. Kate Beckinsale

![Kate Beckinsale](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gx/qu/wh72m2l2_600x392.jpg)

The "Underworld" actress isn't afraid to admit that things can take a crazy turn every now and again. As lucky and as blessed that you might feel, it's okay to also feel like you're about to explode. The important thing is to have an awesome support system, people that are going to help you when you're feeling like that exorcist lady.

Well, there you have it. I absolutely love celebrities that can be candidly and totally honest. After all, if you've got a voice, you should use it to relate to others, right? If you're a mother, did you feel connected to any of these celebrities while reading their thoughts/opinions on motherhood?
