# 17 Best Female Celebrity Smiles in Hollywood: Iconic Women Who Light Up the Room

A dazzling white smile is more than just a red carpet accessory; it's a window into personality and confidence. From Hollywood icons to chart-topping singers, these women have captured our hearts with their infectious joy. Whether they are walking the Oscar stage or just being the woman next door, their [beautiful smile](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tips-for-a-perfect-smile/) is truly unforgettable. Here are 17 of the best female celebrity smiles that prove happiness is always in style.

## 1. Charlize Theron

![Charlize Theron](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ug/gk/mhnrg6bh_395x594.jpg)

It doesn’t matter which hairstyle Charlize is currently sporting; they all look fabulous paired with her insanely beautiful smile. She’s such a fabulous looking woman, and in my opinion, I think she has one of the best female celebrity smiles of all time.

## 2. Jennifer Lopez

![Jennifer Lopez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rh/dv/rgq53m1r_600x861.jpg)

JLo is such a sexy woman and performer, but when you see her smiling, she’s just the relatable woman next door. More than any other celebrity I can think of, Jennifer looks so different when she’s smiling to when she’s not. I think she just gets better looking as she ages. You can find more about her journey on her [official website](https://www.jenniferlopez.com/).

## 3. Julia Roberts

![Julia Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ci/mj/bq20xxy7_600x900.jpg)

There couldn’t be a celebrity smile list without including Julia. She won us over with her smile in one terrific movie after another. This pretty woman’s smile is just infectious. Next time you watch one of her movies and she’s smiling, I’ll bet you’re smiling too.

## 4. Catherine Zeta-Jones

![Catherine Zeta-Jones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/g5/xrdjxnwi_486x619.jpg)

I first saw Catherine in The Mask of Zorro, and I thought (like millions of others) that she was an incredible beauty and one to watch. When she’s on the big screen, you just can’t take your eyes off her and in print, her stunning smile lights up the page.

## 5. Kate Hudson

![Kate Hudson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kt/0w/rzf7jy0h_600x915.jpg)

Kate has to be one of the most charming women in Hollywood. Her smile just illuminates her entire face. I’ve long been a fan of her mother, Goldie Hawn, but I think Kate is even more captivating and will continue to have a stellar career.

## 6. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/06/eq/bh01xg1l_600x900.jpg)

When you think of [female celebrities](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/top-hollywood-actresses/), Jessica stands out as a natural beauty. She’s considered a sex symbol, but I don’t get that vibe from her at all. Rather, I see Jessica in the roles she most loves—that of a wife, mother, and the successful founder of [The Honest Company](https://www.honest.com/).

## 7. Kate Beckinsale

![Kate Beckinsale](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u4/m2/vq0x7sin_465x647.jpg)

Kate has brought her lovely smile to America from London, England where she was born. She keeps busy with numerous acting roles and plenty of charitable works. Don’t write Kate off as just another pretty face; she was a Modern Languages student at university before taking up acting full time.

## 8. Farrah Fawcett

![Farrah Fawcett](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sn/za/f60zy96j_539x714.jpg)

That hair! That poster! That incredible smile! To me, Farrah was always the poster girl for being fit and fabulous. She left us way too early, but this Angel’s legacy continues to shine.

## 9. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/q6/ydjwkhsu_393x550.jpg)

As a brunette or red head, and every other color she has experimented with, the one constant besides her singing talent is her gorgeous smile. As hot as she looks, there’s still a vulnerability about her that draws you in. Rihanna is a very giving person and has been involved with a large number of charities.

## 10. Shania Twain

![Shania Twain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uq/kw/yloy09nt_384x517.jpg)

When she sang "Man! I feel Like a Woman!" that was certainly a statement song and did she ever look hot in that iconic outfit. Now, I’ll be honest, I don’t think Shania has a traditional powerhouse voice, but she has a great voice for her style and she’s an incredible performer. Did I mention she has a great smile?

**Why We Love These Iconic Smiles:**
*   They radiate confidence and positivity.
*   They remind us that beauty comes from within.
*   They brighten up every red carpet and magazine cover.

## 11. Christie Brinkley

![Christie Brinkley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k6/0c/jblgcjg0_600x881.jpg)

She’s on TV all the time selling that Total Gym, and she’s probably sold millions of them. But the Christie I’m celebrating today is the woman with the pearly white smile and a heart of gold. Christie is actively involved in at least 20 charities.

## 12. Oprah Winfrey

![Oprah Winfrey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m8/qr/0yxkvhcm_587x779.jpg)

Wow, it’s hard to find a picture where Oprah isn’t smiling. Sure, you can say you’d be smiling all the time if you had her bank account, but Oprah is such a giving person. She’s involved with many charities and a few foundations that bear her name.

## 13. Gwyneth Paltrow

![Gwyneth Paltrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/on/lg/solp46vr_583x794.jpg)

You won’t find that much of a difference if you look at images of Gwyneth through the years. Always with the wide smile and a few varying lengths of blonde hair, with my favorite just hitting her shoulders or shorter. It doesn’t matter; she’s a timeless beauty.

## 14. Britney Spears

![Britney Spears](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ky/ou/p4qe5dpc_600x900.jpg)

Britney’s smile, when she was young and captivating, could almost rival Farrah’s. Today, there’s almost something poignant about it, which makes me sad, because I thought she was just an exceptional looking young woman. Still, worth a spot on this list for old times’ sake.

## 15. Sandra Bullock

![Sandra Bullock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vj/nl/0wnpj4uj_375x500.jpg)

In contrast to [Britney](#14), Sandra just gets better and better. Such a beautiful spirit and such a beautiful smile. Long ago, I read that Sandy loves to salsa dance and since that's something that I also love, I know if I could just meet her, we’d be BFFs.

## 16. Eva Longoria

![Eva Longoria](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ni/mt/tu5nvvav_600x826.jpg)

Can I just say I love her in the Sheba cat food commercials? Hot! Hot! Hot! Eva’s smile just beams with good will. She’s such a philanthropic person. The [Eva Longoria Foundation](https://www.evalongoriafoundation.org/) helps Latinas build better futures and she’s involved with over 35 charities.

## 17. Shirley Temple

![Shirley Temple](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fu/hq/wnr0168f_600x747.jpg)

Shirley’s smile lit up the world. Of course, there have been other popular child stars, but Shirley Temple was beloved by a nation in the midst of the Great Depression. While she passed away in 2014, her legacy lives on through her iconic films and her work as a diplomat. Her [official legacy site](https://www.shirleytemple.com/) continues to inspire.

Keeping this list to only 17 names is not easy, but every woman deserves to celebrate her own unique beauty. So tell me, which of your favorite celebrities with a knockout smile did I leave out? Is there anyone here you think I should have skipped?
