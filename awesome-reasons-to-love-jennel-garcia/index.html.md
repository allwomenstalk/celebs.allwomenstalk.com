# 7 Awesome Reasons to Love Jennel Garcia ...

There are so many fantastic reasons to love Jennel Garcia. If you're an X Factor fan, you probably fell in love with her right after her incredible audition. She's definitely an original artist on a mission to bring rock n' roll back to our generation. She's got quite the voice on her which makes me think she'll make it big in no time. Check out some of the cool reasons to love Jennel Garcia.

## 1. Her X Factor Audition

For her audition, Jennel sang "Paris (Ooh La La)" by Grace Potter and the Nocturnals. What was so interesting about her audition was how she hit the stage looking very shy but as soon as she opened her mouth, she was a whole other person. She's got such a HUGE voice! She definitely impressed all the judges and had all of them rooting for her. Her audition is just one of the reasons to love Jennel Garcia.

## 2. Her New Single

Jennel recently released her debut single, "Hate To Love You"! It's the perfect song to have on blast while driving to the beach this summer. My friends and I have already learned all the lyrics - now we play it constantly and sing (scream) along. I think the single definitely shows off her style in a great way. If you haven't heard it, it's available on iTunes already!

## 3. Her Insta-vids

I'll be honest, I was never a big fan of the 'video' feature on Instagram until recently. I love that artists can use it to share their music with their fans in a much quicker and easier way. Jennel's constantly posting 15-second covers on her Instagram account (@jennelgarcia). It's a great way to listen to her belt out her high notes!

## 4. Her Love for Her Fans

Jennel's really good at showing her fans love. She's constantly interacting with them on social media and thanking them for their constant support. It's really sweet to see someone take their time and acknowledge the people who are rooting for their success.

## 5. Her Summer Tour

Jennel recently hit the road to go on a mini tour across the U.S! I actually got to watch her live in her New Jersey stop and she was amazing! She's such an awesome performer - a true musician. Have you had the chance to see her live yet?

## 6. Her Positive Attitude

If you follow Jennel on Twitter (@jennelgarcia) then you know that she doesn't care what anyone thinks of her. She embraces all her awesome and quirky ways and turns them into her strong suits. She's always posting motivational quotes on social media which is really awesome to see. She knows that if you love yourself and believe in yourself, nothing else matters.

## 7. She's Gorgeous

Jennel's constantly posting selfies on her Instagram account but I'm not complaining! She's so beautiful! You know those people who radiate positivity and happiness? That's Jennel. She's got a contagious smile and always lets her inner beauty shine through!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome reasons to love Jennel Garcia. She's a definitely a force to be reckoned with in the music biz and I'm super excited to see where her career takes her. If you're a fan of hers, what's your favorite thing about her?
