# Golden Globe 2011 Nominations ...

The Golden Globe Nominations for 2011 were announced earlier today. As usual, there were no big surprises in the nominations though a couple of points might just raise an eyebrow.

The much hyped role of Justin Timberlake in The Social Network which has been creating a lot of Oscar buzz, was not considered nomination worthy, though the movie did get some nods.
