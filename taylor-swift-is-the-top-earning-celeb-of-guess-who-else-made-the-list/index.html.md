# Taylor Swift is the Top\-Earning Celeb of 2016 – Guess Who else Made the List 🤔?

Credit it to her quarter-billion dollar 1989 World Tour, or her ad deals with Apple, Diet Coke, and Keds (or both): Taylor Swift tops the Forbes list of the top-earning celebs of 2016 with a whopping $170 million. That's a lot of money, and she's definitely earned every penny. Which other celebs are cashing in this year, and how much have they made? Let's have a look at the top ten earners of 2016.

## 1. Taylor Swift

![hair, blond, person, woman, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k5/hg/c0wcnr9j5787c6d364a49841891173_600x600.jpg)

At 26, Taylor Swift has control over her image, her music and her bank account. Her fans adore her, and it shows in her bottom line.

## 2. One Direction

![vacation, people, photograph, person, sea,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l0/t0/raed03ia5787f7826cf7b759054384_600x600.jpg)

Boy band One Direction comes in second behind Taylor Swift with a cool collective $110 million in 2016.

## 3. James Patterson

![flower, floristry, floral design, THE, NUMBER,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dq/r7/g1o88sdp5787f7c418ebf220700131_600x749.jpg)

You'd be hard pressed to find another author as prolific, popular, or well-paid as James Patterson. He's earned the third spot with an estimated $95 million in 2016.

## 4. Dr. Phil

![person, profession, businessperson,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ih/bs/vkrqa1qq5787f848a8a01790942718_600x600.jpg)

Apparently, I'm the only person on the planet who isn't fond of TV Talk Show relationships guru Dr. Phil; he's set to bring home $88 million this year.

## 5. Cristiano Ronaldo

![player, ball game, sports, athlete, football player,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/df/je/junhrcam5787f8a83055e158476412_600x749.jpg)

Tied with Dr. Phil is Portugal's superhuman soccer star and all-around gorgeous god Cristiano Ronaldo.

## 6. Kevin Hart

![person, man, profession,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d2/cg/hkcpccj55787f9327ed48104735337_600x749.jpg)

Hart will being home $87.5 million this year, making him the first comedian to out-earn funnyman Jerry Seinfeld - that's pretty amazing.

## 7. Howard Stern

![people,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bp/sj/bvi39lbh5787f9d944e95877442011_600x600.jpg)

Shock jock Howard Stern will earn $85 million this year, basically for insulting women and minorities and being brash.

## 8. Lionel Messi

![UNICEF, player, ball game, athlete, sports,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ap/m2/kuqtyal75787f95c829cb079488985_600x600.jpg)

Another soccer legend makes the top ten this year! This Argentinian star will bring home $81.5 million in 2016.

## 9. Adele

![hair, person, hairstyle, blond, woman,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/et/ho/uof710jm5787fa1121924381597510_480x480.jpg)

Adele is back this year, after being absent from last year's top ten. Her earnings, mostly from her new album 25, total $80.5 million. Hello indeed.

## 10. Rush Limbaugh

![person, profession, real estate, professional, businessperson,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k2/mh/trmx29vg5787ff9226b00097883524_600x600.jpg)

Apparently, it pays quite well to be a conservative blow-hard; for his particular brand of bigoted rant, Limbaugh will bring home $79 million this year.
