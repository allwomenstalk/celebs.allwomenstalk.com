# 11 Celebs Who Have Cheated ...

There are so many **celebs who have cheated**. Celebrities are people that we all look up to, admire, and even envy! It may seem like their lives are always glamorous and joyful, but they experience heartbreak just like the rest of us. This list compiles the stories of eleven **celebs who have cheated** on their significant others. Some may surprise you!

## 1. Kevin Hart

As you might expect, there was a lot of rumor around this story, but Kevin openly admitted to cheating on his pregnant wife, Eniko Parrish, during an interview with “The Breakfast Club”. And he's just one of the surprising **celebs who have cheated**.

## 2. Kris Jenner

The Kardashian klan has been in the media for AGES, and one of the first scandals was when Kris Jenner wrote about cheating on her husband, Robert Kardashian, in her 2011 memoir. She used a fake name in the book, but her good friend Todd Waterman came forward later as the mystery man.

## 3. Arnold Schwarzenegger

Early in his marriage to Maria Shriver, Arnold fathered a child with his family's housekeeper. Seems a bit cliche to me, but apparently he has a strong, healthy relationship with his son.

## 4. Kristen Stewart

Fans of the Twilight book series will remember that fairytale moment when Kristen Stewart and Robert Pattinson (Bella and Edward in the film) were together in real life. That didn’t last long though when pictures of Kristen and a director of one of her other movies (Snow White and the Huntsman) surfaced.

## 5. Jude Law

Jude Law and Sienna Miller were a picture perfect Hollywood couple until Jude got a little too cozy with the kids' nanny, Daisy Wright. Law then published an apology to Miller, but the two eventually broke it off for good.

## 6. Jay-Z

There aren’t many celebrities higher on the totem pole than Beyonce herself, but even Queen B has been hit by the heartache. Although still together and working through it, Beyonce and Jay-Z have been talking about the affairs in albums such as Lemonade and 4:44.

## 7. Jesse James

Jesse James cheated on Sandra Bullock for a long time (with multiple women) and became the most hated man in the world. He eventually checked into rehab for sex addiction, and Bullock swiftly filed for divorce, although she decided to continue the adoption process of her son.

## 8. David Boreanaz

David Boreanaz simply admitted to his lack of faithfulness during an interview with People magazine in 2010. He chose to make this admission after he was involved in an alleged extortion attempt.

## 9. Nick Young

Iggy Azalea broke it off with her fiance when she suddenly tweeted that she had security footage of him bringing girls into their home. It came out that Young was expecting a baby with his ex-girlfriend Keonna Green - the baby was definitely conceived while Azalea and Young were still together.

## 10. Donald Trump Jr

The president’s son met singer Aubrey O’Day on season 5 set of his father’s reality series Celebrity Apprentice in 2011. According to multiple sources, the two carried on an affair from late 2011 until March 2012 until wife Vanessa found emails between him and the former Danity Kane band member. They were divorcing as of March 2018, after 12 years of marriage.

## 11. Tristan Thompson

Arguably the biggest scandal in the media currently, Tristan appeared to kiss another woman at a club in New York City in April 2018. The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star was nine months pregnant with their first child when the news broke, and gave birth just a few days later.

Celebrities may be living the dream life, but bad things happen in heaven too!
