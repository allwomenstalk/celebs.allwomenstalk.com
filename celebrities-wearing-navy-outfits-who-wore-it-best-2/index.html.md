# 9 Celebrities Wearing Navy Outfits  Who Wore It Best?

We’ve spotted a number of celebrities wearing navy outfits lately, both on and off the red carpet. Navy is a timeless colour but can often be overlooked. It’s not a colour you would usually expect on the red carpet, however, this year we’ve seen plenty of celebrities donning navy dresses. Off the red carpet, navy is perfect for putting together a polished, understated look. Take a look at these celebrities wearing navy outfits and be sure to let us know which navy look you like best.

## 1. Dianna Agron

![Dianna Agron](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/31204446_6172_550x1100.jpg)

First up on our list of celebrities wearing navy outfits is Dianna Agron. She teams a navy Miu Miu dress with an embellished black coat and cut out ankle booties. The colour combination of black and navy is quite classic but the bright blue clutch and embellished collar adds fun, playful elements to the overall look.

## 2. Amanda Seyfried

![Amanda Seyfried](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/31204445_5804_550x1100.jpg)

If you had previously thought of navy as an understated colour then think again. Amanda Seyfried stepped out in this navy number. The dress features metallic polka dot details and is made from a sheer chiffon fabric. She finishes off her outfit with strappy black heels and a bold red lip.

## 3. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/31204445_7400_550x1100.jpg)

Selena Gomez shows us that navy doesn’t always mean boring when it comes to the red carpet. Here she is wearing a navy Atelier Versace gown. It features studded trims, a thigh high split, and risqué bustier detail. If she was hoping to shake off her Disney image then she’s definitely succeeded here.

## 4. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/31204445_6980_550x1100.jpg)

Jennifer Lawrence is another celebrity who has opted to wear navy on the red carpet. She wore this Christian Dior gown while promoting ‘The Hunger Games: Catching Fire’. The bandeau style dress features an asymmetrical hem and sheer skirt detail. Teamed with her cropped hairdo and dark nail polish, the overall look comes off quite rock’n’roll.

## 5. Jennifer Garner

![Jennifer Garner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/31204445_1418_550x1100.jpg)

Jennifer Garner looks fresh faced wearing a draped navy gown by Vionnet. The dress has a simple, column silhouette with slight draping at the waist. A bit of bling and a bright pink lip helps light up her look.

## 6. Naomie Harris

![Naomie Harris](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/31204446_6283_550x1100.jpg)

Here, Naomie Harris wears a plunging navy gown by Vionnet. It features net detailing and cape-like sleeves. She keeps the accessories to a minimum with a slim bangle and small box clutch. It’s quite a striking gown that only needs minimal styling.

## 7. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/31204452_5774_550x1100.jpg)

A navy coat is a wardrobe staple. Alexa Chung wears her navy coat over a black velvet mini dress. She also wears T-bar shoes and carries a chain link handbag. A tousled hairdo and [natural looking makeup](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/benefits-of-the-natural-look/) completes this effortlessly stylish look.

## 8. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/31204452_4290_550x1000.jpg)

Miranda Kerr does off-duty style in this outfit of a printed dress and draped navy coat. It’s quite a polished look, especially when combined with nude pumps and a structured ladylike handbag. Little details like the matching red lipstick and nail polish also helps bring the entire look together.

## 9. Rosie Huntington-Whitely

![Rosie Huntington-Whitely](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/31204453_2781_592x888.jpg)

Rosie Huntington-Whitely works a typical model-off-duty look in skinny jeans and a navy coat. She also wears ankle boots and is toting a structured handbag. This outfit is ideal for winter and one that you could easily take inspiration from.

These are just some celebrities who have embraced navy outfits. Navy gowns seemed to enjoy their moment in the spotlight on the red carpet, and navy dressing was just as chic off the red carpet as well. Which of these navy looks do you like best?
