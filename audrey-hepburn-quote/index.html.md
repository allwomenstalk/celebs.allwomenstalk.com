# A Fabulous Audrey Hepburn Quote for Life ...

There's an **Audrey Hepburn quote** for so many situations. Audrey Hepburn was more than elegant and beautiful. She had something enduring. Feminine and yet strong. There is a quote from Ms. Hepburn that really says it all:

“I believe in pink. I believe that laughing is the best calorie burner. I believe in kissing, kissing a lot. I believe in being strong when everything seems to be going wrong. I believe that happy girls are the prettiest girls. I believe that tomorrow is another day and I believe in miracles.”

This **Audrey Hepburn quote** shows some depth. It is possible to believe in the soft and pretty colour pink and be strong at the same time. It is also important to seek out happiness and pleasure in kissing and laughing. Being happy does make someone more attractive - it is true. Who doesn't feel drawn to happy people? This quote also talks about determination, strength, and hope. Hepburn has described herself here. She was quite elegant and feminine - just like the colour pink. She was also strong in the face of adversity and never lost hope. Breakfast at Tiffany's was originally supposed to feature Marilyn Monroe and yet because she was unable to do it, Hepburn took on the iconic role and made it her own.

We live in a different time now, but it is sometimes helpful to look back and see what people in the past believed. Ms. Hepburn's statement is both sweet and holds some eternal truth.
