# 7 Awesome Reasons to Love Shay Mitchell ...

There are so many fantastic reasons to love Shay Mitchell! The Pretty Little Liars star has become a huge success but she's never let it go to her head. She's sweet and caring and all about her fans, which is awesome. One of my favorite things about her is that she's constantly interacting with her fans. Check out some of the cool reasons to love Shay Mitchell.

## 1. Live Tweeting

Shay live-tweets through pretty much every new episode of Pretty Little Liars! She even uses the hashtag #PLLay, where fans can send her questions about the show. I love that she spills set secrets, behind the scenes photos, and tries her best to answer all of her fans. Whenever she does promo for Pretty Little Liars, she's really good at stopping to take pics with fans! This is just one of the reasons to love Shay Mitchell.

## 2. She's Gorgeous

When Shay gets flamed up for a red carpet event, it's not even fair how pretty she is. She's one of those people who just radiates positivity and therefore always looks gorgeous! If you follow her on Instagram, you know that she always posts gorgeous selfies! Often times, she rocks a makeup free face, proving just how naturally beautiful she is!

## 3. She Loves Her Fans

Shay is SO sweet when it comes to her fans. When she was the cover girl for Seventeen Magazine, I went to a signing at my local Barnes & Noble. Hundreds of fans showed up and Shay made sure she signed and posed for a picture with everyone. I love when celebrities show love and appreciation for the fans!

## 4. She's Really Talented

Shay is easily one of my favorite actresses from Pretty Little Liars. I think she's perfect for the role of Emily Fields! I love that she's constantly challenging herself in her acting. When she spoke of her character, she said that playing a lesbian on TV doesn't bother her at all. I love that about her!

## 5. She's Humble

Even though she's a major name now, she's never let the fame go to her head. Shay is far from thinking she's "too cool for school" and I really love that about her. I feel like Shay is really gracious and humble, she always acts like she could be your best friend!

## 6. Besties with Co-stars

I love seeing that the Pretty Little Liars stars are real-life best friends. Shay is constantly posting pictures with co-star Ashley Benson! The two even met up with Troian at Coachella! I don't know about you, but I'd love to be best friends with Shay and the rest of the Pretty Little Liars cast.

## 7. Blogger

Shay's got one of the coolest blogs around! I love keeping up with everything that she writes. She's always posting cool new fashion trends, recipes, and always asking for fans to contribute! If you follow her on her social media accounts like Twitter and Instagram (@shaymitch), she's always posting the link to her blog. I definitely recommend checking it out!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome reasons to love Shay Mitchell. She's crazy talented and I can't wait to see all of her upcoming projects! If you're a fan of Shay, what's your favorite thing about her?
