# 8 Fabulous Classic Looks Kate Middleton Would Wear to a Holiday Party ...

What To Wear To A Holiday Party – if this is a question you’ve been asking yourself lately, why not take a cue from one of the newest leading ladies herself, Kate Middleton. The Duchess of Cambridge has been awarded much praise from style watchers, as she effortlessly combines classic and modern dressing. If you’re unsure of what to wear to a holiday party, take a look at the following classic looks.

## 1. Alexander McQueen 3D Jacquard Knit Dress

![Alexander McQueen 3D Jacquard Knit Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hk/wd/h06okbe654c935d1e6cb9780585294_300x384.jpg)

**Price:** $970.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
With a figure like hers, Kate is made for fitted dresses such as these. This dress has a demure design with a high neckline, cap sleeves, and knee-length hemline. However, the punchy red colour, body-hugging fit, and jacquard texture are all daring and contemporary touches. Fittingly, the head designer at Alexander McQueen is Sarah Burton, who also designed Kate’s wedding dress.

## 2. Topshop Zip Back Crepe Dress

![Topshop Zip Back Crepe Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j1/d8/jvviv0xk54c935d2a65e8375797929_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/b8tUKC)
The Duchess of Cambridge has often been seen sporting neutral stone coloured dresses. This Topshop number is perfect for those wanting to imitate Kate’s style on a budget. It has flutter sleeves, slouchy pocket details, and a zip back fastening. When looking at what to wear to a holiday party, team it with colourful cocktail jewels and patent pumps for an elegant look.

## 3. Issa Gathered Silk-Jersey Dress

![Issa Gathered Silk-Jersey Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/og/i0/i6zql19554c935d3741ee226296990_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $465.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/z3ScYX)
Kate Middleton was the perfect advertisement for Issa when she wore one of their dresses at the public announcement of her engagement for Prince William. While the one she wore to that event was a dark royal blue, this one is a brighter aquamarine colour – perfect for a fun party look!

## 4. Diane Von Furstenberg Maja Silk Dress

_ **Price:** $585.00 at mytheresa.com_
This dress has a classically demure design, but comes in a punchy contemporary green colour. Kate was actually spotted wearing this dress on an official visit to Los Angeles, but I think it would make a fun party number as well. Dressed up with an embellished clutch and drop earrings, this dress would look even more divine.

## 5. Tibi One Shoulder Fine-Jersey Gown

![Tibi One Shoulder Fine-Jersey Gown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/yc/ofw6jfqu54c935d526064695949343_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $385.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/eaEQqA)
When looking at what to wear to a holiday party, maxi-length gowns are a stylish option. Being the future Queen of England, Kate will no doubt wear a few gowns in her lifetime. She’s been spotted wearing one-shouldered dresses, as well wearing the colour red. So why not put two and two together with this lovely party dress?

## 6. Topshop Ponte Pleated Bodycon Dress

![Topshop Ponte Pleated Bodycon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fh/vg/kp917a2754c935d5e2ef7627340659_267x397.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/K4o7g6)
A fitted dress with cap sleeves seems to be the Kate’s signature look. We all need a LBD in our wardrobes, and I’m thinking this one would look right at home in Kate’s wardrobe. It has diagonal pleat detailing at the front, and a zip fastening at the back. For a classic evening look, wear this dress with pearl jewellery and almond-toe glitter pumps.

## 7. Erdem Margot Ruched Lace Dress

![Erdem Margot Ruched Lace Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mz/ax/emvsmlsd54c935d6cb087944293628_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $2845.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/oDZDe7)
A lace dress is timeless. This Erdem dress is fit for a princess, what with its demure elbow-length sleeves and high neckline. It has a navy lace overlay, sheer sleeves, and is lined in nude silk. This dress is perfect for modern royalty (and has the price tag to match).

## 8. Issa Twist Front Dress

![Issa Twist Front Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iy/vm/yn0vz0ns54c935d7abb22697135810_300x368.jpg)

**Price:** $531.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Another one from the Issa brand, this dress is similar in colour to the one Kate wore at her engagement announcement. However, this one has shorter sleeves which is creates a much more casual appearance. This dress would look stylish worn with a heavy necklace, and patent pumps.

When it comes to what to wear to a holiday party, sticking to dresses in classic cuts is a stylish option. Take inspiration from Kate Middleton, now the Duchess of Cambridge, and inject some regal glamour into your holiday look. What are your best tips on what to wear to a holiday party?
