# 11 Celebrities Wearing Navy Outfits – Who Wore It Best?

Navy is certainly a popular colour at the moment, and there have been plenty of celebrities wearing navy outfits. Navy is a classic colour and this shade of blue is great for those wanting to wear dark colours without having to resort to basic black. The colour is versatile and can be worn in a range of styles, shapes, and fabrics. Take a look at these celebrities wearing navy outfits and let us know who you think rocked this blue hue the best.

## 1. Chloe Moretz

![Chloe Moretz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/1_chloe-moretz_550x1100.jpg)

Kicking things off on our list of celebrities wearing navy outfits is Chloe Moretz. Here she is wearing a smart navy wool jacket and skirt. She teams this ensemble with opaque stockings, pointed heels, and a ladylike handbag. Bookmark this look for some possible fall/winter outfit inspiration.

## 2. Kirsten Dunst

![Kirsten Dunst](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/2_kirsten-dunst_550x1100.jpg)

Kirsten Dunst shows us how to do navy dressing in this chic coat dress. She pairs her long-sleeved dress with fishnet stockings and two-tone strappy heels. A coat of bright red lipstick adds a dash of colour to this otherwise dark look.

## 3. Rooney Mara

![Rooney Mara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/3_rooney-mara_550x1100.jpg)

Navy is often associated with a timeless, polished take on dressing. However, Rooney Mara plays up the navy trend in a ruffled crop top and culottes by J.W. Anderson. Since the outfit is quite busy, she keeps the rest of her look fairly basic. Simple strappy black heels and a sleek up-do complete her look.

## 4. Dakota Fanning

![Dakota Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/4_dakota-fanning_550x1100.jpg)

Dakota Fanning works a youthful look in this navy two-piece. She teams a slightly cropped navy ribbed top with an embellished mini skirt. She also wears sheer black stockings and black booties. The red lipstick adds a bit of a glam touch to the entire outfit.

## 5. Florence Welch

![Florence Welch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/5_florence-welch_592x888.jpg)

Here, Florence Welch wears a floor-skimming navy gown with an embellished collar. There’s also some matching embellished scarf action going on around the neck. The thigh high split on the skirt prevents this outfit it from heading into ‘mumsy’ territory.

## 6. Liv Tyler

![Liv Tyler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/6_liv-tyler_592x888.jpg)

Liv Tyler works a polished look in this navy and black Christian Dior ensemble. She wears a navy bodice with black tailored pants and a black scalloped coat. Pointed black pumps and a compact clutch complete this feminine take on tuxedo dressing.

## 7. Lena Dunham

![Lena Dunham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/7_lena-dunham_592x888.jpg)

Lena Dunham hits the red carpet in this navy and white Roksanda Ilincic dress. She glams up this long-sleeved midi dress with some eye-catching gold pumps. This kind of dress could easily be worn during the day with neutral flats and then dressed up at night with some killer heels and jewellery.

## 8. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/8_taylor-swift_592x888.jpg)

Taylor Swift works a retro ladylike look in this full-skirted navy dress. The dress has a bit of a '50s inspired feel to it, but she teams it with some very modern embellished toe-cap heels. Oversized stud earrings, a red lip, and perfectly coiffed hairdo complete this polished ladylike look.

## 9. Drew Barrymore

![Drew Barrymore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/9_drew-barrymore_592x888.jpg)

Here, Drew Barrymore rocks a navy tuxedo with a vintage-look t-shirt and brightly coloured heels. The t-shirt is an unexpected twist and gives this eveningwear look a bit of a dressed down feel. She also totes a cute pink purse.

## 10. Shailene Woodley

![Shailene Woodley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/10_shailene-woodley_592x888.jpg)

If you’re after a more modern way to wear navy then take a leaf out of Shailene Woodley’s book with a chic navy leather dress. The basic shift-style cut of this dress is quite timeless and could be styled in a range of different ways. Here, she sticks to a simple look by teaming her dress with neutral strappy heels.

## 11. Chloe Sevigny

![Chloe Sevigny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/08/11_chloe-sevigny_592x888.jpg)

Chloe Sevigny keeps it casual in this get-up of a glittery navy top and printed shorts. She also wears white lace-up shoes and carries a compact black and white bucket bag. This is the kind of outfit that you could recreate for a cool and casual daytime look.

This season, navy comes in all shapes and styles. These are just a few examples of how celebrities have been rocking this classic blue hue. What do you think? Which of these celebrity looks do you like best?
