# 25 Hottest Supermodels ...

Supermodels are a bunch of beautiful creatures, almost like a separate species from the rest of us mortals. I for one, absolutely adore them and feel so awed when I watch them rock the catwalks and own their respective looks in full power. So if I was to pick 25 of the hottest supermodels, this would be the qualifying list!

## 25. [Stephanie Seymour](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/stephanie-seymour/)

![Stephanie Seymour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ge/ox/v7r04f7i54c8e4f0490d2222033159_500x375.jpg)

Image source: [alwaysgirls.com](https://goo.gl/ecMSXi)
This American model was a raging hit throughout the 90's. She has appeared in more than 300 magazine covers and has been shot by some of the world's most renowned photographers. She also appeared in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit calendar and later on for Playboy. Not surprisingly, she was a Victoria's Secret angel too! I especially liked Stephanie's lean yet curvy figure, something which set her apart from her contemporaries. Her jaw structure and sharp features were something else altogether. She later on went ahead and did a bit of acting as well. Her latest modelling assignment involved a shoot for Salvatore Ferragamo for their Fall/Winter '08 season.

## 24. [Helena Christensen](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/helena-christensen/)

![Helena Christensen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m7/z1/fegt1bh054c8e4f17bdf7696979300_500x375.jpg)

Image source: [trailerfest.files.wordpress.com](https://trailerfest.files.wordpress.com/2008/01/helena_christensen_23-1024.jpg)
What a gorgeous face! I love Helena, fondly nicknamed the "Danish Angel" by her peers, although she is half Danish and Peruvian ethnicity. After representing Denmark in the Miss Universe pageant in 1986, Helena went ahead and began her modelling career in Paris. As is usual for most supermodels, Helena was also a Victoria's Secret angel, featuring in signature campaigns. Her best feature are her eyes, I love the color and intensity. She has one son, and is now a hands-on mother. Her addiction? Cheese!!! Ha, who would've guessed!

## 23. [Angela Lindvall](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/angela-lindvall/)

_ Image source: xmages.net_
This American supermodel and a VS angel has a smoking hot figure and epitomizes the "american-girl" look. I like her casual country look which she maintains even though she's worked for the biggest european fashion houses. She's also graced the covers of numerous fashion magazines. Apart from this, she was also featured on my favorite clothing brand Zara's 2007 campaign. Angela currently has been signed on a renewed contract with Jimmy Choo and has also designed a line of eco-friendly clothing, where all proceeds go to charity.

## 22. [Iman](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/iman/)

![Iman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w6/rl/t6qtbi6o54c8e4f3d2bf3519425290_333x500.jpg)

Image source: [1.bp.blogspot.com](http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_FW86_jO7k_A/RxbGRC7k8yI/AAAAAAAAM_U/p-0MHe2XT_g/s1600/Iman_ProjectRunwayCanada.jpg)
Iman was born in Mogadishu, Somalia and went to Nairobi University for her education. While at university, she was discovered by American photographer Peter Beard, and subsequently moved to the United States to begin her modelling career. Her first assignment was for Vogue magazine, and Iman hasn't looked back since. Iman has breath-taking features, from her dark exotic skin tone to her fabulous structure. I think her slender lean frame are complete supermodel material. Iman is now married to David Bowie and lives between Manhattan and London.

## 21. [Laetitia Casta](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/laetitia-casta/)

![Laetitia Casta](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zj/se/nk4e00u954c8e4f4bad0e799928216_500x340.jpg)

Image source: [madrie.m.a.pic.centerblog.net](http://madrie.m.a.pic.centerblog.net/thrc07b2.jpg)
Laetitia was raised in a small village of France. At age 15 while she was vacationing with her family in Corsica, she was discovered by an agent working for the Madison Models' Agency. After a few weeks in Paris Laetitia was already walking the runway for Jean Paul Gaultier. With modelling and print campaign contracts swiftly following, Laetitia had truly arrived. Her landmark accomplishments include being featured on the Pirelli calendar, and being the face of Dior, Chanel and Guess. I think she is extremely endearing and her warm expression, remind me of a village belle. However, her versatility stands out with the ease in which she transforms into an haute couture goddess in front of the camera. In 2010, she opened the spectacular Louis Vuitton Fall/Winter 2010 show.

## 20. [Elle MacPherson](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/elle-macpherson/)

![Elle MacPherson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o4/bv/uqq92ffe54c8e4f5ceb44225628093_380x500.jpg)

Image source: [modelsblog.info](http://www.modelsblog.info/content/2009/11/elle-macpherson_2.jpg)
This 6 feet tall Australian model was discovered in Aspen, Colorado. Her plans of studying pre-law took a backseat once Click modelling agency signed her up and she was on her way to fame and glamour. The leggy Elle has a body to die for, with an international face and with having been in the center of the fashion world. She later on went ahead to work in a bunch of movies too, from Alice in Wonderland to playing Batman's object of affection in Batman and Robin. Elle also possesses excellent entrepreneur skills, having launched her own lingerie label amongst many other brands.

## 19. [Christy Turlington](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/christy-turlington/)

![Christy Turlington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ed/a9/tcsckwrm54c8e4f6cce12025142938_500x375.jpg)

This American beauty made the fashion fraternity sit up and take notice when she entered the scene in the late 1980's. Being widely recognised in the business, Christy is known for her professional yet warm attitude towards everyone. Now that's what I like about divas! After signing up for multi-million dollar contracts with various couture giants and fragrance brands, Christy ruled the roost. Later on she launched her own range of skin care and beauty products, titled "Sundari", meaning beautiful woman in Sanskrit, an ancient Indian language.

## 18. [Tyra Banks](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/tyra-banks/)

![Tyra Banks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jk/sn/w7yknfb054c8e4f7b5a70346700576_392x500.jpg)

Image source: [images.starpulse.com](https://goo.gl/lYiGDF)
Tyra is an African-American goddess! Born in California, Tyra began modelling in the 11th grade, after which she moved to Paris to try her luck. Designers were so enraptured by her that she was signed on for 25 shows!!! That's quite a feat for a newcomer in my opinion. Tyra was also the first African-American supermodel to grace the cover of the Victoria's Secret catalogue. Tyra has a beautiful face and deep-set eyes, which quite reminds me of an Egyptian goddess. Subsequent to her modelling days, Tyra now runs a very successful show called "America's Next Top Model".

## 17. [Eva Herzigova](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/eva-herzigova/)

![Eva Herzigova](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gr/fn/o7ipsi8j54c8e4f8d9200424537571_500x375.jpg)

Image source: [pulsarmedia.eu](https://www.rury-kominowe.pl/)
Yes, she's European as well. The Czech beauty's claim to fame began at age 16 when she won a beauty contest in Prague. She was famously dubbed the "Marilyn Monroe of the 1990's". She appeared for the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit calendar, the VS catalogue and also became covergirl for Playboy magazine! Eva has endorsed some of the biggest brands such as Alberta Ferretti, Dior, Louis Vuitton, Burberry, Dom Perignon and Givenchy amongst many others. Eva's blonde hair and plump lips especially when painted red, completely remind me of the luscious Ms. Monroe, thus justifying her nickname.

## 16. [Carolyn Murphy](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/carolyn-murphy/)

_ Image source: 3.bp.blogspot.com_
Carolyn was born in Fort Walton Beach, Florida. Having spent her initial years in a trailer park, she was discovered by a local modelling agency in her teens. She then moved to Milan to kick-start her career. Things went into high power when a hair job gone wrong caused her to cut her hair super short, thus turning her into a trend-setter. She then posed as covergirl for major magazines such as Harper's Bazaar and Vogue. Her other ad campaigns included Dolce & Gabbana, Anne Klein, Tiffany & Co. etc.

## 15. [Cindy Crawford](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/cindy-crawford/)

_ Image source: artknowledgenews.com_
I love her name. I love her face. I love everything about her! Cindy Crawford, with that beauty spot on her face and super cool attitude created quite a flutter when she arrived. During the 80's and 90's, Cindy was one of the supermodels who was splashed all over the place, from runways to magazine covers and ad campaigns. The most entrancing quality about her? She is a beauty with brains. Yes, modelling by day and studying chemical engineering (heavy stuff) on scholarship is what Cindy did. Legendary designer Karl Lagerfeld has been quoted "She is a classic beauty and the all-American dream girl". I don't disagree at all!

![Claudia Schiffer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zp/r6/uwauts2a54c8e4fbbd7c8549484918_500x375.jpg)

After moving to Milan and a few assignments later, she joined the ranks of the world's supermodels when Karl Lagerfeld appointed her as the face of Chanel. Her tall figure, blond hair and blue eyes immediately got her international recognition. Claudia has done her fair share of ad campaigns, magazines and of course, countless walks on the runway. Perhaps at the cost of coming off as a snob, Claudia once said "Supermodels, like we once were, don't exist any more". Ouch.

## 13. [Linda Evangelista](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/linda-evangelista/)

![Linda Evangelista](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xa/em/liklyxt854c8e4fce7408142361833_363x500.jpg)

Image source: [fanpix.net](https://fanpix.famousfix.com/picture-gallery/791/1624791-linda-evangelista-picture.htm)
Linda is Canadian, of Italian origin. Raised in a traditional catholic family, Linda decided she wanted to model ever since she was 12. A few years later after being discovered by an agent, she moved to New York to sign up with Elite Model Agency and then moved to Paris. Along with Christy Turlington, Naomi Campbell and Cindy Crawford, Linda is regarded one of the true, few supermodels who changed the face of fashion in the late 80's and early 90's. She was also known for her dramatic hairstyle changes, which turned out to be big trends. Her famous yet controversial quote is "We supermodels don't wake up for less than $10,000 a day". Arrogant? Maybe. True? Oh yes.

![Heidi Klum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/gb/ofxoyq4854c8e4fde16ef476796382_375x500.jpg)

Heidi is a German American supermodel, entrepreneur, television host and producer. After being discovered in Cologne and winning a nationwide modelling contest, Heidi quickly rose in her career. Her milestones were being appointed as a Victoria's Secret angel, and posing for numerous other ad campaigns, and walking the runway. After 13 years Heidi decided to hang up her wings and quit Victoria's Secret. After her success, Heidi then became a television hostess, producer and judge in the popular show "Project Runway" in which fashion designers compete for the winning spot. Heidi is married to British musician Seal and has 3 children with him, and 1 child from her relationship with Flavio Briatore.

She has appeared on the covers of Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit calendar. A slew of ad campaigns with major brands also followed. I love Bridget's straw blonde hair and soft features. Makes her look like a summer nymph.

Whoever said that beauty cannot be used for a cause!?
