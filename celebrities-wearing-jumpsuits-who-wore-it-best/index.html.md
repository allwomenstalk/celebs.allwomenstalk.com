# 9 Celebrity Jumpsuit Looks That Actually Work \(and How to Copy Them\)

Jumpsuits are enjoying a major resurgence, and for good reason—they're the ultimate one-and-done outfit. But as any fashion lover knows, pulling off a jumpsuit can be tricky. If you want to master this look, checking out how your favorite celebrities wear their jumpsuits is a great way to pick up some [styling tips](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-a-jumpsuit/). From [red carpet](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/red-carpet-fashion-trends/) glamour to casual street style, these stars show us exactly how to rock the trend (and what to avoid). Check out these celebrities wearing jumpsuits and let us know who you think wore theirs best.

| Celebrity | Style | Key Accessory |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dree Hemingway | Chambray | Platform Heels |
| Miley Cyrus | Tuxedo-inspired | Gold Jewelry |
| Charlize Theron | Burnt Orange | Black Blazer |

## 1. Dree Hemingway

![Dree Hemingway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/1_dree-hemingway_592x888.jpg)

There are plenty of celebrities wearing jumpsuits both on and off the red carpet. Here, Dree Hemingway works a relaxed look in a chambray jumpsuit. To stop it from looking too much like something a mechanic would wear, she teams her jumpsuit with platform heels, a cute clutch, and clear sunglasses.

## 2. Jessie J

![Jessie J](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/2_jessie-j_592x888.jpg)

Jessie J makes a statement in a busy printed jumpsuit. She works a pretty polished look by teaming it with a gleaming white tailored coat and structured black handbag. Blue booties and a coat of bright red nail polish complete her look.

## 3. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/3_miley-cyrus_592x888.jpg)

Miley Cyrus works a more tailored angle in this strapless jumpsuit. It's a bit tuxedo-inspired, with a white band around the bodice and a fitted cut throughout. She accessorises her jumpsuit with some strappy heels and gold jewellery. Bright red lipstick completes this evening wear-inspired look.

## 4. Kristen Wiig

![Kristen Wiig](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/4_kristen-wiig_592x888.jpg)

If there's anything we've learned from celebrities wearing jumpsuits, it's that they can be dressy wardrobe options. Kristen Wiig's lace [Stella McCartney](https://www.stellamccartney.com/) jumpsuit is a dressed up take on the trend and you could easily imagine it being worn to cocktail parties and night time events. Since the jumpsuit is quite statement-making, she keeps the rest of her jewellery and accessories to a minimum.

## 5. Charlize Theron

![Charlize Theron](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/5_charlize-theron_592x888.jpg)

Charlize Theron dabbles in a bit of power dressing when styling her jumpsuit. She teams her burnt orange jumpsuit with a black blazer, slim black clutch, and strappy heels. The plunging neckline on the jumpsuit and a long gold chain necklace add a bit of glamour to the entire look.

### Perfect Occasions for a Jumpsuit:
- **Cocktail parties**
- **Casual weekend outings**
- **Formal evening events**
- **Office power dressing**

## 6. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/6_emma-stone_550x1100.jpg)

Emma Stone works a colourful night time look in a speckled green jumpsuit. It has a tailored fit, much like the [structured style](#3) seen on Miley Cyrus, and also features a bit of halterneck action, as well as a cheeky plunging neckline. She teams her green jumpsuit with suede platform heels.

## 7. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/7_jessica-alba_550x1100.jpg)

Jessica Alba works a monochrome look in a black and white jumpsuit. It's quite a demure style of jumpsuit, with a high neck and long sleeves. She teams it with a studded black clutch and black satin shoes. Bright pink lipstick adds a pop of colour to her overall look.

## 8. Carrie Underwood

![Carrie Underwood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/8_carrie-underwood_550x1100.jpg)

Carrie Underwood goes for a more glamorous look in her jumpsuit. It features sheer paneling, draped details around the bodice, and a tailored fit throughout the leg. She teams her jumpsuit with a compact clutch, two-tone heels, and some blinged-out hoop earrings.

## 9. Nina Dobrev

![Nina Dobrev](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/9_nina-dobrev_550x1100.jpg)

Nina Dobrev makes a statement in this bright red jumpsuit. The draped, one shoulder style definitely hints at glamour. She teams her jumpsuit with a small studded clutch and gold wedge heels.

### Quick Jumpsuit Styling Tips:
- **Define your waist**: Use a belt or choose a tailored cut to avoid a boxy look.
- **Choose the right length**: Ensure the hem works with your choice of shoes.
- **Layer it up**: A blazer or denim jacket can completely transform the vibe.
