# Kyle Richards Not Sure about Being a Housewife…

With The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills being picked up for a second season, we would have expected the same fun, the same melodrama and the same cast as last time right? Well, if Kyle Richards is to be believed, things could be a little different this time.

"The show has been picked up for a second season, but I'm not sure if I'm coming back yet," Richards has been quoted as saying on Sunday at the Staples Center.

Richards further added that she might not be the only one who is wondering about their return to the show.

"We're not really sure who's coming back yet, so we'll see," she said.

Richards further said that the decision to come back was not only her own but her family’s too, "Right now we're deciding. It's a family decision between my husband, my kids and I, and we have a little time to decide and I'm kind of deciding what I want to do."

Not that I am the biggest fan of the show, but I do wonder how much of the original cast will be retained for the show and how many new faces we’ll be seeing.
