# All the Things That Make Landon Liboiron so Lovable ...

There are so many reasons to love Landon Liboiron. He's an incredible actor who's remained humble and genuine in this industry. I think he's got such a long career ahead of him and I can't wait to see all of his future projects! Check out some of the reasons to love Landon Liboiron and then let me know why YOU love him!

## 1. Hemlock Grove

If you haven't watched Hemlock Grove, what exactly are you waiting for?! I don't why or how it took me THIS long to watch it but once I started it, I was hooked! I went through both seasons in just a week! Landon's character, Peter, has a pretty complicated history but you can't help but love him. Both seasons are available to stream on Netflix! This is just one of many reasons to love Landon Liboiron.

## 2. He Loves His Fans

Landon always makes sure to show his fans some love. He tries his best to interact with fans on Twitter (@77Flipped) and give them some insight on his day to day life! He even posts tons of awesome Hemlock Grove-related photos, so I definitely recommend checking it out! In interviews, Landon always acknowledges fans and thanks them for their unconditional support with the show.

## 3. He Used to Be on Degrassi

Long-time Landon fans probably know him for his role as Declan Coyne on 'Degrassi: The Next Generation.' I was a huge fan of that show and Landon's character. He definitely brought a certain charm to the role, so that even if you didn't like him, you still rooted for his happy ending. I think it takes a very talented actor to do that!

## 4. His 'dream Job' Description

When asked what his dream job would be, Landon gave an amazing answer. He said, "A job that doesn’t feel like a job and is full of imagination." That would have been my answer, too! After all, if you do what you love, you never have to work a day in your life, right? He was spot on with this.

## 5. He Admits to Procrastination

Like the majority of us, Landon deals with the consequences of being a procrastinator. In a recent interview, Landon called procrastination his "dirty little habit." I couldn't have said it better myself! Looks like we have something in common, Landon!

## 6. He's Humble

Even though he's making a huge name for himself with all of his recent projects, Landon's never let the success get to his head. "I will always approach life from a small-town vibe. It makes experiences more fantastical," he said. He's also very grateful that his family is not part of the Hollywood world! "Every time I get to go back home, I get to be Landon. It’s humbling to be able to have that as my home and be able to go back to. Nothing has really changed for me."

## 7. His Musical Abilities

In case you didn't know, Landon plays the guitar! "I do take a lot of comfort in music and being able to come home and thrash out on the guitar," he's said. He's also cited The White Stripes and Tom Waits as his favorite artists. "I’m not really picky when it comes to music, as long as it’s honest," says Landon. I feel the same way! I'd love to see Landon explore his musical side!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome reasons to love Landon Liboiron. If you're a fan of his, what's your favorite thing about him?
