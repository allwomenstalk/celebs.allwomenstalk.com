# Emma Watson's Red Carpet Looks for Harry Potter ...

I love how Emma Watson has the ability to make us all go WOW every single time we see her on the Red Carpet! Now that Harry Potter has officially come to an end (can't wait to watch the last installment btw), let's pay a quick ode to some of the amazing dresses Emma has sported on her promotional tours for Harry Potter and The Deathly Hallows: Part 2, starting from my least favorite to my most favorite.

## 6. Emma Watson at the "Late Show with David Letterman"

Emma flaunted her legs in a sparkling Balmain mini at the "Late Show with David Letterman". Kudos for trying something edgy but I'm not completely sold. I do love the Louboutin wedges though and her hair and makeup as always are flawless!
_ Photo Credit: backseatcuddler.com_

## 5. Emma Watson at the Today Show

How adorable is this baby blue Prada pleated shift dress on Emma Watson. I love the color and the Peter Pan collar. Emma sure does a fine job of channeling the 60's!

## 4. Emma Watson at the London Photocall

Maybe this look was a little ott for a photocall but it was so freakin' fabulous that I'm not complaining. I love everything about this Rafael Lopez dress from the gold bodice to the lace to the adorable tulle ruffled skirt!

![Emma Watson at the London Photocall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/07/emma-watson-photocall-352x560_352x560.jpg)

Photo Credit: [contactmusic.com](https://www.contactmusic.com/emma-watson/news/emma-watson-shines-in-rafael-lopez-dress-at-photo-call_1231530)

## 3. Emma Watson at the New York Premiere

The only word that popped to mind when I saw this look was, dramatic and oh how I love it! With that slicked back hair, the smoky eyes (which perhaps should have been a tad toned down) and of course that radical Bottega Veneta dress, Emma looks like an edgy rockstar!

![Emma Watson at the New York Premiere](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/07/emma-bottega-355x560_355x560.jpg)

Photo Credit: [hollywoodcrush.mtv.com](https://www.mtv.com/)

## 2. Emma Watson at the World Premiere- after Party

While I'm all for experimenting, I think my favorite kind of look on Emma Watson will always be the demure, pretty lass look and this adorable Elie Saab dress fits the bill perfectly! She looks effortlessly elegant and so incredibly gorgeous!

![Emma Watson at the World Premiere- after Party](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/07/emma-elie-373x560_373x560.jpg)

Photo Credit: [redcarpet-fashionawards.com](https://goo.gl/dpCNOJ)

## 1. Emma Watson at the World Premiere in London

I don't even think it's a surprise that this is my most favorite of all of her looks for Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2. This divine Oscar de la Renta tulle ballgown was in my opinion the perfect dress for this occasion and Emma literally looked like she had stepped out of a fairy-tale or something. Breathtaking! Then again it is an Oscar de la Renta masterpiece...
_ Photo Credit: hollywoodlife.com_

I just had one of those moments again where I can't believe that this is the same bushy haired little girl from the first movie. I sure am going to miss Harry Potter and the whole gang! Before I go on babbling, tell me which of these Emma Watson red carpet looks was your favorite?
