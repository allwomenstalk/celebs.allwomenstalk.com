# 10 Celebrities Wearing Denim Jackets  Who Wore It Best?

Denim jackets are a well worn favourite so it’s no wonder that there are plenty of celebrities wearing denim jackets out there. There’s a lot to love about denim jackets and they make for great off-duty outfit options. They look good worn with anything from summery dresses to cropped trousers. Take a look at these celebrities wearing denim jackets and let us know who you think wore theirs the best.

## 1. Cameron Diaz

![Cameron Diaz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/1_cameron-diaz_600x944.jpg)

First up on our list of celebrities wearing denim jackets is Cameron Diaz. She tackles a double denim look by teaming her faded jacket with dark blue skinny jeans. She breaks up the look with a white t-shirt and an eye-catching red bag. Dark sunglasses and wedge sandals complete her look.

## 2. Cara Delevingne

![Cara Delevingne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/2_cara-delevingne_592x888.jpg)

Cara Delevingne shows off her cool girl credentials in this street-smart look. She wears her denim jacket layered over a hooded sweatshirt and teamed with light skinny jeans and hi-top trainers. She accessorises her outfit with a colourful beanie and long pendant necklace.

## 3. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/3_emma-stone1_592x888.jpg)

Emma Stone opts for a denim jacket in a darker wash. She sticks to a fairly timeless look of a striped top, black skinny jeans, and ankle boots. The ripped jeans do add a bit of edge to the entire look. She accessorises with a black beanie, light tan bag, and wayfarer sunglasses.

## 4. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/4_miranda-kerr_592x888.jpg)

Miranda Kerr shows us how to make a faded denim jacket look smart and polished. She teams her denim jacket with a white collared shirt, black skater skirt, and taupe ankle boots. A light brown hat and animal print tote bag complete the outfit.

## 5. Reese Witherspoon

![Reese Witherspoon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/5_reese-witherspoon_592x888.jpg)

Reese Witherspoon works a fairly on-trend look in a light denim jacket, short printed dress, and light ankle boots. She keeps her outfit looking fairly casual with oversized sunglasses and a studded tan handbag. This is the kind of outfit you could wear on those cooler spring and summer days.

## 6. Hailee Steinfeld

![Hailee Steinfeld](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/6_hailee-steinfeld_592x888.jpg)

Hailee Steinfeld opts for a fun look by teaming her denim jacket with a brightly printed dress. The bright yellow clutch ties in well with the print of the dress. While she could have gone for a more feminine shoe, she adds a bit of edge to her look with a pair of black biker boots.

## 7. Dianna Agron

![Dianna Agron](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/7_dianna-agron_592x888.jpg)

Toughen up your summer maxi dresses by popping on a denim jacket. Here, Dianna Agron wears a black and white printed maxi with an acid washed denim jacket. She accessorises with some colourful earrings and dark sunglasses.

## 8. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/8_rihanna_592x888.jpg)

Rihanna’s outfit screams tomboy style. She pulls off a case of double denim by wearing her denim jacket tied around her waist and with distressed boyfriend jeans. She completes the look with a great sweatshirt, oversized sunglasses, and Timberland boots.

## 9. Nicole Richie

![Nicole Richie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/9_nicole-richie_592x888.jpg)

Nicole Richie uses her denim jacket to dress up her exercise gear. She teams her denim jacket with a plain black top, tights, and running shoes. The oversized sunglasses, beanie, and designer handbag all add some glamour to her look.

## 10. January Jones

![January Jones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/10_january-jones_350x700.jpg)

January Jones teamed her denim jacket with a lace top, leather leggings, and stiletto ankle booties. A bright red lips and high up-do completed her look.

These are just a few celebrities who have been spotted wearing denim jackets. From the red carpet to the gym, it goes to show that the denim jacket is a versatile piece of clothing. Which of these celebrity looks do you like best?
