# 11 Celebrities Wearing a Striped T\-shirt – Who Wore It Best?

The striped tee is a classic and we’ve spotted quite a few celebrities wearing a striped t-shirt out and about. While celebrities are known for trying out all the latest trends, that doesn’t mean that their wardrobes aren’t full of chic staples as well. The striped t-shirt is timeless and no wardrobe would be complete with out it. Check out these celebrities wearing a striped t-shirt and how they styled this wardrobe staple.

## 1. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031422_7054_592x888.jpg)

Kicking off our list of celebrities wearing a striped t-shirt is the ever so stylish Alexa Chung. Here she wears a basic striped Breton tee casually tucked into a pair of skinny jeans, cuffed at the ankles. She accessorises with a black floppy hat and espadrilles for a cool and casual off duty look.

## 2. Ashley Madekwe

![Ashley Madekwe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031422_4422_592x888.jpg)

Ashley Madekwe glams up a casual summer uniform of a striped t-shirt and denim shorts by teaming them with strappy metallic heels. She also adds some extra glamour with a Union Jack clutch and gold hoop earrings.

## 3. Nikki Reed

![Nikki Reed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031422_5166_550x1100.jpg)

Nikki Reed is another celebrity who knows how to dress up a striped t-shirt. Here she hits the red carpet in a grey striped t-shirt and leather pencil skirt. She also wears assorted pendant necklaces and slim strappy heels. This polished outfit could easily transition from day to night, depending on the accessories you style it with.

## 4. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031421_1623_599x900.jpg)

Miranda Kerr works a casual off-duty look in a striped t-shirt and skinny jeans. It’s a classic outfit combo that she teams with a pair of must-have wedge sneakers, an oversized black tote, and oversized sunglasses. Thin necklaces and assorted bracelets finish off her daytime attire.

## 5. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031421_2724_438x700.jpg)

Here, Olivia Palermo mixed feminine and masculine trends. She teams her striped t-shirt with a white skirt and a taupe handbag. A pair of silver metallic brogues adds a bit of a mannish touch to an otherwise girlie outfit.

## 6. Chloe Sevigny

![Chloe Sevigny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031422_6351_592x888.jpg)

Striped t-shirts and denim are a classic combination. Here, Chloe Sevigny steps out in a summer-ready uniform of a striped t-shirt and denim cut-off shorts. Wayfarer sunglasses, a small black crossbody bag, and hi-top sneakers complete this casually stylish look.

## 7. Liv Tyler

![Liv Tyler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031423_2716_592x888.jpg)

Here, Liv Tyler puts together an outfit of classic wardrobe pieces. She teams a striped top with black jeans, ballet flats, and a timeless trench coat. This is an ideal tran-seasonal outfit and would last you season after season.

## 8. Sienna Miller

![Sienna Miller](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031423_1911_592x888.jpg)

Sienna Miller incorporates the striped theme from head to toe by wearing her striped t-shirt with striped heels. She teams them with black skinny jeans, a stand out red cape coat, and an effortlessly chic fedora.

## 9. Diane Kruger

![Diane Kruger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031423_4456_405x795.jpg)

Diana Kruger works a relaxed daytime look in a slouchy striped t-shirt and a floral printed skirt. She pairs them with a [black satchel](https://bags.allwomenstalk.com/best-black-satchel-bags/), printed flats, and accessorises with a slouchy cap and oversized sunglasses.

## 10. Fearne Cotton

![Fearne Cotton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031423_1048_592x888.jpg)

English television and radio presenter Fearne Cotton works a playful look in a playsuit. She teams a bold black and white striped t-shirt with a fitted black playsuit and mauve pointed heels. She also injects an extra bit of colour into her look with a bright pink lipstick.

## 11. Kylie Minogue

![Kylie Minogue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30031424_6724_550x1100.jpg)

Singer Kylie Minogue opts for a bold red and white striped tee rather than the classic black or blue and white combinations. She teams her striped top with a denim pencil skirt and strappy espadrilles. The outfit has a certain nautical-inspired feel to it.

These are just some celebrities we’ve spotted wearing the classic striped t-shirt. It’s a versatile wardrobe piece and is perfect for putting together a range of off-duty looks. Which of these celeb looks do you like best?
