# 9 Celebrities Wearing Printed Sweaters  Who Wore It Best?

Printed sweaters are one of the biggest trends right now and we’ve spotted a fair few celebrities wearing printed sweaters out and about. A printed sweater is a great statement maker and is perfect for jazzing up a casual outfit. While celebrities are more likely to wear the latest designer wear, that doesn’t mean that you can’t recreate the look on a budget. Check out your favourite affordable fashion stores for a range of more budget-friendly styles. Take a look at these celebrities wearing printed sweaters and let us know who you think wore theirs the best.

## 1. Hailee Steinfeld

![Hailee Steinfeld](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/05194907_9686_592x888.jpg)

First up on our list of celebrities wearing printed sweaters is Hailee Steinfeld. Hailee works a mostly black and white look in a black printed sweater, graphic print skirt, collared shirt, and knee high boots. The metallic lip print on the sweater is by far the focal point of the outfit. This is a preppy schoolgirl inspired look but with a risqué edge.

## 2. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/05194905_8682_592x888.jpg)

Rihanna works a fresh street style look in cult Kenzo pieces. She wears an Eiffel Tower print sweater with cut-off denim shorts, leopard print boots, and a bright green baseball cap. A structured handbag is a ladylike addition to the look.

## 3. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/05194904_9323_550x1100.jpg)

Jessica Alba works a casual look in ripped skinny jeans and a printed sweater. She teams them with black ankle boots, dark sunglasses, and a quilted side bag. Animal motifs have been pretty popular lately, so it’s definitely a trend to try this season.

## 4. Alessandra Ambrosio

![Alessandra Ambrosio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/05194904_4918_592x888.jpg)

Model Alessandra Ambrosio works a sporty casual look in coloured jeans and a white tiger print sweater. Wedge sneakers and wayfarer style sunglasses complete this relaxed off-duty outfit.

## 5. Emma Roberts

![Emma Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/05194905_4920_592x888.jpg)

Another celebrity sporting an animal print sweater is actress Emma Roberts. Here she teams a grey printed sweater with light blue jeans. The black patent Mary Janes add a preppy touch to the outfit which she also wears with a backpack.

## 6. Diane Kruger

![Diane Kruger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/05194904_2323_592x888.jpg)

Always one to stay on top of trends, Diane Kruger wears her printed sweater with cuffed boyfriend jeans. The animal print is off centre and covers the sleeve of her sweater. She accessorises her outfit with ladylike cat eye sunglasses, pointed flats, and a boxy cross body bag.

## 7. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/05194905_9753_592x888.jpg)

Taylor Swift does preppy dressing to a tee in a black and white polka dot print sweater. She teams it with cropped burgundy pants and bow flats. A pearl necklace, wayfarer sunglasses and leather satchel add the finishing touches to her outfit.

## 8. Rita Ora

![Rita Ora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/05194906_1221_592x888.jpg)

When it comes to fashion forward outfits, Rita Ora seems to be our girl these days. Here she wears an oversized printed sweater with thigh-high black boots. The boots definitely adds some edge and balances out the slouchiness of the oversized sweater.

## 9. Gwen Stefani

![Gwen Stefani](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/05194906_8311_592x888.jpg)

Never one to blend in to the crowd, Gwen Stefani knows how to rock a statement print. Here she wears a leopard print knit sweater over layered black basics and with tall black boots. A bright coloured lipstick completes this daytime look.

Celebrities love a good trend and these are just a few that have been rocking printed sweaters lately. What do you think of this season’s must-have printed sweater? Which of these celebrity looks do you like best?
