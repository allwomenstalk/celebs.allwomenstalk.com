# 10 Celebrities Wearing Lace Dresses  Who Wore It Best?

There are plenty of celebrities wearing lace dresses on and off the red carpet. The lace dress is a wardrobe staple these days and can be dressed up or down to create a range of different looks. Slouchy lace dresses can look casually boho while structured dresses can appear much more prim and proper. No matter which way you go, the lace dress is a fashion favourite. Check out these celebrities wearing lace dresses and let us know who you think wore theirs the best.

## 1. Eva Mendes

![Eva Mendes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/1_eva-mendes_592x888.jpg)

Eva Mendes is the first of our celebrities wearing lace dresses. Here she wears a ladylike pink and yellow lace dress. She teams her lace dress with burgundy heels, an embellished gold clutch, drop earrings, and assorted rings.

## 2. Emma Roberts

![Emma Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/2_emma-roberts_592x888.jpg)

This is a fun daytime look from Emma Roberts. She wears a short red lace dress with edgy eyelet detailing at the waist. She teams her lace dress with oversized sunglasses, a slouchy black bag, and matching red flats.

## 3. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/3_taylor-swift_592x888.jpg)

Taylor Swift looks very ladylike in this white lace dress. The dress has a high neck with long sleeves and a full knee-length skirt. She teams her dress with peep-toe platform pumps and keeps the accessories to a minimum

## 4. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/4-alexa-chung_545x818.jpg)

Here, Alexa Chung sports a grey long-sleeved lace dress. She dresses down her short lace dress with a woven tote and metallic flats. Her signature tousled locks and minimal makeup complete the look.

## 5. Kristen Stewart

![Kristen Stewart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/5_kristen-stewart_592x888.jpg)

Here, Kristen Stewart glams it up in a black lace dress. The figure-hugging dress features coloured flower embellishments and sheer sleeves. Pointed pumps, a bright red lip, and high ponytail complete the look.

## 6. Olivia Wilde

![Olivia Wilde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/6_olivia-wilde_550x1100.jpg)

Olivia Wilde works a popular colour trend in her turquoise lace dress. The dress has a scalloped hem, cap sleeves, and features an interesting lace pattern. She toughens up what would otherwise be a pretty girly dress with some black ankle boots.

## 7. Katy Perry

![Katy Perry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/7_katy-perry_550x1100.jpg)

Katy Perry works a retro inspired look in this blue lace dress. She accessorises the dress with a matching blue headband, cat eye sunglasses, and statement drop earrings. The mirrored pointed pumps add a modern twist to the entire look.

## 8. Keira Knightley

![Keira Knightley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/8_keira-knightley_550x1100.jpg)

Coloured lace is proving to be a pretty popular trend at the moment. Here, Keira Knightley wears an elegant green lace dress. She lets the dress do all the talking by keeping the jewellery to a minimum and wearing neutral pumps.

## 9. Lauren Conrad

![Lauren Conrad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/9_lauren-conrad_550x1100.jpg)

Lauren Conrad works an ultra feminine look in lace. The cream lace dress has a [structured top](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/structured-tops-perfect-for-working-the-boxy-trend/), gathered skirt, and features a cute bow detail at the waist. She keeps the entire outfit fairly neutral by accessorising with nude pumps and a taupe handbag.

## 10. Elizabeth Olsen

![Elizabeth Olsen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/10_elizabeth-olsen_550x1100.jpg)

Elizabeth Olsen sticks to an all-black look when wearing her little lace dress. She teams her black lace dress with peep-toe pumps and a compact boxy clutch. She toughens up the entire look with a black bomber-style leather jacket.

These are just a few examples of how celebrities have been styling their lace dresses. As you can see, there are a variety of lace dresses to choose from and they can be styled in all sorts of different ways. Which of these looks do you like best?
