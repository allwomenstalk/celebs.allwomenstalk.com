# 10 Celebrities Wearing Floral Dresses  Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities wearing floral dresses are a dime a dozen these days. Floral dresses are a fashion favourite and everyone seems to have at least one in their wardrobe. There’s a floral dress for every season and occasion, from dark winter dresses to fun and flirty sundresses. Take a look at the following celebrities wearing floral dresses and let us know who you think wore theirs the best.

## 1. January Jones

![January Jones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/1_january-jones_592x888.jpg)

First up on our list of celebrities wearing floral dresses is January Jones who looks cool, calm, and collected in a blue and white floral dress. The dress has a relaxed design with a short hemline, cinched waist, and concealed pockets. She teams her dress with a classic trench coat, strappy heels, and a coat of red lipstick for a more polished look.

## 2. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/2_taylor-swift_592x888.jpg)

Taylor Swift looks super ladylike in this retro-looking floral dress. The dress itself has halter neck straps, a sweetheart neckline, and knee-length skirt. It’s the perfect kind of dress for summer garden parties or picnics in the park with friends. Taylor keeps the rest of her look fairly simple with minimal jewellery, cream heels, and oversized sunglasses.

## 3. Chloe Sevigny

![Chloe Sevigny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/3_chloe-sevigny_592x888.jpg)

Here, Chloe Sevigny rocks a chic black and white floral dress. The dress has a flattering V-neckline and wrap-style skirt. She teams her dress with a complementary coral clutch and pink booties. The bow at the waist adds a sweet touch to the entire outfit.

## 4. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/4_alexa-chung_592x888.jpg)

Alexa Chung wears a colourful printed dress as she rides her bike around town. She teams her bold floral dress with a plain black tote bag and Charlotte Olympia kitty heels. The outfit is quirky and cute, as is her signature style.

## 5. Leigh Lezark

![Leigh Lezark](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/5_leigh-lezark_592x888.jpg)

DJ Leigh Lezark keeps her look strictly grunge. Recreate her look by teaming a floral dress with a leather jacket layered over a denim jacket and lace up ankle boots. Add a smudge of eyeliner and you’re good to go.

## 6. Vanessa Hudgens

![Vanessa Hudgens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/6_vanessa-hudgens_592x888.jpg)

Vanessa Hudgens is known for her boho-inspired street style looks. Here she dons a floor length floral maxi dress. The dress features a bold floral print, cutout details, and splits to the side. She teams her dress with tan platform shoes, dangly earrings, and oversized sunglasses.

## 7. Dita Von Teese

![Dita Von Teese](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/7_dita-von-teese_592x888.jpg)

Dita Von Teese is all about the glamour. Here she works a retro-inspired daytime look in a floral dress and peep-toe heels. She completes her look with a turban-style headband, round sunglasses, and structured handbag.

## 8. Eva Mendes

![Eva Mendes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/8_eva-mendes_592x888.jpg)

Here, Eva Mendes steps out in a white rose print dress. It’s quite a demure and ladylike dress. She teams it with cream heels, a large tan handbag, and oversized sunglasses.

## 9. Sarah Jessica Parker

![Sarah Jessica Parker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/9_sarah-jessica-parker_592x888.jpg)

Sarah Jessica Parker wears her floral dress with plenty of neutral accents. The dark blue dress reaches to the knee and features a cream floral print. She teams her dress with a tweed jacket (I wouldn’t be surprised if it was Chanel), two-tone handbag, and pointy pumps.

## 10. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/10_miranda-kerr_592x888.jpg)

Miranda Kerr looks set for summer in her yellow floral dress. The dress has a plunging V-neckline and gathered waist. She keeps her look quite polished by teaming the dress with a ladylike hand bag and nude heels.

These are just a few examples of how celebrities style their floral dresses. From ladylike to grunge looks, the floral dress is truly versatile. Which of these celebrities do you think wore their floral dress the best?
