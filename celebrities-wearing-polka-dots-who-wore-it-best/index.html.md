# 10 Celebrities Wearing Polka Dots  Who Wore It Best?

Polka dots are having a huge moment right now and there’s no shortage of celebrities wearing polka dots. Polka dots seem to be a favourite with celebrities both on and off the red carpet. Whether it’s a cute dress, chic blouse or tailored coat, these celebrities show us that there are plenty of different ways to wear your polka dots. Check out these celebrities wearing polka dots and let us know who you think pulled them off the best.

## 1. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/1_alexa-chung_550x1100.jpg)

Trust Alexa Chung to be all over the polka dot trend. Here she works a relaxed look in a navy and white polka dot blouse tucked into high-waisted shorts. She keeps it chic by wearing black pointed shoes. As far as celebrities wearing polka dots go, this is a great example of an outfit that you could easily recreate on a budget.

## 2. Solange Knowles

![Solange Knowles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/2_solange-knowles_550x1100.jpg)

Solange Knowles sure knows how to rock a bold print. Here she works bold polka dot separates. While the tailored top and skirt combination seems quite polished, a bared midriff adds a cheeky touch to the entire look. She completes her outfit with statement-making pointed pumps and a swipe of bright red lipstick.

## 3. Emmy Rossum

![Emmy Rossum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/3_emmy-rossum_550x1100.jpg)

Emmy Rossum looks ever so ladylike in this polka dot dress. The dress comes in a classic navy and white colour combination and has a figure-hugging design. She incorporates splashes of red into her outfit in the form of shoes, lipstick, and nail polish. Statement drop earrings complete the glam look.

## 4. Sarah Jessica Parker

![Sarah Jessica Parker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/4_sarah-jessica-parker_550x1100.jpg)

As far as fashion icons go, Sarah Jessica Parker is up there with the best of them. Here she works a demure look in a tiered polka dot dress and grid-patterned cardigan. She adds some flair in the form of pink pointed pumps. Mixing and matching patterns can be a tricky task, but here SJP shows us how it’s done.

## 5. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/5_taylor-swift_550x1100.jpg)

Here, Taylor Swift works a preppy look in polka dots. She teams her polka dot jeans with a maroon short-sleeved shirt. She accessorises with a structured handbag, Ray-Ban sunglasses, and cat-shaped slippers. A pearl necklace completes the preppy look.

## 6. Whitney Port

![Whitney Port](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/6_whitney-port_592x888.jpg)

Whitney Port opts for a bold look when wearing her polka dots. Here she wears a short polka dot and floral dress with a ruffled hemline and sleeves. She teams it with a light tan side bag for the day, but it could easily be dressed up at night with a glossy patent clutch and strappy heels.

## 7. Gwen Stefani

![Gwen Stefani](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/7_gwen-stefani_592x888.jpg)

Here, Gwen Stefani works her signature street-style look in a bold polka dot sweater. She teams her sweater with khaki trousers, black ankle boots, and graphic cream sunglasses.

## 8. Kristen Bell

![Kristen Bell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/8_kristen-bell_592x888.jpg)

Kristen Bell works a tailored look in her polka dots. She teams her blue and black polka dot pants with a white blouse, black blazer, and nude peep-toe pumps. A statement necklace adds a fun touch to the outfit.

## 9. Emma Roberts

![Emma Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/9_emma-roberts1_592x888.jpg)

Emma Roberts goes for a casual look by teaming a polka dot tank top with denim shorts and Converse trainers. She accessorises with a slouchy tan handbag, oversized sunglasses and simple silver watch. This is a cool and casual look that you could easily replicate during summer.

## 10. Mischa Barton

![Mischa Barton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/10_mischa-barton_592x888.jpg)

Mischa Barton works a more risqué look in a sheer polka dot blouse. She teams it with tight leather trousers, peep-toe pumps, a taupe handbag, and a black bra which can be seen underneath the sheer top. A high ponytail and statement winged eyeliner complete the look.

These are just a few celebrities who have embraced the polka dot trend. Black, navy, and white colour combinations are classics but this season we’re also seeing a trend toward brighter polka dots. Which of these celebrity polka dot looks do you like best?
