# 59 Photos of 45th Annual Academy of Country Music Awards ...

Here's something for all country music fans. Photos from the 45th Annual Academy of Country Music Awards held at MGM Grand Garden Arena, [Las Vegas](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/las-vegas/), NV. April 18, 2010. Fashion, glamor and glitz assured! Check it out.
Camila Alves showed of her legs in a flowing blue gown. I just wish the top half was more fitted.
I love Lady Antebellum's "Need you Now" and I'm so glad they won Song of the year, Single record of the year and Top Vocal group of the year! And how gorgeous does Hilary Scott look in her one shouldered, floor length pink gown.
Kristen Bell looked super sexy in her Hervé Léger by Max Azria chevron illusion mini dress which she paired with Brian Atwood Katie Lee sandals. What can I say, nude is sexy!
Not my favorite dress of the evening but Laura Bell Bundy sure has the legs to carry this off!
Singer Whitney Duncan, dressed in white showed off her U.S flag tattoo.
Faith Hill sported a chic white dress on the red carpet. I'm not a fan of the hair. What do you think?
Ugly Ugly Ugly! I really hate this Basil Soda dress that Julianne Hough wore. Doesn't she also look a little orange?
Nicole Kidman wore a black L’Wren Scott Fall 2010 mini tuxedo dress. It's a little too blah for Nicole. I wish she'd picked something more elegant and dramatic. What do you think?
What is that around Jennifer Nettles neck?!
Leann Rimes stayed safe in a blue Lanvin Ruffle Trim Strapless dress.
Taylor Swift looked like a divine angel in a gorgeous lavender Marchesa Spring 2010 gown. Love the dress! Love the hair! Guess who's getting my vote for Best Dressed!
Carrie Underwood looked cute as a button in a buttercup Basil Soda Spring 2010 Couture cocktail dress. Love the ruffled effect on her shoulder!
You know the drill. Pick your favorite celebrity!
