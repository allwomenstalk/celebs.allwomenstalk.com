# 10 Celebrities in Red Dresses – Who Wore It Best?

Plenty of celebrities are embracing the red dress and picking who wore it best can be a tough decision to make. There’s no doubt that the red dress is one of the biggest trends on the red carpet right now. It’s bold and bright, and perfect for making a statement in. Take a look at the following celebrities embracing the red dress trend and let us know who wore it best.

## 1. Zooey Deschanel

![Zooey Deschanel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/1_zooey-deschanel_350x700.jpg)

Red dresses command attention, so it makes sense then to pick a dress with a simple, classic cut. This Oscar de La Renta dress that Zooey Deschanel wore to the Golden Globes has a classic sweetheart neckline, fitted bodice, and full skirt. She accessorises it well with pearl jewellery. When it comes to who wore it best, I think Deschanel is going to be a tough one to beat.

## 2. Kate Hudson

![Kate Hudson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/2_kate-hudson_350x700.jpg)

Kate Hudson dares to bare in this red dress by Prabal Gurung at the amFAR Inspiration Gala. The dress has a fairly fitted design with a slight flare at the bottom and features a plunging neckline and shoulder cut outs. Red dresses are great for oozing confidence and Hudson does just that with a megawatt smile.

## 3. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/3_jennifer-lawrence_350x700.jpg)

Jennifer Lawrence is quickly becoming one to watch on the red carpet. At this year’s Golden Globes she stepped out in a brilliant red Dior gown. The dress had a fairly classic cut but was accessorised with modern accessories like a metal waist belt and metallic clutch.

## 4. Michelle Obama

![Michelle Obama](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/4_michelle-obama_350x700.jpg)

The First Lady’s fashion choices are always heavily anticipated. At the Inaugural Ball, Michelle Obama stepped out in this statement-making red Jason Wu gown. The design of the gown was flattering and made the most of showing off her toned arms and shoulders.

## 5. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/5_emma-stone_350x700.jpg)

Here, Emma Stone accessorises her red dress with matching red accessories. She teams her Lanvin cocktail dress with a ruby red statement necklace, brooch belt, and red heels. Usually this might be a case of colour overkill, but Stone wears it well.

## 6. Claire Danes

![Claire Danes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/6_claire-danes_350x700.jpg)

The recent Golden Globes seemed to be awash with red dresses. Claire Danes wore a daring Versace halterneck gown with a thigh high split. Her tousled hair and smoky eye makeup completed the bombshell look.

## 7. Freida Pinto

![Freida Pinto](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/7_frieda-pinto_350x700.jpg)

A list of red dresses wouldn’t be complete without a Valentino design. Freida Pinto wore a vintage Valentino gown to the Dubai International Film Festival. The dress had a simple bandeau top and flared skirt. She kept the accessories to a minimum with a compact clutch and simple updo.

## 8. Helen Mirren

![Helen Mirren](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/8_helen-mirren_350x700.jpg)

Helen Mirren looked as elegant as ever in the bright red Escada dress that she wore to the Palm Springs International Film Festival Awards Gala. She teamed her long sleeved red dress with a bold statement necklace and compact clutch.

## 9. Jennifer Garner

![Jennifer Garner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/9_jennifer-garner_350x700.jpg)

Jennifer Garner rocked a dark red Vivienne Westwood Couture at the Golden Globes. The simple and sleek cut of the dress was complemented by the interesting texture of the fabric. She accessorised her glamorous gown by teaming it with large gold drop earrings and stacked bracelets.

## 10. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/10_rihanna_350x700.jpg)

Never one to shy away from showing a bit of skin, Rihanna rocked a Calvin Klein slip dress at the GQ Men of the Year party. She paired her minimalist dress with strappy red heels, a bold red lip, and a sleek hairdo.

From glamorous red carpet gowns to fun and flirty cocktail dresses, celebrities can’t seem to get enough of the red dress. As you can see here, a red dress is definitely an easy way to shake up your look. Which of these celeb red dresses do you like best?
