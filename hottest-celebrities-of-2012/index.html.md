# 10 Hottest Celebrities of 2012 ...

**The Hottest Celebrities** keep us interested, they grab our attention and don't let it go. I know we’re barely getting through the first month of the new year, but Hollywood works 24/7 and has already shown us the cr?me de la cr?me in entertainment who are sure to be the 10 top [hottest celebrities](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-sexiest-women-celebrities/) of 2012. Now you have likely heard of or seen the works of these celebs already but based on their performance thus far, they are destined to be one of the hot celebrities this year and beyond.

## 1. Ryan Gosling

![Ryan Gosling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/02/1_1238x1650.jpg)

Although Gosling has been in the public eye since he was knee-high to a grasshopper, the post-pubescent version has garnered more attention than his early days as a crooner at the Mickey Mouse Club. We fell in love with him in The Notebook, then he stole our hearts again as the good hot guy in Crazy Stupid Love. Wonder what this **hot celebrity** has in store next?

## 2. Rooney Mara

![Rooney Mara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/02/2_901x1138.jpg)

We first saw her in the remake of A Nightmare on Elm Street and more recently as the ex-girlfriend to Mark Zuckerberg in The Social Network. Mara took on a much different, dark and daring role as Lisbeth Salander in The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo. One of the reasons she's considered one of the **hottest celebrities** of the year is because she's so intriguing.

## 3. Justin Bieber

![Justin Bieber](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/02/3_500x599.jpg)

Whether you are a Belieber or not, Bieber continues to crank out hits and remain in the spotlight. He tried his hand at acting in Never Say Never and on CSI. Now Mark Wahlberg was quoted as saying that he's looking into making a film with the Biebs. Triple threat?

## 4. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/02/4_500x598.jpg)

This Bajan beauty has been singing in the U.S. since she was 16 and is the youngest solo artist in Billboard history to have eleven #1 singes on the Hot 100 List. With her stylish yet daring outfits and rump-shaking songs, Rihanna is sure to continue to be one of the **hottest celebrities** in showbiz. What do you think?

## 5. Johnny Depp

![Johnny Depp](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/02/5_1000x1229.jpg)

Johnny Depp is definitely one of the **top hottest celebrities**. I mean, does this guy ever age or stop working? We last saw him in The Rum Diary and we can look forward to seeing him in the remake of cult TV series Dark Shadows -- a Tim Burton Joint, of course.

## 6. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/02/6_445x600.jpg)

Emma's not just one of the year's **hottest celebrities**, she's also one of the cutest. She was super cute and cool as Jules in Superbad and smart and sassy in The Help. You can see her in the latest Covergirl ads and in the upcoming, highly anticipated The Amazing Spider-Man.

## 7. Channing Tatum

[img.allw.mn](https://img.allw.mn/content/celebs/2012/02/7_650x859.jpeg)

Model and actor Channing Tatum melted our hearts in Dear John and showed us that he's got moves like Jagger in two Step Up films. We can look forward to seeing Tatum in the upcoming 21 Jump Street, which is a remake of the late 1980s television series starring Johnny Depp, another **hot celebrity**.

## 8. Nicki Minaj

![Nicki Minaj](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/02/8_502x650.jpg)

Hip hop had been lacking a strong female artist and along came Nicki Minaj. She rocked our socks with her lightning-fast rap skills and colorful and crazy ensembles. She has toured with Britney Spears and has her own line of OPI nail polish.

## 9. Kim Kardashian

![Kim Kardashian](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/02/9_634x387.jpg)

Despite her recent short-lived marriage, Kim continues to keep working hard and endorsing products such as Midori Liqueur, ShoeDazzle.com, and the Sugar Factory, just to name a few. America loves Kim Kardashian and she's sure to remain on the **hottest celebrity** lists. After all, admit it -- even if you don't love her, you love to hate her!

## 10. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/02/10_600x720.jpg)

She sings, acts, and is heavily involved in charity. She's the youngest UNICEF Ambassador and she's sold over 1 million records. She's also cute as a button, so is it any wonder she's one of the top **hot celebrities**?

There you have the list of 10 **Hottest Celebrities** of 2012 in no particular order. The year is sure to erupt with more popular celebrities, but we'll have to wait and see who came out big. For now, who tops your list of hottest celebrities?
