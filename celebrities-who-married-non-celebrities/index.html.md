# 7 Celebrities Who Married Non\-Celebrities ...

There are a lot of celebrities who married non-celebrities out there! All the public ever hears about are the famous couples that get together and break up, but what about the other people? There are a lot of celebrities out there who don't care if they're with another celebrity and even prefer it because it keeps them out of the spotlight. Let's take a look at some celebrities who married non-celebrities.

## 1. Matt Damon

![Matt Damon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mh/zi/cd/llkc5akk_600x831.jpg)

Matt Damon met his wife, Luciana Barroso, in 2003. The couple met while he was filming "Stuck on You" in Miami at a bar where she was working as a bartender. They were married in 2005 and now have four children together. Matt has been quoted as saying that Luciana is his soulmate and that he hit the jackpot when he found her. Who would have thought that Matt Damon was one of the celebrities who married non-celebrities.

## 2. Reese Witherspoon

![Reese Witherspoon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/la/oy/da/dgjldusb_600x774.jpg)

After her marriage with Ryan Philippe ended, Reese said she never planned on marrying again, but all that changed when she met her now husband Jim Toth. The two met at a party where a friend of Jim's made a drunken fool of himself in front of Reese. Afterwards, the two started dating. Reese said that Jim gave her something she had never had before and that is what pushed her to marry him.

## 3. Julia Roberts

![Julia Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dz/gt/dt/05j2qq2u_592x888.jpg)

In 2001, while working on "The Mexican," Julia Roberts met Danny Moder. At the time Danny was married and Julia was dating Benjamin Bratt, but in 2002 after Danny's divorce was finalized, the two were married. They have three children together. Julia has called Danny her Prince Charming and said that it's the love of her good man that keeps her looking young.

## 4. Patrick Dempsey

![Patrick Dempsey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0k/xq/d4/v5yr9uo7_600x872.jpg)

When Patrick Dempsey made an appointment to get his haircut, I'm sure he didn't think he would meet his future wife, but that's exactly what happened. Jillian Flink didn't believe that it was really Patrick Dempsey coming to her for a haircut, but when he walked through the door there was no doubt. They were both dating other people so they didn't get together for three years, but now they're together and he still goes to her for his haircuts.

## 5. Seth Rogen

![Seth Rogen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c1/l7/h2/hmn9dznv_600x840.jpg)

Seth Rogen met his now wife, Lauren Miller, at a bar in 2004 while he was filming "50/50." Seth credits his best friend and inspiration for the movie for introducing him to Lauren. Their wedding was said to be filled with more laughs than anything else. Well, I guess if you're going to marry Seth Rogen you better have a great sense of humor too!

## 6. Jimmy Fallon

![Jimmy Fallon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y8/iw/pj/w96l85ku_600x842.jpg)

Jimmy met his now wife, Nancy Juvonen, in 2005 while filing "Fever Pitch" with Drew Barrymore. Nancy is Drew Barrymore's production partner so it's likely that she introduced the couple. They became engaged in 2007 and married later that year. Jimmy has come out and said that they still have a lot of fun even though they're married.

## 7. Tina Fey

![Tina Fey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ah/xp/z5/wo223kkb_600x896.jpg)

Tina Fey met her now husband Jeff Richmond in Chicago while they were trying to make names for themselves. They had their first date at Chicago’s Museum of Science and Industry and then dated for seven years before finally getting married. They have no been married for 11 years and have two daughters.
