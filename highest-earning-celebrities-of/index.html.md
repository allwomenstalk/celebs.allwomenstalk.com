# The Highest Earning Celebrities of 2010: A Look Back at the Year's Top Earners

The holidays are always a season for sharing, and in 2010, this elite list of **highest earning celebrities** certainly had plenty to share! Forbes released their annual report, and while some names were expected, others were quite surprising. Let's look back at the **2010 celebrities** who dominated the bank accounts and the headlines.

I'll skip the suspense and move directly to the second-highest earner, because let’s face it: no one gets points for guessing who took the top spot. [Oprah Winfrey](https://www.oprah.com) continued her reign with an incredible $315 million. While _Avatar's_ other-worldly experience thrilled audiences worldwide, [James Cameron's](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000116/) thrill was of a totally different kind. The director with the magic touch rose to the second position with $210 million in earnings.

Interestingly enough, the third position belongs to filmmaker [Tyler Perry](https://www.tylerperry.com), who ended up with $125 million. While the rest of the list continues with a few more "ahhs" and "I thought as much," the last entry in the top 20 was a real surprise at the time.
