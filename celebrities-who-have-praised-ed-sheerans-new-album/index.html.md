# 7 Celebrities Who Have Praised Ed Sheerans New Album ...

With Ed Sheeran's new album out, it's all people have been talking about. Every track on the album is amazing, I don't even think I can pick out a favorite! Check out some of the celebrities who have praised Ed Sheeran's new album, 'X'.

## 1. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lp/rb/by68eyqh_600x805.jpg)

We all know that Taylor's been a huge supporter of Ed. The two are actually super close - I love their friendship! Taylor recently posted the most adorable photo on her Instagram account (@taylorswift) where Ed posed with a kitty. She captioned it, "If you're on the fence about buying Ed Sheeran's new album, X, allow me to convince you with this image:" I'm sure that's all it took! She's just one of many celebrities who have praised Ed Sheeran new album, 'X'.

## 2. Camila Cabello

![Camila Cabello](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yw/0s/bo5cuhfm_500x720.jpg)

If you know Fifth Harmony's Camila, you know that she's an Ed fanatic. Turns out Ed loves her voice just as much! When Fifth Harmony covered Ed's "Lego House", he shared the link with his 9 million followers on Twitter letting everyone know just how impressed he was. When 'X' came out, Camila tweeted, "my favorite songs off of Ed's album so far are 'Thinking Out Loud" and "The Man". The sweetness of the first and the intensity of the latter is amazing"

## 3. Lucy Hale

![Lucy Hale](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ez/fz/p8swa9yt_600x800.jpg)

Pretty Little Liars' Lucy Hale has major love for Ed Sheeran. She's tweeted about his music multiple times and recently, she posted a photo on Instagram where she posed next to cardboard cutout of Ed! She was blowing kisses and captioned the photo, "everyone wanted a piece of Ed" LOL! When his album came out, Lucy posted a screenshot of her listening to 'Tenerife Sea' (one of my personal favorites!)

## 4. James Mcvey

![James Mcvey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uk/l5/c44snn52_500x700.jpg)

Who knew Ed was such good friends with The Vamps' James McVey? James tweeted a screenshot of a text conversation between the two about the new album, where Ed thanked him for the support. How awesome would it be if Ed collaborated with The Vamps? Fingers crossed for that in the near future.

## 5. Jake Roche

![Jake Roche](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uz/wz/a62w4hjq_424x594.jpg)

Rixton's leading man, Jake Roche, is super close with Ed! The two are constantly joking around and tweeting each other silly things. When Ed's album came out, Jake tweeted a screenshot of the song 'Photograph' with the caption, "Jeeez, what are you doing to me, Edward :(" Been there, Jake! Ed's songs bring SO many emotions.

## 6. Chelsea Briggs

![Chelsea Briggs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ru/ez/gkpgrp8v_396x594.jpg)

'Hollywire' host, Chelsea Briggs, was pretty much addicted to Ed's album! She tweeted, "Can't stop listening to @edsheeran's new album. Obsessed. #Feels." That pretty much sums up all my feelings too! I love how supportive Chelsea was about the new album.

## 7. Tori Kelly

![Tori Kelly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pv/09/l4y8mznh_600x806.jpg)

After touring together, Tori and Ed became really good friends. They're constantly tweeting each other and being supportive of one another. When Ed's album came out, Tori took to Twitter (@torikelly) to let her fans know what they should be listening to! She called Ed a "true gentleman" and said that it's "great to see genuine people succeed!" I couldn't agree more! It's awesome to see Ed taking over the music scene.

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many celebrities who have praised Ed Sheeran's new album, 'X'. If you ask me, every song on that album is just pure perfection! Which tracks do you still have on repeat?
