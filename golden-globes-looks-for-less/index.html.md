# 10 Golden Globes Looks for Less ...

**Golden Globe Fashion** can be the perfect inspiration for getting dressed up every now and then. There a lot of clever ways to put your spin on Golden Globe fashion, from pinpointing colour trends to embracing certain silhouettes. Without copying the looks outright, you can take some pointers from this year’s Golden Globe fashion trends to create your own stylish outfits.

## 1. Modcloth ‘Right on Corsage’ Dress (Mila Kunis)

![Modcloth ‘Right on Corsage’ Dress (Mila Kunis)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/1_modcloth-lsquo-right-on-corsage-rsquo-dress-mila-kunis_227x560.jpg)

**Price:** $84.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Mila Kunis wore a Dior dress, Cartier jewels, and Jimmy Choo heels to the Golden Globes. While not everyone can match these threads, take a leaf out of her book by wearing sheer black dress with an asymmetrical shoulder strap.

## 2. Joseph ‘Gwyneth’ Maxi Dress (Rooney Mara)

![Joseph ‘Gwyneth’ Maxi Dress (Rooney Mara)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/2_joseph-lsquo-gwyneth-rsquo-maxi-dress-rooney-mara_227x560.jpg)

**Price:** $192.50 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Make like Rooney Mara and choose a dress with daring cutout details. Her choice of dress was quite a contemporary take on **Golden Globe fashion**. Team your dress with a slicked back hairstyle and polished makeup.

## 3. BB Dakota Red Gown (Reese Witherspoon)

![BB Dakota Red Gown (Reese Witherspoon)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/3_bb-dakota-red-gown-reese-witherspoon_227x560.jpg)

**Price:** $96.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Take a cue from Reese’s choice of gown and opt for a brilliant red dress for your next special occasion. A dress with a fitted bodice and sweetheart neckline is feminine and flattering.

## 4. Topshop Metallic Shift Dress (Nicole Richie)

![Topshop Metallic Shift Dress (Nicole Richie)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/4_topshop-metallic-shift-dress-nicole-richie_227x560.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/3pvgxF)
Nicole Richie stepped out in an eye-catching metallic gown. This Topshop shift dress is a more casual take on this particular **Golden Globe fashion**. Team it with neutral accessories to play down the glitz of the metallics.

## 5. Dorothy Perkins Peplum Dress (Julianne Moore)

![Dorothy Perkins Peplum Dress (Julianne Moore)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/5_dorothy-perkins-peplum-dress-julianne-moore_227x560.jpg)

**Price:** $27.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Work a new season silhouette by choosing a dress with peplum detailing at the waist. Julianne Moore’s stunning gown featured a delicate peplum waist. Take a cue from her **Golden Globe fashion** accessories as well and team your dress with green tassel earrings.

## 6. Topshop Purple Drape Dress (Emma Stone)

![Topshop Purple Drape Dress (Emma Stone)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/6_topshop-purple-drape-dress-emma-stone_227x560.jpg)

**Price:** $92.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/qoFuLa)
The flowing burgundy dress that Emma Stone wore made quite a statement. Embrace this dramatic hue in a similar way with this Topshop dress. It comes in a vivid purple colour and also features draping which is reminiscent of Emma’s Golden Globe dress.

## 7. Dress up by Topshop Bow Dress (Charlize Theron)

![Dress up by Topshop Bow Dress (Charlize Theron)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/7_dress-up-by-topshop-bow-dress-charlize-theron_227x560.jpg)

**Price:** $160.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/xWTjvP)
Charlize’s take on **Golden Globe fashion** was quite flirty and feminine. Recreate this look in this froufrou dress from Topshop. Finish it off with soft wavy hair and a slim diamante headband.

## 8. Modcloth Teal Dress (Sofia Vergara)

![Modcloth Teal Dress (Sofia Vergara)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/8_modcloth-teal-dress-sofia-vergara_227x560.jpg)

**Price:** $74.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Sofia Vergara made a splash in her teal dress. While ball gowns aren’t the most practical for us common folk, a shorter teal dress would make excellent cocktail attire.

## 9. Badgley Mischka One-Shoulder Dress (Paula Patton)

![Badgley Mischka One-Shoulder Dress (Paula Patton)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/9_badgley-mischka-one-shoulder-dress-paula-patton_227x560.jpg)

**Price:** $137.60 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Yellow has become an increasingly popular colour on the red carpet. Take your cue from this **Golden Globe fashion** trend and amp up your evening wardrobe with a punchy yellow dress.

## 10. Tory Burch Wrap Dress (Angelina Jolie)

![Tory Burch Wrap Dress (Angelina Jolie)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/10_tory-burch-wrap-dress-angelina-jolie_227x560.jpg)

**Price:** $450 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/EjwJ9u)
While still fairly pricey, this dress is still no doubt a fraction of the cost of Angelina’s. The cream and red colour combination is a stylish choice, and one that can be worked into your wardrobe. Either wear a two-tone dress or team a cream dress with red accessories.

Taking inspiration from **Golden Globe fashion** can be as simple as choosing a certain coloured dress. Dresses with shorter hemlines also make translating Golden Globe fashion into your wardrobe an easier task. What were your favourite Golden Globe fashions this year?
