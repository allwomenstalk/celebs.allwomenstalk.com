# 9 Celebrities Wearing Boyfriend Jeans \- Who Wore It Best?

When they’re not on the red carpet, there are plenty of celebrities wearing boyfriend jeans. Whether you love this denim trend or loathe it, there’s no denying that boyfriend-style jeans are here to stay. The boyfriend jean is a popular choice for those wanting to work a casual and comfortable off-duty look. Check out these celebrities wearing boyfriend jeans and let us know who you think wore theirs best.

## 1. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/1_jessica-alba_592x888.jpg)

Jessica Alba works a fairly clean-cut look in her boyfriend jeans. She wears them cuffed at the ankle and teamed with a black and white polka dot sweater, beige scarf, and black ballet flats. She accessorises with a black bag and oversized sunglasses. This example of celebrities wearing boyfriend jeans is one that you could recreate with pieces from your own wardrobe.

## 2. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/2_kate-bosworth_592x888.jpg)

Kate Bosworth opts for faded denim when it comes to her boyfriend jeans. She wears them cuffed at the ankle and with a speckled knit sweater and suede ankle boots. The structured handbag adds a bit of polish to this fairly relaxed look.

## 3. Sienna Miller

![Sienna Miller](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/3_sienna-miller_592x888.jpg)

Here, Sienna Miller manages to dress up her boyfriend jeans. She makes this look work by sticking to a mid-wash blue denim, free from rips and any over-the-top distressed details. She teams her jeans with a long-sleeved black top, chain-link handbag, and platform heels.

## 4. Vanessa Hudgens

![Vanessa Hudgens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/4_vanessa-hudgens_592x888.jpg)

Vanessa Hudgens takes a break from her usual boho outfits with this streetwear-inspired look. She pairs her ripped denim boyfriend jeans with a black singlet and two-tone jacket. The studded stiletto heels and beaded cross necklace add some edginess to the outfit.

## 5. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/5_rihanna_592x888.jpg)

Rihanna wears her boyfriend jeans out at night by teaming them with a sheer black blouse and chunky chain link necklace. A burgundy lipstick and structured handbag give the outfit a polished feel. While she wears sneakers here, you could easily glam this kind of outfit up even more by popping on a pair of heels.

## 6. Gwen Stefani

![Gwen Stefani](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/6_gwen-stefani_592x888.jpg)

Gwen Stefani sticks to her distinct brand of urban cool with this outfit. She pairs her distressed boyfriend jeans with a military-style shirt and heeled ankle boots. The boxy white handbag is an unexpected, classic touch.

## 7. January Jones

![January Jones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/7_january-jones_592x888.jpg)

January Jones works a laid back, summery look with her boyfriend jeans. She wears them cuffed at the ankle with a yellow patterned blouse and cut-out shoes. A straw hat, striped tote, and geometric framed sunglasses complete the look.

## 8. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/8_miley-cyrus_592x888.jpg)

Here, Miley Cyrus works a revealing look in her boyfriend jeans. Well, she’s technically only wearing half a pair of boyfriend jeans. The other half resembles sweat pants. She teams her pants with a leather crop top, patent jacket, and pointy white heels. She accessorises with assorted necklaces and a black quilted bag.

## 9. Reese Witherspoon

![Reese Witherspoon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/9_reese-witherspoon_592x888.jpg)

Reese Witherspoon sticks to the basics by teaming her boyfriend jeans with a slouchy navy sweater. She wears her jeans cuffed at the ankle, such is the trend, and shows off a pretty pink pair of ballet flats. Oversized sunglasses complete the outfit.

These are just a few celebrities who have been snapped out and about in boyfriend jeans. What are your best tips for styling boyfriend jeans? Which of these celebrities do you think pulled off this denim trend the best?
