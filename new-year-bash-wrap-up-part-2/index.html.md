# New Year Bash Wrap\-up: Part 2 ...

Chapter Two: Kim Sings To A New Tune...

Kim Kardashian played host to a kick-ass party at one of Vegas' most favored party locations - Tao.

As host, not only did Kim K do all that was expected of her - look downright stunning, entertain the crowd, do the countdown, etc - but she also gave the partygoers at Tao a sneak peek of her new single!

Check out the video below for a sneak peek at the song. Kim was shooting some footage on her video camera, part of which will reportedly be used in the upcoming video for the song.

Chapter 3: What of New York and Miami? ...

Though the best parties were definitely at Las Vegas, New York and Miami were not far behind in their celebrations.

While New York had its traditional ball drop and Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve with Ryan Seacrest 2011, it was far from ummm.. boring. With Ke$ha and Taio Cruz performing live from Times Square, how can it be! And of course, Ke$ha's new year resolution "to not be a douchebag" helped us all look at her with renewed respect!

At the same time as the festivities in New York, Miami was partying it up big time as well. One celebrity who can vouch for that is Penn Badgley who was clearly a little toasted as he partied away from ex Blake Lively. Also helping to bring in the New Year was Leona Lewis with her magical voice.
