# 10 Celebrities in Crop Tops – Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities are keen to adopt trends so there’s no shortage of celebrities in crop tops right now. Crop tops are a big trend for spring and these days it seems that no outfit is complete without a bit of bared midriff. There are plenty of different ways to wear your crop tops, as you’ll see from the following celebrities. Take a look at these celebrities in crop tops and let us know who you think wore it best.

## 1. Diane Kruger

![Diane Kruger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/1_diane-kruger_550x1100.jpg)

The first on the list of celebrities in crop tops is actress Diane Kruger. She works a sleek look in a white crop top and red leather skirt. The combination of the crop top with a high waisted skirt means that there’s only the slightest hint of skin on show. She completed the outfit with neutral strappy pumps.

## 2. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/2_jessica-alba_550x1100.jpg)

Jessica Alba was seen during Paris Fashion Week wearing this cropped sweater with some belted trousers. There was only a peek-a-boo hint of skin on show thanks to the high waisted cut of the trousers. It was a mainly dark outfit with the only colour coming from a rich burgundy lipstick.

## 3. Amanda Seyfried

![Amanda Seyfried](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/3_amanda-seyfried_550x1100.jpg)

Just because a top is cropped doesn’t mean you have to always show some skin. Amanda Seyfried shows us how to pull off a crop top in a demure and polished way. She teams her lacy crop top with a high waisted black pencil skirt, leather coat, and ankle strap heels. The addition of a coral colour lip is a fun touch.

## 4. Brooklyn Decker

![Brooklyn Decker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/4_brooklyn-decker_550x1100.jpg)

Brooklyn Decker bared her midriff with a turtleneck crop top and blush knee length skirt. The combination of a halterneck style top and bared midriff means that you’d have to be pretty toned and confident to pull this look off. She keeps the rest of her outfit fairly simple with a gold cuff and pointed black pumps.

## 5. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/5_selena-gomez_550x1100.jpg)

Selena Gomez wears a colourful ensemble to the SXSW Music, Film + Interactive Film Festival. She teams a bright orange pencil skirt with a printed crop top. It’s more of a bustier but there’s still some midriff action going on so we’ll call it a crop top anyway. She completes the look with coral coloured lipstick, side swept hair, and nude pointed pumps.

## 6. Ginnifer Goodwin

![Ginnifer Goodwin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/6_ginnifer-goodwin_550x1100.jpg)

Ginnifer Goodwin works a demure, ladylike look in her crop top and skirt combination. The cropped sweater-style top and printed skirt by Jonathan Saunders leave no room for any bared midriff. She teamed her outfit with classic patent pumps and oversized sunglasses.

## 7. Ashley Madekwe

![Ashley Madekwe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/7_ashley-madekwe_550x1100.jpg)

Ashley Madekwe opts for a matching ensemble of a printed crop top and skirt. The trapeze style crop top could potentially lead to a few wardrobe malfunctions but the overall look is feminine and put-together

## 8. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/8_rihanna_550x1100.jpg)

Rihanna sticks to a fairly minimalist look in this Miu Miu ensemble. She sticks to a neutral colour palette with a black crop top, white silk skirt, and grey heels. The loose crop top is balanced out by the slim fitting, mid-length skirt and she keeps her jewellery to a minimum.

## 9. Christina Ricci

![Christina Ricci](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/9_christina-ricci_550x1100.jpg)

Christina Ricci shows of her stomach in this black and white get up from Marc Jacobs. She wears a three-quarter sleeve crop top with a matching slim skirt. She finishes off her look with a black and white striped clutch and pointy white heels.

## 10. Kristin Stewart

![Kristin Stewart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/10_kristin-stewart_550x1100.jpg)

Kristin Stewart bares her midriff in this sleek black and white outfit. She teams a plunging, fitted crop top with high waisted black trousers and pointed pumps. She finishes off the look with a pulled back ponytail and dark coloured lip.

These are just some celebrities who have embraced the crop top trend. As you can see, you don’t always have to show off some skin when rocking a crop top. Which of these crop top looks do you like best?
