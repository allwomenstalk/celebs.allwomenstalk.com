# Are These the Most Stylish Couples of All Time?

We admire many stars for their sense of style – yes we know they have an entourage of stylists, makeup artists and hairdressers, but some still manage to outshine the rest and make their mark as a style icon. It’s even better when two style mavens get together and we get a double dose of awesomeness when they step out as a couple. Let’s see which celebrities as a couple at a certain time have made style statements through the years (in no particular order!).

## 1. George and Amal Clooney

![George and Amal Clooney](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x9/gv/jdly586o5708d19b822b7739770145_600x900.jpg)

A high-powered, beautiful lawyer and one of Hollywood’s most eternally gorgeous leading men - it’s almost too good to be true!

## 2. John and Jackie Kennedy

![John and Jackie Kennedy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/vl/u2zznelw5708d212495bf796174720_600x609.jpg)

These two were the absolute style icons that defined an all too short period of the White House, and of course, Jackie’s fashion status has been revered for decades since.

## 3. Beyoncé and Jay-Z

![Beyoncé and Jay-Z](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ab/ku/bsdkwsys5708d2cc3f70b596265702_600x884.jpg)

You don’t get to be the most powerful couple in show business without looking damn good along the way.

## 4. Kanye West and Kim Kardashian

![Kanye West and Kim Kardashian](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ee/ti/qwop1cpz5708d3595534e844571443_600x859.jpg)

Whether you love them or hate them, you can’t deny that Kimye have definitely got it going on when it comes to fashion and style!

## 5. Axl Rose and Stephanie Seymour

![Axl Rose and Stephanie Seymour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/th/ih/jxo6g4cj5708d3ab093ff478189753_499x758.jpg)

One of the greatest rock stars in the world dating one of the original supermodels? It was bound to be stylish!

## 6. Grace Kelly and Prince Rainier of Monaco

![Grace Kelly and Prince Rainier of Monaco](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/h8/kd1l659r5708d4712c574814134830_600x799.jpg)

Combine Hollywood royalty with actual royalty and you get a couple so stylish that they looked like something out of a fairy tale!

## 7. Marilyn Monroe and Joe DiMaggio

![Marilyn Monroe and Joe DiMaggio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gy/c4/muz5kxsq5708d4ada5cde144279514_600x885.jpg)

The iconic blonde bombshell has plenty of marriages, but none so stylish as with baseball heartthrob Joe DiMaggio.

## 8. Brigitte Bardot and Gunter Sachs

![Brigitte Bardot and Gunter Sachs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ov/dc/zug6yjt45708d50b5ba53444299080_416x616.jpg)

The original femme fatal and one the world’s biggest international playboys were always going to look amazing together.

## 9. Lauren Bacall and Humphrey Bogart

![Lauren Bacall and Humphrey Bogart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j4/u3/zv1usrg05708d5e4d8c8d419308456_500x725.jpg)

Lauren and Bogie oozed class and had the kind of love story that could only be set in the stylish years of the Golden Age of Hollywood.

## 10. John Lennon and Yoko Ono

![John Lennon and Yoko Ono](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ap/vn/g5he2cq65708d6a115932529770185_450x555.jpg)

Rather than following a tradition, John and Yoko made up their own rules and created their own kind of style.

## 11. David and Victoria Beckham

![David and Victoria Beckham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j5/fm/w8lxemg55708d6ea5a3a9999781450_600x958.jpg)

Posh and Becks basically ruled the late 90s and 2000s with their tabloid presence, and in not one photo did they look anything other than perfect!

## 12. David Bowie and Iman

![David Bowie and Iman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/du/s0/zl6fe5vs5708d74ac29ba253951712_589x1000.jpg)

One was a music and style icon, the other was an international supermodel and renowned beauty. They had to be on this list!

## 13. Mick and Bianca Jagger

![Mick and Bianca Jagger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bs/ry/e609b13h5708d78282f97920095731_600x820.jpg)

Seeing photos of these two together makes you long for the carefree, super stylish days of the 60s and 70s.

## 14. Audrey Hepburn and Mel Ferrer

![Audrey Hepburn and Mel Ferrer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w0/vx/x91hlgp05708d7db11e06895615122_600x789.jpg)

Any man standing next to the gorgeous simplicity and Audrey Hepburn was always going to look great, but Mel Ferrer had a regal style of his own as well.

## 15. Kate Moss and Jamie Hince

![Kate Moss and Jamie Hince](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pv/vk/rc3sfhnx5708d8a1a3b35823896035_600x795.jpg)

They might conform to the cliché of rock star marrying supermodel, but Kate and Jamie sure look good together!

## 16. Prince William and Kate Middleton

![Prince William and Kate Middleton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rs/if/x4j30r0a5708d905ae016549581653_600x901.jpg)

From the moment we all first saw them together, we knew that a couple who looked that great were going to go all the way!

## 17. Tom Brady and Gisele Bundchen

![Tom Brady and Gisele Bundchen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ua/xs/zirn3xln5708d9b86c96a065393962_600x969.jpg)

Tom and Gisele have taken that high school superstar and head cheerleader vibe to the next level, but we don’t mind because they look amazing together!

## 18. Debbie Harry and Chris Stein

![Debbie Harry and Chris Stein](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/wh/nxrm2lrc5708d9f7f41b8206389159_600x849.jpg)

They made sweet music together in Blondie and sweet music together in their personal lives; one of rock music’s most stylish couples for sure.

## 19. Elizabeth Taylor and Richard Burton

![Elizabeth Taylor and Richard Burton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s2/ke/d4gjo0oi5708db0fc5d2e906782501_551x800.jpg)

One of the great love stories of the 20th Century. These two were quite literally made for each other, and despite their drama, they never failed to be immaculately stylish.

## 20. Neil Patrick Harris and David Burtka

![Neil Patrick Harris and David Burtka](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p1/eg/b2ytr7495708db916c9c3499123009_600x790.jpg)

These two always absolutely slay any time they are seen together on the red carpet; both have cheekbones to die for!

## 21. Chrissy Teigen and John Legend

![Chrissy Teigen and John Legend](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hz/t2/dp65awpl5708dbce302d4654995843_600x903.jpg)

From naked Instagrams to red carpet elegance, this show business power couple have really got it all going on.

## 22. Elvis and Priscilla Presley

![Elvis and Priscilla Presley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/ni/ztwiu9xq5708e09485f03699508922_470x572.jpg)

Elvis all shook up the world of music and with his gorgeous wife, gave the fashion establishment something to think about too.

## 23. Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie

![Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y2/a8/gzg49n8q5708dc57abafd350201566_435x580.jpg)

It feels like the whole world became obsessed with Brad and Angie in the early years, and they never disappointed us on the style and fashion front.

## 24. Jane Birkin and Serge Gainsbourg

![Jane Birkin and Serge Gainsbourg](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ok/a4/y3zks99p5708dcc2ccc73551250186_600x755.jpg)

This couple effortlessly oozed style and charisma. Jane was so fashionable she even had a handbag named after her!

## 25. Vivien Leigh and Laurence Olivier

![Vivien Leigh and Laurence Olivier](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u3/l4/wd3gkk415708dd3f0779e645686790_500x619.jpg)

Another couple that instantly make you wish you were a young starlet in the golden age of Hollywood, jewels and fur coats galore!

## 26. Queen Victoria and Prince Albert

![Queen Victoria and Prince Albert](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qc/mt/i85rgibx5708dd845f9d4022762328_600x648.jpg)

They may not seem like it to look back at, but these two actually inspired an entire new generation of Victorian fashion, including wearing a white dress at your wedding! And never was black more fashionable than when Victoria went into mourning.

## 27. Heidi Klum and Seal

![Heidi Klum and Seal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ep/x2/wh6dg6cv5708ddd4b5380299586699_600x832.jpg)

Though they are no longer together, this supermodel and suave soul singer always knocked it out of the park on the red carpet.

## 28. Lady Gaga and Taylor Kinney

![Lady Gaga and Taylor Kinney](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xs/aq/bk2s4bx75708de0f0a544060880028_600x888.jpg)

This couple like to keep their business low key, but they never fail to make an impact when seen together.

## 29. Goldie Hawn and Kurt Russell

![Goldie Hawn and Kurt Russell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/an/dk/b9f8alx85708ded8c2f72005431791_600x851.jpg)

These two have come through thick and thin to be one of Hollywood’s longest lasting partnerships, a relationship that has spanned many different fashion trends and eras!

## 30. Ellen DeGeneres and Portia De Rossi

![Ellen DeGeneres and Portia De Rossi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aj/zu/p7fq1vpa5708dfea86d1f841352493_478x900.jpg)

Portia always just knows how to complement Ellen's signature style of blazers and sneakers.
