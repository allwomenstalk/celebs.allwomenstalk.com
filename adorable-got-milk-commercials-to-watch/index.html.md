# 7 Adorable Got Milk Commercials to Watch ...

I’ve been a **fan of Got Milk commercials** since the first campaign aired in the 1990s, and I’m an even bigger fan now… the ads are so clever and so funny! If you’ve seen the recent one with Salma Hayek, you know what I mean… if you haven’t seen it, or any of my favorite funny, give me ten minutes and you’ll be giggling, too! Here are 7 adorable Got Milk commercials to watch… and laugh at!

## 1. Experiment

[youtu.be](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mMnE04gpAvo&feature=youtu.be)

While the technology in this **Got Milk commercial is outdated** (a college student who’s NOT playing CoD or Skyrim?), the concept is still hilarious. Oh, he’s actually looking forward to being in this experiment… until he finds out exactly how long and dreadful these next 30 days are going to be…

## 2. Board

[youtu.be](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k2m--tzNObc&feature=youtu.be)

I love this Got Milk commercial! It’s so true! I don’t know if this is what obscenely wealthy people actually do all day long, but it sure seems realistic, doesn’t it? If I ever get so filthy rich I need a Board of Unnecessary, I’ll let you know.

## 3. Shake

[youtu.be](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nuKDSuxqtuI&feature=youtu.be)

I wasn’t **sure where this Got Milk commercial** was going until almost the end… so cute! Parent everywhere are probably wondering what THEY’VE done to inspire nightmares… obviously, no more shaking the soy milk carton before bed!

## 4. Salma Hayek’s «Midnight Run»

[youtu.be](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IsDqnNgmpt4&feature=youtu.be)

Gorgeous Salma Hayek is on a mission - her little girl needs milk for her cereal… but it’s Mission Impossible tonight… she can’t seem to get her hands on a gallon of milk, and by the end of the night, she’s a hot mess. Love it!

## 5. Who Shot Alexander Hamilton?

[youtu.be](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OLSsswr6z9Y&feature=youtu.be)

This is the one that started it all, the very first Got Milk commercial. It’s hilarious… this poor guy… he’s been waiting his whole life for the moment when his obsession with Alexander Hamilton will pay off, and it’s him time… but he’s out of milk… oh no!

## 6. Where Am I?

[youtu.be](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hnIjgw5A-iI&feature=youtu.be)

Ha! As if this greedy day trader (or other corporate The Man) would end up in Heaven. This **Got Milk commercial is funny** because we all love to see karma in action, and very little in this world (or the hereafter) is as horrible as not having milk to go with your cookies.

## 7. Does It Really?

[youtu.be](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_I07kRgym0Y&feature=youtu.be)

At first, I thought this was a spoof. I hadn’t heard that calcium can lessen the symptoms of PMS… but it does make sense. And I would be pretty pleased if m sweetheart brought me a gallon of milk… so funny…

## 8. Wanna Dance, Old Man?

[youtu.be](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EVogigFsZ8o&feature=youtu.be)

I love this **Got Milk commercial**! These three punks think they can push people around… until they meet three REAL men powered with milk. HEAD BUTT! This commercial makes me laugh every time I see it.

Okay, admit it **- these Got Milk commercials** made you laugh, at least a little! Did any of them make you thirsty, too? And they’re educational, too… now we all know who shot Alexander Hamilton, and that calcium helps with PMS. Which of these Got Milk commercials do you like best? Or is there another one you enjoy more? Please share!
