# 10 Celebrities Wearing All White Outfits  Who Wore It Best?

Lately there’s been a trend for celebrities wearing all white outfits. Whether it’s a chic white dress or a crisp white top and trousers, an [all white outfit](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/tips-on-how-to-wear-an-all-white-outfit/) is one of the hottest looks to try this spring and summer. While an all-white outfit might not sound like the best idea, especially if you’re prone to spills, it can look extremely chic when done right. Check out the most recent batch of celebrities wearing all white outfits to see how they make this tricky trend work.

## 1. Kate Hudson

![Kate Hudson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/1_kate-hudson_592x888.jpg)

Kicking off the list of celebrities wearing all white is Kate Hudson. Here she teams a long-sleeved, knee-length knitted number with neutral pumps and a compact clutch. A white knitted dress might sound basic enough, but Kate Hudson makes hers red carpet-worthy.

## 2. Nicole Richie

![Nicole Richie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/2_nicole-richie_592x888.jpg)

Nicole Richie is always one to experiment with fashion trends. Here she works an all white look in a long-sleeved shift dress and pointed pumps. She keeps the jewellery to a minimum with some large metallic earrings and a few rings.

## 3. Harley Viera-Newton

![Harley Viera-Newton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/3_harley-viera-newton_592x888.jpg)

DJ/model/It-girl Harley Viera-Newton is fast becoming a favourite of fashion critics. Here she wears a knee-length white dress and pointed pumps. From the outset it’s all very ladylike, but the coloured sheath underneath adds a fun touch to the entire look. A bright red lipstick adds another pop of colour to the entire outfit.

## 4. Karlie Kloss

![Karlie Kloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/4_karlie-kloss_592x888.jpg)

Model Karlie Kloss shows off her body in this daring all white outfit. She wears a cut-out crop top with a knee-length pencil skirt and strappy heels. The sleek side-parted bob gives her entire look a very 1990s supermodel-inspired feel.

## 5. Elle Fanning

![Elle Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/5_elle-fanning_592x888.jpg)

The Fanning sisters have probably worn more designer clothing in their teen years than some people have in a lifetime! Here Elle Fanning rocks an all white look in a brocade dress and lace-up boots. While I’m not entirely sold on the boots, being young is all about making questionable fashion decisions, right?

## 6. Zoe Saldana

![Zoe Saldana](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/6_zoe-saldana_550x1100.jpg)

Zoe Saldana strikes a pose in a white long-sleeved jumpsuit. She wears it unbuttoned to reveal a lacy white bra underneath. A slicked back ponytail and bright red lip complete this striking all white outfit.

## 7. Kerry Washington

![Kerry Washington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/7_kerry-washington_550x1100.jpg)

The white dress and pointed white heels combination seems to be pretty popular with celebs and their stylists. Here, Kerry Washington wears a structured white shift dress with, you guessed it, white pointed pumps. Sleek wavy hair and berry lipstick complete the look.

## 8. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/8_rihanna_550x1100.jpg)

Rihanna’s all white outfit is a mish mash of trends. She works a casually polished look in a white blazer, matching tailored shorts, and two-tone capped sneakers. In true Rihanna style, she opts for not wearing anything under her blazer and loads up on the bling.

## 9. Emma Roberts

![Emma Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/9_emma-roberts_550x1100.jpg)

Emma Roberts teams her flirty white dress with strappy pointed pumps. She keeps the rest of her jewellery and accessories to a minimum. A compact yellow clutch is the exception, and adds a hint of colour the all white look.

## 10. Jaime King

![Jaime King](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/10_jaime-king_550x1100.jpg)

Jaime King works a minimal look in a white sheath dress and strappy white heels. She keeps the rest of her outfit fairly simple, with a compact metallic clutch the only statement accessory.

Try out one of hottest trends this season by putting together an all white outfit. These are just a few celebrities rocking an all white look. Which of these celebrities do you think worked their all white outfit the best?
