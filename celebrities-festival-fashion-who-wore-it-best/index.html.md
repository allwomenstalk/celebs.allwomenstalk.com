# 10 Celebrities' Festival Fashion \- Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities’ festival fashion varies as much as their personalities. Some prefer simple outfits of cut-off shorts and tees while others go for much more fashion forward looks. What we do know is that if there’s a place for celebrities to be seen ‘off duty,’ it’s at a music festival (or at one of the many pre- and post-festival parties). Let’s take a look at the recent Coachella festival and the celebrities’ festival fashion.

## 1. Rita Ora

![Rita Ora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/1_rita-ora_592x888.jpg)

Kicking off this list of celebrities’ festival fashion is songstress Rita Ora. She makes a statement in a graphic tee and full midi skirt. She grunges up what would otherwise be a very ladylike lilac skirt with a contrasting orange t-shirt, sneakers, and reflective sunglasses.

## 2. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/2_alexa-chung_592x888.jpg)

Alexa Chung and festival fashion go hand in hand. Here she takes a Wednesday Addams approach and wears a [simple black dress](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-your-little-black-dress-different-ways/) with a lace Peter Pan collar (of course). She finishes off her Coachella look for the day with fairy floss pink sunglasses and black strappy sandals.

## 3. Solange Knowles

![Solange Knowles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/3_solange-knowles_592x888.jpg)

Solange Knowles is becoming quite well known for her colourful style. Here she rocks a bright lemon print shift dress. Since the dress makes such a statement, she kept the rest of her outfit fairly simple with some metallic sandals and minimal accessories.

## 4. AnnaSophia Robb

![AnnaSophia Robb](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/4_annasophia-robb_592x888.jpg)

Actress AnnaSophia Robb shows us that Coachella fashion doesn’t have to be all about skimpy dresses and cut-off shorts. Here she wears a multicoloured striped midi dress with classic Converse trainers. She accessorised her colourful dress with some eye catching embellished sunglasses.

## 5. Whitney Port

![Whitney Port](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/5_whitney-port_381x555.jpg)

Colour is key to festival dressing. Whitney Port opted for a bold colour combination with this Coachella outfit. She teamed her turquoise tank dress with a punchy red handbag and simple strappy heels.

## 6. Agyness Deyn

![Agyness Deyn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/6_agyness-deyn_426x639.jpg)

Here, model Agyness Deyn rocks a cool and casual festival look. She teams a colourful printed shirt with cuffed jeans and turquoise sneakers. Oversized sunglasses and a fedora top off this casual festival look.

## 7. Katy Perry

![Katy Perry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/7_katy-perry_592x888.jpg)

Katy Perry isn’t one to shy away in the fashion stakes. Here she wears a bustier and skirt combination straight off of the runways of Dolce & Gabbana. It’s a risqué look but if anyone’s going to get away with it, it might as well be Katy.

## 8. Katharine McPhee

![Katharine McPhee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/8_katharine-mcphee_408x594.jpg)

Katharine McPhee’s festival outfit is one of the more street smart looks on this list. She wears a button up denim top with a striped maxi skirt and a statement necklace. This outfit would be just as appropriate in a city setting as it is in the middle of the Coachella buzz.

## 9. Sophia Bush

![Sophia Bush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/9_sophia-bush_412x594.jpg)

Here, Sophia Bush works a colour coordinated look. She teams her dark blue printed pants with a dark blue crop top and blue studded side bag. The cat shape shoes are a quirky addition to the outfit. She also manages to work in some boho touches with her jewellery.

## 10. Riley Keough

![Riley Keough](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/10_riley-keough_592x888.jpg)

Model and actress Riley Keough donned the festival favourite of faded denim shorts. She teamed her shorts with a blush blouse, which she wore unbuttoned and tied to reveal a bright red bikini underneath. Instead of the usual festival-friendly sneakers or boots, she opted for leopard print loafers.

These are just a few celebrity outfits from the recent Coachella music festival. As you can see, some outfits are simple and low key while others scream “look at me.” Which of these celebrity festival outfits do you like best?
