# 7 Delightful Street Style Looks from Nicole Richie ...

There are so many awesome street style looks from Nicole Richie. She's really come such a long way fashion-wise and has become a red carpet sensation. I think she has the ability to pull off anything! I mean, who else looks as cool as she does with purple hair?! Check out some of my favorite street style looks from Nicole Richie. They're all easy enough to recreate this summer!

## 1. Kimono Cardigan

![Kimono Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/if/yx/zj0fkoyr_500x790.jpg)

Kimono cardigans are a must-have for every fashionista. I recently bought one myself and I can't help but want to wear it over everything! It's a really great addition to any simple outfit - like jeans and a tee. It'll add some flare, especially if it's got a cool pattern! You can find some at [Forever 21](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). This is just one of the cool street style looks from Nicole Richie.

## 2. Printed Top

![Printed Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mk/fn/dbj9eifa_432x594.jpg)

Even while keeping it simple, Nicole keeps things fashionable. One of my favorite street style looks from her is actually really easy to recreate. Find a printed top (I'd for some zebra or leopard animal print), and pair it with some black skinny jeans. You'll look really put together and totally stylish. For shoes, go with some black flats! If you feel like adding some edge to your outfit, swap them for combat boots!

## 3. Red Leather

![Red Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/lm/d565vc4d_396x594.jpg)

Nicole's always rocking a leather jacket over her outfits. I think it adds an instant 'effortlessly cool' vibe! However, she doesn't always go for the boring black leather - she adds a pop of color! You can find some cool colors at [Charlotte Russe](http://www.charlotterusse.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). The pop of color will complement any outfit!

## 4. All-black Ensemble

![All-black Ensemble](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e9/s6/a4fbqap2_500x948.jpg)

I'm a huge fan of the 'all black' look. I think it makes you look really put together and sophisticated, without even trying so hard. Nicole's always rocking an all-black ensemble and making it look fierce! To recreate the look, go with black skinnies, black pumps, and a black blouse! To complete the look, don't forget your oversized sunglasses and purse.

## 5. Crop Top + High Waisted Pants

![Crop Top + High Waisted Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yl/tb/gbs9r735_600x1065.jpg)

This summer, keep up with the latest fashion trends. That means, crop tops + high waisted pants are totally in. I really like the idea of matching tops and pants. Coordinate the colors and patterns to make a fashion statement!

## 6. Mix + Match Patterns

![Mix + Match Patterns](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q7/cz/b8da6g5q_450x750.jpg)

Nicole's always taking fashion risks. I love that she's so daring and let's be honest, she can pull off ANYTHING! This summer, be bold with your look and mix and match different colors and patterns. You'd be surprised at just how many unexpected things look good together. Rock some stripes and polka-dots, or plaid and floral. Experiment with your style like Nicole does!

## 7. Maxi Dress

![Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hc/nm/k8x6svvx_600x860.jpg)

A maxi dress is a must-have for every girl's closet. It's definitely my go-to look. No matter if you're running errands, hitting the mall, or meeting up with your gal pals for lunch, a maxi dress is always a good idea.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many delightful street style looks from Nicole Richie. I don't know about you, but she's been one of my style icons for years now! She always looks fantastic. Of the list, which look is your favorite? Which look will you be recreating this summer?
