# Ellen Degeneres VS Jimmy Fallon: Who Won Their Lip Sync Battle?

Ellen DeGeneres and Jimmy Fallon are the two most lovable talk show hosts in America. They're both adorable, hysterical, and incredibly sweet--so it's about time they were pitted against each other. After all, how else would we finally figure out who the superior celeb was?

[Popsugar.com](https://www.popsugar.com/celebrity/Ellen-DeGeneres-Jimmy-Fallon-Lip-Sync-Battle-Video-38352813) has us covered. They posted the YouTube video of Ellen and Jimmy's lip syncing segment on their website. Jimmy sang catchy songs like Mr. Brightside by The Killers and Watch Me (Whip / Nae Nae). Meanwhile, Ellen sang, Do You Know Where You're Going To by Diana Ross and B*tch Better Have My Money by Rihanna.
