# 9 Celebrities Who Were in 90s Commercials ...

Believe it or not, there are so many big-name celebrities who were in '90s commercials. I think it's so awesome to watch them before they made it to super-stardom. Even though some '90s commercials were pretty silly, these celebrities look absolutely adorable in them. They've all gone on to do bigger and better things so kudos to them! Check out some of the celebrities who were in '90s commercials.

## 1. Joseph Gordon-Levitt

Joseph has always been such a cutie! In this commercial, he played a sweet little boy who was concerned with getting his dad to eat a Pop-Tart for breakfast before heading off to work. With an adorable smile like his, I'm sure Joseph helped raise Pop-Tarts sales! He's just one of many celebrities who were in '90s commercials.

## 2. Sofia Vergara

Today, Sofia's a famous spokesperson for Pepsi but did you know her relationship with the soda company goes all the way back to when she was 17? She starred in her first commercial for them in 1990. So in case you were wondering, Sofia Vergara's been gorgeous since her teen years! In this commercial, she turns heads at the beach while making her way to get a cold drink.

## 3. Aaron Paul

The 'Breaking Bad' actor starred in this 1999 commercial for 1-800-COLLECT alongside Mr. T. It's so bizarre that it's actually awesome! Does that make sense? He's not the focus of the commercial but you get a few seconds with him!

## 4. Amy Adams

If you're a fan of 'Grease' and banks, then you get the best of both worlds with this commercial.The famous "Summer Lovin'" song turns into "Summer Bankin'" and it's actually pretty fun to watch. Amy Adams appears in the commercial for a few seconds and shows off her singing voice!

## 5. Leonardo DiCaprio

Anything Leonardo DiCaprio is in is gold. Am I right, ladies? He starred in this 1991 commercial for 'Kraft Cheese', making us all fall in love with Fat Free Kraft Singles. Look at that beautiful smile of his!

## 6. Seth Green

Can you think of anyone better than Seth Green to promote a Nerf Slingshot? The comedic actor starred in this '90s commercial before his career took off. I think I have some mixed feelings about his haircut but, hey, that was the '90s.

## 7. Brad Pitt

In this 1991 commercial for Levis, Brad Pitt made every man want to get himself a pair. I mean, how gorgeous does he look in them? If you ask me, the moral of this commercial is that even though prison might be tough, being away from your Levis is a whole lot tougher.

## 8. Mila Kunis

Mila Kunis starred in numerous commercials before her career took off with "That 70s Show." I think she looked adorable in this '90s commercial for Barbie. I wonder if she ever thinks back on her commercial days!

## 9. Elijah Wood

Elijah Wood is another actor who's just always been such a cutie! In this commercial, he sits around with his camp buddies munching on Wavy Lay's. He even tricks their leader into doing all the work for them!

Well, there you have it. These are just 9 out of MANY big celebrities who starred in '90s commercials. How adorable were they? Of the list, which '90s commercial was your favorite?
