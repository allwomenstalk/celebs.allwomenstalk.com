# Too Much Bling? 10 of the Most Expensive Celeb Engagement Rings ...

Looking at these rings gives you a whole new perspective on the word "expensive." I mean, what's a $100,000 ring when it's compared to the ones that cost millions? Sigh. Our pockets might not be that deep but that doesn't mean we can't admire these beautiful (outrageously expensive) rings that these celebs have!

## 1. Elizabeth Taylor

![Elizabeth Taylor](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k0/pe/s9o76uwk_600x800.jpg)

All I can say is WOW. Elizabeth's 33.19-carat ring had an estimated cost of $8.8 million dollars. I think it's safe to say no one will ever outdo this one.

## 2. Kim Kardashian

![Kim Kardashian](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i2/ie/q5iw1osc_600x800.jpg)

When Kanye proposed to Kim, he slipped a gorgeous 15-carat ring on her finger. The estimated worth is $1.5 million dollars but it's rumored to have cost a whole lot more than that!

## 3. Beyonce

![Beyonce](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l3/mi/luwn1sif_585x936.jpg)

Could we really expect Queen Bey to be rocking anything less than an 18-carat ring? The estimated cost is $5 million!

## 4. Paris Hilton

![Paris Hilton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e6/j0/xz18mcce_600x800.jpg)

When Paris got engaged to Paris Latsis, she was sporting a 25-carat ring - estimated at $4.7 million.

## 5. Jennifer Lopez

![Jennifer Lopez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cv/tu/xyevokw7_600x800.jpg)

When J.Lo was married to Marc Anthony, her 8.5-carat ring had an estimated worth of $4.1 million!

## 6. Vanessa Bryant

![Vanessa Bryant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/em/is/ut91fuym_600x800.jpg)

Her 8-carat ring had an estimated worth of $4 million!

## 7. Mariah Carey

![Mariah Carey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oa/tz/fkm83pc5_432x648.jpg)

This superstar's 10-carat ring had a worth of $2.5 million!

## 8. Blake Lively

![Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zn/h6/a3a0yoo9_403x545.jpg)

Blake's 12-carat engagement ring had an estimated worth of $2.5 million!

## 9. Anna Kournikova

![Anna Kournikova](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/do/sx/tk1orqb4_600x800.jpg)

When Enrique Iglesias got engaged, he spent $2.5 million on this GORGEOUS 11-carat ring.

## 10. Ciara

![Ciara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ca/i8/dasmyrxl_585x936.jpg)
