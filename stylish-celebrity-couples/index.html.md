# 9 Stylish Celebrity Couples ...

Stylish celebrity couples are a force to be reckoned with. It’s not enough these days to be a stylish celebrity, you’ve got to have the stylish partner to match. The most stylish celeb couples in the game are also the ones that seem to garner the most interest and publicity. Here are some of the most stylish celebrity couples around right now.

## 1. David and Victoria Beckham

When it comes to stylish celebrity couples, David and Victoria Beckham are in it for the long haul. With constant appearances on the red carpet, brands to represent, and fashion labels to design for, the Beckham’s have definitely claimed their spot in the style stakes.

## 2. Miranda Kerr and Orlando Bloom

She’s a model, he’s an actor. They’re a pretty textbook couple, right? Factor in that she’s one of the most snapped models around (her off-duty outfits provide so much style content to online websites, blogs, and magazines) and he’s one of the hottest dudes in the business, and you’ve got one heck of a stylish couple.

## 3. Beyoncé and Jay-Z

Beyoncé and Jay-Z aren’t afraid to experiment with trends. He’s got the hip-hop mogul look sorted but can still hold his own in a tux. She has fun with all the latest contemporary fashion but still slays it on the red carpet in a gown. Throw in successful music careers on both parts and you’ve got one hell of an influential couple.

## 4. Jessica Biel and Justin Timberlake

Barring red carpet appearances, these two seem to be a pretty low profile couple. Jessica Biel’s probably one of the more understated stars of the red carpet, combining her fresh faced beauty with glamorous looks. And Justin Timberlake did bring sexy back, after all.

## 5. Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt

They’re one of the most famous couples in the business. There’s no denying the style and star power of the Jolie-Pitts. On the red carpet she knows how to work a glamorous gown and off it she cuts a stylish figure in all-black layered outfits. As for Pitt, he seems to be equally at home in a tuxedo as he is in jeans.

## 6. Diane Kruger and Joshua Jackson

While they may stay pretty much out of the spotlight, apart from red carpet appearance, there’s no denying that this couple have style credentials. Diane Kruger has quickly gained style icon status for many of her outfit choices on the red carpet, and Joshua Jackson does look dapper in a tux.

## 7. Kanye West and Kim Kardashian

Whether you love this couple or loathe them, ‘KimYe’ seem like they’re sticking around for some time yet. Sure, they’ve both had their less than fashionable moments, but in general these two hold major weight in the fashion game. They’re no strangers to wearing designer clothes, or designing clothes for that matter!

## 8. Emma Stone and Andrew Garfield

Both actors, these two are no strangers to style game. Emma Stone is quickly becoming a favourite on the red carpet and Andrew Garfield not only knows how to rock a tux but has even fronted a campaign for fashion label Band of Outsiders. Off the red carpet their dressed-down street style would appeal to a more youthful, hipster audience.

## 9. Daniel Craig and Rachel Weisz

They’re a low key profile but know how to wow on the red carpet. Rachel Weisz is an actor and no stranger to high fashion magazines, and Daniel Craig seems to be the embodiment of James Bond in sleek suits.

As you can see, there’s no shortage of stylish celebrity couples in Hollywood. It’s not always about the women these days, as the men are upping the ante in the fashion game too. Who tops your list of most stylish celebrity couples?
