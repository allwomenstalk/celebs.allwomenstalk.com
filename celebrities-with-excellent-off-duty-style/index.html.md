# 8 Celebrities with Excellent off\-Duty Style ...

**Celebrities With Style** are a favourite for fashion magazines and websites. We’re often emulating the looks of our favourite **celebrities with style**, and it’s a big selling point for brands when a celebrity is spotted in their clothing. While some celebrity looks are obviously styled by professionals, it’s when celebrities can put together looks that are just as good, if not better, than their professionally styled ensembles that you know they have true fashion cred. The following are a few **celebrities with style** to admire.

## 1. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/1_kate-bosworth_280x560.jpg)

Her relaxed, laid-back off-duty outfits make Kate one of the most popular **celebrities with style**. Some of her most notable off-duty looks are those worn when she is attending the popular Coachella music festival. Her wardrobe is quite covetable, and many of the outfits she is seen in are quickly emulated by fashion savvy females the world over.

## 2. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/2_miranda-kerr_280x560.jpg)

This popular model is also a street-fashion favourite. Her outfits combine the right amount of high fashion glamour and casual coolness. Her look is sleek and refined and always complemented by a fresh face. It’s the epitome of ‘model-off-duty’ style.

## 3. Kate Moss

![Kate Moss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/3_kate-moss_280x560.jpg)

Iconic for popularising the waif look, this model celebrity is famed for her off-duty look. Her style is very rock-chic, with skinny jeans and leather jackets being staple pieces of her wardrobe. Kate’s look is also very marketable, despite certain scandals, and her Topshop collections were a way for consumers to emulate her style.

## 4. Ashley Olsen

![Ashley Olsen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/4_ashley-olsen_280x560.jpg)

Quite famous for starting the ‘hobo chic’ trend, the Olsen twins are undoubtedly **celebrities with style**. Despite being twins, these days they have quite distinct styles. Ashley’s look is quite polished with a focus on tailored outfits and minimalist pieces.

## 5. Mary-Kate Olsen

![Mary-Kate Olsen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/5_mary-kate-olsen_280x560.jpg)

You can’t really have one without the other when it comes to the Olsen twins and listing **celebrities with style**. Whereas Ashley has recently taken to more polished and tailored looks, Mary-Kate still retains that eclectic, just-put-together look that made them fashion favourites in the first place.

## 6. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/6_alexa-chung_280x560.jpg)

This TV Presenter and sometimes model seems to be the darling of the fashion world. Her offbeat, indie presence is a hit with younger fashionistas, making her one of the more popular **celebrities with style**. She mixes high fashion with vintage finds in a way that seems to be attainable to the masses.

## 7. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/7_olivia-palermo_280x560.jpg)

This reality star turned fashion icon is well known for putting her best foot forward. Her socialista lifestyle means that her red carpet looks are well documented. However, her off-duty style is also to be admired.

## 8. Rachel Bilson

![Rachel Bilson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/01/8_rachel-bilson_280x560.jpg)

Made famous for her role in cult TV shoe The OC, Bilson has also enjoyed popularity as one of these **celebrities with style**. Her look is fresh and youthful, and is often heavily documented and replicated.

We often look to **celebrities with style** for fashion inspiration, mostly because their outfits are some of the most documented in the world. When it’s not the red carpet, these **celebrities with style** are being snapped on the street in their day to day outfits. Who are your favourite **celebrities with style**?
