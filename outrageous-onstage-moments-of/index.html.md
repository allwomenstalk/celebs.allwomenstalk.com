# 7 Outrageous Onstage Moments of 2013 ...

This year, there were many outrageous onstage moments. That’s my favorite part about watching award shows! There are always outrageous onstage moments that come out of it. Here’s my pick for the top seven that happened in 2013! Check out the list and then tell me if you agree!

## 1. Miley Cyrus’ VMA Performance

The public’s perception of Miley Cyrus changed forever after this performance. She danced provocatively to her song “We Can’t Stop,” and was joined on stage by Robin Thicke. The pair sang “Blurred Lines” and let’s be real, all lines were crossed that night. Miley rocked some nude, patent-leather spandex, pigtails, and the now iconic foam finger. She “twerked” it out and pretty much caused one of the most outrageous onstage moments. I’m actually a big Miley fan and personally, I think she’s just having fun. What do you think?

## 2. Jennifer Lawrence Trips after Winning an Oscar

As strange as it sounds, I think this moment actually made me love Jennifer Lawrence even more than I already did! Dressed in an elegant Dior Couture gown, the starlet took a major fall as she walked onstage to accept her Oscar for “Best Actress” in Silver Linings Playbook. She shook it off and even joked about it in her acceptance speech. That’s why I love Jennifer, she’s not afraid to make fun of herself. It’s definitely something refreshing to see in the movie industry.

## 3. 'N SYNC Reunites

I don’t know about you, but I dreamed about this moment for a really long time. While their performance at MTV’s VMAs was short and sweet, it was still perfect in every way. The guys performed a medley of some of their greatest hits, including “Bye Bye Bye” and “Girlfriend.” Then, Justin Timberlake launched into his solo performance again. Swoon!

## 4. Taylor Swift Disses Ex-Boyfriend

It seems as though the most outrageous moments of 2013 happened at the Video Music Awards! This moment was when Taylor Swift, winner of “Best Female Video of the Year,” gave her acceptance speech. The award was for Taylor’s “I Knew You Were Trouble,” which is rumored to be inspired by none other than Harry Styles. After Taylor’s comment, “I also want to thank the person who inspired this song who knows exactly who he is,” the MTV camera zoomed right into Harry’s face. Awkward.

## 5. Beyonce’s Halftime Performance

All I could say after watching this performance was “Wow.” Beyonce started the performance off with a medley of some of her greatest hits, including “Love on Top,” “Crazy in Love,” “Who Run The World,” and more. She was then joined by her former Destiny’s Child bandmates, Kelly Rowland and Michelle Williams. It was incredible! The performance was so electrifying that it “shut down” the Super Bowl... the Superdome had a 34-minute power outage after the performance!

## 6. Justin Bieber Walks off Stage

During a performance in Brazil this past November, a fan threw a water bottle onstage! The water bottle hit Justin’s arm and knocked his microphone out of his hand. His reaction? Instead of picking the microphone back up, he walked off stage. He didn’t even return to officially close the show. Later that night he tweeted “We had a good time tonight. Brazil goes hard."

## 7. Lady Gaga Cries through Performance

Gaga performed her song “Dope” at the inaugural YouTube Music Awards this past November. She began her performance in tears and quietly said, “This is for my fans. This is for my lover, I love you too.” With every note, Gaga became more and more emotional. Fans even shouted out, “We love you Gaga!” from the crowd. How moving was this performance?

There you have it! Seven outrageous onstage moments from 2013. Award shows and performances are always fun to watch because crazy things always happen. Which outrageous moment of 2013 was the most memorable to you?
