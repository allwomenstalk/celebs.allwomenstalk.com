# 9 Celebrities in Electric Blue Outfits  Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities in electric blue outfits are picking up on one of the most popular colour trends at the moment. We’re seeing more and more trendsetters working this statement colour into their outfits. Electric blue accessories and makeup are some ways to incorporate the trend into your look, but for a high voltage statement you can’t beat a bold, colourful outfit. Check out these celebrities in electric blue outfits and let us know who you think wore this colour trend the best.

## 1. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/1_miley-cyrus_550x1100.jpg)

Miley Cyrus is first on our list of celebrities in electric blue outfits. She was seen wearing this embroidered Pucci jacket in an electric blue hue. She teamed the cropped jacket with a matching silk layered skirt and pointy white pumps.

## 2. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/2_emma-stone_550x1100.jpg)

Emma Stone looks prim and proper in this bright blue Carven dress. It has a demure design with a high neckline and white Peter Pan collar. Since the blue is such a strong colour, she keeps the accessories to a minimum and wears a pair of neutral nude pumps. This is the kind of statement-making outfit that would still appease stricter dress codes.

## 3. Julianne Hough

![Julianne Hough](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/3_julianne-hough_350x700.jpg)

For a high impact look, wear this colour from head to toe. Julianne Hough rocks an electric blue jumpsuit out to a night time event. The jumpsuit has a bit of a seventies feel to it, with its plunging neckline and wide-legged design. The colour, however, is very ‘now.’ Julianne accessorises her outfit with a compact mirrored clutch, heeled sandals, and a slick side-parted ‘do.

## 4. Zosia Mamet

![Zosia Mamet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/4_zosia-mamet_550x1100.jpg)

Monochrome dressing is another big trend right now. Here, GIRLS actress Zosia Mamet dons a head to toe electric blue look. She teams a silky button up blouse with matching tailored trousers and black heels. Assorted rings, a high bun, and pink lipstick complete her look. Try recreating this look if you’re after a way to add some colour into your corporate attire.

## 5. Mandy Moore

![Mandy Moore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/5_mandy-moore_550x1100.jpg)

Mandy Moore works a colourful evening look in this electric blue dress. The dress features a plunging neckline, billowing sleeves, and an asymmetrical hem. She accessorises her dress with an embellished vintage-inspired clutch, neutral pointed pumps, and a slim metallic bangle.

## 6. Jessica Chastain

![Jessica Chastain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/6_jessica-chastain_550x1100.jpg)

Jessica Chastain hit up the publicity trail in this stunning Elie Saab dress. The electric blue dress featured a sheer lace bodice and peplum-style details around the waist. Sapphire drop earrings and metallic gold heels were glamorous finishing touches to this outfit.

## 7. Hailee Steinfeld

![Hailee Steinfeld](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/7_hailee-steinfeld_550x1100.jpg)

Hailee Steinfeld’s electric blue dress had a softer look to it, thanks to its suede-effect finish. The structured Sportmax dress also featured contrasting black panels and a box pleat skirt. She accessorised her dress with a matching blue envelope clutch and cutout sandals.

## 8. Tali Lennox

![Tali Lennox](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/8_tali-lennox_592x888.jpg)

Model Tali Lennox (daughter of singer-songwriter Annie Lennox) was spotted wearing this electric blue suit and gold sweater combo. While most celebs on this list have teamed their electric blue outfits with matching or neutral accessories, Tali takes a different approach with a metallic pink clutch and heels.

## 9. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/9_jessica-alba_592x888.jpg)

Jessica Alba shows us how to work more of an off-duty look in electric blue pants. She teams her pants with a plain white top, leather biker jacket, and studded flats. A casual cross-body bag completes this wearable daytime look.

These celebrities have all embraced the electric blue colour trend. Are you a fan of this blue hue? Which of these celebrity looks do you think works the best?
