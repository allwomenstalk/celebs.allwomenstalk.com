In a fun audience Q&A, Jimmy Kimmel helped reveal People Magazine's 2014 "Sexiest Man Alive!" Using a voice changer and a mask, the sexy man answered "yes" or "no" to fans' questions until somebody got it right! You can watch the reveal right here:

Yup, People's Sexiest Man is Australian hunk, Chris Hemsworth! *Swoon* Did any of you guess it was him during the Jimmy Kimmel Q&A?
