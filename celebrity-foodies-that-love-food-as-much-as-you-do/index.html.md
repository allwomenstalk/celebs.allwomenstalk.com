# 9 Celebrity Foodies That Love Food as Much as You do ...

There are so many celebrity foodies that you should be following on Instagram! These celebs are constantly posting photos of yummy meals and it'll definitely inspire you to get cooking. Fair warning though, following these celebrities will lead to late night cravings of all sorts... LOL! Check out some of my favorite celebrity foodies to follow.

## 1. Kelis

![Kelis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/dy/iq8p3au9_520x520.jpg)

Do her milkshakes really bring all the boys to the yard? Kelis is the ultimate foodie! She had her own cooking show, "Saucy and Sweet" on the cooking channel and even debuted an album titled "Food" earlier this year! The singer even served food from a food truck at last year's SXSW festival! She's just one of many celebrity foodies that love food just as much as you do.

## 2. Jesse Williams

![Jesse Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/ww/h2tvuf37_520x520.jpg)

This hunky Grey's Anatomy star is constantly posting photos of the most delicious meals! Seriously, following him on Instagram (@ijessewilliams) can lead to some serious cravings. How does he manage to eat such yummy foods and still be in such great shape?! Teach us your ways, Jesse!

## 3. Oprah Winfrey

![Oprah Winfrey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tu/e7/pqjcgmix_520x520.jpg)

Oprah has proven time and time again that she loves baking and cooking for all her friends and family! Can you imagine being invited to one of those dinner parties? I bet there are no leftovers to spare because, based on Oprah's Instagram feed, those meals are very delicious!

## 4. Jordan Dunn

![Jordan Dunn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ez/rc/x7igv4rz_520x520.jpg)

This gorgeous British model hosts "Well Dunn," on Jay-Z's Life + Times. She's an awesome foodie because she's always cooking up yummy (and easy-to-make) meals! You can follow her on Instagram at @officialjdunn.

## 5. Aziz Ansari

![Aziz Ansari](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/wp/t359b16q_600x600.jpg)

Park & Recreation's Aziz Ansari is a major foodie. He's constantly posting photos on Instagram (@azizansari). He also adds funny captions to them! For example, along with the snapshot of pasta, he wrote, "MISSING: Delicious pasta. Last seen on plate in front of Indian man in De Cesare in Roma. Please contact Polizia Roma with any tips. #LivingSoItalian" LOL!

## 6. Chrissy Teigen

![Chrissy Teigen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p6/tm/x28cnib5_600x600.jpg)

John Legend's gorgeous wife keeps the house smelling yummy with all her cooking! She's constantly posting photos of her meal preparations and even includes recipes and how-to's for all of her followers. She definitely inspires me to try some cool new recipes! Follow her on @chrissyteigen.

## 7. Neil Patrick Harris

![Neil Patrick Harris](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wc/dm/oauc21p1_600x600.jpg)

Neil's always posting pictures of yummy meals! His husband is a foodie too! Neil recently posted this photo of a delicious sandwich with the caption, "So I ask David if he wouldn't mind making me a quick sandwich, and this is what he serves. I'm one lucky M'er F'er. #grateful." Aw! You can follow him on @instagranph. Clever, right?!

## 8. Haylie Duff

![Haylie Duff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b2/md/bg0shj1p_600x600.jpg)

Haylie's one of my favorite celebrity foodies to follow! She has a cooking show, "Real Girl's Kitchen" on the cooking channel where she shows off awesome recipes! She even wrote a cook book, "The Real Girl's Kitchen" which I definitely recommend checking out! You can follow @haylieduff or @realgirlskitchen on Instagram!

## 9. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/so/cg/o5pza9t4_600x600.jpg)

If you follow @taylorswift on Instagram, then you're well aware of how much this girl loves to bake. She's constantly posting photos of cookies, cupcakes, and pies! Looking at these photos inspire me to get my late-night baking on!

Well, there you have it. These are just nine out of many celebrity foodies! Can you imagine a dinner party where ALL of these awesome celebrities contributed a meal? That'd be quite the feast. Which celebrity foodie do you love to follow?
