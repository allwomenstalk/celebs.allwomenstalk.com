# 7 Women Who Prove You Can Be Happy without a Husband ...

You don't need a husband in order to feel fulfilled. You're perfectly capable of finding happiness on your own. Even if you find the man of your dreams, you don't need to marry him in order to make your relationship feel important. Don't believe it? Well, according to _Women's Health Mag,_ these celebrities prove that you can be happy without a husband:

## 1. SARAH SILVERMAN

![SARAH SILVERMAN](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ti/i5/ufgjq07256a66c006db52608575718_600x849.jpg)

Sarah doesn't need a man in order to be happy with her life, because her career, friends, and family make her happy enough. She even said on Twitter, "Just read that I wanna get married which is hilarious b/c I will never get married.”

## 2. Shonda Rhimes

![Shonda Rhimes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gn/t8/z1284ujw56a66c10cee8c641344427_600x831.jpg)

This clever woman was the creator of Grey’s Anatomy. Even though a man once proposed to her, she turned the offer down, because she didn't believe that marriage was right for her. She was happy enough without it.

## 3. DIANE KEATON

![DIANE KEATON](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gh/wq/ee90ar4h56a66c26df7fb610118314_500x667.jpg)

This amazing actress once said, "I'm attracted to men, and I love playing around with them. But a life shared together? That's a different world. I think you have to be somebody who can compromise and be realistic. I could never do it. Ever." Marriage just isn't meant for some people.

## 4. DIANE KRUGER

![DIANE KRUGER](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vb/lz/ooyih1g656a6207a559d0320875353_600x600.jpg)

Diane is a beautiful actress and model. Even though millions of men would kill to have her, she has been quoted saying that she's "not married and doesn't intend to be."

## 5. KRISTIN DAVIS

![KRISTIN DAVIS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pt/kf/ph0shizk56a66bc7d4946108725011_600x750.jpg)

She has said, “I do remember being young and thinking, 'Why are all these people getting married?’” Some people just don't understand the hype. If that sounds like you, then don't feel pressured to get married.

## 6. OPRAH WINFREY

![OPRAH WINFREY](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/jk/t0oi0v5956a66b8ff2340210990747_600x800.jpg)

Oprah proves that you can have a long-term relationship, but avoid marriage. She's been dating her boyfriend for thirty years! She doesn't feel the need to get married in order to validate her feelings.

## 7. KOURTNEY KARDASHIAN

![KOURTNEY KARDASHIAN](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bm/ow/b3qjtc3c56a66baf4c0fd803149674_600x899.jpg)

Kourtney is another celeb who had a long-term boyfriend, but refused to marry him. She proves that you can be happy with a man without agreeing to marry him. A wedding won't change your relationship, after all.

Are you happy without a husband?
