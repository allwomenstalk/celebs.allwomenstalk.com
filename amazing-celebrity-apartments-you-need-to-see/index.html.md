# 7 Amazing Celebrity Apartments You Need to See ...

Home is [where the heart is](https://books.allwomenstalk.com/reasons-you-should-read-billie-letts-where-the-heart-is/), and in the case of these amazing celebrity apartments, home is where the lavish decor is. We all know celebrities have it good, but their pads are truly something to gawk at. In a big city like NYC, there's so many small apartments and essentially glorified closets that people pay a pretty penny for, but these city spaces are big, beautiful, and far from closet-like. As such, you can only imagine the price tags that come with them. Check out these amazing celebrity apartments and prepare to have a little home-envy:

## 1. Cameron Diaz

![Cameron Diaz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k8/yc/skc2tw69_600x543.jpg)

One of the first amazing celebrity apartments you need to see is Cameron Diaz's. She purchased the incredible Chelsea flat last year and the functional and design renovations are absolutely spectacular. The 2,400-square-foot pre-war apartment designed by Kelly Wearstler features textured wallpaper, bright yellow accents, chic turquoise kitchen cabinets, and hints of bright gold in every room.

## 2. Nicole Kidman and Keith Urban

![Nicole Kidman and Keith Urban](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w1/dg/nlwlpkpf_428x285.jpg)

Also located in Chelsea, the actress and country singer's condo has unbelievable views. The completely unobstructed giant windows throughout the space let in enormous amounts of light and overlook the water for a picture-perfect view. If that's not glamorous enough, the building also includes a garage lift that opens up right into your apartment. Ever heard of a 17th floor garage? Neither have we, and it's pretty immaculate.

## 3. Joan Rivers

![Joan Rivers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tu/xf/mqyrdb8p_600x450.jpg)

Joan's sense of humor is sometimes a bit foul, but her fit-for-a-queen Upper East Side apartment is anything but. The French Classic condo has original architecture restored by museum-trained artisans. Wow! No wonder her space looks like something out of a palace.

## 4. Leonard DiCaprio

![Leonard DiCaprio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b5/ay/qnsro8u4_466x333.jpg)

The Wolf of Wall Street actually resides in Greenwich Village-in a spectacular $10 million apartment. There are unbelievable amenities in the building, such as "vitamin C-infused" showers, circulated aromatherapy, and posture-supported heat reflexology flooring. Why go to the spa when you can stay at home, right?

## 5. Mary-Kate Olsen and Olivier Sarkozy

![Mary-Kate Olsen and Olivier Sarkozy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/zw/ruu42w5r_600x547.jpg)

For an apartment located in bustling Midtown, you wouldn't expect Mary-Kate's pad to have such stunning 17th century charm. The pad has a hallway of mirrors, a ballroom, and a private garden, among many other jaw-dropping features.

## 6. Derek Jeter

![Derek Jeter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x6/bk/lwd4xoxi_500x370.jpg)

Although the soon-to-be-retired baseball legend has sold his amazing Trump Tower penthouse (for $15 million, down from the $20 million it was originally listed at), the pad is still something worth mentioning. The 5,425-square-foot apartment boasts amazing views and has 4 bedrooms and 5.5 baths.

## 7. Tommy Hilfiger

![Tommy Hilfiger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r0/hc/uxd8hrnt_498x389.jpg)

We're saving the priciest for last. Tommy's duplex apartment in the lavish Plaza Hotel is worth a whopping $80 million dollars. That's 8-0. Phew! Just the art alone in the apartment is worth a fortune, such as an original Warhol and Bert Stern photos of Marilyn Monroe.

Ah, to live the life of the rich and famous. These stellar celebrity pads are to-die-for, and we only wish we could spend a night in one of them! It's pretty remarkable to think that some actual celebrity homes (not in NYC) are more than half the price of some of these famous digs. These celebrities are living life and living it large. Would you ever live in an immaculate pad like these or do you enjoy the simple life? Please share in the comments!
