# These Celebs Are Just IMPOSSIBLE to Hate!

Nowadays, it seems like there's always some kind of scandal surrounding a celebrity. People are quick to judge those in the limelight and are often changing their opinions of them. However, I doubt there's any way someone can NOT love the celebrities on this list:

## 1. Ellen Degeneres

![Ellen Degeneres](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b0/i9/gy1t6z9w_600x901.jpg)

Ellen's got one of the biggest hearts in the business. There's nothing horrible anyone can even say about her! She's hilarious, generous, and shows love to everybody.

## 2. Betty White

![Betty White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bq/l8/pvmbf264_600x793.jpg)

Must I really explain why Betty White is so lovable?! Whenever I find myself watching "The Golden Girls" reruns, I can't help but laugh. Betty's such a sweetheart and just makes you want to hug her!

## 3. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r9/eg/o13bivdk_600x761.jpg)

Emma's probably one of the most genuine people in the business. After achieving global success with Harry Potter, she still knows what's important. She even took time off from acting to pursue a degree at Brown University!

## 4. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cb/mr/s1m9dx81_600x777.jpg)

Emma Stone seems like the kind of person that could keep you laughing for hours. Her charisma and hilarious interviews make it impossible to hate her!

## 5. Neil Patrick Harris

![Neil Patrick Harris](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xw/oa/m9ng8h8m_600x800.jpg)

Neil's that kind of person that everyone wants to be friends with. He radiates positivity and has a great sense of humor. Most importantly, he's legen-wait for it-dary!

## 6. Anna Kendrick

![Anna Kendrick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ah/y8/n38j35ip_600x721.jpg)

Do you ever just cyberstalk Anna Kendrick and wish she was your best friend? Yeah, same. She stole Hollywood's heart with her role in Pitch Perfect, proving that she can sing AND act. She'd be so cool to hang out with!

## 7. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ou/md/erz3bhus_600x800.jpg)

I think it's safe to say that J.Law is my soul-sister. This girl cracks me up in every interview she gives! She's humble, genuine, and hasn't let her fame change her in the slightest. Wouldn't you love to be friends with this Hunger Games star?

Which other celebs do you think are just impossible to hate?
