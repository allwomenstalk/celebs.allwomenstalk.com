# 10 Celebrities Wearing Yellow  Who Wore It Best?

There are plenty of stylish celebrities wearing yellow these days. Yellow is a notoriously tricky colour to pull off but when it’s worn well it can look really stylish. If you’re thinking about adding some yellow clothing to your wardrobe, take inspiration from the trendsetters. Check out these celebrities wearing yellow and let us know who you think wore it best.

## 1. Kate Mara

![Kate Mara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/1_kate-mara_350x700.jpg)

To kick off our list of celebrities wearing yellow we have actress Kate Mara in a cute Dior dress. The dress has a fairly simple cut with a flourishing dip hem design. She keeps it simple with a slick hairdo, gold necklace, purple clutch, and satin peep toe shoes. This is a fun way to wear a sunshine yellow dress on the red carpet.

## 2. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/2_olivia-palermo_350x700.jpg)

A bright yellow piece of clothing can really pack a punch. Trendsetter Olivia Palermo let her Old Navy yellow coat do all the talking by teaming it with a simple black sweater, leather leggings, velvet slippers, and a textured carryall. A yellow coat like this one is a great way to brighten up a winter wardrobe.

## 3. Jessica Chastain

![Jessica Chastain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/3_jessica-chastain_350x700.jpg)

Actress Jessica Chastain glammed it up in a yellow Stella McCartney lace dress. She teamed it with nude pointy Christian Louboutin pumps, minimal jewellery, and a bright red lip. Lace can be very ladylike, but the sheerness of this particular dress gives it a risqué edge.

## 4. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/4_miranda-kerr1_350x700.jpg)

Model Miranda Kerr was snapped in Whole Foods wearing a bright yellow blazer. She wore it with a printed top, bandage skirt, pointed pumps, and tortoiseshell shades. I don’t know about you, but I definitely don’t get this dressed up to do the grocery shopping.

## 5. Kristen Stewart

![Kristen Stewart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/5_kristen-stewart1_350x700.jpg)

Kristen Stewart has been rocking some seriously fun designer clothing lately. She wore this yellow Christian Dior mini dress to the Madrid premiere of The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn Part 2. She kept the look fun and youthful by teaming her dress with studded heels and slicked back ponytail.

## 6. Emmy Rossum

![Emmy Rossum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/6_emmy-rossum_350x700.jpg)

Yellow mini dresses are super fun and summery. Actress Emmy Rossom wore her Dior mini dress out at night with a diamond bracelet, sparkly clutch, and satin sandals. She shows us that it’s easy to dress up a yellow frock at night.

## 7. Nikki Reed

![Nikki Reed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/7_nikki-reed_350x700.jpg)

Nikki Reed wore a gold Tory Burch sweater with black jeans and ankle boots to a Gillette Event in New York. The addition of a statement necklace and bright red lip was also a great way to spice things up. This is the kind of outfit that you could easily recreate in your wardrobe.

## 8. Elizabeth Olsen

![Elizabeth Olsen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/8_elizabeth-olsen_350x700.jpg)

Mustard yellow is one of those divisive colours. Some people love it while others loathe it. Here, actress Elizabeth Olsen wears a furry mustard yellow coat over a floral shirt, black skinny jeans, and boots. This outfit is a great example of how to casually style a yellow jacket in the winter time.

## 9. Diane Kruger

![Diane Kruger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/9_diane-kruger_350x700.jpg)

Actress Diane Kruger is a favourite with fashion critics. This sleek Calvin Klein dress certainly packs a punch with its cut out design and split hem. The slicked back ponytail and simple heels kept the whole look minimal and fresh.

## 10. Lauren Conrad

![Lauren Conrad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/10_lauren-conrad_350x700.jpg)

Lauren Conrad wore a fun yellow dress by Yoana Baraschi to a book signing for her book Starstruck. It’s the type of dress that you could easily dress up or down. Since the dress is quite detailed, she kept the rest of her look fairly simple with suede pumps and wavy, beachy hair.

Yellow is such a fun and cheery colour. These celebrities show that it can look stylish both on and off the red carpet. Who do you think wore this colour best?
