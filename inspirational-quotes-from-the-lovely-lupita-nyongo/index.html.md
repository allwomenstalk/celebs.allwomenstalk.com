# 7 Inspirational Quotes from the Lovely Lupita Nyongo ...

Lupita Nyong'o is more than a beautiful actress. She's also a wise woman. According to [Essence,](https://goo.gl/qRXx55) here are some of the best Lupita Nyong'o quotes:

## 1. Be Yourself

![Be Yourself](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b1/kt/qo63xqqt56d9a25d888e1621823514_600x390.jpg)

Never pretend to be someone you're not. That imaginary person isn't any better than you are.

## 2. Black Beauty is Beautiful

![Black Beauty is Beautiful](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r1/hy/fet1d0oo56d9a25dd871d335140133_600x390.jpg)

This quote speaks for itself, don't you think? Be proud of who you are!

## 3. You Can't Run from Pain

![You Can't Run from Pain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nh/lq/dnc1cbun56d9a25e0e0d2259697935_600x390.jpg)

Everyone experiences pain. Dealing with it is the important part.

## 4. Being Called Beautiful is a Compliment

![Being Called Beautiful is a Compliment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i0/bv/hyza5uuq56d9a25e9dda4470204859_600x390.jpg)

Compliments are always welcomed! Of course, it's normal to want to be seen as more than a pretty face.

## 5. You Should Always Be Improving

![You Should Always Be Improving](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p3/jh/cucnp7uv56d9a25ea2fd2980231117_600x390.jpg)

You're always going to have things to work on. But that's how you grow as a person.

## 6. Your Dreams Are Valid

![Your Dreams Are Valid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ip/gr/xrusjx6e56d9a25ea5270990200867_600x390.jpg)

Never give up on those dreams! You can achieve them if you try.

## 7. You Can't Eat Beauty

![You Can't Eat Beauty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ep/t5/xddde5hv56d9a25f01e65930892061_600x390.jpg)

It's natural to spend your morning in front of the mirror and buy flattering clothes. Of course, you have to remember that there's more to life than what you look like.
