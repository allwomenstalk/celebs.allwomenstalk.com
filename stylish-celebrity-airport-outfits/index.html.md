# 8 Stylish Celebrity Airport Outfits ...

Celebrity airport style is another one of those things that tends to separate celebrities from ‘normal’ people. How do they look so fresh after a long haul flight? Why don’t my travel outfits look that chic? When it comes to travelling in style, celebrities seem to have the formula well and truly sorted. Next time you’re planning your travel outfit, take inspiration from these stylish celebrity airport outfits.

## 1. Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

![Rosie Huntington-Whiteley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/06/1_rosie-huntington-whiteley_290x580.jpg)

A must for any travel outfit is an oversized scarf (hint: it also doubles as a blanket). Here, Rosie Huntington-Whiteley uses a stylish leopard print scarf to update a basic outfit of jeans and a blazer. As far as celebrity airport style goes, this outfit is every part the model-off-duty look that Huntington-Whiteley is famed for.

## 2. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/06/2_taylor-swift_290x580.jpg)

Taylor Swift works a casual travel look in a striped sweater, cropped jeans, and flat lace-up shoes. The oversized sunglasses are a smart way to hide tired eyes, too! This outfit is fuss-free and suits her youthful style.

## 3. Ashley Greene

![Ashley Greene](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/06/3_ashley-greene_290x580.jpg)

This outfit of jeans, camel cardigan and nude pumps is timeless. What I like about Ashley Greene’s celebrity airport style is that while she’s dressed up in designer threads, she also manages to keep it real by carrying a sporty gym bag. While I’m not too keen on the idea of trekking through airports in high heels, you could always keep them in your hand luggage to pop on at the last minute.

## 4. Kirsten Dunst

![Kirsten Dunst](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/06/4_kirsten-dunst_290x580.jpg)

Well known for her quirky and offbeat fashion choices, Kirsten Dunst steps it up a notch in a Louis Vuitton patterned top. This travel outfit is completed with black skinny jeans, strappy sandals, and dark black shades.

## 5. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/06/5_miranda-kerr_290x580.jpg)

Model Miranda Kerr looks truly stylish wearing a classic combination of a striped t-shirt, dark denim jeans, and signature Louis Vuitton bag. The bright red heels add some va-va-voom to the entire look. Even if you don’t have the money for a designer handbag, this celebrity airport style can easily be recreated on a budget.

## 6. Michelle Williams

![Michelle Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/06/6_michelle-williams_290x580.jpg)

Wearing jeans, especially on long haul flights, isn’t always the most comfortable of travel outfit options. For more freedom and comfort, take a leaf out of Michelle Williams’ book and throw on a cute dress over a pair of tights. The coat also ties the entire look together.

## 7. Fergie

![Fergie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/06/7_fergie_290x580.jpg)

Fergie shows that celebrity airport style doesn’t always have to involve an off-duty look. Here she works leather leggings, gold sandals, and a Balenciaga print top. An oversized Celine tote completes the designer look.

## 8. Leona Lewis

![Leona Lewis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/06/8_leona-lewis_290x580.jpg)

Singer Leona Lewis takes a ladylike angle when styling her travel outfit. She combines a high waisted polka dot skirt with a cropped tie-front shirt and nude pumps. The oversized cat eye sunglasses give it a bit of a retro twist as well.

As you can see, when it comes to celebrity airport style, most celebrities opt for a more dressed down approach. Which of these stylish celebrity airport outfits do you like best? What are your best tips for travel dressing?
