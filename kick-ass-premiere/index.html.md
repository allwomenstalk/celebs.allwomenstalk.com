# Pick Best Dressed at the Kick\-Ass Premiere in L.a ...

While I love to ogle at beautiful celebrities and their gorgeous attire, I will never pass on an opportunity to criticize them if they deserve it. Premiere photos offer the best of both worlds. Check out the celebrities who attended the [Los Angeles](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/los-angeles/) premiere of Kick-Ass.
What happened to Nicolas Cage's hair?! Get some hair stylist help pronto!
Aaron Johnson who plays Dave Lizewski poses with Cage. They're dressed so similar aren't they? By the way, I'm still shocked by Cage's hair!
Chloe Grace Moretz aka Hit-Girl looked absolutely adorable in a darling hot pink dress! I love the giant bow. It's so Alice in Wonderland!
Who are the Dudesons and why am I looking at their bums?!
Lyndsy Fonseca is a gorgeous girl and I love the color of her dress. Not too sure of the dress itself though. What do you think?
I absolutely love the Alice + Olivia ‘Elena’ embellished tank that Nicky Hilton wore! I also love that she paired it with leather leggings. She definitely has the legs to carry them off. Pose away Nicky!
Aaron posed with his pregnant wife, Sam Taylor Wood. That's one too many hearts for me!
I much prefer Sam's heart dress to Tanya Jone's horror of a dress.
I'm sorry but Jaime King in this Paule Ka number is just way too Dynasty for me! Maybe if it was better fitted?
I do like her hair though!
And the Best Dressed in my opinion was Chloe followed by a very close Nicky Hilton. Your opinions please?
