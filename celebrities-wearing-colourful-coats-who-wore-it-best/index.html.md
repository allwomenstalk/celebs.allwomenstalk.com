# 11 Celebrities Wearing Colourful Coats  Who Wore It Best?

With the arrival of the cooler weather, we’ve spotted a number of celebrities wearing colourful coats. Colourful coats seem to be a popular choice at the moment, and they’re a great alternative to the usually dark and subdued colours of fall and winter. From the polished minimalist style of Olivia Palermo to urban-inspired outfits from Rihanna, there are a number of ways to wear a colourful coat this season. Take a look at these celebrities wearing colourful coats and be sure to let us know who you think wore theirs the best.

## 1. Jessica Chastain

![Jessica Chastain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203511_6893_592x888.jpg)

Jessica Chastain is first on our list of celebrities wearing colourful coats with this bright red knee-length coat. It has a double breasted design with a large rounded collar. She teams her coat with strappy platform heels and wears it buttoned up for a polished daytime look. If you really want to pack a punch take a leaf out of Jessica’s book and sport a lipstick red coat this season.

## 2. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203510_3406_589x960.jpg)

Olivia Palermo is known for being able to mix casual trends with preppy polish. Here she teams some ripped skinny jeans with a coral coat. The coat has a boxy, sleek design with ruching at the shoulders. She accessorises this smart daytime outfit with wayfarer sunglasses and multicoloured ballet flats.

## 3. Zosia Mamet

![Zosia Mamet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203511_6377_592x888.jpg)

Actress Zosia Mamet wears a bright [pink coat](https://streetstyle.allwomenstalk.com/street-style-ways-to-wear-pink-coats-for-fall/) over a black outfit at night. The pink coat is a must have for fall and winter and it’s a popular choice for celebrities and trendsetters alike. Zosia works some colour clashing by teaming her pink coat with bright red heels.

## 4. Rita Ora

![Rita Ora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203511_9764_592x888.jpg)

Singer Rita Ora makes a statement in a lilac graffiti print coat. She teams it with black clothing and chunky, strappy, statement-making shoes. She also accessorises with a pendant necklace and gold bangles and rings.

## 5. Rose Byrne

![Rose Byrne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203511_2656_592x888.jpg)

If bright, punchy colours aren’t your thing then make like Rose Byrne and pop on a mustard coloured coat for fall. It’s still colourful but a bit more subdued than the other coats on this list. She keeps the rest of her outfit fairly simple with a striped top, skinny jeans, and short ankle boots.

## 6. Eva Mendes

![Eva Mendes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203511_9054_592x888.jpg)

Here, Eva Mendes steps out in a candy pink coat. It has a boxy, almost oversized, design which is something we saw a lot of on the designer runways. Here, she teams her ladylike coat with black clothing, fishnet tights, and grey ankle booties. The boxy patterned handbag adds a quirky touch to the outfit.

## 7. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203512_3163_592x888.jpg)

Rihanna knows how to do urban glamour. Here, she wears a street inspired look of a pit bull print t-shirt, leather-look skater skirt, and backwards cap. She teams it with a boxy bright orange coat and ladylike pointed pumps.

## 8. Solange Knowles

![Solange Knowles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203514_6239_592x888.jpg)

While most other celebrities on this list have teamed their colourful coats with fairly basic outfits, Solange takes it up a notch with a citrus-inspired look. She works a spot of colour blocking by teaming her orange coat with a yellow dress, shoes, and clutch. She tops off her statement-making look with gold glittery sunglasses.

## 9. Lea Michele

![Lea Michele](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203513_1781_592x888.jpg)

Lea Michele was spotted on the set of Glee wearing this bright purple coat. It has a military-inspired double breasted design with a full skirt and tie waist detail. She teams it with black pumps for a very ladylike look.

## 10. Allison Williams

![Allison Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203512_2151_550x1100.jpg)

GIRLS actress Allison Williams wears white separates with a punchy orange coat. It’s a fresh combination and an on-trend take on minimalist dressing. She accessorises with a large clutch and strappy heels.

## 11. Fearne Cotton

![Fearne Cotton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203513_7384_592x888.jpg)

English TV and radio presenter Fearne Cotton was spotted out and about in a sky blue coat. She wears it with skinny jeans, black ankle boots, and a slouchy beanie for a casual everyday look.

From pastel pinks to punchy oranges, colourful coats seem to be all the rage right now. Whether you’re working a sophisticated or casual look, colourful coats are an on-trend addition to your outfits this season. Which celebrity rocked their colourful coat the best?
