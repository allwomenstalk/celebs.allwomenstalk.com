# This is What Happens when Bill Gates is Your Secret Santa! I'm Jealous!

Did your Secret Santa stick some candy canes in a bag and call it quits? Well, after reading this story, you're going to be wishing that your Secret Santa was Microsoft co-founder, Bill Gates! He joined the annual Reddit Secret Santa gift-exchange and generously gifted one lucky person.

![](https://img.allw.mn/content/wa/xx/wudst2ra_320x240.gif)

Reddit user Calid7 was the lucky recipient and, like any of us would, she freaked out when she saw her gift! "I can not believe it. I am literally shaking right now. I AM IN SHOCK," she wrote on [redditgifts.com](https://www.reddit.com/). "The amazing human being Bill Gates sent me a Loki Helmet which I have been dying to own ever since the first Thor movie came out!! He read my "pie in the sky wish" that I filled out in my questionnaire and fulfilled it! I also got an amazing coffee table book of gorgeous pictures of Africa (somewhere I desperately want to go, its on my bucket list!) with an inscription and a Giant microbes polio virus stuffed animal!" Of course, Bill didn't just stop there. He also donated to Shot@Life, an organization that provides vaccines to children living in poor countries, in her name! "I can't even begin to describe how incredible it makes me feel knowing that he made this donation in my name! I always donate what I can for charities, which is never much 'cos money is always extremely tight; it really makes me glow!"

Awesome, right?! How do you think he'll top his Secret Santa gift next year? What's your best Secret Santa gift?
