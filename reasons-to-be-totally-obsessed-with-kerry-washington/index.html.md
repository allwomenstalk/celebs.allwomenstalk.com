# 7 Reasons to Be Totally Obsessed with Kerry Washington ...

There are SO many reasons as to why you should love Kerry Washington! To be honest, narrowing down this list to only seven was pretty difficult. However, I do think these seven reasons are worthy of a spot on the list! If you're a fan of Scandal, then you're more than likely already obsessed with this amazing woman. if you're not, check out why you SHOULD be obsessing over Kerry Washington!

## 1. Her Sense of Style

![Her Sense of Style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/cp/py/i0qccxv6_600x903.jpg)

Kerry always looks flawless on the red carpet. She's said that she's developed a "character" for when she gets glammed up, and that person is "Red Carpet Kerry." I love that she's not afraid to take any risks! She's always been really fashion forward and I think it's definitely been to her benefit. Did you see her at the Oscars? That baby bump was just TOO cute! This is just one of the reasons to be obsessed with Kerry Washington!

## 2. Scandal

![Scandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v8/q8/gx/kppgzh6y_600x969.jpg)

I'm not the only one who thinks you should be watching "Scandal"! Critics everywhere are hooked. Kerry scored Emmy, Golden Globe and SAG Award nominations for her role as Olivia Pope. She actually even made history when she was cast as the lead in the show. It marked the first time since 1974 (when Teresa Graves starred on ABC's "Get Christie Love!") that a black actress was cast as the lead in an American network drama. WOW!

## 3. Loyalty

![Loyalty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lc/fh/ca/sqolzfkm_464x650.jpg)

Like Olivia Pope, Kerry demands loyalty from all her friends and family. Her group of friends have been the same since high school! She once told Elle Magazine, "My girlfriends from high school are the smartest and strongest women I know." I admire the fact that she's still running with the same crowd as she did as a teenager. It shows that she's a very loyal friend and her friends, in turn, are very loyal to her.

## 4. She's Involved in Politics

![She's Involved in Politics](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gn/01/o5/ikzlp0pt_587x842.jpg)

I love when actors can donate a huge amount of their personal time to bigger and better causes. In 2012, Kerry Washington did just that. During the presidential election, the "Scandal" actress spoke during the Democratic National Convention. Today, Kerry is a member of the President's Committee on the Arts and Humanities, which advises the White House on cultural issues. Yup, Kerry Washington. Not 'Olivia Pope'.

## 5. Keeps Her Mystery

![Keeps Her Mystery](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c6/rc/bf/ftguophe_600x796.jpg)

Kerry is a firm believer in keeping her personal life, personal. She didn't even acknowledge her pregnancy until she had a huge belly! I love that she keeps those types of things personal. It kind of makes things feel like she's just like us. Someone with personal affairs that would rather not have it broadcasted.

## 6. She's Gorgeous

![She's Gorgeous](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mx/ub/s8/d224coby_600x781.jpg)

Let's be real, Kerry Washington is drop dead gorgeous. Of course, people are taking notice! Kerry has lent her gorgeous looks to both Neutrogena and L'Oréal Paris. She has a gorgeous face and what I love the most is the INNER beauty that shines right through her. She does so much and still keeps her humility intact. She's beautiful inside and out!

## 7. She's Intelligent

![She's Intelligent](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ee/s7/y2/qkqy9dna_600x898.jpg)

Kerry was blessed with beauty AND brains! The actress studied at George Washington University's Columbian College of Arts & Sciences. She even designed her own major that combined her studies in theater, dance, music, psychology, anthropology and sociology. I'm already impressed. She was honored as a Presidential Scholar in the Arts for all four years at GWU!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven of MANY reasons why you should love Kerry Washington! What do you love most about her?
