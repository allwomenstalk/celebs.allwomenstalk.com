# 7 Cool Reasons to Love Alexz Johnson ...

There are so many fantastic reasons to love Alexz Johnson. If she's not on your radar, there's something wrong! Personally, I think she's got one of the best voices around. I really don't think it'll be long before she starts getting major recognition. Check out some of the reasons to love Alexz Johnson. Feel free to add your own in the comments section!

## 1. Instant Star

Long-time Alexz Johnson fans probably know her for her role as Jude Harrison in the show Instant Star. When I tell you that show was my favorite show, I'm not lying. I still jam out to the series soundtrack because those songs will just never get old. Of course, Alexz has long moved on from her Instant Star days - but that doesn't mean I can't dream about a tour where she plays songs from the show. How epic would that be? This show is just one of many reasons to love Alexz Johnson!

## 2. She Loves Her Fans

Alexz is constantly showing love to her fans! She interacts with them on her social media accounts and always poses with fans after a performance. I think it's awesome that she makes it a priority to meet all of her fans. She's always so sweet and that makes me adore her even more.

## 3. Upcoming Summer Tour

Alexz is hitting the road this summer with YouTube sensation Max Schneider. I can't wait to see the two together; I think their music styles complement each other so well. Alexz is offering VIP packages, so make sure to check out her official website for more info on how you can meet her at a show! She'll be performing fan favorites along with some brand new material from her upcoming album.

## 4. She's Gorgeous

Alexz is one of those people who just radiate positivity. It seems like she's always got a smile on her face, and to me, that makes anyone beautiful. Her personality is proof that she's truly beautiful on the inside and out! I've seen her perform live a few times and I'm always so blown away by how gorgeous she is!

## 5. Her Lyrics

Have you ever stopped and really listened to Alexz's lyrics? They're pretty amazing. Every song is powerful in its own way. My favorites, to just a name a few, include 'Skipping Stone,' 'Thief,' 'Skin,' and 'Walking.' A favorite line of mine is, "Can't stay with you, but I miss you." If that doesn't relate to every girl then I don't know what will!

## 6. Her Youtube Videos

Alexz is really good at keeping her fans updated via YouTube. She's constantly posting fun footage of performances, which is such a treat to watch. She's got close 13,000 subscribers who eagerly await her next video! Whether it be a new original or some concert footage, I love that she takes the time to share it with us all.

## 7. She's Genuine

Despite her success, Alexz doesn't think she's 'too cool for school,' and that's really refreshing to see. She seems so down to earth, like someone who could easily become your best friend. To her, making music is just about making music. She doesn't really care about 'fame' and that's my favorite kind of artist. When I met her, she was SO sweet. Granted, I didn't say much since I was starstruck and all!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome reasons to love Alexz Johnson. I hope she keeps making music and acting for the rest of her life because she'll always have a fan in me! What's your favorite thing about Alexz?
