# 7 Reasons Why Alexz Johnson Is Still the Indie Queen You Need to Be Listening To

There are so many fantastic reasons to love Alexz Johnson. If she's not on your radar yet, it's time to change that! Personally, I think she possesses one of the most powerful and unique voices in the industry today. It’s been incredible to watch her evolution from a teen star to a powerhouse independent artist who consistently delivers soulful, high-quality music. Whether you're a long-time fan or just discovering her, check out some of the top reasons to love Alexz Johnson. Feel free to add your own favorites in the comments section!

## 1. The Instant Star Legacy

Long-time Alexz Johnson fans probably first fell in love with her as Jude Harrison in the hit series _Instant Star_. That show was a defining moment for many of us, and the series soundtracks are still absolute bops that never get old. While Alexz has grown tremendously since her _Instant Star_ days, her ability to capture raw emotion through music remains her greatest strength. It’s always fun to look back at where it all started, especially since she still occasionally treats fans to acoustic versions of those classic tracks.

## 2. She Has a Deep Connection With Her Fans

Alexz is constantly showing love to her community! In the modern era, she has taken fan engagement to the next level through her [Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/alexzjohnson) and various social media accounts. She makes it a priority to interact with her supporters, often hosting exclusive livestreams and sharing behind-the-scenes glimpses of her creative process. She is incredibly sweet and genuine, making it easy for fans to feel like they are part of her journey. If you love supporting [indie musicians you should know](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/indie-musicians-you-should-know/), she is the perfect artist to follow.

## 3. Her Success as an Independent Artist

One of the most inspiring things about Alexz is her commitment to her craft on her own terms. After navigating the complexities of major labels, she launched her own independent label, Laydee Spencer Music Inc. Her most recent album, _Salvage_, released in 2025, is a testament to her growth as a songwriter and producer. She has successfully used fan-funding to bring her visions to life, proving that you don't need a massive corporate machine to create world-class music. You can find her latest updates and merch on her [official website](https://www.alexzjohnson.com/).

## 4. She Radiates Authenticity and Positivity

Alexz is one of those people who truly radiate positivity. Whether she’s performing on stage or sharing updates about her life as a mother and artist, she always seems to have a genuine smile on her face. Her personality is proof that she's beautiful inside and out. Having seen her perform live, I can tell you that her energy is infectious—she leaves everything on the stage and makes every member of the audience feel seen.

## 5. Her Incredible Songwriting

If you haven't stopped to really listen to Alexz's lyrics, you are missing out. Every song is a powerful narrative. From early favorites like "Skipping Stone" and "Skin" to newer masterpieces on her _Salvage_ and _Seasons_ albums, her songwriting is deeply relatable and poetic. She has a way of articulating complex feelings that resonate with women everywhere. Here are a few must-listen tracks to get you started:

- **"Skipping Stone"** – A hauntingly beautiful fan favorite.
- **"Slow Burn"** – A standout track from her 2025 album _Salvage_.
- **"Ruthless"** – Showcasing her incredible vocal range and grit.
- **"Walking"** – A soulful anthem that highlights her storytelling skills.

## 6. Engaging YouTube and Digital Presence

Alexz is a pro at keeping her fans updated via her [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/user/alexzjohnsonvids). She’s constantly posting fun footage of performances, music videos, and personal vlogs. With a dedicated subscriber base that has followed her for decades, she continues to provide a treat for the eyes and ears. Whether it’s a new original or a stripped-back cover, her digital presence allows fans from all over the world to stay connected to her music.

## 7. She Is Truly Genuine

Despite her long-standing success, Alexz remains incredibly down-to-earth. She doesn't care about the traditional trappings of fame; for her, it’s all about the music and the message. She feels like someone who could easily be your best friend, and that authenticity is what makes her such a compelling artist. When you meet her, she is just as kind and thoughtful as you’d hope. She’s a true [incredible female vocalist](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/incredible-female-vocalists/) who stays true to herself.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven of the many reasons why Alexz Johnson continues to be a force in the music world. I hope she keeps creating for years to come because she'll always have a fan in me! What's your favorite Alexz Johnson era or song? Let us know in the comments!
