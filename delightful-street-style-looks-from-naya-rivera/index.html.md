# 7 Delightful Street Style Looks from Naya Rivera ...

There are so many fantastic street style looks from Naya Rivera. She's managed to become everyone's #WCW (Woman Crush Wednesday) on Instagram and can you blame them? She always looks incredibly stylish - even when she's just walking around in sweatpants. She's become such a fashion icon on and off the red carpets. Check out some of my favorite street style looks from Naya Rivera!

## 1. Lace + Leather

![Lace + Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m4/kt/ttrzhl01_600x889.jpg)

I absolutely love the idea of pairing lace and leather together. It's girly meets edgy and they balance each other out perfectly. There's so many ways that you can wear lace and leather. If you want to recreate Naya's look, pair a lace crop top with some leather leggings. You could also swap the leggings for a skirt! This is just one of the awesome street style looks from Naya Rivera.

## 2. Graphic Tee + Ripped Jeans

![Graphic Tee + Ripped Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/fm/dg1ru1qf_500x698.jpg)

This is one of the easiest looks to recreate this summer. Graphic tees are a must-have in every girl's wardrobe. It's what allows you to be expressive - I can't even tell you how many graphic band tees I have in my closet. It's my go-to in the summer. You can pair you graphic tees with some skinny jeans for a laid-back and stylish outfit.

## 3. Sporty Chic

![Sporty Chic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aj/0q/w9s07f1u_600x827.jpg)

No one does sporty chic like Naya does! To recreate this look, pair a cropped jersey with a pencil skirt. What makes it sporty is adding some sneakers to the look. You'll look effortlessly cool just like Naya!

## 4. Pop of Neon

![Pop of Neon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y5/tz/yammwp97_600x900.jpg)

The summertime is all about vibrant, neon colors. These colors look great on sun-kissed skin! It's really easy to incorporate neon into your outfit. For example, you could use some colorful accessories like bangles or chunky necklaces. If you want to rock neon like Naya does, find some neon heels. They're sure to add some serious pizzazz to any outfit.

## 5. Casual

![Casual](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g9/x7/m85b0azf_600x800.jpg)

When you want to keep things casual, that doesn't mean you can't also be stylish. Naya knows that the secret to spicing up an otherwise bland outfit is to add a pop of color. Add a flannel button-up tied around your waist. It'll give your basic tee + skinny jeans outfit a more put-together feel. For shoes, Converse or combat boots will do the trick! How do you like dress up your casual outfits?

## 6. Jumpsuit

![Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jx/wk/a4khqf7b_420x571.jpg)

I'm a huge fan of the jumpsuit trend. It's sleek, sexy, and totally sophisticated. Personally, I love an all-black ensemble, so I'd go with a black jumpsuit (you can find some at [H&M](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)). Of course, you could always swap it for a more colorful jumpsuit, or one with a cute summer pattern (like the ones at [PacSun](http://www.pacsun.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)).

## 7. Floral

![Floral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bk/es/w8ijnvrs_600x899.jpg)

You can never go wrong with floral. It's the perfect summertime pattern! It's cute and girly and can be styled in so many different ways. You can find some cute and affordable floral dresses at [ASOS](https://goo.gl/p6NQ76). Since it's already a statement piece, I'd keep the shoes and accessories really simple.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome street style looks from Naya Rivera. This Glee star always looks incredibly put-together, no matter what she's wearing. She's become quite the fashionista, huh? Of the list, which look was your favorite? Which look will you be trying to recreate this summer?
