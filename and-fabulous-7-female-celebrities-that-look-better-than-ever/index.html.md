# 40 and Fabulous: 7 Female Celebrities That Look Better than Ever ...

Women Over 40 are fabulous, chic, beautiful and much, much, much different than their younger, crazier colleagues. Their drunken photos never appear on the first page of a gossip magazine, they are careful not to “flash” their privates in public, they dress better, talk better and (drumroll)… look better! In fact, they look so good, you couldn’t tell them apart from their much younger colleagues. And if celebrity men over forties are like fine wine, celebrity women over 40 must be something even better – a vintage Chanel, perhaps? But, let me cut the long story short and go straight to 7 famous women over 40 that I admire very much:

## 1. Demi Moore

![Demi Moore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/Demi_560x385.jpg)

Oh-la-la, this is one hot mamma and if you still doubt women over 40 can look even better than the ones in their 30’s or even 20’s, text Ashton, see what he has to say about it. LOL! Can you believe this foxy lady will have to blow a lot more than 40 candles this November? I’m positively chocked and I must say I think she looks absolutely fabulous not to mention waaaay better than ever!

## 2. Salma Hayek

![Salma Hayek](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/Salma_500x375.jpg)

A few years younger and just as ravishing as her fellow actress, Salma is THE star of every male fantasy! Am I surprised? Hell no! She’s beautiful, she’s talented, she’s happy and she’s 45 so all I can tell you is watch and learn, ladies! Yes, she probably visits her plastic surgeon once in a while but let’s not forget that, although plastic surgery is amazing, it can’t do miracles. What does that mean? That means she’s working very hard to keep that sexy bod beach ready! Hurray for women over 40, hurray for all the fabulous ladies who take good care of themselves and, of course, hurray for this diva!

## 3. Angelina Jolie

![Angelina Jolie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/Angelina-Jolie-Golden-Globe-13-560x386_560x386.jpg)

You can say whatever you want about her but Angie is my absolute number one and although it’s pretty obvious she visits her “doc” quite a bit, all I can do is pray that I’ll look at least as half as good when I’m 44. With so many children to take care of and so many films to produce and star in, Angie still manages to maintain her glam appearance and, if I may be so free, I think her style has improved drastically over the years.

## 4. Halle Berry

![Halle Berry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/halle_berry_120-560x399_560x399.jpg)

Proving that women over 40 don’t have to lose even an ounce of their charm, sexiness and beauty, world’s sexiest Cat Woman deserves a big applause. Halle was always beautiful, her body was always flawless but if I must choose, I’ll definitely say she looks waaaaay better now. Her style is different, more elegant, more refined and, quite honestly, she doesn’t appear a day older.

## 5. Toni Braxton

![Toni Braxton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/Toni-560x376_560x376.jpg)

Well, this rosebud has blossomed quite nicely, that’s for sure, and judging by the way she looks today, I doubt the verb “withering” is even in her vocabulary! But, seriously, ladies, how old do you think she is (and don’t Google!)? Well, guess what – she’s 43! The sad part is… she looks better than some 20 and something year old girls I know!

## 6. Sandra Bullock

![Sandra Bullock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/sandra-bullock-oscars-2011_550x360.jpg)

Sandra is the sweetest, the funniest celebrity ever and during the whole drama with Jesse she’s really managed to prove that beauty and charm are just some of many qualities she is blessed with. But, speaking about beauty, about charm, talent and especially the fact that Sandra could still enter any beauty pageant and take 99% of other contestants out of the game – have you ever even stopped to think about how old she is? Probably not, because, for me, she’s still Miss Congeniality! LOL! But, I’ll keep you guessing and just so you know, she’s waay over 40 years old.

## 7. Courtney Cox

![Courtney Cox](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/Courtney-C_500x322.jpg)

And I’ve decided to finish this list of fabulous, beautiful women over 40 by mentioning one lady I personally never believed will age so good- Courtney Cox! Do you remember Ross’s sister? Neat-freak Monica? Well, you’ll agree she wasn’t the prettiest member of the cast, at least when compared to Rachel (Jennifer Aniston). Well, surprise, surprise, take a look at this lady now – she’s literally blossomed! Still slim but less boyish-looking, Courtney does appear older in that glamorous proud-fab-happy-woman way.

Don’t these fab women over 40 deserve a big applause? Yes, you can say the look good because they have money or because they don’t have a 9 to 5 job to go to every day but let’s face it, this world is full of beautiful, non-celebrity women over 40 and they deserve to be praised and admired just as much, if not more!
