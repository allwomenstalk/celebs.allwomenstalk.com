# 7 Awesome Reasons to Love Emmy Rossum ...

There are so many fantastic reasons to love Emmy Rossum. She's an incredible actress and even more, an incredible person. If she wasn't on your radar before, it's time to change that. Check out some of the cool reasons to love Emmy Rossum.

## 1. #100HappyDays

Have you heard of that viral Instagram challenge? Well, Emmy takes part in it! Every day, she posts something that makes her happy. It can be something as a simple as a garden or even a picture of your best friend. It's a great message to send and a great thing to get all your friends involved with! It raises the question, can you be happy for 100 days? You can follow Emmy's posts on her account, @emmyrossum. This is just one of the many reasons to love Emmy Rossum.

## 2. Shameless

Emmy's the leading lady in one of my all-time favorite series, Shameless. Seriously, if you haven't seen the show already, you're living life the wrong way! It's on Showtime and it's a must-see. Emmy shows off some incredible acting skills on the show and she even live-tweets through every new episode. It's fun to see all the fun facts that she shares about the behind-the-scenes!

## 3. She's Gorgeous

Emmy's seriously so beautiful, inside and out. She's one of those people who just radiates positivity. She's got quite the contagious smile, don't you think? She seems to be happy all the time and I can't tell you how many times I've heard, "happy girls are the prettiest girls." It definitely rings true.

## 4. She's Humble

Even with all her major success, Emmy's never let the fame get to her head. She's still a down-to-earth girl who could double as your best friend. Another thing that shows just how great she is, is the fact that her co-stars have nothing but nice things to say about her.

## 5. She Loves Her Fans

Emmy is always interacting with her fans on social media. She even holds these contests on Instagram where her fans can design/edit potential profile pictures. She then chooses her favorite one and gives her a fan a major shout-out! It's really cool to see a celebrity put in time to show love for their fans. She even tries to interact with fans on Twitter whenever she live-tweets an episode of Shameless!

## 6. Her Style

When Emmy gets glammed up for red carpet events, it's ridiculous how amazing she looks. I mean, have you seen the gorgeous dresses she's rocked? Her sense of fashion is like no other! She always looks incredibly stylish while still managing to take some fashion risks. When she's not on a red carpet, her street style is still really cool. That's what I call a serious fashionista!

## 7. She Sings

In case you didn't already know, Emmy's got some serious pipes on her. She has three very successful albums on iTunes and I suggest you listen to them whenever you get the chance! She has a very soulful, jazz-like voice and it's really, really impressive. Her latest album, 'Sentimental Journey,' is one that I have on repeat daily. I can listen to her sing for hours on end.

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome reasons to love the oh-so-talented Emmy Rossum. I can't wait to see all of her upcoming projects, TV-wise and music-wise! What's your favorite thing about Emmy?
