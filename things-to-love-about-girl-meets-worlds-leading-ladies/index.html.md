# 7 Reasons to Love Rowan Blanchard and Sabrina Carpenter: The LadiesofDisneyChannel

There are so many fantastic reasons to love Rowan Blanchard and Sabrina Carpenter, the iconic **ladiesofdisneychannel** from _Girl Meets World_. These two are incredibly genuine and sweet, which is truly refreshing in this industry. Since the show first aired, it was clear these women were destined to be superstars. They've accomplished so much at such a young age, and they deserve every bit of their success. Check out some of the awesome things to love about these leading ladies.

## 1. Real Life BFFs

Rowan and Sabrina play Riley and Maya, the inseparable best friends. Their friendship translates so well on camera because they are actually real-life BFFs. They're constantly sending sweet and encouraging messages via social media. They're super supportive of each other and have made it clear they'll be each other's rock through the crazy fame roller coaster. Their adorable friendship is just one of the many reasons to follow these [talented stars](https://www.disneyplus.com/series/girl-meets-world/1v6H6n0Z8j0n).

## 2. They Love Boy Meets World

With their show being a success, the two never forget to credit _Boy Meets World_. They're constantly showing their gratitude to Ben Savage and Danielle Fishel, who've taken them under their wings. It's awesome that they were already fans of the original series before signing onto the spin-off. It’s a great example of [their love for fans](#7) and the legacy they're part of.

## 3. They Sing

In case you didn't already know, Sabrina Carpenter is signed to [Hollywood Records](https://www.hollywoodrecords.com/). Pretty impressive, right? The multi-talented actress released her EP and received an overwhelmingly positive response from her fans. Rowan's also got a voice on her. The two teamed up to record the show's theme song, "Take on the World," which you can find on most streaming platforms.

## 4. They're Pranksters

The two have admitted to being total pranksters on set. There's even a prank war going on between the cast. Who wouldn't want to be a part of that? It’s fun to see how they keep the energy high during long filming days. Who do you think is the better prankster, Rowan or Sabrina?

## 5. Their Acting

After watching _Girl Meets World_, there's no doubt that these leading ladies are both amazing actresses. They deliver their lines in a way that allows the audience to fall in love with them instantly. If you're a _Spy Kids_ fan, you might recognize Rowan from _Spy Kids: All the Time in the World_. It’s wonderful to see her shine in her role as Riley Matthews.

## 6. Their Wisdom

Even though they started fairly young, they've always had old souls. They are often seen sharing inspirational quotes on their social media accounts. Rowan once shared a profound thought: "The ability to simplify means to eliminate the unnecessary so that the necessary may speak." It's rare to see such maturity and insight from such young stars.

## 7. Their Love for Fans

Rowan and Sabrina are constantly showing love to their fans on their social media accounts. They're always replying to and retweeting fans who send encouraging messages. They also use their platforms to upload behind-the-scenes photos from the set, exclusive videos, and more. You can follow their journeys and see how they continue to inspire the next generation of [celebrity role models](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/).

Well, there you have it! These are just seven of the many reasons to love Rowan Blanchard and Sabrina Carpenter, the leading ladies of Disney Channel's _Girl Meets World_. If you've been following their careers, what's your favorite thing about them? They truly are some of the most inspiring women in the industry today.
