# 7 Awesome Reasons to Love Iggy Azalea ...

There are so many fantastic reasons to love Iggy Azalea. She's been on the music scene for quite some time now but is finally starting to get the attention she deserves! Her 'The New Classic' album skyrocketed on the iTunes charts, along with her hit single, 'Fancy', featuring UK singer, Charli XCX. She's all over the place now and If you haven't hopped on the Iggy 'bandwagon', it's time to do so. Check out the awesome reasons to love Iggy Azalea.

## 1. She Loves Her Fans

Iggy is constantly showing love to her fans (who refer to themselves as 'azaleans'). She's always tweeting/following her fans on Twitter (@IggyAzalea) or commenting/liking fan's photos on Instagram (@thenewclassic)! It's so awesome to know that she takes her time to really go through everything her fans send her. When she runs into fans in person, she's super sweet. She tries her best to take photos and have conversation with everyone! This is just one of the reasons to love Iggy Azalea.

## 2. Ariana Grande Collab

Ariana Grande recently released her new single, "Problem", featuring Iggy. The song climbed up the charts and hit #1 in just 37 minutes, hitting a new iTunes record! Personally, I'm a huge fan of both artists. It's like Christmas to me when two of my favorite artists make music together! Iggy's fan base definitely grew with this collaboration. I've honestly had the song on repeat since its release!

## 3. She's Gorgeous

If you've seen her music videos, you already have an idea of how beautiful she is. In person, she's even more beautiful (if that's possible). I recently saw her in New York City, in Times Square, wearing a hoodie and baggy sweats - yet she still looked flawless. She was really sweet and even took a photo with me and a few other fans!

## 4. Her Story

If you didn't already know, Iggy is Australian! She came a from a town that has a population of 3,000. Now, she's taking over the US rap game and making herself a household name. I think it's incredible how far she's come! She's proof that you can make anything happen as long as you work hard for it. One of her lyrics, "No money, no family, 16 in the middle of Miami", shows just how much she's overcome.

## 5. She's Humble

Iggy's becoming more and more successful each day but she hasn't let it go to her head. She's super down to earth and humbled by it all. I love that she still acts like a regular girl, like someone who could easily be your best friend. Wouldn't it be so much fun to be able to hang with her?

## 6. Her Style

Iggy always looks fierce! No, seriously, she even makes sweats and hoodies look amazing. I love that she's all about staying comfortable but still looking fashionable. When she gets glammed up for red carpet events, it's not even fair how amazing she looks!

## 7. 'the New Classic' Tour

Iggy's tour sold out! From the pictures and videos that I've seen, it looked amazing. I might be guilty of going on Instagram every night after a performance and looking at the #newclassictour hashtag to watch vids, whoops! Have you been to a show? What was your favorite part about it?

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome reasons to love Iggy Azalea. I have no doubt that she's going to take over the music biz. What do you love most about Iggy Azalea?
