# 33 Photos of the Met Costume Institute Gala ...

Check out photos from The Met Annual Costume Institute Gala at the Carlyle Hotel in [New York](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/new-york/). As expected stars were dressed in their finest and dazzled like stars!
Rachel Bilson looked adorable in a Louis Vuitton 2010 dress. The Jimmy Choo shoes don't hurt either ;)
Alexa Chung wore a 3.1 Phillip Lim Fall 2010 pant suit which I think is simply not appropriate for an event like this. Well, thankfully she kept her makeup extremely feminine.
Aww! I love Morgan Freeman!
I'm not sure if I like the color of Melissa George's J.Mendel gown but she does look lovely! I just wish she'd picked a red for her lips though.
Kate Hudson wore a Stella McCartney dress. What I'm really interested in is her boob job. Did she or not?!
Sean Lennon was seen with model Charlotte Kemp Muhl. Now she does have eccentric taste but I think it takes guts to wear something like that or lack of sight :p
Emma Roberts looked absolutely delightful in head to toe Marni!
Brooke Shields' gold gown by Michael Kors is a little too golden for me but I'd love to hear what you think!
Once again, yellow is just not my color but I'd love to know what you think of Rachel Zoe's velvet yellow gown by Marc Jacobs.
Needless to say, don't forget to tell me who you thought was best dressed and who wasn't.
