I probably watch too much TV, but how can I cut back when I would be missing out on seeing all of these hotties?! **Current Show:** Vampire Diaries

Steven R. McQueen is one of the hunkiest of hunky new actors! ## 2. Samuel Larsen

## 4. Adan Canto

**Age:** 31
**Current Show:** The Following

If you don’t watch The Following, I highly suggest you start! I know Kevin Bacon is the star, but I couldn’t keep my eyes off of Adan Canto! He has some lusty scenes in the show, including a threesome and a shower scene. He is in one upcoming movie, X-Men: Days of the Past, as well as another TV series, Mixology. I, for one, am looking forward to both!

## 5. Donald Glover

![Donald Glover](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/5_donald-glover_500x409.jpg)

**Age:** 29
**Current Show:** Community

Donald Glover has been a hysterical stand-up comedian for quite some time, but the laid-back, former jock character he plays on Community has made everyone pay a bit more attention to him in the past four years. Not only is he charming, charismatic and funny, but he is gorgeous to boot! He has the best facial expressions and you can usually spot him making a goofy face, but when he’s not acting crazy, you can really see what a handsome guy he is. Look out for him in The To Do List, coming out later this year!

## 6. Jay Ryan

![Jay Ryan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/6_jay-ryan_360x180.jpg)

**Age:** 31
**Current Show:** Beauty & The Beast

Jay Ryan made his debut in the Young Hercules and Xena TV shows in the late ‘90s, but over the past few years he has had a number of roles in several series such as Go Girls, Terra Nova and is currently starring in the CW show Beauty & The Beast. I think he’s just to die for in that show, scar, beast transformation and all! He will be in the upcoming drama Ned Rifle in 2014.

## 7. Joseph Morgan

![Joseph Morgan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/7_joseph-morgan_500x333.jpg)

**Age:** 32
**Current Show:** The Vampire Diaries

Well, I started this list with Vampire Diaries and it looks like I’m going to finish it that way as well. I had a hard time deciding which picture to display for Joseph Morgan, as they are all amazing! He has deeply penetrating eyes that look like they would see right into your soul, and those cheekbones! He is just absolutely perfect and I don’t care if he’s the “bad guy,” I’d be all over him! Look for him later this year in Open Grave, as well as 500 Miles North.
