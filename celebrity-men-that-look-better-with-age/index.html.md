Well, let me just say that although Daniel always looked great a slightly different hairstyle as well as lines the years have literally blessed him with are what made him so sexy. Not all celebrity men are born to be pretty boys and may I just say, thank God for that, because Daniel is worth more than 10 pretty boys!

## 3. Johnny Depp

![Johnny Depp](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/Johnny-D-560x393_560x393.jpg)

Am I the only one who thinks Johnny Depp looks way better now than he used to look at the beginning of his career? Remember that boyish face, wacky hairstyles and hobo style? Well, glad to see that he’s taking better care of himself now. In fact, I almost always like his outfit and he always manages to stand out and look damn hot. After all, a man who manages to look hot even with a thick line of eyeliner definitely deserves to be on my list of celebrity men the camera loves for decades!

## 4. Daniel Radcliffe

![Daniel Radcliffe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/Daniel-R-560x389_560x389.jpg)

But, hey, why not mention Mr. Potter as well? After all, we all had a chance to watch him grow up and I bet we’d all agree he looks much better now. Daniels growing up allegedly used to be a big problem for the producers of Harry Potter and I’ve even read they have been thinking about re-casting him so, if that’s really true, the only thing I can say is that keeping him is the best decision they could make. Daniel is still young but he certainly has a lot of potential and I’d really like to see his name on many future lists of celebrity men that look better with each year that’s passed.

## 5. Pierce Brosnan

![Pierce Brosnan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/Pierce-B-560x373_560x373.jpg)

Former Mr. 007 could still play the role of the world’s most famous secret agent, win the heart of the much younger Bond Girl as well as the hearts of women all across the world! And the fact that he’s been on this world for slightly over a half century certainly isn’t an obstacle! Gosh, some celebrity men really have amazing genes!

## 6. Denzel Washington

![Denzel Washington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/Denzel-W-560x367_560x367.jpg)

Fifty seven and counting- Denzel will need two birthday cakes to hold all those candles at least 10 years BEFORE his handsome exterior starts showing any signs of deterioration! In fact, I bet he’ll start aging backwards the moment he turns 60! He always was one of my favorite actors, I’ll admit that, but I don’t think I’m being even the tiniest bit subjective when it comes to his looks and talent. Dress him in a suit, put him in a big office and start filming because nobody was ever able to fit the presidential role as perfect as Denzel!

## 7. Chris Noth

In fact, I had a chance to see a few recent photos of him (taken during his visit to Serbia last month) and I must say I’m looking forward to seeing him in the next Sex and The City sequel! Raise your hand if you think Christ Noth deserves to be on the list of celebrity men who found a way to make their fifties better than their twenties!
