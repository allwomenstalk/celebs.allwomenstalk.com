# 7 Celebrity Bangs That Will Inspire You to Get Your Own ...

Do you ever just hop on Pinterest and do a search on celebrity bangs? I sure do. There are SO many celebs who are rocking the bangs trend and they're totally inspiring me to do the same! Granted, there are TONS of fabulous women who are rocking it - there are just seven of my favorite, iconic looks. Check out these celebrity bangs and then let me know who inspired you to go get your own!

## 1. Zooey Deschanel

![Zooey Deschanel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lh/ei/nvob14kj_600x900.jpg)

New Girl's Zooey Deschanel has become known for rocking those amazing bangs. I mean, some would even say she's unrecognizable without them! I think she looks perfect with them. She's the queen of all things hipster and the hair is just the finishing touch with her. She always wears them full, which I think is great because it draws major attention to her blue eyes. When they're not full, her bangs are slightly parted, which still looks amazing! This is just one example of celebrity bangs that is sure to inspire you.

## 2. Lea Michele

![Lea Michele](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s7/fa/xyhp35s8_504x680.jpg)

I don't know about you, but I've been loving Lea's recent look. Her ombre hair looks awesome with her full bangs. It's such a cute look to try out this summer. I like that she always keeps her hair in natural curls and just straightens her bangs. Is that how you style yours?

## 3. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fm/jo/rzz56dse_467x673.jpg)

Emma's been sporting a short bob recently and I'm loving it. She's got ombre going on with her red locks and I think it looks awesome. I love that her full bangs frame her face so nicely, calling attention to her beautiful eyes! Since it is such a short look, I like that the bangs are the focal point.

## 4. Olivia Wilde

![Olivia Wilde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hn/j9/bnujv7jh_592x888.jpg)

Does Olivia Wilde EVER look bad? The answer is no, my friends. No matter what her hair color/style may be, Olivia always looks fierce. When she's not rocking her sleek, full bangs, she wears them swept to the side. They frame her face fantastically.

## 5. Michelle Williams

![Michelle Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bx/q1/0l8n0sn3_600x799.jpg)

If you've just gotten a pixie cut and don't know how to style your bangs, Michelle Williams should ALWAYS be your go-to for inspiration. I adore her haircut and her long side-swept bangs. A lot of people shy away from this look because they think they wouldn't be able to pull it off, but I say go for it!

## 6. Dianna Agron

![Dianna Agron](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yy/ws/nxy1wflh_460x683.jpg)

I love Dianna's choppy bangs. When people rock bangs they usually wear them straight, side-swept, or full, but not Dianna. I like that she keeps them messy. Somehow, it still manages to look really put-together. What do you think of this look?

## 7. Kerry Washington

![Kerry Washington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kt/cu/0h8xrdmm_600x900.jpg)

Scandal's Kerry Washington is always looking fabulous. The talented actress has been rocking bangs and I have to tell you, she's who's inspiring me to go get some of my own! She just makes them look so stylish and she looks effortlessly chic with them.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of MANY celebrities who have been rocking bangs and look fab doing so. Narrowing this list to just seven was pretty tough but I do think that these are all worthy of a spot on the list! Who do you think rocks bangs the best? Who's inspired you to get your own?
