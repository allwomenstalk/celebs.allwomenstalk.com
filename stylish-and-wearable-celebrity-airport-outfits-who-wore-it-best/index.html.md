# 9 Stylish and Wearable Celebrity Airport Outfits  Who Wore It Best?

Celebrity airport outfits are a great source of inspiration for the jetsetter. Putting together a travel outfit can be tricky at the best of times and it can be hard to get that mix of comfort and style right. Celebrities are always jetting in and out of places so you’d think they’d have this travel wardrobe thing sorted, right? While some celebrity looks can be hard to recreate in the real world, you won’t have any trouble trying to recreate these celebrity airport outfits.

## 1. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/14203430_2015_350x700.jpg)

Celebrity airport outfits are a great way to get a sense of a celebrity’s off-duty style. Emma Watson has enjoyed quite the style evolution since her Harry Potter days and here she works a cool streetwear-inspired look. A sporty bomber jacket, grey sweatshirt, skinny jeans, and patent ankle boots are all cool and casual options but the structured handbag adds a polished touch to the overall look.

## 2. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/14203430_5253_350x700.jpg)

Emma Stone works a casually polished look in a tailored coat, cable knit sweater, and red skinny jeans. A collared shirt worn underneath the sweater and metallic brogue shoes add preppy touches to the outfit. The different layers of this outfit make it perfect for someone travelling through a range of climates – just add and subtract layers as needed.

## 3. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/14203430_8330_350x700.jpg)

Taylor Swift sticks to her preppy off-duty style even when travelling. Here she wears a fitted mint green knit sweater with tan trousers. She also wears velvet smoking-style slippers and carries a leather satchel. When travelling, take a leaf out of Taylor’s book and swipe on a coat of lipstick when you reach your destination. It’ll brighten up your features and make you look polished and put together, even if you’ve been travelling for hours on end.

## 4. Olivia Munn

![Olivia Munn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/14203430_2960_350x700.jpg)

Here, Olivia Munn works a sophisticated layered look in a long beige coat, a grey draped knit, and black skinny jeans. If you’re thinking of recreating this look, down to the knee-high heeled ankle boots, make sure you have enough room in your carry-on luggage to stash the boots and a back-up pair of comfortable shoes just in case you need to give your feet a break.

## 5. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/14203431_3599_350x700.jpg)

Kate Bosworth seems to be the go-to girl for off-duty style. Here she wears a pair of statement red leather-look trousers with a black top and thick grey coat. A red and blue checked handbag, black pumps, and dark sunglasses complete the outfit. While I wouldn’t initially think leather trousers would be the most practical travel attire, a loose cut and soft material could make all the difference.

## 6. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/14203431_3508_592x888.jpg)

Alexa Chung sticks to her preppy retro-inspired style with this denim shift dress and Peter Pan collar top. Strappy flat shoes, a khaki trench coat, and playful pink sunglasses complete her look. Dresses are great for travelling as you can always slip on a pair of tights underneath for colder climates.

## 7. Elle Fanning

![Elle Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/14203437_8630_592x888.jpg)

Elle Fanning uses a printed jacket to add some punch to a simple outfit of a grey top and tan skirt. Brown ankle boots and aviator sunglasses are stylish additions and the small red side bag adds a pop of colour to the look. A small bag is also a great way to keep your most important items and documents with you at all times when travelling.

## 8. Liv Tyler

![Liv Tyler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/14203437_0375_592x888.jpg)

Liv Tyler dresses for the cold with a long wool coat and fluffy hat. Underneath, the outfit of a button up chambray shirt, skinny jeans, and Converse sneakers is one that you could easily recreate on a budget. Wear it travelling or just out for a casual everyday look.

## 9. Florence Welch

![Florence Welch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/14203437_1880_592x888.jpg)

Florence Welch adds a dash of 70s cool to her travel outfit in the form of a fur-collared coat. Her printed blouse also has a retro vibe about it and she wears it tucked into skinny jeans with black boots and a large Mulberry handbag. While not all of us can afford designer goods, you could easily recreate a similar look with a thrifted retro coat and large handbag.

These are just some celebrity airport outfits worth noting. What are your best tips for putting together a travel outfit? Which of these celebrity outfits do you like best?
