# 7 Celebrities with Cotton Candy Hair ...

There are so many celebrities with cotton candy hair! The pastel trend is HUGE this summer. I've been meaning to try it out but just haven't found the courage to do so yet. The celebrities on this list can pretty much rock any hairstyle/color so it's no surprise that they look fabulous with these pastel strands. Check out some of the celebrities with cotton candy hair. Which would you recreate this summer?

## 1. Vanessa Hudgens

![Vanessa Hudgens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z4/t5/u9woafuw_400x600.jpg)

When Vanessa revealed her new teal ombre on Instagram (@vanessahudgens), fans made sure to let her know how much they were loving it. I think it's super adorable for the summer time. I might've even saved a couple of pictures on my phone as a reference for my hair stylist! I love the color and I like the fact that she did this light ombre with dark hair rather than the usual blonde hair. She's just one of many celebrities with cotton candy hair.

## 2. Kesha

![Kesha](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c3/s2/cxe5teqt_400x599.jpg)

Even though Kesha's no longer rocking this look, I loved her pastel hair phase. She had a two-toned look where half of her head was dyed a teal color while the other was dyed a soft pink. This hairstyle is definitely for the ones who like to turn heads and make a statement. Would you sport this look?

## 3. Nicole Richie

![Nicole Richie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a3/lo/werzvl4x_600x899.jpg)

Nicole Richie recently dyed her hair a sweet lavender color and I've been obsessed with it ever since. She always styles it in such a cute way. Lavender's actually a pretty popular color - if I were to take the plunge and dye my hair a pastel color, I'd definitely go with this one. I think it would look good on just about anyone!

## 4. Katy Perry

![Katy Perry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l5/yw/y0765dpt_600x753.jpg)

Katy Perry's always been one to experiment with [different hair colors](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/interesting-hair-color-meanings/). She's had blue hair, pink hair, purple hair, etc. Now, she's rocking a slime green color. I actually think it looks really cute! I think it's definitely a less common color so if you feel like being different from the crowd, choose a soft green color for your hair!

## 5. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mv/bs/y60v564s_600x900.jpg)

Rihanna recently debuted a pink pixie cut and it's adorable! However, she's back to her natural color now. I have no doubt that she inspired many girls to go dye their hair pink - LOL!

## 6. Kelly Osborne

![Kelly Osborne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hv/sn/gvnhvy49_504x680.jpg)

Kelly's had lavender colored hair for some time now and I think it looks absolutely fantastic on her. Like Nicole Richie, she always styles it really well. I think the color really suits her - she still manages to make it look really classy and sophisticated.

## 7. Demi Lovato

![Demi Lovato](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ne/di/pgs9wftl_500x757.jpg)

Demi Lovato's no stranger to dying to her hair. She's had ombre, pink hair, blue hair, you name it. She recently revealed her NEW hairstyle, lavender/teal ombre! I think it looks great on her and she's always posting cool photos to show it off on her Instagram account (@ddlovato).

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many celebrities who have rocked cotton candy strands. If you want to try out these pastel colors but don't want to dye your whole head, just dye a few strands. You could even try out the ombre look before you settle on dying your whole head one single color! Which color/look was your favorite?
