# 7 Celebs from CWs Arrow That Are Social on Twitter ...

There are so many celebs from CW's Arrow that are constantly live-tweeting the show, interacting with fans, and just being social on Twitter! First off, if you haven't already jumped on the "Arrow" band-wagon, what are you waiting for? It's quickly become one of my favorite must-see shows for so many reasons. There's the obvious man-candy on the show, some serious drama, and overall, fantastic acting. Take a look at some of the celebs from CW's Arrow that are very social on Twitter!

## 1. Stephen Amell

![Stephen Amell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g3/za/hzngvktz_600x811.jpg)

The show's leading man, Stephen Amell, does a fantastic job at staying social on Twitter. He's constantly live-tweeting through the episodes, even if he's busy on set. He spills secrets about shooting locations and it's really fun to see the behind-the-scenes pictures of Stephen dressed as "Oliver Queen", or, "the arrow". You can follow Stephen at @amellywood. He's just one of the many celebs from CW's Arrow to be interactive on Twitter!

## 2. Colton Haynes

![Colton Haynes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/im/nc/glilsqxx_600x900.jpg)

Bad boy, Roy Harper, is played by the incredibly handsome, Colton Haynes. You can follow him on Twitter at @coltonlhaynes, where he posts behind-the-scenes pictures from set, and even spills some "Roy" secrets. If that's not enough, Colton's constantly posting selfies on Twitter - feel free to look at him all day! When new episodes air, Colton's constantly thanking fans for their continuous support.

## 3. Katie Cassidy

![Katie Cassidy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dk/cc/dqs6jqjo_600x875.jpg)

Katie's one of my favorites from the show. I think she plays Laurel Lance fantastically. You can follow her on Twitter at @mzkatiecassidy where she posts set pictures (mostly with Stephen) and funny tweets about the episodes. I love that through her tweets, you can tell Katie is a super down to earth girl.

## 4. Emily Bett Rickards

![Emily Bett Rickards](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zq/xg/cb8csfsw_600x770.jpg)

If you're a fan of Felicity Smoak, you need to be following Emily (@emilybett). Her tweets are just as clever as Felicity's witty remarks - and let's be real, who isn't a fan of that? P.S Emily's constantly posting pictures with Colton. I love seeing that these co-stars have genuine friendships with one another!

## 5. Willa Holland

![Willa Holland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j7/u4/me1dklah_600x900.jpg)

I think Willa Holland is such a sweetheart. She just seems like a genuine soul. Is it weird that I've made this judgement based off her Twitter profile? Hmmm. Either way, you can follow Willah at @willaaaah. She doesn't post very often, but when she does, it's usually cast photos and fun Arrow-related things!

## 6. Caity Lotz

![Caity Lotz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ch/k3/s2gp2qdj_600x862.jpg)

Any Sara fans? I'll admit, it took me a while to warm up to the idea of Oliver/Sara becoming a "thing", but I think I've come to accept it! You can follow Caity Lotz at @caitylotz, where she posts tons of behind the scenes pictures including cast hangouts, hair and makeup routines, and more!

## 7. Paul Blackthorne

![Paul Blackthorne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d8/ld/plzk04ee_600x921.jpg)

We can't forget about Detective Lance, now can we? You can follow him on Twitter at @paulblackthorne. He's actually really good when it comes to live-tweeting episodes and he's always trying to interact with fans online. In fact, he always has Q&As with them, which is pretty awesome. I don't know about you, but I can't NOT follow Paul Blackthorne.

Well, there you have it! These are just some of the Arrow actors that love to be social on Twitter. If you weren't already following them, make sure you are so that you can keep up with all the live-tweeting/ set secrets. Who's your favorite actor/character to keep up with?
