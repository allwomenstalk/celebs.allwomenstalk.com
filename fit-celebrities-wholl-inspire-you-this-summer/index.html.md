# 7 Fit Celebrities Who'll Inspire You This Summer ...

If you're looking for some motivation to hit the gym this summer, these fit celebrities will help you get there. I love that they share their diet/exercise tips! They obviously work really hard to keep their bikini bodies, but if they can do it, so can YOU! Nothing should keep you from staying active. Check out some of the fit celebrities who'll inspire you this summer!

## 1. Demi Lovato

![Demi Lovato](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qc/lx/dh7gyeyp_600x860.jpg)

Demi's a celebrity who's battled with her weight over the years. Now, she's made fitness apart of her lifestyle and couldn't be happier about it. She's always posting post-gym selfies on Instagram (@ddlovato) with inspirational captions! I think it's amazing that she's using her voice to motivate others to get healthy. She's just one of the fit celebrities that'll get you working out this summer!

## 2. Lea Michele

![Lea Michele](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uk/yi/r2xngxu7_600x969.jpg)

If you follow Lea on any of her social media accounts, it seems like you can always find her hiking. The talented singer/actress loves getting her workouts in by hiking with friends. It's a fun and easy way to get your cardio exercise done! Don't forget to bring lots of water with you to keep yourself hydrated. Follow her on Instagram (@msleamichele) to see her motivational photos.

## 3. Jennifer Lopez

![Jennifer Lopez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vm/xw/wzxvhmb1_570x817.jpg)

Who isn't inspired by J.Lo's body?! Her workout routine includes lower-body exercises like squats, single-leg squats, and reverse lunges to front kicks. Sounds tough, right? Power through it! You can have that killer body in no time if you're motivated enough.

## 4. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dx/cv/rjl6sdse_600x900.jpg)

Jessica showed off her awesome bikini bod in the movie 'Honey' and she's been my 'thin-spiration' ever since. She still keeps up with those dance moves by incorporating them into her fitness routine. It includes dance and hip-hop, indoor cycling and sprint intervals! "Anything where I have to do something but I can get my mind off of doing it, that's so much better for me than anything that's repetitive. Or the music has to be loud and I have to be moving," says Jessica. I feel the same way!

## 5. Naya Rivera

![Naya Rivera](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/hd/oip1p4xk_600x880.jpg)

Naya Rivera has become everyone's #WomanCrushWednesday and it's no surprise why. The talented star swears by indoor cycling and hot yoga to stay in fantastic shape. Personally, I've tried hot yoga and I think it's AMAZING! It definitely makes you hurt but in all the right ways.

## 6. Jennifer Anniston

![Jennifer Anniston](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y6/o4/ogemdyvu_550x751.jpg)

Jennifer's had an amazing bikini bod for as long as I can remember! Her secret? A combination of hardcore cycling and super-toning yoga. If it's working for her, there's no reason why it shouldn't work for you. Sign up for a yoga class and I promise you, you'll fall in love with the workout! I don't know how I even lived before having yoga in my life.

## 7. Pink

![Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ic/ox/nccnslti_600x900.jpg)

Pink has one of the best bodies and if she doesn't inspire you to hit the gym, I don't know who will. Her workout routine consists of an hour of cardio and an hour of P90X or yoga. Of course, I'm sure her skills as an aerialist definitely gives her a good workout.

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many celebrities that'll help keep you motivated this summer! Remember ladies, it's important to stay active and there are so many easy ways to do so. Of the list, who's your 'thin-spiration'?
