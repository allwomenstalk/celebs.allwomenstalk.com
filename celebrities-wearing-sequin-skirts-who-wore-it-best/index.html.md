# 10 Celebrities Wearing Sequin Skirts  Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities are prone to their share of glitz and glamour so it’s no wonder you see quite a few celebrities wearing sequin skirts. Whether they’re on or off the red carpet, sequins seem to be a celebrity’s best friend. When all-out sequin gowns are just too much, a sequin skirt can be a great trade-off. It brings a little bit of sparkle to an outfit without being too over the top. Check out these celebrities wearing sequin skirts and let us know which look you like the best.

## 1. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/1_kate-bosworth_350x700.jpg)

Kate Bosworth kicks off our list of celebrities wearing sequin skirts in this muted number. Here she mixes up textures with a glitzy sequin skirt and a furry grey jumper. She completes the look with a black clutch and black pumps. This is the kind of outfit you could wear for an evening out with the girls.

## 2. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/2_emma-stone_592x888.jpg)

Emma Stone does ladylike glamour in this Burberry ensemble. She wears an emerald green sequin skirt with a strapless navy peplum top. The mix of jewel tones is quite on-trend and is a great way to incorporate colour into a classy evening look.

## 3. Poppy Delevingne

![Poppy Delevingne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/3_poppy-delevingne_592x888.jpg)

British model and socialite Poppy Delevingne dazzles in her glitzy outfit. She clashes her sequins with a patterned long-sleeved top and green striped skirt. She toughens up her outfit with a black ankle boot and adds some extra glamour with a bright red lip.

## 4. Jennifer Lopez

![Jennifer Lopez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/4_jennifer-lopez_592x888.jpg)

Jennifer Lopez is no stranger to sequins. Here she channels her inner diva and wears a draped silver sequin skirt and billowing neutral blouse. She teams her outfit with platform pumps and a long, draped beaded necklace.

## 5. Jessica Szhor

![Jessica Szhor](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/5_jessica-szhor_592x888.jpg)

Sequins can be quite attention-grabbing, and here Jessica Szhor shows us how to wear sequins in a more subtle way. She works an all-black look and pairs her sequin skirt with a striped top, blazer, stockings, and peep-toe heels.

## 6. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/6_selena-gomez_317x560.jpg)

Selena Gomez shows off some skin in this Dolce & Gabbana bustier top and skirt combination. The cropped bustier top looks quite lacey while the skirt is just covered in sequins. She plays up the look by popping on some bright pink pumps.

## 7. Blake Lively

![Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/7_blake-lively_600x934.jpg)

Blake Lively is another celebrity who isn’t shy of sequins. Here she wears a shimmery black sequin skirt over a blue boyfriend-style collared shirt. She then adds and extra element with a loosely looped tan belt. She finished off the look with black ankle booties.

## 8. Chrissy Teigen

![Chrissy Teigen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/8_chrissy-teigen_600x902.jpg)

Model Chrissy Teigen works a neutral look in a peach and gold sequin skirt. She teams it with a neutral tank top, white blazer, and gold strappy heels. She accessorises with a thin necklace, metallic cuff, and cream clutch.

## 9. Carrie Underwood

![Carrie Underwood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/9_carrie-underwood_560x859.jpg)

Carrie Underwood hits the stage in a glittery gold skirt. She opts for a more polished look by teaming it with a plain white tee and black blazer. A gold necklace and gold heels complete her look.

## 10. Kristin Cavallari

![Kristin Cavallari](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/10_kristin-cavallari_309x560.jpg)

The sequin skirt and blazer combination is a popular one. Here, Kristin Cavallari teams a lace-trimmed sequin skirt with a black top and blazer. Pointed black pumps and a black clutch complete this night time look.

These are just a few celebrities who have been spotted rocking sequin skirts. Are you a fan of sequins? Which of these sequin looks do you like best?
