# 7 Celebrities Proving That Bangs Are Back ...

While some may believe that bangs have worn out their welcome, some celebrities are proving otherwise. Newly seen haircuts sporting bangs have emerged on the streets lately and it’s causing a buzz among the world of celebrity style. Bangs are quickly making their way back and now, more than ever, it’s alright to sport your bangs. Here are 7 celebrities proving that bangs are back.

## 1. Jennifer Garner

![Jennifer Garner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vx/m2/wul6uadr54c937a2b03df915807625_300x462.jpg)

Jennifer Garner sports the long flowing hair with a sweep of bangs across the front. Her look is both classic and beautiful. Her hair has received lots of attention over the past few months as this beautiful mom of two let her softer style speak for itself.

## 2. Olivia Wilde

![Olivia Wilde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b5/ga/t44a3oef54c937a3bebf3103944497_300x400.jpg)

Olivia Wilde has turned some heads with her stunning display of bangs this year. This star has many fans heading to the beauty shop to have some bangs cut. She is simply stunning and her new bangs draw attention to her high cheekbones and big eyes.

## 3. Sandra Bullock

![Sandra Bullock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cu/c3/jwis5mc554c937a4b44ad301214596_300x300.jpg)

Sandra Bullock was all the buzz at the 2011 Golden Globes Awards. She came sporting her new bangs and sent the tabloids into a frenzy. It seems that bangs are working well for Sandra though! She has the beautiful eyes to pull them off effortlessly!

## 4. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v8/l9/gv8tnmi054c937a5a64e6656095785_300x300.jpg)

This star wears her bangs so beautifully! It seems that bangs give her a more grown up look, taking her from teenage pop star to a woman in her own right. After a couple of rocky years with relationships and tabloid troubles, it’s nice to see Rihanna changing her look to something both mature and fierce!

## 5. Victoria Beckham

![Victoria Beckham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s5/f6/rnll28xw54c937a6b3a84391127873_300x450.jpg)

Leave it to Posh Spice to be a trend setter! This dream girl is capturing hearts with her short stack in the back and wispy bangs in the front. Her bangs frame her face with a soft look making her even more enchanting.

## 6. Kiera Knightly

![Kiera Knightly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wi/dp/yt0yd8ka54c937a7afff1726136819_300x400.jpg)

This Hollywood princess has also brought back the bangs this year. Her beauty is so natural that bangs just complement her look. Bangs suit her facial shape and give her an extra punch of class to that casual beauty that she was already famous for!

## 7. Reese Witherspoon

![Reese Witherspoon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/qv/hu0u2nzi54c937a8cefe2697949465_300x434.jpg)

This southern belle brought bangs back into the picture and it works well for her! The side swept bangs are soft and romantic, a perfect accent to her “girl next door” looks that made her an instant Hollywood sensation.

These 7 celebrities are proving that bangs are back! So what about you? Will you jump on the bangs bandwagon and give your locks a new look?
