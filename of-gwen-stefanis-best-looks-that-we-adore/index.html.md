# 7 of Gwen Stefanis Best Looks That We Adore ...

It was so hard to narrow down Gwen Stefani’s best looks because she is one to always kill it. The No Doubt front woman grows with the different stages of her life while growing her status as the world’s coolest rock-goddess. The No Doubt leading lady has never been one to shy away from taking a risk, which has paid off on multiple Best Dressed lists. From Harajuku Lover and punk princess to sophisticated working mother, these are Gwen Stefani’s best looks.

## 1. 2005 MTV Video Music Awards

![2005 MTV Video Music Awards](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ea/g0/zn/ywqmo6vj_600x941.jpg)

Gwen stayed true to herself in this leopard printed dress, which is why this is the number one choice out of all Gwen Stefani’s best looks. The hair up, the red lips and this dress speak volumes to her personality and some of her original looks. This dress is from her L.A.M.B. line and fits perfectly to this beautiful singer.

## 2. World Premier of Paul McCartney's “My Valentine” Video

![World Premier of Paul McCartney's “My Valentine” Video](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jl/x7/ap/qdeb60ge_600x687.jpg)

Wow. This is a killer outfit to make a statement in. The sheer sides show off the fit figure of Gwen while the peplum top accentuates her midriff. The mother of two wore a pair of classic black Louboutins, which finished off the look perfectly. Oh and we can't forget her signature red lip! To die for.

## 3. Street Style

![Street Style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/go/c4/e6/wliiptsa_600x901.jpg)

Gwen always brings it even when she is running around doing errands or bringing her kids to school. This look is effortless and chic in those amazing striped pants and the plain black top. What I love and respect about Gwen is she is always rocking heels. Super chic and always one to set the fashion trend (not follow!), this is a great look for just your regular day.

## 4. Old School

![Old School](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yi/tb/g8/j2iazn9d_600x800.jpg)

When Gwen first stepped into the Hollywood spotlight, this was her signature look. With the blue hair, gemstones on her forehead and of course her bright red lips, this was Gwen. Back in the day she would always sport crop tops and bathing suit tops to show off her ultra fit stomach. I have to say, I love this Gwen!

## 5. Her Wedding Day

![Her Wedding Day](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/ni/ug/dby972is_600x897.jpg)

How can this not be on the list? Gwen made a huge statement when she walked down the aisle to marry her husband, Gavin Rossdale. With the pink ombre bottom and the beautiful white lace veil, Gwen made history with this daring look.

## 6. AmfAR Cinema against Aids Cannes 2011

![AmfAR Cinema against Aids Cannes 2011](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v4/de/gu/vo5uzvvt_600x938.jpg)

Gwen wore this stunning black L.A.M.B. dress (which she had made for herself!), which featured a black lace neck and a golden belt that tied the black dress all together. If you want it done right, you have to do it yourself!

## 7. Leopard Print Suit

![Leopard Print Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/u3/ly/albu9kfh_600x900.jpg)

This is so something I would assume Gwen would wear, which is why it works so well. Staying true to her rocker self, Gwen donned this head to toe leopard suit pulled together with the same leopard pattern for a shirt. That sounds a bit much, right? But the picture says it all. The black shoes tie the outfit together and of course, her red lips are the flawless finishing touch.

Although Gwen's outfits are always a statement, she stays true to what she loves and always adds her personal touch. What is your favorite Gwen look? Do you think she rocks her No Doubt outfits better than her glam Hollywood outfits?
