# 11 Celebrities Wearing the Tartan Trend – Who Wore It Best?

Tartan is set to be a huge trend this fall and you can pretty much take your pick of celebrities wearing the tartan trend already. This season’s tartan is all about putting a modern spin on a traditional look. From reworked red carpet dresses to dressed-down street style looks, tartan has been popping up in all sorts of different forms. Take a look at these celebrities wearing the tartan trend and let us know who you think wears this pattern the best.

## 1. Cara Delevingne

![Cara Delevingne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203647_2554_592x888.jpg)

Cool girl Cara Delevingne kicks off our list of celebrities wearing the tartan trend. Here she rocks a pair of blue and green tartan trousers. She wears them with a dark grey top, black cardigan, and black high top sneakers. A slouchy backpack, beanie, and rounded sunglasses complete this grungy street-inspired look.

## 2. Chloe Moretz

![Chloe Moretz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203646_0891_592x888.jpg)

Chloe Moretz wears her tartan in a classic red and black colourway. The short skater dress is modern and youthful. She opts for a more rock’n’roll vibe by teaming her tartan dress with short ankle boots. Her fresh makeup and relaxed hairstyle make this a look that you could easily recreate for your own wardrobe.

## 3. Diane Kruger

![Diane Kruger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203646_3314_592x888.jpg)

Diane Kruger takes a more polished approach when wearing her tartan. Here she dons a ladylike tartan dress on the red carpet. The strapless number is sleek and modern, and the black and yellow tartan pattern is quite understated. Simple strappy heels, a bright lipstick, and a low side ponytail complete this relaxed red carpet look.

## 4. Atlanta De Cadenet

![Atlanta De Cadenet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203827_0522_592x888.jpg)

Celebrity offspring Atlanta De Cadenet is quickly making a name for herself in the style stakes. Here she wears a '90s inspired outfit of a tartan mini skirt and a classic band t-shirt. Teamed with stockings and heels, this is quite a night time ready look but you could also dress it down for the day with a cropped denim jacket and flats.

## 5. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203646_2049_592x888.jpg)

Emma Stone wears a boxy tartan jacket while walking her dog. She works a classic look by teaming it with skinny jeans, a cream blouse, and black ballet flats. This is a chic everyday look that you can dress up with a structured handbag and pearls for the full ladylike effect, or dress down with sneakers and a messenger bag for something more casual.

## 6. Kiernan Shipka

![Kiernan Shipka](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203646_6667_592x888.jpg)

Kiernan Shipka works a fall-ready look in a dark tartan coat. The tartan coat is quite timeless and works across all age groups. The combination of the tartan coat, skinny jeans, and sneakers is perfect for wearing during those crisp fall and winter days.

## 7. Zooey Deschanel

![Zooey Deschanel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203647_1503_592x888.jpg)

She’s well known for her quirky red carpet choices and here Zooey Deschanel takes a risk in a tartan gown. Since the gown is quite busy, she keeps her jewellery and accessories to a minimum. Smokey eye makeup and a red lip add some sultry glamour to the look.

## 8. Rita Ora

![Rita Ora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203647_2168_500x900.jpg)

Rita Ora works a classic look in her tartan trousers. Here, she wears a pair of wide-legged red tartan trousers with a crisp white shirt and pointed red pumps. She also accessorises with a chunky gold watch and assorted rings. It’s a polished daytime look from the songstress.

## 9. Coco Rocha

![Coco Rocha](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203649_5974_600x808.jpg)

Model Coco Rocha wears a statement pair of tartan trousers. She keeps the rest of her look pretty sleek by teaming her trousers with a fitted black blazer and black ankle boots. You could recreate this outfit for either a funky daytime or night time look.

## 10. Anna Kendrick

![Anna Kendrick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203647_6257_550x876.jpg)

Anna Kendrick works a fall-appropriate look in a Vivienne Westwood tartan skirt. She teams it with a crisp white shirt and white pointed pumps. The dark purple biker jacket ties in nicely with the tartan pattern on the skirt. This is a modern take on wearing ladylike tartan.

## 11. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/29203648_7284_550x1100.jpg)

Olivia Palermo wears her tartan in the form of a long sleeved shift dress. She teams them with grey thigh high suede boots and a slim zippered clutch. The thigh high boots help give this traditional heritage look a retro sixties-inspired vibe.

These are just a few celebrities we’ve spotted wearing the tartan trend. From street style looks to red-carpet occasions, tartan is truly versatile this season. Which of these celebrity looks do you like best?
