# 7 of Hollywood's Newest Fashion Icons ...

Some of Hollywood’s newest fashion icons are actresses, singers, or models that have actually been around for a while. It’s not always instantaneous that their style becomes known. As their careers evolve, people start paying more attention to their style, or it’s the other way around sometimes! Here’s a list of 7 of Hollywood’s newest fashion icons.

## 1. Zoe Saldana

![Zoe Saldana](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26214355_9782_600x809.jpg)

I love this girl. She’s just super cool all the way around. It also helps that she has absolutely incredible style. Hands down, my favourite outfit of hers was that purple Givenchy gown she wore to the Oscars in 2010. It was from that moment on that I thought of her as one of Hollywood’s newest fashion icons. Her fashion choices are bold, fierce, and downright flawless. Plus, her street style fashion is incredible as well!

## 2. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26214354_9251_600x770.jpg)

Everyone loves J. Law, and as her career gets hotter and hotter, so does her style. She’s pretty much cemented her status as one of Hollywood’s newest fashion icons. After her gorgeous Oscar’s gown, all eyes have been on her both on and off the red carpet. She has a completely attainable style, which is the best part about it! With the success of The Hunger Games and a whole slew of new movies she’s in, we’ll be seeing a lot more of her!

## 3. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26214354_7481_600x878.jpg)

After her Burberry campaign, Emma Watson became one of Hollywood’s newest fashion icons. She’s grown up right in front of our eyes, and the best thing about her is that she’s an icon to all types of fashionistas. Never too ostentatious, and always sexy, demure and youthful, she has the kind of style that anyone can emulate. Of course it helps that she has a slew of designers begging to dress her. But like Emma says, when it comes to clothes it’s always good to leave a bit of mystery!

## 4. Clémence Poésy

![Clémence Poésy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26214354_5354_580x905.jpg)

I’ve always loved her style. Ever since she appeared in the Harry Potter series, she become known around the world. Then she played Chuck's new love interest in Gossip Girl, and she’s been hot on the fashion radar ever since. First off she’s French, and you know those French women are impossibly classy! With her career taking off here in America, she’s quickly becoming one of the best-dressed celebrities out there, and definitely one of Hollywood’s newest fashion icons.

## 5. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26214355_7876_553x862.jpg)

I think a lot of us have a girl crush on Emma Stone; I know I sure do! Not only is she hilarious, but she’s incredibly talented, beautiful, and has an incredible fashion sense. Both on the red carpet and on her days off, Emma’s style has made her one Hollywood’s newest fashion icons. Her style is bold, fun, and never too serious, just like her!

## 6. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26214354_0202_550x770.jpg)

I’ve always liked Selena as she just seems like such a cool, down to earth person. She always dressed beautifully, but after her breakup with Justin, well hell-o! Every time she showed up on the red carpet, she was a hit. It’s happened way too often now for her fashion choices to just be considered a coincidence. She’s one of Hollywood’s newest fashion icons, and I couldn’t be happier for her! May Justin eat his heart out.

## 7. Zooey Deschanel

![Zooey Deschanel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26214400_1090_500x653.jpg)

No one does retro style like Zooey, plus this girl is cool and quirky to boot! She single-handedly made having bangs cool again. Also she constantly reminds us on New Girl that you can be cool and slightly nerdy at the same time and look incredible. She puts her own personal stamp on everything she wears, and it’s a gorgeous mix of high and low fashion. As far as I’m concerned, that makes her one of Hollywood’s newest fashion icons.

It must be exhausting looking this good all the time, but these celebrities have all managed to wow us all with their red carpet looks and how they dress when just walking their dogs. That is a pretty good indication that they’ve become fashion icons! Tell me, who are your newest fashion icons in Hollywood?
