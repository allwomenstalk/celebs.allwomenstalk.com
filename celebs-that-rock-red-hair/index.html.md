# 8 Celebs That Rock Red Hair ...

We all remember when red heads were always referred to as hot-tempered troublemakers. This is no longer the case. Red hair has become increasingly popular even bringing over the blondes and the brunettes into the ranks of the red heads. Celebrities have also joined in the ranks, and some redheaded fames are making a name for themselves on the big screen. Here are 8 celebs that rock red hair.

## 1. Rhianna

![Rhianna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tz/ao/zptcg39454c92e651ab91239666333_400x250.jpg)

This Barbados native is an award winning smash success. Loved by her fans, her music is praised all over the world. Though this girl’s red hair may not be all natural, her voice is, and she has won the hearts of many.

## 2. Debra Messing

![Debra Messing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tz/dm/okptn05q54c92e6666dd9672415686_400x571.jpg)

Just as beautiful as Audrey Hepburn, this actress with her grand locks of strawberry, has captured the eyes of many men throughout her career. She is most commonly known for her leading role in the sitcom Will and Grace.

## 3. Nicole Kidman

![Nicole Kidman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/je/ub/xn2pu68f54c92e67826d5953559334_400x300.jpg)

This red-topped Australian actress has made a name for herself all over the world. She began her career as a child playing a role in the Five Mile Creek series and has since charmed the big screen in movies such as Days of Thunder and Nine. She is married to country star Keith Urban.

## 4. Lucille Ball

![Lucille Ball](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kn/gb/ongtq6dl54c92e68cd962850896801_400x541.jpg)

Most likely the most famous red-head of all time, this hot headed girl brought fun and sass into the living rooms of millions of people across America. Most famous for her leading role in the show I Love Lucy, this actress built an empire that will not be matched. Married to Cuban born, Desi Arnaz, she revealed a life of pain and disappointments after his death.

## 5. Conan O’Brien

![Conan O’Brien](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ad/tb/srpw3kdi54c92e69db62f902009995_400x433.jpg)

Currently the host of his own late night show this actor, comedian and writer sports his red hair everywhere he goes. This famous actor is also known for writing scripts for The Simpsons and Saturday Night Live.

## 6. Julianne Moore

![Julianne Moore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qc/w3/a29rn4j754c92e6add5b2706391507_400x537.jpg)

This red head lived the life of an army brat before her acting career took off. Her most recent success in film is The Kids Are Alright. She is a published author, wife and mother.

## 7. Marcia Cross

![Marcia Cross](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lq/c3/fdshlcl554c92e6c0f088825208581_400x300.jpg)

This Desperate Housewives star is a real go-getter. The frame of red around her face enhances her beauty. She is currently working on some surprises for her fans.

## 8. Prince Harry

![Prince Harry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/if/k4/p9zcqkpi54c92e6ce8e1a778001489_400x380.jpg)

Famous by default, this dreamy young man has just recently steamed up the news in spite of his brother’s wedding fanfare. Harry is eligible and has women swooning at his sight.

Being redheaded is no longer a plague, but an opportunity to show off your spirit. While all read-heads are not spunky and outspoken, these celebrities have all forged their own paths through society and made quite a name for themselves. These are just 8 celeb that rock red hair, but plenty of everyday individuals show off their red locks too! Have you ever gone red?
