# Baby Boom: the Cutest Celebrity Baby Announcements of 2014 ...

Celebrities have been known to make their baby announcements via social media and I love how creative they get with them! Here's a look at some of the cutest baby announcements this year:

## 1. Carrie Underwood

![Carrie Underwood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sx/n0/t3uz7sjg_560x560.jpg)

Carrie announced her first pregnancy with husband Mike Fisher on Instagram with her adorable pups! She captioned it, "in honor of 'Labor' Day, Ace & Penny would like to make an announcement.. their parents couldn't be happier…" How cute are those puppy onesies? LOL.

## 2. Gwen Stefani

![Gwen Stefani](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/j9/rgxbx2zj_562x560.jpg)

Back in January, Gwen announced her pregnancy with her third baby boy with the caption, "I was ready to hand over the crown. But I guess I am still queen of the house. #itsaboy #surroundedbyboys."

## 3. Kelly Rowland

![Kelly Rowland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xc/d7/tgjsj3q6_560x560.jpg)

Back in June, Kelly announced that she was expecting her first child with this cute Instagram post featuring baby Jordans next to adult-sized ones. "I'll be stuntin like my daddy," she wrote.

## 4. Pete Wentz

![Pete Wentz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/k5/v7170mnn_561x560.jpg)

Back in February, Pete and his girlfriend Meagan Camper announced that they had a baby on the way. The photo shows Wentz touching Camper's bare belly! "We're super excited to announce we're expecting a baby!" he wrote.

## 5. Alicia Keys

![Alicia Keys](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ny/xv/j9v6qoe0_500x500.jpg)

Alicia Keys shared this incredibly cute photo with the caption, "Happy Anniversary to the love of my life @therealswizzz !! And to make it even sweeter we've been blessed with another angel on the way!! You make me happier than I have ever known! Here's to many many more years of the best parts of life!"

## 6. Ashton Kutcher

![Ashton Kutcher](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fb/ln/l2s26rip_600x900.jpg)

Ashton's officially a daddy! Of course, he couldn't share a photo of his baby girl, he had to keep fans guessing. He shared these photos with the caption, ""Mila and I would like to welcome Wyatt Isabelle Kutcher to the world. May your life be filled with wonder, love, laughter, health, happiness, curiosity, and privacy. Can you guess which one is ours, or does it really matter? All babies are cute. -Ashton"

Can you think of any other baby announcements that should be added to the list?
