# 7 Inspirational Harper Lee Quotes You Should Remember Her by ...

Harper Lee, the author of To Kill a Mockingbird and Go Set a Watchman, recently passed away. However, her memory will live on for years to come. According to [Elle,](https://www.elle.com/culture/celebrities/news/a34246/harper-lees-best-quotes/) here are a few of Harper Lee's best quotes:

## 1. Don't Try to Look for Anything

![Don't Try to Look for Anything](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ko/nu/fsi61iez56cb17c24ca1e720547478_554x388.jpg)

Unfortunately, this is why some people can be so prejudiced.

## 2. Learn to Live with Yourself

![Learn to Live with Yourself](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q6/tu/qcy5l8wa56cb17c2edc11895902146_600x600.jpg)

The hardest person to learn to love is yourself.

## 3. Know Your Enemies

![Know Your Enemies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x4/ew/pb89pifl56cb17c34575e633867378_600x600.jpg)

You've heard the phrase: "Keep your friends close, but your enemies closer."

## 4. Don't Be Insulted by Insults

![Don't Be Insulted by Insults](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d4/w4/kbrfchtn56cb17c27dc29493576979_500x367.jpg)

This is why you shouldn't get too upset when people call you names. It says more about them than it does about you.

## 5. See Others for What They Really Are

![See Others for What They Really Are](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/or/i7duxz8556cb17c385c43012293980_600x450.jpg)

You need to open up your eyes and really get to know a person before you can see their inner beauty.

## 6. Learn as Much as You Can

![Learn as Much as You Can](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p1/wm/h103ai5o56cb17c3e1add324791177_600x744.jpg)

Life is a learning experience, after all.

## 7. See Life from Their Point of View

![See Life from Their Point of View](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/v6/glf6cglv56cb17c34ccfa987247541_564x588.jpg)

This is why you shouldn't judge anyone until you think of a situation from their point of view.
