# 7 Attention Grabbing Pieces of Celebrity Sex Advice ...

Ever searched the ‘net for celebrity sex advice? No, me neither. Still, it seems that our favorite PR people are willing to reveal more than ever to get their celebs on the front page, and social networking has made the celebs themselves a lot more open. So if you were wondering what celebrity sex advice would be like, wonder no more.

## 1. Taylor Momsen…

![Taylor Momsen…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/1_taylor-momsen_500x612.jpg)

Yep, Taylor has been handing out celebrity sex advice – on the topic of masturbation. Speaking in an interview, Momsen revealed, “I’m a promoter of masturbation. Don’t sleep around – learn yourself first. Guys do, but girl’s don’t. And that’s why so many have bad experiences. But you can know your body, know yourself, know what feels good…You don’t have to give yourself away to have sexual relevance. Because I don’t think sex is something that people should be afraid of. It’s part of human nature, so I don’t think it should be shameful – especially for girls.” While the interviewer seemed a little lost for words, it seems Taylor may have a point. This year will mark the first big campaign encouraging masturbation in women. It seems Taylor would make a great poster girl – watch this space!

## 2. Cameron Diaz…

![Cameron Diaz…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/2_cameron-diaz_600x770.jpg)

Cameron is used to being a sex symbol, after the huge media attention she received during Charlie’s Angels. Her advice? Keep it real. “You have to really enjoy being sexy. Not fake anything. Sexy is being in the moment, whether that means being coy or coming on hard. Faking is always lame, and it never comes across the way you want it to.” Sounds like she’s speaking from experience!

## 3. Heidi Klum…

![Heidi Klum…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/3_heidi-klum_600x798.jpg)

Heidi Klum might have ended her eight-year marriage to Seal last year, but she certainly knows how to keep things hot in the bedroom. When asked about her best sex advice, she revealed, “Some people are more experimental in bed and others are more boring. If you are wild and crazy, bring it on so that the other person is well aware that you have little devil horns that come out every once in a while. It’s good to make an effort to dress up sometimes, to do things outside of the norm.” It’s excellent advice, too. Don’t feel pressure to be what you are not, and don’t think you have to try everything. Just be yourself, and you’ll work it out.

## 4. Lena Dunham…

![Lena Dunham…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/4_lena-dunham_534x800.jpg)

Lena took a stand about sexism and inequality in her advice, and reminded us all that there is more to life than looks. “People questioning the idea that a woman could sleep with a man who defied her lot in the looks bracket hews so closely to these really outdated ideas about what makes a woman worth spending time with. Really? Can you not imagine a world in which a girl who is sexually down for anything and oddly gregarious pulls a guy out of his shell for two days?” Lena’s right. Don’t judge based on looks – and don’t worry about what other people think. Chemistry is key.

## 5. Jada Pinkett Smith…

![Jada Pinkett Smith…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/5_jada-pinkett-smith_600x797.jpg)

Despite constant rumors about her impending relationship doom, Jada always seems relaxed and secure. Her secret? Keeping it fresh. “Be sneaky – your girlfriends house at a party. The bathroom. The bedroom. Think of places out of the ordinary, and switch it up. Anything like that can keep it going. Anything it takes to keep the flame alive.” Just remember to keep it legal – nobody wants a dodgy criminal record. Just imagine trying to explain that!

## 6. Russell Brand…

![Russell Brand…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/6_russell-brand_541x720.jpg)

Russell is well known for his self-diagnosed sex addiction, and it’s rumored that it could even be the catalyst behind his sudden split from ex-wife Katy Perry. In an interview to the Rolling Stone, he gave some surprisingly good advice about being wary with threesomes – with a scary level of detail. “I would urge people, men especially, to avoid the two-man threesome. You are essentially getting less than you would have got anyway. Half the involvement, and at least double the risk of someone farting in the room.”

## 7. Olivia Wilde…

![Olivia Wilde…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/7_olivia-wilde_600x760.jpg)

The best celebrity sex advice has to come from Olivia Wilde – she manage to sound amazing yet utterly baffling at the same time. The style icon simply said, “You can lie to your relatives at Christmas dinner and tell them everything on the home front is just peachy. But you cannot lie to your vagina.”.

Some celebrity sex advice is just so matter-of-fact that it’s cringey, and some is utterly crazy – but anything that gets people talking has to be a good thing. Just don’t forget to stay safe! Have you heard any great celebrity sex advice? Post it in the comments!
