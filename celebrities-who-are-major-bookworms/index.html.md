# 7 Celebrities Who Are Major Bookworms ...

There are so many celebrities who are bookworms! I love when celebs are just as obsessed with fictional characters as I am, because boy am I obsessed. Of course, being a bookworm doesn't just mean you're all about reading novels, short stories, etc. For some of these celebs, it just means their noses are constantly buried in a book for school/work. I'm looking at you, Franco! Check out some awesome celebrities who are bookworms.

## 1. Emma Roberts

![Emma Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ly/m4/ychto65c_600x777.jpg)

Emma Roberts always has a book in hand! She's always sharing her newest/current reads with her fans via Twitter or Instagram. It's great because fans can get a sense of Emma through what she reads! She even makes great recommendations to her fans. The latest purchase she made was "Different Seasons" by Stephen King. She posted it on Instagram and captioned it, "currently reading... Absolutely amazing." She's just one of the celebrities who are bookworms!

## 2. James Franco

![James Franco](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ml/c8/w6zonnv2_600x901.jpg)

Does it really come as a surprise that James Franco is a total bookworm? Just one summer after Franco received his bachelor's degree from UCLA in 2008, he enrolled in several graduate programs simultaneously at different schools in New York City. I definitely admire how much of a hard worker this man is. He's all about making his craft better, all the time. I honestly don't know where he finds the time to get all his studying done, but I definitely applaud him for it!

## 3. Camila Cabello

![Camila Cabello](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ma/vy/aq560n6d_600x641.jpg)

Fifth Harmony's Camila Cabello is a self-proclaimed bookworm. Whenever the band is on tour, she takes a stack of books with her to keep her occupied whenever she's not killing it on stage. What I love the most is when she shares whatever she's reading with her fans via Instagram. She's always posting pictures of her newest purchases, or inspirational quotes from her current read. I love that reading books is her favorite pastime!

## 4. Isabelle Fuhrman

![Isabelle Fuhrman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gw/m2/wc2sue5d_600x900.jpg)

This 'Hunger Games' star is a MAJOR bookworm. Before her role in the movie, Isabelle read the entire series a total of nine times. What?! She also admitted to reading the first of the trilogy, 'The Hunger Games,' a total of 13 times. I'd say the talented actress was very well prepared for nailing her role as Clove.

## 5. Joanna "jojo" Levesque

![Joanna "jojo" Levesque](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gp/m1/cbprxumb_600x819.jpg)

This incredibly talented singer is always sharing her latest reads with her fans. One thing I admire about her is that she's also all about perfecting her craft. She's constantly reading books about music, which I love. Some of her latest shares include "The Power of Music" by Elena Mannes and "The Inside Out Revolution" by Michael Neill that Glee's Amber Riley passed along to her!

## 6. Natalie Portman

![Natalie Portman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jk/bw/mzk6h6jt_600x747.jpg)

Natalie Portman has beauty AND brains. The talented actress has a degree in psychology from Harvard! She's said, "I studied psychology in school and the best psychology is in literature." Even now that she's an actress, Natalie still reads in her free time. She's admitted to reading "like 250" Baby-sitters Club novels, which is pretty awesome if you ask me.

## 7. Josh Radnor

![Josh Radnor](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cl/ix/mvpr2rwa_600x906.jpg)

How I Met Your Mother's Josh Radnor is also a major bookworm. He's all about the liberal arts and the film that he wrote, directed, and starred in back in 2012 proves just that!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome celebrities who are bookworms. Isn't it fun to know that these celebs spend their free time reading a bunch of different books? Were you surprised to any of the names on this list?
