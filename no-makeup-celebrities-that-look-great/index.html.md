# 7 No\-Makeup Celebrities That Look Great ...

No-makeup celebs are hard to find aren't they? Especially no-makeup celebs that actually look great without being done up. Well girls, I've got 7 celebs that would turn heads all over the place, even without makeup! These no-makeup celebs are beautiful on the inside and out and it shows! So, without anymore comments, let's kick off this top 7 no-makeup celebs list right with our very own Lady Gaga!

## 1. Lady Gaga

![Lady Gaga](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/05/gaga_628x802.jpg)

Lady Gaga is one of those no-makeup celebs that you might not think would look good without all of her crazy makeup, but she does! She actually tweeted a picture of herself recently that looked so beautiful and that got me thinking, 'what other no-makeup celebs' are out there? Let's check out number 2!

## 2. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/05/taylor_900x1352.jpg)

Taylor Swift is another no-makeup celeb that looks absolutely incredible without any makeup. She's got that girl-next-door type feel and it shows! Recently, she did a photo-shoot without any makeup at all and it was a huge hit! I love that in the photo shoot, she left her hair curly too. It's really breathtaking ladies, you should check it out!

## 3. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/05/selena_416x625.jpg)

I think that Selena Gomez is as cute as kittens anyway, but this no-makeup celeb is adorable without makeup. She looks almost exactly the same as she does with makeup, minus the dramatic eyes. What do you ladies think of her no-makeup look? I love her eye color and her lips still look just as full as they do with makeup!

## 4. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/05/emma_800x1116.jpg)

Emma Watson is another celebrity that I just love. Everything she is in (okay, I am a huge Harry Potter fan) is incredible and recently, I saw a few photos of her sans makeup! She still looks amazing and will always be that smarty from Hogwarts to me! Also, have you seen her haircut? Super, super cute!

## 5. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/05/ri_968x1290.jpg)

Rihanna had actually been tweeting pictures of herself all over the place without makeup! It seems that this no-makeup celeb is ditching the makeup and just going all-natural most of the time! What do you think of Rihanna without makeup ladies?

## 6. Mila Kunis

![Mila Kunis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/05/milaa_630x478.jpg)

Friends with Benefits is by far one of my favorite movies of all time and That 70's Show? She was adorable in that! Mila is also adorable without any makeup at all! She's got all of the beauty, brains and confidence ladies, watch out for Mila! Even when her eyes aren't filled in with shadow and liner, they still look beautiful without any makeup at all!

## 7. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/05/miles_650x975.jpg)

Finally, Miley is the last no-makeup celeb that we're going to talk about. This girl is all over the place without any makeup and I love that! She has the confidence to ditch the colors and just go natural. Good for you Miley!

So girls, that is my top 7 no-makeup celebs list, what about yours? Any no-makeup celebs that I missed? Have anyone to add? Share 'em!
