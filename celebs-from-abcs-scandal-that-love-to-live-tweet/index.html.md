# 7 Celebs from ABCs Scandal That Love to Live\-Tweet ...

There are so many celebs from ABC's Scandal that LOVE to live-tweet through new episodes. It's actually so funny to see what the actors have to say about to scenes in the show. If you've kept up with Scandal, then you know just how crazy this season was. I mean, really Shonda Rhimes? Way to keep me biting my nails all season long! The finale was actually my favorite episode to see all of these celebs from ABC's Scandal live-tweeting. Check out which stars were taking Twitter by storm!

## 1. Kerry Washington

![Kerry Washington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i9/yd/ycabm5g5_600x864.jpg)

All hail Olivia Pope! Everything she does is flawless. Am I right, ladies? I love when Kerry and her team tweet during the show. She even tries to reply to fans as much as she can! Kerry's also constantly showing love to her co-stars praising their talents on and off screen. If you don't already do so, you need to be following her on Twitter (@kerrywashington)! She's just one of the celebs from ABC's Scandal who are always live-tweeting!

## 2. Katie Lowes

![Katie Lowes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ys/wr/ov3p4bi2_600x818.jpg)

Remember when Katie played good-girl Quinn? Those days are long behind her, huh? I hate to say it, but she's still one of my fave characters from the show! Katie's a fantastic actress. She's always tweeting through new episodes, especially when it's a steamy Huck/Quinn scene! I think it's hilarious to see her thoughts on that. You can follow her at @KatieQLowes!

## 3. Darby Stanchfield

![Darby Stanchfield](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/sl/cym13x0g_600x711.jpg)

I'll be honest, Darby is probably my favorite Scandal star to follow on Twitter. If you've witnessed one of her live-tweeting sessions, then you know how hilarious she really is. She's always tweeting play-by-plays of the show with really witty remarks. She even requests that Jake has his shirt off 24/7. I'm right there with you, Darby! You can follow her on @DarbySOfficial!

## 4. Scott Foley

![Scott Foley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/au/02/jjd1gnu7_600x621.jpg)

Scott plays Jake, who let's be real, is one of the favorite characters of every girl. You can follow him on @scottkfoley where he tweets witty remarks throughout the show. He even posts selfies with his co-stars which is so fun to see! My favorite, of course, is when he posts selfies with Fitz on set. I could look at those all day.

## 5. Tony Goldwyn

![Tony Goldwyn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/d0/qdahl1xs_600x904.jpg)

Tony's really good at live-tweeting. Even when he's busy filming on set, he tries to make time to interact with his fans. I love that he's constantly posting set photos and giving us all a behind the scenes look! He's super sweet about replying to fans on Twitter. Have you gotten a reply from President Fitz?

## 6. Bellamy Young

![Bellamy Young](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d2/ez/reywfvj3_600x791.jpg)

Bellamy plays Mellie; someone you can either love or hate on the show. I sort of have mixed feelings about her character but I sure love Bellamy! She's super sweet and really fun to follow on Twitter. She always posts selfies with her co-stars and tweets witty remarks during the episodes.

## 7. Guillermo Diaz

![Guillermo Diaz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ny/rm/bebpikm1_600x901.jpg)

Huck is definitely one of my favorite characters from the show. I feel like he's so misunderstood and just needs to be loved! I'm not sure how I feel about the whole Huck/Quinn situation but I'm excited to see where that storyline goes. You can follow him on @guillermodiazyo to keep up with his live tweeting!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many celebs from ABC's Scandal that love to live tweet through episodes. If you're a Scandal fan, I definitely recommend following the cast on Twitter! You won't be sorry. Who's your favorite actor to follow?
