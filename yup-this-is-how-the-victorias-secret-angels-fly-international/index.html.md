# Yup, This is How the Victoria's Secret Angels Fly International ...

It's that time of year again, ladies! You know, the time that we drool over VS' latest collections. In preparation for the lingerie's annual fashion show, the Victoria's Secret angels flew out of JFK on Sunday, November 30th (in a pink private jet) to London.

The gorgeous passengers include the likes of Candice Swanepoel, Adriana Lima, Alessandra Ambrosio, Doutzen Kroes, Behati Prinsloo, Lily Aldridge and Karlie Kloss. They were all matching in "ANGEL 14" hoodies, which you buy on _victoriassecret.com_! Check out these awesome photos of the girls traveling:
