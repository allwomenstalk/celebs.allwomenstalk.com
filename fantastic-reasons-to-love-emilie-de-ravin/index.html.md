# 7 Fantastic Reasons to Love Emilie De Ravin ...

There are so many awesome reasons to love Emilie de Ravin. I might be a bit biased, seeing as she starred in two of my all-time favorite TV shows, but hey, there are lots of valid reasons on this list. I love the fact that she's not the "scandalous" type of actress, making her a good role model for young women to look up to. She's got an awesome sense of humor, which makes her major BFF material! Check out some of the reasons to love Emilie de Ravin.

## 1. Lost

If you've never watched 'Lost,' what are you possibly waiting for?! I remember binge-watching the show on Netflix and having it consume my life for those few weeks. I regret nothing of course! Emilie played one of my favorite characters, Claire Littleton. She's so great at what she does that she makes you fall in love with her character and feel incredibly invested in her throughout the series. This is just one of the many reasons to love Emilie de Ravin.

## 2. Once on Abc

Emilie brought fairy-tale princess Belle to life in ABC's hit show, "Once." I think she pulls off that role so effortlessly; I mean, she even resembles the princess! She brings a certain elegance and personable charm to the character and that makes it easy to love her and root for her and her happiness. If you're a fan of the show, do you love Emilie as Belle?

## 3. She Loves Her Fans

This talented actress is constantly showing her fans love by interacting with them on social media. She always takes the time to thank her fans for their unconditional and ongoing support with all that she does! If you follow her on Twitter (@emiliederavin), there's a good chance that she'll even reply to you! Share your thoughts about the show with her - she always tries her best to see everything!

## 4. She's Sweet and Humble

Even though Emilie is really successful, she hasn't let it go to her head. I honestly think she's one of the sweetest people in the entertainment industry! I've never met her but from all the fan stories that I hear, she is as wonderful and delightful as we all expect her to be.

## 5. Her Sense of Humor

Not only is Emilie really sweet and humble, she's also got an awesome sense of humor. If you watch her interviews on YouTube, you'll see that she's not afraid of laughing at herself. She's very light-hearted and that's exactly the type of person I'd love to hang around everyday! You can find out for yourself by watching some of her recent interviews.

## 6. She's Gorgeous

When Emilie gets glammed up for red carpet events, it's not even fair how gorgeous she looks. She's got this "old-Hollywood" and glamorous look to her, which I love. What's incredible about Emilie is that when she's not at events, she chooses to go make-up free and keep things casual with her style.

## 7. She's Australian

I don't know about you, but I love everyone that has an accent - LOL! Emilie's Australian and I'm sure she's done her country proud with her major success here in the states!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome reasons to love Emilie de Ravin. I think she's a fantastic actress and a great role model to young women. I can't wait to see what else she does with her career! What do you love most about Emilie?
