# 9 Celebrities Who Have Beaten Breast Cancer to Be Inspired by ...

Celebrities who have beaten breast cancer and told their stories are to be admired. With the influence celebrities have on us, it’s good to hear the good stories and the not so good. How they handle adversity is just as important as how they handle their career and the rewards it brings. Are you ready to be inspired by some celebrities who have beaten breast cancer? Read on!

## 1. Kylie Minogue

![Kylie Minogue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07225226_2021_600x954.jpg)

Having been diagnosed with breast cancer back in 2005, Kylie Minogue has successfully fought against it and been given the all clear. The diminutive and brilliant Australian songstress is one of the most famous celebrities who have beaten breast cancer and has since returned to the stage and become an author. It was an inspiration to other sufferers the way she spoke publicly about her illness and helped them see that there was light at the end of the tunnel. Her determination to win and willingness to talk about the illness led to many being tested and her actions could have saved many lives.

## 2. Olivia Newton-John

![Olivia Newton-John](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07225306_0027_500x750.jpg)

Since fighting and overcoming the disease 20 years ago, Olivia Newton-John works to raise awareness of breast cancer. She was prepared to speak about it at a time when it was not openly discussed and she is also contributing funds to breast cancer charities. Like other breast cancer-beating celebrities, she has managed to combine the continuation of her career with fighting to help others.

## 3. Christina Applegate

![Christina Applegate](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07225350_4735_600x754.jpg)

When Christine Applegate was diagnosed in 2008 she may have been relieved that it was in one breast. This will have soon been overshadowed by the fact that she has the gene that means the cancer is likely to return. She had a preventative double mastectomy and reconstructions and is an inspiration to other women as she can show they can still be sexy without their own breasts.

## 4. Judy Blume

![Judy Blume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07225501_6218_600x871.jpg)

It may have been that best–selling author Judy Blume feared the worst as she was 74 before being diagnosed with breast cancer. Due to her high profile it is possible she has inspired millions of women to check their own breasts, and also let them see that age is not a barrier to recovery, and that a healthy lifestyle is no guarantee of not getting breast cancer in the first place – anyone can succumb.

## 5. Peter Criss

![Peter Criss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07225522_0559_600x901.jpg)

It is not only women who suffer from breast cancer, as Peter Criss – the drummer in Kiss – found out. He did not talk about his illness right away, but since his recovery he has traveled to educate men to the fact that they can suffer too. Like other celebs who have overcome breast cancer he is counting himself lucky and encouraging men to check themselves out.

## 6. Edie Falco

![Edie Falco](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07225624_7163_600x850.jpg)

Not all celebrities who have beaten breast cancer make their condition public right away, but by not doing so can inspire other people to carry on as normal while undergoing treatment. Edie Falco did not discuss her breast cancer until a year after her diagnosis and having beaten it changed her lifestyle, including attending Alcoholics Anonymous. She has the great attitude that we should all live for the moment but also shows that she believes she has a future with adopting 2 children.

## 7. Cynthia Nixon

![Cynthia Nixon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07225047_3487_374x483.jpg)

SATC star Cynthia Nixon, like other cancer-beating celebrities, has been a great advocate and her fight against breast cancer will have inspired many women to have a mammogram. She is stressing how important this is and women should not be afraid of them.

## 8. Suzanne Somers

![Suzanne Somers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07225643_9065_600x935.jpg)

It is now 12 years since Suzanne Somers began her battle against breast cancer. Like many celebs who overcome breast cancer she refused to receive chemotherapy, although she did undergo surgery and receive radiotherapy while trialing alternative remedies, including mistletoe injections. She is campaigning for bio-identical hormone replacement therapy and has given others hope of recovery by writing a novel based on the topic.

## 9. Melissa Etheridge

![Melissa Etheridge](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07225134_6747_600x786.jpg)

While her refusal to make light of the treatment she underwent may have seemed to frighten others, it would have prepared many women for what they would face with chemotherapy. Seeing how singer Melissa and other celebs who beat breast cancer have survived and now not only continue with their careers but work with cancer charities and lobby for improved research, can motivate other women to fight with all their might.
