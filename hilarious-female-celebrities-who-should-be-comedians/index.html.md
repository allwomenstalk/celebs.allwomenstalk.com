# 13 Hilarious Female Celebrities Who Should Be Comedians ...

Whether you are into watching live comedy sketches or prefer your laughs to be delivered in 140 words or less, these female celebrities who should be comedians need no introduction. I've always been a fan of anyone who is a master at 'LOL' moments and can cause me to combust in joyous tears. It's time that the world embraces the fact that women are just as comedic as men are! For reference, take a look at any work done by Tina Fey, Amy Poehler and Kristen Wiig and you will be sure to agree. After taking a look at this list featuring female celebrities who should be comedians, you'll find it hard to fault these queens of comedy.

## 1. Mindy Kaling

![Mindy Kaling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12212400_3723_395x594.jpg)

At the mention of female celebrities who should be comedians, there is no argument that Ms. Mindy Kaling more than earned a spot on this list. From her hilarious one-liners on both “The Office” and her show “The Mindy Project” to her hilarious Twitter musings, I've always loved how down-to-earth Mindy Kaling seems. She knows how to please her audience at the hands of a well-placed tweet.

## 2. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12212410_0360_600x900.jpg)

I fully believe that this amazingly candid actress is one of the funniest people around in Hollywood. I love how she is able to turn any situation into a comedic moment! Whenever I watch any of her red carpet interviews, I can't help but crack up at her often outlandish statements and stories.

## 3. Issa Rae

![Issa Rae](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12212409_6151_469x700.jpg)

Issa Rae rose to fame with her YouTube video series “The Misadventures of Awkward Black Girl.” After watching her videos, it isn't hard to see why the media loves her! Not only is she beautiful, she also knows how to make 'mockumentaries' seem like the best things since sliced bread.

## 4. Anna Kendrick

![Anna Kendrick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12212409_7260_600x781.jpg)

Is it weird to admit that I fell in love with this actress on Twitter before falling for her movies? Follow her @AnnaKendrick47 for tweets such as “When I misspell a word so badly that there are no replacement suggestions the sense of shame follows me all day,” and “There r few things in this world as perversely satisfying as popping zits, but I've thought of one. Ripping off false lashes, amirite ladies?” You will find it hard to not like her!

## 5. Rashida Jones

![Rashida Jones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12212409_2018_600x900.jpg)

This beauty certainly chooses her roles wisely. I love how incredibly skilled she is at portraying comedic women without making them seem cheesy. Whether you know her from her days on “The Office” or “Parks and Recreation,” it will be hard to dispute this claim.

## 6. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12212400_5318_544x755.jpg)

“My parents are both very funny but they're also relatively soft-spoken, normal human beings while I'm just a lunatic. I don't know where this loud, ballsy, hammy ridiculousness came from. I'm just glad I followed my goals and my parents did too.” Emma Stone is one of the sweetest and funniest people around. I love the fact that she tries not to take life too seriously! If you don't believe me, head to Tumblr to find screencaps of the many silly faces that she makes in her interviews.

## 7. Raven-Symoné

![Raven-Symoné](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/12212409_2122_580x885.jpg)

There is a reason that her carefree demeanor has won over the hearts of her audience and critics alike. From her Disney days to each role that she has played, it is clear that she has a goofy side. Growing up, Raven-Symoné was my biggest idol; there is just something about her personality that makes her incredibly likable!

## 8. Isla Fischer

![Isla Fischer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/15144455_5232_500x600.jpg)

After seeing Isla Fishcher in "Wedding Crashers" I loved her. She was so easily believable as this crazy, clingy, and obsessive woman that I hoped she would continue to be funny. She was not a disappointment. She went on to do "Confessions of a Shopaholic" and more recently "Arrested Development." She may be able to to other roles that are a little more serious, but with ease she can transform into a hilarious and entertaining actress.

## 9. Emily Blunt

![Emily Blunt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/15144454_5030_600x913.jpg)

Ok I know Emily Blunt isn't known for her hilarious movie roles or anything like that, but she is really funny! Not only is she married to John Krasinski (a.k.a Jim Halpert from "The Office) who is absolutely hilarious, but she has popped up in quite a few funny movies. She was in "The Five Year Engagement" with funny man Jason Segal and also in "The Muppets." Personally, I think you have to have a great sense of humor to want to do a movie with "The Muppets."

## 10. Olivia Munn

![Olivia Munn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/15144455_5068_426x640.jpg)

I absolutely fell in love with Olivia Munn's hilarity in the short lived comedy "Perfect Couples." After that I realized that I was late on the Olivia Munn train and that she is actually not only known for being gorgeous, but also really funny! She has appeared in many funny roles and regularly appears as herself on "Attack of the Show" where she is able to hold her own and make people laugh.

## 11. Kristen Bell

![Kristen Bell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/15144455_4132_450x676.jpg)

Who doesn't love Kristen Bell? Not only is she super adorable, but she's funny too. She's appeared in quite a few funny shows and movies such as "Forgetting Sarah Marshall," "Party Down," and "Burning Love." Her acting isn't the only thing about her that's funny. She's married to Dax Shepard, so she must have a great sense of humor, and her Twitter is definitely worth following.

## 12. Kat Dennings

![Kat Dennings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22164324_8438_560x724.jpg)

I love Kat Denning's Twitter! Her tweets are really funny and so is her acting. Before regularly appearing on "2 Broke Girls," she had already appeared in a few funny shows and movies. She voiced characters on "American Dad" and played the daughter in "The 40 Year Old Virgin." You may not be a fan of her show, but that doesn't mean her sarcastic wit isn't hilarious.

## 13. Alison Brie

![Alison Brie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22164417_8635_600x799.jpg)

I don't know much about Alison Brie, but from what I hear she's a favorite among hilarious celebrities. What I do know about her is that she is on "Community" which has quite a following and from what I've seen she's pretty funny on the show. She's also made appearances in other shows and movies that have tickled the audience's funny bone. She's definitely an underrated hilarious celebrity.

Watching any videos featuring these lovely celebrities makes me feel as if I am watching a live stand-up comedy performance. These fabulous ladies certainly know how to make their audience laugh without having to try too hard to do so. Are there any other women that you feel should be celebrated for their hilarious nature?
