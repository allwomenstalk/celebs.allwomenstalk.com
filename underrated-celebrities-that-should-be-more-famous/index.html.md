# Emmy Rossum and Other Underrated Celebrities You Need to Add to Your Radar ...

Unfortunately, having talent doesn't always mean you'll steal the spotlight. There are so many cases where people who are so ridiculously talented are never brought to the forefront. Everyone on this list has their own fan base and have all managed to find success, in their own right. However, these underrated celebrities deserve a lot more attention!

## 1. Emmy Rossum

![Emmy Rossum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kg/n0/xa4fq8p3_600x757.jpg)

As a huge Shameless fan, I think the world of Emmy Rossum. She fearlessly dives into every TV/movie role she has! As Fiona Gallagher on Showtime's Shameless, Emmy finds the perfect balance between sultry and girl-next-door. She's starred in some blockbuster hits like Beautiful Creatures, The Phantom of the Opera, and The Day After Tomorrow. However, I don't think she's given the recognition as an actress that she deserves!

## 2. Katharine Mcphee

![Katharine Mcphee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/m7/xjowl7u8_600x835.jpg)

I can seriously go on and on about how underrated Katharine McPhee is. Not only is she an incredible singer, she's also an incredible actress! I absolutely LOVED her in NBC's Smash and have no shame in saying I bawled like a baby when I found out the show had been canceled. LOL. Now, she's gracing the silver screen again with a role on CBS' Scorpion. She's really fantastic and I don't think people give her the attention she deserves!

## 3. Lake Bell

![Lake Bell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c4/qs/dzpoywn3_600x741.jpg)

Lake Bell's one of my favorite actresses and she's definitely been floating under the radar! Back in 2008, she co-starred in What Happens In Vegas with Cameron Diaz and Ashton Kutcher and she has this certain cool factor about her that draws people to her. Some of her other projects include Over Her Dead Body, No Strings Attached, and a guest spot on FOX's New Girl!

## 4. Lykke Li

![Lykke Li](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oe/ak/x5hpjhmu_600x900.jpg)

If Lykke Li isn't on your music radar, she should be. She's got a captivating voice and looks like she could be a runway model. Back in 2008, Drake even remixed one of the Swedish singer-songwriter's songs, "Little Bit". Remember that?! I wish she was more well-known in the music industry!

## 5. Lizzy Caplan

![Lizzy Caplan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zb/iu/d7mq9ygm_600x900.jpg)

Lizzy Caplan has definitely come a long way since her role in 2004's Mean Girls. I know, it doesn't even look like the same person! She's gone on to be one of the most prolific, yet overlooked, actresses in Hollywood. She always manages to find the perfect balance between funny and sexy and has starred in movies like Hot Tub Time Machine, Bachelorette, and had a leading role in Showtime's Masters of Sex. She's an awesome actress who doesn't deserve to be so overlooked!

## 6. Jane Levy

![Jane Levy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x0/ps/gfrtq0l3_600x689.jpg)

Jane Levy is too good of an actress to be overlooked! She made her TV debut as Mandy on Showtime's Shameless (season 1) but later landed the lead role on ABC's show, Suburgatory. She's got a natural "bad girl" look going for her and I think it makes her very memorable. She's also starred in Evil Dead and Fun Size, alongside Victoria Justice.

## 7. Aubrey Plaza

![Aubrey Plaza](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hr/na/dc5w2a7m_600x900.jpg)

If we're being honest, it saddens me that the genius that is Aubrey Plaza is still unknown to some people. She absolutely kills it on every and any Parks & Recreation episode and is a total scene-stealer. She's been apart of some really cool movies like The To-Do List, Safety Not Guaranteed, and Scott Pilgrim vs. The World. She's gorgeous, hilarious, and totally underrated.

## 8. Camilla Belle

![Camilla Belle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cb/rd/dhaqi595_600x902.jpg)

This Brazilian actress deserves way more recognition. She's managed to impress me with every movie role! She's starred in The Quiet, When A Stranger Calls, and 10,000 BC. You might also recognize her as Joe Jonas' ex-girlfriend. Whatever the case, she's a great actress who's totally underrated.

## 9. Emily Blunt

![Emily Blunt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bi/lp/c2xa6bmg_600x900.jpg)

Am I the only one who feels like Emily Blunt should be a lot more famous than she is? She's a truly incredible actress! If you've watched the recently-released film "Into The Woods," you know what I'm talking about! Of course, she's also starred in The Devil Wears Prada, The Five Year Engagement, The Adjustment Bureau, and Looper, just to name a few. It's time more people knew her name!

Well, there you have it. These are just 9 of many celebrities who definitely deserve A LOT more attention! These are just some of my picks - who are yours? Who do you think is underrated?
