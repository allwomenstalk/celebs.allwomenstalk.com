# 10 Celebrities in Little Orange Dresses  Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities in orange dresses are a common sight lately. Orange seems to be the colour of the moment and a little orange dress is great for commanding attention. It’s an on-trend alternative to the classic little black dress and would be a fun choice for any upcoming parties this spring/summer. Take a look at the next couple of celebrities in orange dresses and let us know who you think wore it best!

## 1. Beyonce

![Beyonce](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/1_beyonce_550x1100.jpg)

As far as celebrities in orange dresses go, Beyonce has been seen sporting this look more than once. Here, Beyonce wears a fitted Antonia Berardi sheath dress in a warm orange colour. She teams her dress with gold perforated Jimmy Choo pumps and side swept hair.

## 2. Ashley Greene

![Ashley Greene](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/2_ashley-greene_550x1100.jpg)

Ashley Greene strikes a pose in a bright orange Yigal Azrouel dress. The sleeveless dress has a subtle lace-like pattern on it, which adds that extra bit of detail to the dress. She teams her dress with red lipstick and nails, and wears neutral suede pumps.

## 3. Ginnifer Goodwin

![Ginnifer Goodwin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/3_ginnifer-goodwin_550x1100.jpg)

Ginnifer Goodwin’s Raoul dress would be perfect for a cocktail party. This orange number comes with its own in-built jewellery thanks to a dazzling embellished neckline. Like many other celebrities on this list, she teams her orange dress with simple, neutral shoes.

## 4. Leslie Mann

![Leslie Mann](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/4_leslie-mann_550x1100.jpg)

Leslie Mann was snapped wearing this ladylike Monique Lhullier dress on the ‘ParaNorman’ red carpet. The fitted top and full skirt has a very 1950s housewife feel to it – and the inclusion of pockets in any dress is a bonus! The neutral pointed pumps complete the ladylike feel of the whole outfit.

## 5. Lauren Conrad

![Lauren Conrad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/5_lauren-conrad_550x1100.jpg)

Lauren Conrad wore this orange Harlyn dress to a Miami bookstore for a meet and greet with fans. She kept her accessories to a minimum and teamed her dress with nude platform pumps. The relaxed, wavy hair makes this an ideal summer-inspired look.

## 6. Victoria Beckham

![Victoria Beckham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/6_victoria-beckham_550x1100.jpg)

This bright orange dress that Victoria Beckham is wearing is from her own clothing label. She wore this figure-hugging number while celebrating her birthday last year. She kept the accessories fairly simple with neutral peep toe pumps and a small clutch.

## 7. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/7_emma-stone_550x1100.jpg)

Emma stone wore this contoured Stella McCartney shift dress at the Berlin International Film Festival. She teamed her dress with taupe Christian Louboutin stilettos, hoop earrings, gold rings, and a bright red lip. This kind of little orange dress and could easily be dressed down for the daytime with a denim jacket and flat sandals.

## 8. Taylor Schilling

![Taylor Schilling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/8_taylor-schilling_550x1100.jpg)

Actress Taylor Schilling wears an orange dress from Prabal Gurung’s ICB label. The sleeveless dress featured diagonal slashed details to the front. Since the dress is quite busy, she wore little to no jewellery and teamed the dress with grey suede flats.

## 9. Jennifer Hudson

![Jennifer Hudson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/9_jennifer-hudson_290x580.jpg)

Jennifer Hudson wears a stylish orange wrap dress with tan platform heels and statement earrings. If you look closely you’ll notice that she has painted her nails a bright turquoise colour. This is a fun touch and turquoise and orange work really well together.

## 10. Keira Knightley

![Keira Knightley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/10_keira-knightley_550x1100.jpg)

Keira Knightley’s Richard Nicoll dress is made from an orange jacquard fabric. The ladylike dress features subtle cut out details to the sides that show off a hint of skin. She teams her orange dress with strappy heels and loosely swept up hair.

Rest assured, you don’t have to spend a fortune to get your hands on your own little orange dress. You can easily pull off this look on a budget – just keep an eye out for a flash of orange next time you go dress shopping. Who do you think wore their little orange dress the best?
