# 9 More Best Dressed Celebs at the Golden Globes ...

Ahh, the list of the best dressed celebs at the Golden Globes, an annual tradition that follows every single major awards show. It’s a guilty pleasure of mine, and of tons of you I’m sure, to read the list of who we think the best dressed celebs are! So without further ado, here’s my list of the 9 best dressed celebs at the Golden Globes 2014!

## 1. Emma Roberts in Lanvin

![Emma Roberts in Lanvin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/Nk/lG/rAGWYZ8V4y3TG3NxJpduG3jo_600x823.jpg)

Why is Emma on the list of best dressed celebs at the Golden Globes? Because this is the most grown-up I’ve seen her. She’s one of the most talented young stars in the business, and she’s been doing this for a while now. Sometimes you can pinpoint when a girl matures and turns into a woman. Emma, in this dress at this years Golden Globes is definitely one of those moments!

## 2. Emma Watson in Christian Dior

![Emma Watson in Christian Dior](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xw/qI/nvpRIH2mEEMy65HHB7ctPs3x_600x923.jpg)

Check out the back of this dress, because the second Emma on this list sure knows a thing or two about being one of the best dressed celebs at the Golden Globes. She also knows a heck of a lot about what it means to be a fashionista. Floor length dress in the front, backless in the back with pants underneath. It sounds like a mess, but looks fierce.

## 3. Amber Heard in Versace

![Amber Heard in Versace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kt/in/GouKgDNbtAW5zH48hHNMpUXV_600x978.jpg)

Amber, Amber, Amber, Johnny Depp is a lucky man isn’t he? Not only is she one of the best dressed celebs at the Golden Globes, she also gets to look like a million bucks in a dress that reminds the world of how young and gorgeous she is plus, she also gets to go home to one of the most coveted men in Hollywood. So haters around the world, eat your heart out!

## 4. Kate Beckinsale in Zuhair Murad

![Kate Beckinsale in Zuhair Murad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o0/ah/AOlKQPITHWv1ryhwAU8Dj61b_600x735.jpg)

Kate looks like a million dollars, super shiny, and reminds me of true, Hollywood royalty. If she’s not one of the best dressed celebs at the Golden Globes this year, then I have no idea what kind of criteria the people who write these lists have in mind. She looks gorgeous.

## 5. Olivia Wilde in Gucci

![Olivia Wilde in Gucci](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/V3/0o/QSKFoHrp0TYLfcUiQjnne1JM_600x926.jpg)

I don’t know what they give pregnant women in Hollywood, but whatever it is, I want some. Maybe it’s a gorgeous dress like this one that puts you on the list of best dressed celebs at the Golden Globes, or maybe it’s perfect ombre hair. Doesn’t matter, Olivia Wilde pretty much stuns us in whatever she’s wearing.

## 6. Reese Witherspoon in Calvin Klein

![Reese Witherspoon in Calvin Klein](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oN/Ww/lStDFo08SmNK6fL15N69jySZ_600x1011.jpg)

Calvin Klein has a certain ability; the ability to make the biggest statements in the simplest dresses. We all remember the orange CK number Emma Stone wore, and now Reese Witherspoon is part of the best dressed celebs at the Golden Globes list. Simple, stunning, and perfect accessories, we all applaud you Reese!

## 7. Lizzy Caplan in Emilio Pucci

![Lizzy Caplan in Emilio Pucci](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bk/jQ/QQqNWrTGB1M3huDvUxyS2EKE_600x993.jpg)

I love this dress, and I think she looks absolutely gorgeous. It’s different, it’s unique, and it reflects her personality. A little bit of edge, a little bit of glamour, and a whole lot of elegance, Lizzy is definitely one of the best dressed celebs at the Golden Globes. It takes a special person to pull off a dress like this, and she does a fine job at it!

## 8. Helen Mirren in Jenny Packham

![Helen Mirren in Jenny Packham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uH/ea/DlRpwCQmxq0hWY1Tk9c0QQrU_394x612.jpg)

A perfect example of how a mature, beautiful and celebrated actress should show up for an awards ceremony, Dame Helen Mirren just oozes elegance in this exquisitely draping dress. It was one of the more unusual and stand out colors of the evening and the Brit sure rocked it with more than a touch of class.

## 8. Leonardo DiCaprio in Gorgio Armani

![Leonardo DiCaprio in Gorgio Armani](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zM/s0/hDkPiQxbd3U1birbNZudo3cI_600x969.jpg)

This list would not be complete without a gorgeous man in a gorgeous suit looking gorgeous while winning a Golden Globe. Hopefully Leo will finally win an Oscar, but for now he’s on our list of best dressed celebs at the Golden Globes because Leo is awesome like that.

So ladies, what do you think of my list of best dressed celebs? Is there anyone I’ve missed? Or maybe someone who shouldn’t be there? We all have a different eye for fashion, so lets put our tastes together and maybe one day, we’ll find a gorgeous dress we can all agree on!
