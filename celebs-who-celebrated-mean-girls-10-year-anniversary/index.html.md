# 7 Celebs Who Celebrated Mean Girls' 10 Year Anniversary ...

One of the most iconic things about April was the fact that it was the Mean Girls' 10 year anniversary. Being the huge movie that it is, everyone participated in the celebration. I, of course, wore my 'On Wednesdays we wear pink' beanie! What did you do to celebrate? Let me know in the comments! Check out some of the celebrities who celebrated Mean Girls' 10 year anniversary.

## 1. Lindsay Lohan

![Lindsay Lohan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/c9/ucn2ez38_600x836.jpg)

The Mean Girls leading lady made sure to show the hit movie some love on its 10 year anniversary. She posted a hilarious photo of a sketch from the scene where Damien attends the school's all-girls meeting, shouting out the line, "she doesn't even go here!" She captioned it, "He has a point #happybirthday #10years #meangirls". LOL! Isn't it funny how people STILL use that line? I know I do. Lindsay's just one of the celebs who celebrated Mean Girls' 10 year anniversary!

## 2. Amanda Seyfried

![Amanda Seyfried](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nb/i2/fyz80ibg_600x900.jpg)

Amanda's another Mean Girls star who showed love on its anniversary. She posted a picture of her character with the caption, "On Wednesdays we wear pink." She then posted a photo of the phrase, "You CAN sit with us" on her Instagram account captioning it, "In honor of the 10 year anniversary of Mean Girls, I alongside @kindcampaign say to you, "You CAN sit with us." Repost this picture with the hashtag #youcansitwithus in an effort to bring an end to girl-against-girl bullying. #youcansitwithus #eventhoughyourenotwearingpink."

## 3. Lacey Chambert

![Lacey Chambert](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u7/rs/hr6n4ft0_600x777.jpg)

Mean Girls' very own Gretchen Wieners went all out for the movie's 10th birthday. The actress changed her Twitter profile picture (and everything else about her profile) to that of her character, Gretchen. She even tweeted some awesome Mean Girls quotes! You gotta appreciate someone who put that much effort into celebrating such an iconic movie.

## 4. Jonathan Bennet

![Jonathan Bennet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mx/tc/mkc7pcwn_600x890.jpg)

Remember Aaron Samuels? Well, on Mean Girl's 10 Year Anniversary, he made sure to show the movie (and his fans) some major love. He took to Twitter and said, "I am so grateful for the support of the fans for the past 10 years of #MeanGirls, thanks for being so fetch. You guys are grool. Yes, I did." LOL! I couldn't have thought of a better tweet than that.

## 5. Toaster Strudel

![Toaster Strudel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ai/hx/vskg784g_600x897.jpg)

Every Mean Girls fans remembers Gretchen's famous line, "I don't think my father, the creator of Toaster Strudel, would be too pleased to hear about this." Well, Toaster Strudel themselves celebrated the movie's 10th birthday! They tweeted a funny picture of the movie, alongside a toaster strudel, with a note that read, "The inventor of Toaster Strudel IS pleased to celebrate the 10-year anniversary of Mean Girls." Aww!

## 6. Daniel Franzese

![Daniel Franzese](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bq/cs/b5kn7rpk_600x785.jpg)

The oh-so-lovable Damien was one of my favorite characters! On the movie's birthday, he took to Twitter to talk to some big fans. He wrote, "Gonna be a lot of #MeanGirls RTs today. Its #MeanGirls10thAnniversary and #DanFranFans are giving a lot of love. It means so much! xo DF"A lot of those retweets included tweets from fans claiming they wanted their pink shirt back. LOL!

## 7. Shay Mitchell

![Shay Mitchell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tv/bt/ougvuf2e_600x818.jpg)

Pretty Little Liars' Shay Mitchell followed Amanda's footsteps and posted the same Kind Campaign photo reading, 'You CAN sit with us.' It's really cool that people used this a way to promote the Kind Campaign!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven celebs out of many who celebrated the movie's tenth birthday. How did you celebrate?
