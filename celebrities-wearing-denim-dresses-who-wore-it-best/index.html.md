# 10 Celebrities Wearing Denim Dresses – Who Wore It Best?

The denim dress is a stylish option for the season ahead and there have been plenty of celebrities wearing denim dresses. A denim dress is a great alternative to your usual denim shorts and skirts for summer. Best of all, they’re available in a variety of designs and washes. Dark denim varieties are perfect for working a more sophisticated and polished look while faded denim dresses are great for casual wear. Check out these celebrities wearing denim dresses and be sure to let us know who you think wore theirs the best.

## 1. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/1_kate-bosworth_600x900.jpg)

Kicking off our list of celebrities wearing denim dresses is Kate Bosworth. Always one to stay on top of trends, Bosworth has been sporting denim dresses for the past couple of years now. Here she wears a faded skater-style dress which she teams with a neutral handbag, flat shoes, and oversized shades.

## 2. Kiernan Shipka

![Kiernan Shipka](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/2_kiernan-shipka_592x888.jpg)

Young actress Kiernan Shipka wears a cute shift style denim dress to a Chanel event. The denim dress has a patterned finish and contrasting trims, which adds something a bit different to the design. Shipka teams her denim dress with a hot pink bag and black ankle boots.

## 3. Rachel Bilson

![Rachel Bilson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/3_rachel-bilson_592x888.jpg)

Rachel Bilson works a more urban look in a long-sleeved denim dress. The dress is quite oversized, which could be tricky to style if you’re not a size zero. Bilson teams her dress with black stockings, studded ankle boots, an oversized handbag and wayfarer sunglasses.

## 4. Diane Kruger

![Diane Kruger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/4_diane-kruger_545x818.jpg)

Diane Kruger works a look straight off the runway in this Chanel denim dress. The dress has a quite an exaggerated skirt, which definitely adds a wow factor to the design. She teams her dress with a chic black cap, compact clutch, and studded shoes.

## 5. Chloe Moretz

![Chloe Moretz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/5_chloe-moretz_600x900.jpg)

Chloe Moretz wears her denim dress on the red carpet. The collared dress, which she belts in at the waist, has an eye-catching patchwork pattern. She keeps the rest of her look fairly simple by teaming her dress with cute T-bar shoes.

## 6. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/6_alexa-chung_550x825.jpg)

Alexa Chung works a cool daytime look in a baby doll-style denim dress. It’s quite a girly style but she keeps her look contemporary by teaming it with flat shoes and a compact leopard print bag.

## 7. Katie Holmes

![Katie Holmes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/7_katie-holmes_592x888.jpg)

Here Katie Holmes works a casual look in a short-sleeved denim dress. She breaks it up with a tan waist belt and adds a pop of colour in the form of bright green flats. Round tortoiseshell shades and a slouchy tan handbag complete the look.

## 8. Anne Hathaway

![Anne Hathaway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/8_anne-hathaway_320x480.jpg)

Anne Hathaway works a casual daytime look in her denim dress. This style of dress is the kind you could easily dress up or down for summer. She teams hers with a black cap, pendant necklace, and studded black tote bag.

## 9. Ashley Madekwe

![Ashley Madekwe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/9_ashley-madekwe_533x800.jpg)

‘Revenge’ actress and blogger Ashley Madekwe works a simple, polished look in her denim dress. She teams her button-down dress with classic black ballet flats and adds a splash of colour with a green bag.

## 10. Michelle Williams

![Michelle Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/10_michelle-williams_575x933.jpg)

Michelle Williams dresses up a casual denim dress up by teaming it with a knee-length tailored coat. She teams her dress with studded pumps, which ties in nicely with the studded detailing on the dress.

These are just a few celebrities who have been spotted wearing denim dresses. What do you think of the denim dress trend? Which of these looks do you like best?
