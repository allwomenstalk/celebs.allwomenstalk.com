# 10 Celebrities in Cut out Dresses – Who Wore It Best?

There have been plenty of celebrities in cut out dresses recently. The cut out trend was a favourite last year and is still going strong. Subtle peek-a-boo details are the most popular option when it comes to celebrities in cut out dresses. Dresses can range from simple and sleek designs to bold and colourful creations. Read on and let us know who you think wore their cut out dresses best!

## 1. Emily Blunt

![Emily Blunt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/1_emily-blunt_350x700.jpg)

First up on our list of celebrities in cut out dresses is Emily Blunt. She wore a soft-hued Emilio Pucci dress to the National Board of Review Awards. The dress features subtle cut out details at the shoulders and sides. She teamed her dress with metallic heels which added some extra punch to the overall look.

## 2. Kerry Washington

![Kerry Washington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/kerry-washington-django-unchained-rome-dress-fa-w352_352x612.jpg)

Kerry Washington wore a fun Peter Pilotto printed dress to the Rome Premiere of ‘Django Unchained.’ The bold printed dress features a cut out detail just under the bust and large ruffle details on the skirt. She balances out the busy print on her dress by keeping the rest of her look fairly simple.

## 3. Gwyneth Paltrow

![Gwyneth Paltrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/3_gwyneth-paltrow_550x1100.jpg)

Gwyneth Paltrow strikes a pose in a sleek black cut out dress. The Michael Kors sheath dress features triangular cut outs at the bust and sides. She completed the look with a sleek hairdo, black cuff, and pointy white shoes.

## 4. Katharine McPhee

![Katharine McPhee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/4_katharine-mcphee_350x700.jpg)

Katharine McPhee’s lace dress features a midriff baring cut out detail. The sheer lace skirt also creates a cut out effect. I probably would have preferred a more ladylike choice of shoe, like a classic pump, rather than the ankle booties she’s wearing here.

## 5. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/5_miranda-kerr_350x700.jpg)

Miranda Kerr shows off a bit of midriff in a playful printed dress at the Global Green party. The dress is by Peter Pilotto and has a cut out detail just underneath the bust. She teams her busy printed dress with simple black heels and a clutch.

## 6. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/6_kate-bosworth_290x580.jpg)

It seems that celebrities are particularly loving printed cut out dresses. Here, the trendsetting Kate Bosworth wears a printed Altuzarra to the CFDA Awards. The dress had a draped design with large cut out detail to one side. She anchored her look with black heels and accessories.

## 7. Keira Knightley

![Keira Knightley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/7_keira-knightley_350x700.jpg)

Here Keira Knightley wears a jacquard dress by Richard Nicholl. Jacquard fabrics can look a bit too polished or stuffy, but the cut out details on this dress make it fresh and on-trend. She kept the jewellery to a minimum and teamed her dress with neutral coloured sandals.

## 8. Freida Pinto

![Freida Pinto](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/8_freida-pinto_550x1100.jpg)

Freida Pinto’s floor length Alexander McQueen gown featured half-moon cut outs around her midriff. The elegant black and gold dress really makes a statement. She kept her accessories to a minimum, choosing only to accessorise with a simple gold watch (which happened to be by the same jeweller holding the event – funny that).

## 9. Krysten Ritter

![Krysten Ritter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/9_krysten-ritter_350x700.jpg)

Actress Krysten Ritter wears a fitted navy dress with sparkly shoulder details and cut outs at the sides. She teams her dress with stud earrings, strappy sandals and a compact clutch. The look is fun and flirty and something you could definitely wear on a night out with the girls.

## 10. Jamie Chung

![Jamie Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/10_jamie-chung_350x700.jpg)

Jamie Chung’s bright blue dress features a belly-baring cut out as well as a smaller cut out detail at the shoulder. She teams her dress with metallic cap-toe pumps and wears her bangles pushed up on her arms.

These are just a few celebrities who have been seen wearing cut out dresses. Instead of showing masses of skin, it’s all about the subtle peek-a-boo details. Which of these celebrities do you think wore their cut out dress best?
