# 10 Celebrities Rocking Thigh\-High Splits – Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities wearing thigh high splits are a pretty common sight. Whether it’s a floor-sweeping skirt or gown, thigh high splits have become a popular feature on many a celebrity outfit. While celebrities wearing thigh high splits are a common sight on the red carpet, now we’re seeing a trend for it off the red carpet too. Check out these celebrities who have decided to put their best, ahem, leg forward.

## 1. Kate Hudson

![Kate Hudson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/1_kate-hudson1_592x888.jpg)

Kicking off our list of celebrities wearing thigh high splits is Kate Hudson. Here she works a very glamorous look at a Breast Cancer Research Foundation gala. She wears a black velvet gown with a gathered waist and thigh high split. Drop earrings, a cuff bracelet, and peep-toe pumps complete the look.

## 2. Drew Barrymore

![Drew Barrymore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/2_drew-barrymore_592x888.jpg)

Drew Barrymore works a more casual look in a tie-dye maxi skirt. The floaty skirt features a thigh high split which stops the entire outfit from becoming too frumpy. She sticks to an entirely black and white colour palette by teaming her skirt with a white top, black jacket, and black ankle boots.

## 3. Zoe Kravitz

![Zoe Kravitz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/3_zoe-kravitz_592x888.jpg)

Here, Zoe Kravitz styles an otherwise plain black maxi skirt. She teams it with a neutral ruffled blouse and black pumps. The thigh high split, lacy bra peeking through, and multiple rings add some extra edge to the overall look.

## 4. Lea Michele

![Lea Michele](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/4_lea-michele_592x888.jpg)

The thing about thigh high splits is that they can transform a plain or dowdy looking dress into something much more daring. Here, Lea Michelle strikes a pose in a cream Valentino dress. She teams her dress with drop earrings, a small clutch, and strappy white heels.

## 5. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/5_alexa-chung_592x888.jpg)

Instead of a floor-skimming skirt, Alexa Chung wears a knee-length skirt that features a thigh high split. She works a more tailored look by combining her split skirt with a buttoned up coat. Metallic platform sandals and a black patent clutch complete the look.

## 6. Anne Hathaway

![Anne Hathaway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/6_anne-hathaway_592x888.jpg)

Thigh high splits are perfect for showing off a statement shoe. The gladiator-style thigh high heels Anne Hathaway wears here are definitely a daring choice. They’re quite a contrast to the glamorous black gown that she’s wearing.

## 7. Chloe Sevigny

![Chloe Sevigny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/7_chloe-sevigny_592x888.jpg)

Chloe Sevigny’s outfit shows us that thigh high splits aren’t just reserved for floor-sweeping gowns. She wears a printed monochrome dress and teams it with a compact black clutch and lace-up ankle boots. A bit of bright red lipstick adds a pop of colour to the entire look.

## 8. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/8_olivia-palermo_550x1100.jpg)

I’m not sure many people would choose to rock a leopard print gown but here Olivia Palermo does just that, with a megawatt smile to boot. The draped gown features stomach cut outs and a very revealing thigh high split. She accessorises with a compact clutch, cuff bracelet and tribal print heels.

## 9. Vanessa Hudgens

![Vanessa Hudgens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/9_vanessa-hudgens1_550x1100.jpg)

True to her style, Vanessa Hudgens hits the red carpet in a very boho-inspired gown. The embellished top, feathery skirt and thigh high split make for quite a memorable combination. Since it’s quite a busy gown, she keeps her hair and makeup fairly simple.

## 10. Solange Knowles

![Solange Knowles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/10_solange-knowles_550x1100.jpg)

Solange Knowles works a colourful look in a figure-hugging green dress with thigh high split. The fluttery sleeves soften the otherwise bodycon dress and the contrasting collar detail adds a fun element to the look.

These are just some examples of celebrities rocking thigh high splits. As you can see, not all the looks involve floor-skimming gowns. Which of these celebrities do you think pulled off the thigh high split the best?
