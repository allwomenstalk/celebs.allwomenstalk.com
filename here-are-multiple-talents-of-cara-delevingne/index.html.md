# Here Are Multiple Talents of Cara Delevingne ...

There are plenty of talented actors, musicians and models out there, but not many can say that they are all in one. Luckily there is one, who fascinates us all. Cara Delevingne, an English fashion icon, first began conquering the world with modeling, got noticed for her head-turning looks and bold eyebrows and then ventured out into something more by revealing just about everything she is made of.

## 1. She Can Genuinely Embody a Character

While modeling was something that got her name out there, Cara Delevingne has always had her eyes set on acting. In a special interview with Teen Vogue she said, “The thrill of acting is making a character real. Modeling is the opposite of real.” We completely understand where she is coming from, and we are glad she used the modeling industry as a stepping-stone for growth. Just look at her now, she landed a major role in Paper Towns and is a part of one of the most anticipated films of 2016, Suicide Squad.

## 2. She Can Play the Drums

However, her talents don’t stop there. Our favorite model also happens to be musically inclined! Her edgy personality perfectly lines up with her talents, as one of them is playing the drums. If you check out her drumming debut at the Rock ‘N’ Kohl event, you’ll see with your own eyes that there is really something special there. Her energy bounces off the walls!

## 3. She Can Play the Guitar

Cara plays the drums, but mastering one instrument is not good enough for this gal. During her guest appearance on a Spanish talk show, El Hormiguero, she stunned the crowd with her guitar skills and the knowledge of many Spanish songs. She even played it behind her head!

## 4. She Has Good Vocals

She acts, she plays the guitar and drums, but can she also sing? Oh yes she can! In fact, she's taken the stage to perform a number of times and even has her own song called “Sticks & Stones.” While she might not have the best voice in the musical industry, there is definitely a potential for something more!

## 5. She is Shockingly Good at Beatboxing

Just when you think there is no more room for special talents, Cara Delevingne doesn’t fail to surprise. While appearing on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon, she dropped a sick freestyle beatbox and stunned the crowd along with the whole entire world.

## 6. She is Addicted to Call of Duty

Early in her career, Cara revealed that her interests lie in more than just fashion and modeling. While it may not seem like it, she is a big fan of Call of Duty and even has her own headset! Way to break through the stereotypes, Cara!

## 7. She is One Hell of a Model

In contrast to everything else, it shouldn’t come as a surprise that she is one hell of a model. She has graced the covers of Vogue, LOVE, Jalouse, Glamour, Garage and has walked for Burberry, Dolce & Gabbana, Mulberry and more. Her face covers billboards, magazine stands and all of the Internet! If that’s not a successful modeling career, I don’t know what is.

At just 22 years old, it seems like she has achieved more than some people do in a lifetime!
