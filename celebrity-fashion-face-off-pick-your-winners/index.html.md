# Celebrity Fashion Face\-off: Pick Your Winners ...

A little evil part of me loves it when celebrities end up wearing the same clothes. It's always fun to compare them and decide who did it better. Assuming a lot of you feel the same way, here's a little game. Three looks, six celebrities, one winner for each look and you get to decide! Here we go:

## 1. Gwen Stefani V/s Angela Bassett

![Gwen Stefani V/s Angela Bassett](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/ff-11_560x575.jpg)

Gwen was seen wearing this intriguing Giorgio Armani dress at the '64th Annual Cannes Film Festival in France. Angela Bassett wore hers to 'An Evening of Many Cultures' event in New York. The dress certainly fits Gwen better (I detest spillage) but her makeup is a wee bit scary. Hasn't she heard of the either/or rule when it comes to dark eyes and dark lips? Who wins for me? Guess you'll have to read on to find out.

## 2. Nicki Minaj V/s Jessie J

![Nicki Minaj V/s Jessie J](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/ff-2_293x473.jpg)

Nicki Minaj wore this stone-covered, skintight baby-blue Mark Fast jumpsuit to the Billboard Music Awards while Jessie J wore the same look to the Demons Never Die premiere in London. Both can certainly get away with this eccentric look. I like that Nicki Minaj went all out, candy colored hair et al and I love Jessie J's gold brouges. Of course the outfit itself is *cough* hideous but that's a discussion for another day.

## 3. Lauren Santo Domingo Vs. Emma Stone

![Lauren Santo Domingo Vs. Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/10/ff-3_500x500.jpg)

Firstly, I have to profess my love for this Giambattista Valli skirt. I adore that color! Moving on, Emma wore this look to the ‘Friends With Benefits’ New York premiere while Lauren Santo Domingo wore the same look while hosting a party to celebrate Derek Blasberg’s ‘Very Classy’ book at the Hotel Bel-Air in LA. It doesn't look bad on Lauren but I think this look is perfect for Emma!

So who are my winners? Gwen (inspite of the crazy eyes), Nicki Minaj (the candy pink hair is something else!) and of course the adorable Emma Stone. Your turn!
