# 7 Celebrity Couples Who Never Made It down the Aisle ...

We often hear about many celebrity couples who get divorced shortly after saying 'I Do.' But, did you know that there are many engaged celebrities who never made it down the aisle together? These celebrity couples said 'I Don't' before even getting the chance to say 'I Do'!

## 1. Brad Pitt & Gwyneth Paltrow

![Brad Pitt & Gwyneth Paltrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/1_brad-pitt-gwyneth-paltrow_592x888.jpg)

I wonder if Brad Pitt would have cheated on Gwyneth Paltrow with Angelina Jolie? I'm sure Paltrow is glad she didn't have to deal with a front page scandal with Pitt like Jennifer Aniston did. This celebrity couple never made it down the aisle because Paltrow broke it off six months after their engagement, at 24 years old she just wasn't ready to say 'I Do' to Pitt.

## 2. Jennifer Lopez & Ben Affleck...

![Jennifer Lopez & Ben Affleck...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/2_jennifer-lopez-ben-affleck_600x885.jpg)

With as many marriages and break ups as Jennifer Lopez has experienced, I'm sure we will hear in the future that she is again part of a celebrity couple who never made it down the aisle! Ben Affleck never seemed to be happy with Lopez. I was surprised when they did announce their engagement, but wasn't too shocked when they broke it off in 2004 after two years of dating. Did you know that he was married a year later to wife and actress Jennifer Garner!

## 3. Britney Spears & Jason Trawick,

![Britney Spears & Jason Trawick,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/3_britney-spears-jason-trawick_600x406.jpg)

Just when we thought Britney was getting her life back together, everything that was going good for her started to fall apart. Shortly after announcing that she would no longer be one of the "X Factor" judges, entertainment reports spoke of her split with fiance Jason Trawick. The couple dated for more than three years and Jason seemed to be right at home with Britney's two boys. But it looks like Spears is already on the mend and dating someone new, a fellow named David Lucado. All the best to Miss Spears!

## 4. Johnny Depp & Vanessa Paradis

![Johnny Depp & Vanessa Paradis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/4_johnny-depp-vanessa-paradis_399x400.jpg)

Though they were together for almost 15 years and have two beautiful kids, Lily-Rose (14) and Jack (11), it seems that this celebrity couple who never made it down the aisle just wasn't meant to be. They were cool, chic, one of a kind, and looked like the perfect couple, if that exists! It's been rumored that Depp cheated on Paradis with his co-star Amber Heard. However, in recent news, there are reports claiming that Amber has now moved on to be with a woman...celebrities!

## 5. Sheryl Crow and Lance Armstrong...

![Sheryl Crow and Lance Armstrong...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/5_sheryl-crow-and-lance-armstrong_600x884.jpg)

Perhaps Sheryl Crow knew something before the media did and decided to call it quits with Lance Armstrong only a few months after their engagement. They began dating after Armstrong's divorce and were engaged three years later in the Fall of 2005, but by February 2006 they were already saying 'I Don't.'

## 6. Ryan Reynolds and Alanis Morissette...

![Ryan Reynolds and Alanis Morissette...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/6_ryan-reynolds-and-alanis-morissette_600x473.jpg)

This odd celebrity couple who never made it down the aisle were together for four years before they officially split. Since their break up, Ryan Reynolds has been married, divorced, and now married again! Alanis Morisette is married as well, and gave birth to a baby boy in 2010.

## 7. Zoe Saldana and Keith Britton

![Zoe Saldana and Keith Britton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/7_zoe-saldana-and-keith-britton_600x475.jpg)

Did you know that actress Zoe Saldana was engaged in 2010? She was in a relationship with actor Keith Britton for eleven years, and engaged for just one year when they broke it off in 2011. Zoe has since been linked with Bradley Cooper, but is currently single.
