# Victoria Beckham: Thank God David Looks like Crap...

David Beckham has long held the lustful eyes of women all across the world and has always been considered as one of the best looking men by most reputed lists that are compiled annually. However, his wife Victoria 'Posh Spice' Beckham thinks otherwise!

In her interview with the Vogue UK, the Spice Girl turned WAG (that's Wives And Girlfriends of footballers for the uninitiated) turned fashion designer, Victoria admits that her husband is not always the hottest.

"The other morning I looked across at David just after he'd woken up and thought, You look really crap", Victoria confessed. "Thank God, because this is a man who always looked perfect".

Thank god for that indeed! Now the rest of us mortals can take some solace in that, specially after her recent interview when she had said that hubby David never really had a bad day when it came to looks!

In the interview, Victoria also addresses the issue of her perpetual stone faced expressions: "I always look at my miserable stupid face and think, Why didn't I just smile? Because I'm quite a happy, smiley person".

"When I see the paparazzi, I freeze", she continues. "Maybe that's my safety wall after all the years of crap. Still, at least the bag looks fabulous".

Umm, all right then.
