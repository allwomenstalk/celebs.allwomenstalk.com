# 9 Celebrities Wearing Tuxedo Dresses  Who Wore It Best?

Take a cue from celebrities wearing tuxedo dresses and spice up your evening-wear look. A tuxedo dress can be a chic alternative to a masculine-style tux or flirty cocktail dress. Tuxedo dresses can come in all sorts of designs, from boxy blazer dresses to full ladylike gowns. Take a look at these celebrities wearing tuxedo dresses and let us know who you think worked the trend the best.

## 1. Gwyneth Paltrow

![Gwyneth Paltrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/1_gwyneth-paltrow_592x888.jpg)

Kicking things off on our list of celebrities wearing tuxedo dresses is Gwyneth Paltrow in this two-tone Dior number. This tuxedo dress had a wrap-style design and asymmetrical hem. Despite being quite a tailored dress, Gwyneth wore her hair down and opted for minimal makeup. She also kept the two-tone look going by teaming her dress with contrasting ankle-strap shoes.

## 2. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/2_rihanna_592x888.jpg)

Rihanna was spotted stepping out in this lively tuxedo-style dress. It features an abstract swirly print with solid black lapels. Worn by anyone else, this tuxedo dress might seem dowdy, but Rihanna upped the ante by wearing it without a bra on underneath. Simple black strappy heels and rounded sunglasses completed her look.

## 3. Blake Lively

![Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/3_blake-lively_408x612.jpg)

Blake Lively worked a polished look in a long-sleeved white tuxedo dress. She teamed her dress with opaque stockings and metallic snake-skin heels. Bold jewels and bright pink lipstick added fun touches to this look.

## 4. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/4_alexa-chung_396x594.jpg)

Alexa Chung embraced the tuxedo trend by wearing this form-fitting Chanel dress. It has a very definite tuxedo-style design, with a buttoned bib and black bow tie. She teamed her tuxedo dress with sky high platform heels and a compact clutch.

## 5. Naomi Watts

![Naomi Watts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/5_naomi-watts_290x580.jpg)

Here, Naomi Watts wears a little black dress with a definite tuxedo-inspired twist. The dress has a fitted design and the neckline resembles tuxedo lapels. She kept her look fairly simple with metallic pumps, but you could easily dress up a black tuxedo dress like this with a chain-link purse and statement drop earrings.

## 6. Olivia Munn

![Olivia Munn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/6_olivia-munn_394x550.jpg)

Olivia Munn showed off some skin in this short navy tuxedo dress. It featured contrasting white lapels and a layered pleated skirt. She paired her dress with a striped clutch and peep-toe heels. She completed her super polished look with a slicked back hairdo and dark red lipstick.

## 7. Jennifer Lopez

![Jennifer Lopez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/7_jennifer-lopez_433x650.jpg)

Jennifer Lopez strutted her stuff in this white Rachel Zoe tuxedo dress. Although, considering how short it is, you’d be forgiven for thinking it was a long jacket! She stuck to a fairly monochrome look, teaming her dress with a neutral clutch and white pointed pumps.

## 8. Rita Ora

![Rita Ora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/8_rita-ora_600x900.jpg)

Rita Ora reworked a classic Yves Saint Laurent look in her tuxedo dress. The black and white YSL dress features an oversized black bow tie and fishtail hemline. She topped off her outfit with a floppy black hat and ankle-strap heels.

## 9. Marion Cotillard

![Marion Cotillard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/9_marion-cotillard_600x900.jpg)

Marion Cotillard worked a more feminine look in her Christian Dior tuxedo dress. The top half of the dress featured traditional lapel detailing while the skirt had more of a ball-gown feel to it. Black pointed pumps and a jewel encrusted necklace completed this red carpet outfit.

These are just a few celebrities we’ve spotted wearing tuxedo-style dresses. Are you a fan of this look? Which of these celebrities do you think worked the trend the best?
