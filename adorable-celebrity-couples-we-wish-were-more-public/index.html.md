# 7 Adorable Celebrity Couples We Wish Were More Public ...

Don’t get me wrong, I think celebrities deserve just as much privacy as the rest of us, but there are some really adorable celebrity couples out there who I wish would be just a smidge more public. Wouldn’t you love to see what Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively are like on a daily basis? Some of these adorable celebrity couples are so private that it’s easy to forget they’re even together!

## 1. Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively

![Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mg/zj/drfw5ceg_500x700.jpg)

Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively have the potential to be the greatest celebrity couple of our time, yet sometimes it’s easy to forget they’re even married. They were secretive throughout their entire relationship, and they even got married in secret. On their own, they are both perfect celebrities, but together they’re one of the most adorable celebrity couples. The small, small glimpses we get into their life when they make red carpet appearances just confirm that they are the perfect celebrity couple.

## 2. Javier Bardem and Penelope Cruz

![Javier Bardem and Penelope Cruz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/yp/v37fxavj_600x872.jpg)

I often forget that Javier Bardem and Penelope Cruz are together, but not only are they together, they have two children together. In the fight for privacy, these two celebrities are doing it right. They let people into their lives as they please, waiting months to even reveal their baby daughter’s name.

## 3. Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel

![Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/br/wmepv6xi_600x969.jpg)

In terms of private celebrity couples, Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel definitely are no longer the most private, but at one point they were. I think it’s safe to say that we all spent a little bit of time drooling over their wedding pictures, and we can’t help but be a little jealous when they’re out together. However, they’re so adorable that you also can’t help but love them.

## 4. Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis

![Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/pl/fmq7tifm_600x969.jpg)

Every fan of That '70s Show’s dreams came true when Jackie and Kelso got together in real life. Since announcing their engagement and pregnancy, Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis have become somewhat more public with their relationship, and by “more public” I mean acknowledging their relationship in the first place.

## 5. Emma Stone and Andrew Garfield

![Emma Stone and Andrew Garfield](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kn/uh/qyfadnw1_467x626.jpg)

I’m not quite sure if Emma Stone and Andrew Garfield have ever publicly confirmed their relationship, but it’s definitely common knowledge regardless. The Spider-Man movies have created wonderful opportunities for fans of the two to get some insight into their relationship via interviews and press junkets, without invading the couple’s privacy too much.

## 6. John Krasinski and Emily Blunt

![John Krasinski and Emily Blunt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wf/me/qm4s1te9_384x538.jpg)

John Krasinski recently tweeted a picture of his and Emily Blunt’s newborn daughter, and she is precious. The couple waited three months before even releasing a picture of her. While I respect the couple for being private with their relationship, it also helps keep the illusion that Pam and Jim are real and living in Austin with their two children. A girl can dream.

## 7. Beyoncé and Jay-Z

![Beyoncé and Jay-Z](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/01/wz/otowsmds_371x519.jpg)

Beyoncé and Jay-Z may be far from private now, but that wasn’t always the case. It seems like these days we’re always getting too caught up in their private life, but there was a time when Beyoncé and Jay-Z were so private that they secretly got married! They were the original private celebrity couple of the digital age, a time when privacy is definitely not Hollywood’s strong suit, and that deserves a little recognition.

Which of these private celebrity couples are your favorites? I love Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively, but it’s hard to pick a favorite! Let me know your favorite in the comments!
