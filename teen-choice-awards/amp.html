82 Photos of Teen Choice Awards 2010 ...

By Sheila

Teen Choice Awards 2010. Gibson Amphitheatre, Universal City, CA. August 8, 2010. I'm super excited to check these out!
City: Los Angeles

Kristen Bell was definitely one of my favorites of this night! I adored her in this oh-so-pretty Stella McCartney organza dress. She looks fabulous don't you think?
Ashley Benson sparkled in an LBD and Ruthie Davis Implant Gummy Pumps which I don't particularly like btw. What do you think of these?
She does have gorgeous eyes though, doesn't she?
Chace Crawford was pretty as always except I'm not so sure about that hair.
Cat Deeley chose a sexy Preen LWD for the event. I love the Jimmy Choo clutch. What a fun clutch! I wish she hadn't worn all those colored bracelets though.
Destinee and Paris Monroe kept it classic in black. I don't particularly like Destinee's dress but Paris looks cute in her Hollis embellished shoulder dress.
Also in black was Channing Tatum's very pretty wife, Jenna Dewan dressed in alice + olivia by Stacey Bendet Box Pleat Leather Skirt and a BCBG top.
Am I the only one who thinks Channing is a little too box like?
Is this the same Selena Gomez we know and love?! She looks so grown up, so gorgeous and so sophisticated in this white BCBG dress. Also, how HOT are her Sergio Rossi gladiator sandals.
Hate the Jeremy Scott dress! Hate the thigh high boots! Hate the hot pink lipstick. In fact, I like absolutely nothing about Katerina Graham's look!
I always think Ashley Greene dresses a little boring and while this Valentino dress is pretty, I just want to see her in something fun for a change!
Pretty Little Liar, Lucy Hale wore a Paule Ka LBD which was quite feminine and flirty. What stole the show however, were those Ruthie Davis Spike Dark GaGa heels. Definitely not everyone's cup of tea but I think she carries it off perfectly.
Aww cutie David Henrie! I love me a cute nerd!
Sarah Hyland was dressed in ANI. Love the color! Am I the only one who thinks she looks a little like Mila Kunis?
Victoria surely did this gorgeous Mason dress all the justice it deserved. I'm not sure if those shoes go with the dress though. What do you think?
I don't particularly care much for Kim's Zuhair Murad encrusted shoulder dress.
Which of the Kardashian sister's looks do you like best?
Has anyone watched "Vampires Suck" yet?!!
Yellow is not my favorite color but Jayma Mays sure looks cute in this yellow pleated dress.
Another one of my favorite looks from the night was Lea Michele in this stunning Naeem Khan dress. Great legs! Don't you think?
Awww! Isn't Cory Monteith absolutely ADORABLE!
Naya Rivera looked adorable in this strapless vintage dress!
I love Emma Roberts in this Brian Reyes dress. The hair and makeup are great too!
Mark Salling looked HOT with the whole vest look. *wolf whistle*
Yvonne Strahovski sure has the legs for that LWD! But it's kind of safe, don't you think?
Umm what! Kimberly Wyatt should have just stuck to something safe instead of wearing this fugly jumpsuit.
I'm sorry we had to end on an ugly note. :D
Do tell me who your favorite looks of the night were.
