# 7 Hottest Sons of Celebrities That You Need to Know about ...

The hottest sons of celebrities have their famous parents to thank for their good looks. Great genes make all the guys on this list look pretty great in skin-tight jeans. They might not be quite as famous as their successful parents, but they definitely deserve just as much attention. So without further ado, here are the hottest sons of celebrities that will soon be starring in your next daydream:

## 1. Scott Eastwood

![Scott Eastwood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/21195839_4899_570x737.jpg)

When it comes to the hottest sons of celebrities, they don't make 'em any hotter than Scott. Clint Eastwood's son proved that he was a bona fide babe magnet by posing shirtless for Town & Country magazine. The Internet immediately became enamored with the star, and there were even calls for him to score the lead role in the "Fifty Shades of Grey" movie. Unfortunately, that didn't happen, but you will get to see him in a few upcoming movies. His career might really take off after the World War II drama "Fury," which also stars Brad Pitt, Shia LaBeouf and Logan Lerman. Sadly, it doesn't hit theaters until November 14, 2014.

## 2. Jesse Johnson

![Jesse Johnson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/21195859_2460_476x594.jpg)

Speaking of "Fifty Shades of Grey," this actor is the half-brother of Anastasia Steele actress Dakota Johnson. Their father, Don Johnson, is probably best known for starring in the series "Miami Vice." Jesse is a definitely a dead ringer for his dear ol' dad, but he's not quite as successful just yet. The 31-year-old made his acting debut in 2001 by appearing on another TV show starring his famous father, "Nash Bridges." Since then, the blonde babe has had roles in a handful of movies and TV series. He most recently played John Wilkes Booth in the National Geographic Channel's "Killing Lincoln" movie, and many considered the part his breakout role.

## 3. Ronan Farrow

![Ronan Farrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/21195917_7786_526x768.jpg)

It's hard to believe that this blue-eye blonde is the son of Woody Allen. Ronan looks so little like his actor/director dad that there have even been rumors that his real father is singer Frank Sinatra! However, you have to remember that Ronan's mom is gorgeous actress Mia Farrow, so maybe her genes just won out. Ronan isn't the biggest fan of his dad since Allen cheated on his mom with her adopted daughter, but Ronan definitely has inherited his father's intelligence and sense of humor. However, the Rhodes Scholar has decided to focus on activism instead of acting (he probably got his love of fighting for what's right from his mom). Ronan served as Secretary of State Hillary Clinton's Special Adviser for Global Youth Issues in 2011, and he'll soon be starring on his own MSNBC show, so even though he's not an actor, you'll get to see lots more of his lovely face.

## 4. Oliver Hudson

![Oliver Hudson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/21200126_3888_398x594.jpg)

Did you know that Kate Hudson has a hot brother? Goldie Hawn's gorgeous son is also an actor, and it's possible that you've seen him on "Nashville" without being aware that he comes from a very famous family. Hudson isn't a big movie star like the women in his family, but he's had a lot of success on TV-he's also starred on "Dawson's Creek" and "Rules of Engagement." Oliver had a role alongside his famous mom in the movie "The Out-of-Towners," and you might have seen him in "Grown-Ups 2." His next movie role will be alongside Elizabeth Banks and James Marsden in the comedy "Walk of Shame," which hits theaters on April 12, 2014

## 5. Patrick Schwarzenegger

![Patrick Schwarzenegger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/21200147_5365_600x815.jpg)

The son of Maria Shriver and Arnold Schwarzenegger isn't as pumped up as his famous dad was when he was a young body builder, but Patrick is still scoring modeling work. The young star also has a philanthropic clothing line called Project360, so he definitely has a passion for fashion. Interestingly, he actually starred alongside the celebrity above in the movie "Grown Ups 2," so maybe Patrick will become a big Hollywood star just like his dad. Patrick is definitely popular with young Hollywood. He's been spotted spending time with Taylor Swift, and he recently played Romeo in Ariana Grande's "Right There" music video.

## 6. Jakob Dylan

![Jakob Dylan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/21200320_5816_600x600.jpg)

Bob Dylan's son has baby blues that will make you melt. Jakob isn't as successful as his folk singer father, but you've probably heard his band's popular '90s tune "One Headlight." The Wallflowers weren't exactly one-hit wonders (you might also know their cover of David Bowie's "Heroes"), but they never could match the success of their second album. You can feel some feels about the fact that Jakob married his child sweetheart, and you might want to check out some of his solo work if you're feeling all nostalgic for his soothing, somber voice. And you can debate about whether Trent on "Daria" was based on Jakob Dylan or Trent Reznor.

## 7. Max Irons

![Max Irons](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/21200357_1152_600x885.jpg)

Jeremy Irons's son is probably the most successful celebrity offspring on this list when it comes to scoring movie roles. The young star seemed destined for big things through his connection to "Twilight," with "Twilight" director Catherine Hardwicke casting him in her follow-up film. "Red Riding Hood" was a box office flop, but this didn't stop Max from getting cast in "The Host," which was based on Stephenie Meyer's alien-themed novel. That movie also didn't perform very well, but you definitely haven't seen the last of Max. He's recently starred as King Edward on the BBC series "The White Queen," and he's scored roles in a handful of upcoming movies, including the Christopher Nolan-penned thriller "The Keys to the Street."

While these celebrity sons aren't quite as successful as their parents, they aren't exactly suffering. Some might say that they're living in their parents' shadows, but it's pretty hard to be overlooked when you're that freakin' hot. So which of these sons of celebrities really gets your pulse racing?
