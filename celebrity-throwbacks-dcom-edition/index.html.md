# Celebrity Throwbacks: Dcom Edition ...

Long before these actors became household names, they all starred in Disney Channel Original Movies (DCOMS)! Ah, the good old days. Remember getting excited for these? Honestly, I would rewatch any of these if they were to air them. Check out these adorable throwback photos of these celebs on Disney:

## 1. Taran Killam

![Taran Killam](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cd/zg/bivbft3y_600x400.jpg)

Nowadays, Taran's busy making people laugh (hysterically) with his slot on Saturday Night Live. But do you remember him in the 2004 DCOM, Stuck In The Suburbs with Danielle Panabaker and Brenda Song?

## 2. Hayden Panetierre

![Hayden Panetierre](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iu/ss/vtswtbas_600x400.jpg)

Before becoming Juliette Barnes, the country queen of Nashville, Hayden starred in (and sang for) Tiger Cruise.

## 3. Kat Dennings

![Kat Dennings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/wi/hwcp14t3_600x400.jpg)

Kat Dennings was unrecognizable without her sultry red lips back in the 2002 DCOM, The Scream Team.

## 4. Emmy Rossum

![Emmy Rossum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oy/at/q08md6os_600x400.jpg)

Shameless' Emmy Rossum doesn't look like she's changed a bit! She starred as Claire Addison in 1999's DCOM, Genius.

## 5. Kaley Cuoco

![Kaley Cuoco](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hd/g7/o2r0ku28_600x400.jpg)

Before becoming Leonard's hot neighbor on The Big Bang Theory, Kaley played a high school teen who was part of an intense bowling rivalry in 2000's DCOM, Alley Cats Strike.

## 6. Bryan Cranston

![Bryan Cranston](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zz/ca/hppej3ig_600x400.jpg)

Long before Bryan was the infamous Walt on Breaking Bad, he was the fun uncle we all wanted in 2001's 'Twas the Night.

## 7. Kristen Stewart

![Kristen Stewart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cu/x3/p6gm1t6i_600x408.jpg)

Okay so K-Stew wasn't the leading lady in 1999's The Thirteenth Year, but she did make a cameo! P.S how good was that movie?!

## 8. Justin Timberlake

![Justin Timberlake](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t9/cn/wtv1zs3d_600x447.jpg)

Justin made us all swoon in 2000's Model Behavior, alongside Maggie Lawson!

## 9. Katherine Heigl

![Katherine Heigl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yn/sp/ojk8l5qm_500x366.jpg)

Talk about a serious throwback.. Katherine starred in 1996's Wish Upon A Star!

Which of these throwback Disney movies was your favorite?
