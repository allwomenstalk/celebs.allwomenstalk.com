# 10 Celebrities Rocking the Power Suit  Who Wore It Best?

I love seeing celebrities in power suits. They’re a far cry from the glamorous dresses you usually see them wearing on the red carpet but still look every bit as chic. When it comes to power suits, think matching separates, prints and patterns, bold colour, and a tailored fit. Power suits are all about exuding confidence and showing others who’s boss. Take a look at the following chic celebrities in power suits.

## 1. Heidi Klum

![Heidi Klum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/1_heidi-klum_290x580.jpg)

First on the list of celebrities in power suits is Heidi Klum. Here she wears a crimson Michael Kors suit complete with matching red lipstick. She accessorises her suit with a masculine style watch and simple bangle.

## 2. Blake Lively

![Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/2_blake-lively_350x700.jpg)

Red power suits seem to be a favourite for celebrities. Blake Lively dons a Michael Kors red suit, similar to the previous one that Heidi Klum is wearing. Lively wears a contrasting black singlet underneath her red suit and teams it with black strappy heels as well.

## 3. Rosie Huntington-Whitely

![Rosie Huntington-Whitely](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/3_rosie-huntington-whitely_350x700.jpg)

Model and actress Rosie Huntington-Whiteley strikes a pose in a red Burberry Prorsum suit. She teams it with a black top and a pair of strappy heeled shoes. She keeps the look fresh by wearing her hair out and keeping her makeup fairly natural.

## 4. Freida Pinto

![Freida Pinto](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/4_freida-pinto_290x580.jpg)

Freida Pinto looks smart and sophisticated in a tailored Gucci suit. She accessorises her suit with a striped clutch, black suede pumps, and a cute butterfly brooch. When wearing masculine-style suits, show a bit of skin by choosing tops with plunging necklines.

## 5. Emma Roberts

![Emma Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/5_emma-roberts_350x700.jpg)

Printed power suits are great for making a statement. Emma Roberts cuts a sleek figure in a Nanette Lepore graphic print suit. She accessorises it with a bright red clutch, black pointy heels, and a slick hairdo.

## 6. Solange Knowles

![Solange Knowles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/6_solange-knowles_350x700.jpg)

Here Solange Knowles wears a bold Just Cavalli printed suit. She teams it with a graphic patterned clutch and strappy monochrome heels. She wears her suit jacket buttoned up, which creates the appearance of a plunging v-shaped neckline.

## 7. Karolina Kurkova

![Karolina Kurkova](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/7_karolina-kurkova_350x700.jpg)

Model Karolina Kurkova rocks a blue and white Roberto Cavalli printed suit. She keeps the look strictly tuxedo-inspired with a high neck top and sleek parted hairdo. The pointy silver pumps are a cool modern touch.

## 8. Rashida Jones

![Rashida Jones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/8_rashida-jones_350x700.jpg)

If red isn’t your colour, you could always try a bright blue power suit instead. Here Rashida Jones teams her slim blue suit with a graphic printed blouse, pink pumps, and a compact metallic clutch. The overall look is a fun take on tailored dressing.

## 9. Katy Perry

![Katy Perry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/9_katy-perry_350x700.jpg)

Another celebrity rocking the blue power suit is Katy Perry. She wore her power suit while casting her vote in the last election. She teams her suit with pointy black pumps, an untucked white blouse, black tie, and wayfarer sunglasses.

## 10. Lady Gaga

![Lady Gaga](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/02/10_lady-gaga_290x580.jpg)

This is no doubt one of Gaga’s tamer outfits. She wears her Viktor & Rolf mustard suit with only a bra underneath. She teams her suit with oversized sunglasses (at night of course) and with a sweet bow headband.

These are just a few celebrities rocking bright and bold power suits. Take a lead out of their book and don a matching suit next time you want to exude a bit of extra confidence. Which of these celebrities do you think wore their power suit the best?
