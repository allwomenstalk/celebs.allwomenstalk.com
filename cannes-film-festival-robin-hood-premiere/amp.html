37 Photos of Cannes Film Festival 2010 - "Robin Hood" Premiere ...

By Sheila

Check out photos from the 63rd Annual Cannes Film Festival - "Robin Hood" Premiere. Palais des Festivals, Cannes, France. May 12, 2010. The world premiere of the $200million Hollywood blockbuster, starring Russell Crowe had to be one star studded event!
I love love love (and when I say it thrice I really mean it) Kate Beckinsale's Marchesa strapless blue draped tulle gown. Of course, Kate would look good in anything but this blew my mind!
Frederique Bel's dress was umm...interesting to say the least. I can't tell if she lost a earring or if we can't see it because of the angle.
Cate Blanchett wore Alexander McQueen. I love McQueen but honestly, I don't like the eagle motif on this gown. It's a little too masculine and harsh. Then again, trust Cate to do it differently.
Both Russell Crowe and wife, Danielle Spencer wore Giorgio Armani to the event.
Sara Forestier chose to wear a LBD. It's a little plain but I'm not complaining.
I do like the back on Julie Gayet's red dress!
Israeli socialite and television personality, Hofit Golan wore an elaborately garish red-and-white striped gown, presumably designed by her red carpet escort Christophe Guillarmé. Not pretty!
Samantha Harris wore red to the event. Is it just me or does she look skinnier than usual?
The ever fabulous Salma Hayek was seen in Gucci couture, wearing an asymmetrical deep red gown from the Italian luxury giant’s new made-to-measure line to the opening ceremony of the Cannes Film Festival. Divine!
Natalie Imbruglia looked stunning in a champagne Alberta Ferretti Fall 2010 gown. I just love all the details on the neck of the gown!
I think Lin Peng looks absolutely lovely!
Eva Longoria looked absolutely breathtaking in this Emilio Pucci white silk chiffon gown. I love the extraordinary train! Can you imagine this as a wedding dress?! *goes into dream mode*
Love Aishwarya Rai's Elie Saab dress. Hate the hair!
Helen Mirren wore a classy black, one shouldered gown by Elie Saab and looked as elegant as ever. What I wouldn't give to age gracefully like her.
I like the corset detail on Lea Seydoux's dress but that's about it. The colors are too dull together for my liking and the shoes just don't go.
What a fashionable affair that was! And your favorite look of the evening was....
