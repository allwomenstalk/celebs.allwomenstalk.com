# 7 Best Looking Actresses at the Golden Globes 2014 ...

Actresses always look stunning at red carpet events, but the best looking actresses at the Golden Globes 2014 looked amazing. I have always loved seeing the beautiful dresses that are worn on the red carpet. The construction, fabric, and colors just thrill me. This year, there were many beautiful black dresses worn by the best looking actresses at the Golden Globes 2014. White dresses and red dresses were also very popular. Although it is hard to narrow it down, I have chosen a few of my favorite looks from the Golden Globes to share with you.

## 1. Jenna Dewan-Tatum

![Jenna Dewan-Tatum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/Mg/zZ/qEo7kURhECrkPD8QOXtDDsyE_590x1024.jpg)

Jenna Dewan-Tatum is an absolutely stunning woman, and she was definitely one of the best looking actresses at the Golden Globes 2014. My jaw just dropped when I saw her Roberto Cavalli dress. This black and white gown is so chic. The beading is gorgeous, and highlights Jenna’s beautiful curves. As hard as it is to take my eyes off the beautiful dress, Jenna’s hair and makeup were also perfect. There really isn’t much left to say as she certainly had one of the best looks of the evening.

## 2. Julianna Margulies

![Julianna Margulies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rX/LC/lyghEHbYiZFUM8x11pKdZMyg_600x756.jpg)

I know this was not everyone’s favorite look, but I think it is fabulous. This Andrew Gn dress is very different from most dresses that are seen on the red carpet. The plunging V-neck, cap sleeves, and full skirt all work together so beautifully in this black gown, and the gold details on the sleeves and bottom of the dress add something special. Part of the reason I think Julianna Margulies looks so beautiful is because of the way her hair is styled, as well as the stunning black and gold earrings. I just love those earrings so much, and they are prefect for the dress.

## 3. Michelle Dockery

![Michelle Dockery](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pO/Dk/DudEAlFFnaTJSHM9xYCz9Hhb_600x969.jpg)

Michelle Dockery was one of the actresses who chose to wear a neutral colored dress. Although this Oscar de la Renta gown is neutral in color, it is anything but boring. The bell shaped skirt is just fabulous and so is the slightly shortened length in the front. This is not the first time Michelle Dockery has worn a dress cut like this, which is probably because it is a shape that looks wonderful on her. The dress is made very special by the addition of the beading, and Dockery was wise to forgo a necklace as it would have detracted from the dress. Her hair was also simply yet beautifully styled, which pulled the whole look together.

## 4. Kerry Washington

![Kerry Washington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/OI/Yo/HqsCvZoNbT3bIG4uQ8i7PYwM_600x969.jpg)

The very pregnant Kerry Washington looked fabulous in her Balenciaga dress. This white dress with the beautiful pendant at the bust flatters her baby bump so well. I love how the dress just lightly flows. Kerry Washington is such a beautiful woman, and her hair and makeup looked especially wonderful. She certainly has a glow about her, which matches the sparkling jewelry that she accessorized with.

## 5. Margot Robbie

![Margot Robbie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ST/0q/Ts9LYCD72ixfEy2yOaOiCmYg_600x969.jpg)

No one can deny that Margot Robbie looked like a blond bombshell at the Golden Globes. Her Gucci dress fit her just perfectly. I just love the simple jewel beading on this white gown, and I love that she matched the shoes to the beading. She was wise to keep the jewelry simple and let the dress speak for itself. Her updo was just as fabulous as her dress. It completed the striking look.

## 6. Lupita Nyong’o

![Lupita Nyong’o](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/Wl/vG/xy537o3UzD1pWlYBvmJsirrU_600x969.jpg)

A newcomer to the red carpet, Lupita Nyong’o made my jaw drop when I saw her dress. She looks like a goddess in this Ralph Lauren dress. The color looks so stunning against her skin tone, while the off-the-shoulder look highlights her gorgeous shoulders. I don’t often like capes, but the cape on this dress is sublime; without it the dress would have been very boring. However, this dress was anything but boring. In fact, it was so special that only earrings were needed for jewelry.

## 7. Cate Blanchett

![Cate Blanchett](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/Nd/nO/KYqcM87AegsgMxCRccoYxPFI_600x969.jpg)

I saved the best for last. This was my absolute favorite gown of the evening. Cate Blanchett looked spectacular in this Armani gown. The black lace is so elegant, but the back of this dress has to be my favorite part. The deep, low back with the diamond cutout at the bottom was to die for. As beautiful as this dress is I could stare at it for hours, but I can’t forget to mention Cate’s hair. I adore the old Hollywood glamour of this hairstyle and feel it goes so perfectly with this dress.

The Golden Globes is the first award show during award season, and it is the start to my excitement for seeing the beautiful designer gowns. This year, there were many beautiful dresses, and these actresses all looked exquisite. I can’t wait to see what they wear to other award shows. What was your favorite look from the 2014 Golden Globes?
