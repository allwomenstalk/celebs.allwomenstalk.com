# 10 Celebrities Wearing Striped Dresses – Who Wore It Best?

Stripes are a really popular trend right now, and there are plenty of celebrities wearing striped dresses. If you’re after a statement dress, a striped dress can be a stylish option. Stripes can look anything from classic to fashion-forward, depending on the style of dress you choose. Check out these celebrities wearing striped dresses and let us know who you think wore their stripes best.

## 1. Jamie Chung

![Jamie Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/1_jamie-chung_550x1100.jpg)

Actress Jamie Chung is first up on the list of celebrities wearing striped dresses. The dress has an all over black and white striped design with a high neckline and full skirt. She adds a pop of colour to her mainly black and white look with some bright blue heels.

## 2. Sarah Jessica Parker

![Sarah Jessica Parker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/2_sarah-jessica-parker_550x1100.jpg)

The metallic, multicoloured stripes on Sarah Jessica Parker’s dress are a glitzy take on your usual classic Breton stripes. The figure hugging dress features a gathered bust and multicoloured striped panels. She teams her striped dress with black velvet heels and assorted jewellery.

## 3. Rose Byrne

![Rose Byrne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/3_rose-byrne_550x1100.jpg)

Rose Byrne opts for a pastel look in this sweet striped dress. The dress features a mix of black, white, and blush horizontal panels. It also has a v-neck line and full skirt. She accessorises the dress with a slim silver bangle, a matching blush Chanel handbag, and pointed black pumps.

## 4. Anne Hathaway

![Anne Hathaway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/4_anne-hathaway_550x1100.jpg)

Here, Anne Hathaway wears a subtle striped dress. The dress features contrasting black and grey striped panels and metallic striped details. She teams the dress with opaque stockings, pointed pumps, and a yellow quilted handbag.

## 5. Zooey Deschanel

![Zooey Deschanel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/5_zooey-deschanel_550x1100.jpg)

Zooey Deschanel wears a black and white dress by Kate Spade New York. The dress features a peek-a-boo cut out detail just above the bust. She teams the dress with black stockings and black shoes. Makeup notes included her trademark [winged eyeliner](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/tips-for-girls-who-suck-at-winged-eye-liner/) and a bright red lip.

## 6. Allison Williams

![Allison Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/6_allison-williams_550x1100.jpg)

‘GIRLS’ actress Allison Williams wears a striped sweater dress to an HBO event. She keeps her black and white look fairly simple with minimal accessories and a peep of colour in the form of coral nail polish. She dresses up what would otherwise be a casual sweater dress with black platform pumps and dangly earrings.

## 7. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/7_olivia-palermo_550x1100.jpg)

Olivia Palermo looks dressed to the nines in this Dior dress. She teams it with a black coat, red quilted handbag, and sleek pointed pumps. Makeup notes include winged eyeliner and a statement red lip.

## 8. Chloe Moretz

![Chloe Moretz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/8_chloe-moretz_550x1100.jpg)

Young actress Chloe Moretz looks polished and ladylike in a long sleeved striped dress. The dress has wide, bold stripes which really make a statement. Staying in theme, she teamed her striped dress with peek-a-boo striped Christian Louboutin heels.

## 9. Kerry Washington

![Kerry Washington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/9_kerry-washington_550x1100.jpg)

Kerry Washington is pretty in pink in this sweet striped dress by Dior. The light pink dress has a fitted top and full skirt, and features two bold dark pink stripes. She teams her dress with neutral pumps and complements the look with a matching bright pink lipstick.

## 10. Nikki Reed

![Nikki Reed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/10_nikki-reed_550x1100.jpg)

Nikki Reed works a colourful look in a blue and black striped dress. The dress has a pretty demure cut, with longer length sleeves and a wrap-style waist. She accessorises her dress with a yellow statement necklace and matching yellow pumps.

Striped dresses are an on-trend option for this season. These are just a few celebrities who have stepped out in statement making striped dresses. Which of these striped looks do you like best?
