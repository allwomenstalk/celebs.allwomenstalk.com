# 11 Belly Baring Celebrities – Who Wore It Best?

Crop tops are an increasingly popular choice of attire, and we’ve spotted plenty of belly baring celebrities lately. The trend is a bit reminiscent of the nineties (think Spice Girls, Gwen Stefani’s abs) which kind of makes sense since the nineties seem to be having a big influence on fashion right now. However, it’s not all Sporty Spice-inspired outfits, with some celebrities showing that baring your midriff can be a classy affair. Check out these belly baring celebrities and let us know who you think wore it best (or didn’t wear it, to be more precise).

## 1. Nicole Richie

![Nicole Richie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211343_6400_550x1100.jpg)

Nicole Richie kicks off our list of belly baring celebrities in a playful pink top and skirt. The high waisted skirt and crop top combo is a popular one, as it’s a way to show off some skin without actually exposing your belly button. Here, she teams her matching pink separates with a chunky chain necklace and sleek black pumps.

## 2. Lily Collins

![Lily Collins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211344_4014_550x1100.jpg)

Matching sets seem to be a big trend at the moment and Lily Collins works a fun look in a monochrome prints. She wears a matching crop top and shorts set from Paper London which reveals the slightest hint of skin. She keeps the accessories to a bare minimum and sticks to the monochrome look by wearing black platform pumps.

## 3. Olivia Wilde

![Olivia Wilde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211345_3522_550x1100.jpg)

Olivia Wilde works a ladylike look in a cropped leather t-shirt and red checkered pencil skirt. She accessorises with a slim line clutch and strappy platform heels. The longer length of the skirt and subtle show of skin balances out the edgier elements of the outfit.

## 4. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211344_6906_550x1100.jpg)

Selena Gomez shows off plenty of skin in this shorts and tee combo. She teams her black quilted Topshop shorts with a structured black and green cropped t-shirt. She finishes off this edgy look with a pair of caged knee high shoe boots.

## 5. Vanessa Hudgens

![Vanessa Hudgens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211344_5649_550x1100.jpg)

She’s well known for her boho-inspired off duty looks but here, Vanessa Hudgens works a more tailored look. She wears a sheer long-sleeved cropped top with a pair of white tailored pants. She teams her fresh all-white outfit with strappy nude heels and an oversized black handbag.

## 6. Shailene Woodley

![Shailene Woodley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211344_8902_550x1100.jpg)

Shailene Woodley looks athletic in this belly baring number. Here she wears a black neoprene cropped top with an intricate beaded fringed skirt. Since the skirt does most of the talking, she accessorises with a simple black cuff and pointed heels.

## 7. Dianna Agron

![Dianna Agron](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211346_9779_592x888.jpg)

Dianna Agron sticks to a monochrome look in this demure outfit. She teams a loose white crop top with a black midi skirt, showing only the slightest hint of a midriff. She accessorises her outfit with assorted jewellery and statement [striped heels](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/jazz-up-your-wardrobe-with-these-pairs-of-striped-high-heels/).

## 8. Diane Kruger

![Diane Kruger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211346_5781_592x888.jpg)

Here, Diane Kruger keeps it casual in a floaty cropped top and skinny jeans. The embellished arrow details on her top adds a bit of bling to what would otherwise be a fairly basic daytime outfit. Simple jewel sandals and a grey handbag complete her off-duty look.

## 9. Rita Ora

![Rita Ora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211346_2915_592x888.jpg)

Rita Ora’s belly baring look is more reminiscent of nineties fashion. Here she wears a printed t-shirt, open and exposing a black bra and stomach underneath. She also wears matching printed shorts, round sunglasses, and slide sandals. This urban street look is typical of Rita’s signature style.

## 10. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211346_5211_592x888.jpg)

Showing off some skin is no biggie for Rihanna. Here she works an urban look in a cropped camo top and dark denim jeans from her collection with River Island. She teams her outfit with strappy camo print heels, a gold quilted cap, and assorted bling.

## 11. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/08211347_0107_592x888.jpg)

It seems that nothing is going to stop Miley Cyrus from wearing or doing what she wants. Here she does double denim in denim shorts and an oversized acid wash jacket, revealing a white crop top underneath. Black thigh high boots complete this risqué look.

These are just a few celebrities who don’t mind a bit of belly baring activity. Are you a fan of showing off some midriff? Which of these celebrity looks do you like best?
