If you're a foodie, you need to be following Haylie Duff on Instagram! She's constantly posting photos of her yummy creations or whatever she's dining on when she eats out. Haylie even penned "The Real Girl's Kitchen" which you buy for only $4 on [barnesandnoble.com](https://goo.gl/jl3a4w)! She also has her own show, "Real Girl's Kitchen" on the Cooking Channel!
