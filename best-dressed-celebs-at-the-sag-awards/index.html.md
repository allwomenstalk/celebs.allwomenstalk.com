# 9 Best Dressed Celebs at the SAG Awards ...

The best dressed at the Golden Globes is so last week, this time around it’s the best dressed celebs at the SAG awards! The Screen Actor’s Guild Awards show doesn’t get as much attention as the Golden Globes, but the celebrities that show up there look as gorgeous as ever, and totally worth drooling over! So there are the 9 best dressed celebs at the SAG awards.

## 1. Sofia Vergara, in Donna Karan Atelier

![Sofia Vergara, in Donna Karan Atelier](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pd/ye/r6/eagw4gpx_600x947.jpg)

Sofia was totally one of the best dressed celebs at the SAG Awards; then again, she’s easily the best dressed celeb anywhere she goes! She looked absolutely gorgeous in this silver number; it looks like it’s just gliding on her body. It packs just enough punch yet looks lightweight at the same time. Defintely one of the best dressed celebs at the SAG Awards!

## 2. Julia Louis-Dreyfus, in Monique Lhuillier

The reason Julia is on the list of best dressed celebs at the SAG Awards is because this dress is not only beautiful on her, it’s simple, elegant, and shows off her maturity and her beauty perfectly. Good job Julia!

## 3. Isla Fisher, in Oscar De La Renta

Isla is gorgeous! With that red hair she reminds me of one of those screen sirens from Hollywood’s Golden Age, and at the SAG Awards she completely looked the part! One of the best dressed celebs at the SAG Awards, the only thing more enviable is her gorgeous flowing hair. Why doesn’t my conditioner do that?

## 4. Natalie Dormer in Marios Schwab

You either love it or you hate it – her half shaved head, that is! But Natalie is definitely one of the best dressed celebs at the SAG Awards, if for no other reason than because of just how perfectly daring the whole look is. I personally think she looks amazing!

## 5. Anna Gunn in Monique Lhuillier

This Breaking Bad star looked beyond gorgeous in this navy sparkling number! Definitely one of the best dressed celebs at the show, she knows that when you have a stunning dress like this, there’s not much else you need! Note: no crazy jewelry!

## 6. Lupita Nyong’o in Gucci

![Lupita Nyong’o in Gucci](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p0/e1/kz/fgehuun7_600x904.jpg)

This is a one of a kind Gucci creation on this star, and anything that’s considered a Gucci rarity definitely earns you a spot on the list of best dressed celebs at the SAG Awards. ESPECIALLY when you know how to wear colour like this lady.

## 7. Claire Danes in Vionnet

![Claire Danes in Vionnet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/es/w4/gf/rtd1gf05_600x969.jpg)

There seems to be a bit of a debate about this dress, but I personally think it’s beautiful on her! The best dressed celebs at the SAG Awards always have a little bit of controversy, I think. There’s a lot going on in this dress, but she carries it flawlessly!

## 8. Jennifer Lawrence in Christian Dior

![Jennifer Lawrence in Christian Dior](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g0/gy/v0/h8jiormk_600x899.jpg)

A huge step up from her Golden Globes gown, J.Law came back strong and earned her place on the list of best dressed celebs at the SAG Awards. This gown is modern, youthful, and perfect on America’s newest sweetheart!

## 9. Hannah Simone in Marchesa

This girl needs to make more appearances on the red carpet! Look how beautiful she looks! She’s one of the best dressed celebs at the SAG Awards, but something tells me that is only the beginning; she’ll be popping up on a lot of lists if she keeps showing up like this!

Now if only we could all dress up one night and go to a fabulous awards show and wear gorgeous designer duds! But since we can’t (at least for the time being), ohh-ing and ahh-ing at gorgeous celebs in gorgeous evening wear is as close as we’re going to get. So tell me, who do you think was the best dressed at this year's SAG Awards?
