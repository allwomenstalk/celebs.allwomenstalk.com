# Whoa! do These Celebs Have a Historical Twin?

There are so many theories concerning these photos. I think the best (and funniest) ones are the ones that say these celebs are actually vampires who have been living forever! That would make a lot of sense right?! LOL! Check out some photos of celebrities and their uncanny similarities with some historical figures.

## 1. Maggie GYLLENHAAL and ROSE WILDER LANE

![Maggie GYLLENHAAL and ROSE WILDER LANE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m0/gc/wkpuotdm_600x436.jpg)

Rose Wilder Lane was a renowned writer who is considered to be one of the mothers of the American libertarian movement. Maggie Gyllenhaal, an award-winning actress, shares not only her looks but also an involvement with politics!

## 2. RUPERT GRINT and SIR DAVID WILKIE

![RUPERT GRINT and SIR DAVID WILKIE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g7/oj/p50kc2tp_600x436.jpg)

Sir David Wilkie was a Scottish painter in the early 19th century, and Rupert Grint (a.k.a Ron Weasley) looks like the modern day version of him!

## 3. JENNIFER LAWRENCE & Zubaida Tharwat

![JENNIFER LAWRENCE & Zubaida Tharwat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c8/j9/m5l20o5n_600x450.jpg)

Am I seeing double?! J.Law looks just like the Egyptian actress, Zubaida Tharwat!

## 4. Alec Baldwin & President Millard Fillmore

![Alec Baldwin & President Millard Fillmore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qa/n8/u7wijb36_600x451.jpg)

## 5. Jimmy Fallon & Mahir Cayan

![Jimmy Fallon & Mahir Cayan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mq/ok/g4ulayld_600x361.jpg)

## 6. Jay Z & Random Man
