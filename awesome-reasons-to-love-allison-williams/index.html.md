# 7 Awesome Reasons to Love Allison Williams ...

There are so many great reasons to love Allison Williams. She's a very talented actress and I think she has a very long and successful career ahead of her. She's a great role model for young women and looks fabulous everyday while kicking major butt in the entertainment industry! Check out some of the reasons to love Allison Williams and then let me know what YOU love most about her.

## 1. 'marnie' on Girls

Even though Allison's character, Marnie, might be some people's least favorite character (LOL), I think her acting is fantastic. I don't know about you, but I'd still love to be best friends with Marnie. She seems like she'd be really fun to hang out with! Hey, if she's cool enough to be besties with Hannah, I'd say she's pretty cool. 'Girls' is just one of the many reasons to love Allison Williams!

## 2. She's the New Peter Pan

In case you haven't heard, Allison scored the coveted role of Peter Pan in NBC's live adaptation! There were some mixed feelings and reactions about the news but I'm actually SO excited for her. I think she's going to be great in the role. Her 'Girls' co-star, Lena Dunham, even tweeted, "Allison is about to undertake one of the greatest drag roles in history and we are so proud we could burst. She will slay."

## 3. She's Gorgeous

To state the obvious, Allison's absolutely beautiful! When she gets dressed up for red carpet events, it's not even fair how amazing she looks. Another thing that makes her beauty shine so bright is her inner beauty; that girl just radiates positivity! Those are definitely my favorite people to surround myself with. Don't you agree?

## 4. She Loves Her Fans

Sadly, Allison isn't on any social networks. That means, no tweets and no Instagram posts! However, she always lets it be known that she's very grateful for her fans. When the topic comes up in interviews, Allison always brings up the loyalty and dedication of her fans!

## 5. Her Sense of Style

Allison always looks great at red carpets/press events. She always manages to look sleek, sexy, and sophisticated! When she's not glammed up, she keeps things comfy and cute with her street style. I also love that she chooses to go "make-up free" whenever she's not on set. It shows that she's comfortable in her own skin and I think that's a great message to young girls!

## 6. She Sings

Allison's showed off her vocals in 'Girls' but did you know that the actress actually sings in real life too? She even has a few covers on YouTube! She recorded them back when she was in college. There's even a cover of Kesha's "Tik Tok"! What are you waiting for? Go look up those videos, NOW!

## 7. Her Funny Interviews

After binge-watching all of her interviews on YouTube, I've come to the realization that Allison's ridiculously funny. Seriously, you can find some funny clips online! I love her laid-back personality. I feel like she's so easy to be friends with!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome reasons to love Allison Williams! I think she's a fantastic actress and a great role model for young women. If you're a fan of Allison's, what's your favorite thing about her?
