# 10 Celebrities Wearing White Blazers – Who Wore It Best?

There are plenty of celebrities wearing white blazers these days. The white blazer is as much of a wardrobe staple as a black blazer and it’s a great way to add a fresh and tailored touch to your look. A white blazer is perfect for the summer months, teamed with anything from floral dresses to printed shorts. Check out a few celebrities wearing white blazers, below, and remember to let us know who you think styled theirs the best.

## 1. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/1_taylor-swift_550x1100.jpg)

Taylor Swift kicks off our list of celebrities wearing white blazers with this classy number. She worked a super polished look with a white blazer and tailored trousers. Gold strappy sandals, bright red lipstick, and red nail polish completed the look.

## 2. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/2_rihanna_550x1100.jpg)

Rihanna teamed her white blazer with some matching tailored shorts for an on-trend summertime look. She made it all that more risqué by opting not to wear a top under her buttoned-up blazer. Chanel earrings, assorted bling, and some casual looking sneakers finished off the look.

## 3. Jennifer Lopez

![Jennifer Lopez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/3_jennifer-lopez_550x1100.jpg)

Jennifer Lopez worked a cool and casual look in her white blazer. She teamed it with a white blouse, some light denim jeans, and pink studded Christian Louboutin booties. She accessorised with assorted jewellery and a structured white clutch.

## 4. Elizabeth Olsen

![Elizabeth Olsen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/4_elizabeth-olsen_550x1100.jpg)

Here, Elizabeth Olsen puts a modern twist on power dressing. She wears a white blazer with a black top, knee-length leather pencil skirt, and chunky heeled sandals. While most celebrity outfits are head to toe designer, the blazer and skirt here are actually from Topshop and ASOS.

## 5. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/5_jessica-alba_550x1100.jpg)

Jessica Alba kept it strictly black and white with this outfit. She teamed her white crepe blazer with a black crop top, white blouse and black trousers. She even extended the black and white theme through to her cap toe shoes and envelope clutch.

## 6. Gwyneth Paltrow

![Gwyneth Paltrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/6_gwyneth-paltrow_550x1100.jpg)

Gwyneth Paltrow played with proportion by teaming her boyfriend-style blazer with short shorts. She also wears a checkerboard top, black and gold sandals, and a chunky chain link bracelet.

## 7. Karolina Kurkova

![Karolina Kurkova](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/7_karolina-kurkova_550x1100.jpg)

Model Karolina Kurkova was spotted in this all-white ensemble. She wore a crisp white blazer, top, tailored pants, and pointed pumps. The tortoiseshell sunglasses were the only non-white part of the outfit.

## 8. Katie Holmes

![Katie Holmes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/8_katie-holmes_550x1100.jpg)

Leather-look clothing has been trending for the past couple of seasons now. Here, Katie Holmes gets in on the trend by teaming a pair of quilted leather trousers with a white top and blazer. She completes the look with black pumps and a black handbag.

## 9. Kate Mara

![Kate Mara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/9_kate-mara_550x1100.jpg)

Here, Kate Mara works a bit of colour into a mainly black and white outfit. She wears a white blazer with an orange pleated top, black skinnies, and nude pumps. She accessorises with gold bracelets and an envelope clutch.

## 10. Gemma Arterton

![Gemma Arterton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/10_gemma-arterton_600x873.jpg)

Gemma Arterton works a casual yet polished look with her white blazer. She teams it with a slouchy tee, skinny jeans, and snakeskin pumps. A metallic bag adds a bit of glamour to the entire look. This is one of those outfits that you could easily recreate with pieces from your own wardrobe.

While many of these celebrities went for a monochrome look, you could always wear your white blazer with bright colours or fun prints. Do you own a white blazer? Which of these celebrity looks do you like best?
