# 7 Heartbreaking Celebrity Splits That We Still Cant Get over ...

It seems that anymore in Hollywood there are new heartbreaking celebrity splits every other day. However, some of these break-ups stick out in our minds as more shocking and devastating than others. Some couples just seem so perfect together, so when they call it quits, it’s as is if we’re experiencing the break-up ourselves. Here’s a list of the most heartbreaking celebrity splits.

## 1. Orlando Bloom and Miranda Kerr

![Orlando Bloom and Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/es/0v/guen6mz7_600x461.jpg)

I'm putting this one at the top of the list of heartbreaking celebrity splits. Miranda and Orlando have to be one of the best-looking couples in history, and have the most adorable baby boy together. Reports claim that the separation was amicable and they remain friends, but this split is still fresh on my mind.

## 2. Michael Douglas and Catherine Zeta-Jones

![Michael Douglas and Catherine Zeta-Jones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ns/r3/m1/a951kjl3_560x415.jpg)

I don’t know about you guys, but I always said that if Michael and Catherine split, then there is no hope for any of us. The couple was married for 13 years, which is an eternity in Tinsel Town, but through those 13 years, they endured quite a bit. Michael was diagnosed with cancer and Catherine, bi-polar disorder. Despite these hardships, they stuck by each other, along with the help of their two children. This split was apparently a "break" and the couple is trying to make things work, so I'm still holding out for a reconciliation!

## 3. Brad Pitt and Jennifer Aniston

![Brad Pitt and Jennifer Aniston](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v5/t9/j7/kg89ypy6_600x399.jpg)

Yes, I know this was eons ago, but these two seemed like such a perfect couple. They looked absolutely beautiful together and always shined wherever they went. The Angelina-intervention totally seemed as if some dark force was pulling them a part and made girls everywhere sympathize with Jennifer. What’s more, even though Brad and Angelina have been together for quite some time and have absolutely gorgeous children together, Jennifer has had a lot of difficulty finding the perfect guy for her. Let’s hope her future with Justin Theroux is bright!

## 4. Ryan Gosling and Rachel McAdams

![Ryan Gosling and Rachel McAdams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vx/av/i5/fyfpiw3s_600x484.jpg)

Although we women are very territorial over the gorgeous Ryan Gosling and who he dates, you have to admit that he and Rachel were the it couple. It was almost as if they were playing out their Notebook romance in real life. Not to mention Rachel is such a sweetheart and totally deserving of the incredible super human that is Ryan Gosling. Sigh.

## 5. Seal and Heidi Klum

![Seal and Heidi Klum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/q1/e0/iccur7en_471x377.jpg)

I think the world was just as shocked to see Heidi and Seal dating as they were to see them split. The couple was unexpected, but made an exceptional duo that made four adorable children. The reports of the split are very ugly, with cheating rumors and financial differences. I hope the two can work things out, especially for the sake of their kids!

## 6. Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes

![Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zy/x8/nx/zyy0vd2p_600x400.jpg)

This one wasn’t as saddening as it was shocking. All you heard or read about for the entire time they were together was every little detail about their relationship and how much they loved each other. However, things took a serious turn for the worse when Tom’s obsession Scientology made things messy between the couple. We feel bad for Katie and Suri, and hope daddy has started to turn things around since.

## 7. Arnold Schwarzenegger and Maria Schriver

![Arnold Schwarzenegger and Maria Schriver](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0i/w9/ld/w9abf6uf_600x376.jpg)

Really? A love child with the housekeeper? This one’s both heartbreaking and sickening, and we totally feel for Maria and how awful that news must have been. The two always seemed like a power couple, so the news of this shocking affair definitely pulled at the heart strings. The grass isn’t always greener, fellas.

It’s easy to get caught up in the Hollywood gossip, but these splits are completely real and devastating to the individuals involved. It’s important to step back and think how the couples and their families must be feeling in light of their break-up. These splits were totally heart-wrenching for us and still difficult to get over! Are there any celebrity splits that completely shocked you? Please share in the comments below!
