# Actor Re\-Creates Other Celeb's Looks, is Generally Hilarious ...

You may remember actor Tom Lenk for his recurring role on Buffy, and if you've missed him, know that he's been busy creating something new (and hilarious). Lenk's been using household items to re-create other celeb's looks, and it's really, really funny. He calls these low-budget recreations LENKLEWKS... let's have a peek!

## 1. Cate at the Tony Awards

![clothing, tuxedo, dress, flooring, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/as/gk/f8i1svmk576826530ace0519601640_600x600.jpg)

To get Cate's look for less, all you need is trash bags, duct tape, some non-slipshelf liners, and a "sensible smokey eye."

## 5. Rihanna at Billboard Music Awards

![Las Vegas, clothing, jacket, formal wear, suit,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p9/lx/ytt6kb5457682654846dc768246853_600x600.jpg)

How does Lenk get this look? He takes a barely-worn blazer from his closet, turns it inside out, adds a wig and some makeup, and you can hardly tell the difference between him and Riri.
