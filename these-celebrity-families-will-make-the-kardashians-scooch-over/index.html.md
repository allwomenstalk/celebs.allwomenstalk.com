# These Celebrity Families Will Make the Kardashians Scooch over ...

I don’t know what it is, but lately I’ve been obsessed with all of the cutest families in Hollywood. Whether they’re newly formed or decades old, these families are some of the cutest and most powerful in all of Hollywood, and who knows, they may just become the next Kardashian clan one day!

## 1. The Lively-Reynolds Clan

![The Lively-Reynolds Clan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/am/rn/cbmf9iqr558bd985815a6670718013_600x900.jpg)

Although they’re currently a small family of just three, Blake Lively, Ryan Reynolds, and their assumedly adorable daughter James are easily one of the cutest families in Hollywood. Even though we haven’t seen the baby’s face yet, every outing they’ve had together has been more adorable than the last. Blake and Ryan have both said that they plan to have a big family, so it’s only a matter of time before their brood starts expanding again!

## 2. The Windsors

![The Windsors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fi/p4/o7g018tk558bd9f5a3496268475290_600x774.jpg)

Is there any family as cute as the Royal Family, especially with the recent new addition of Charlotte? William and Kate were a gorgeous couple on their own, and George is easily one of the cutest royal toddlers ever, plus Charlotte, and we have one of the cutest royal families to ever grace the UK!

## 3. The Carters

![The Carters](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nj/s2/lcteyv5f558bda1404909243096772_467x610.jpg)

I know that I’m not the only one hoping everyday that Beyoncé makes a pregnancy announcement sooner rather than later. Beyoncé, Jay-Z, and Blue are so adorable, and talk about powerful. Blue Ivy was featured on her dad’s song mere days after she was born, and became the youngest person on the Billboard charts. Oh, yeah, and her parents are Beyoncé and Jay-Z, which makes them pretty powerful too!

## 4. Taylor Swift’s Family

Taylor Swift already gets a lot of attention for being one of the coolest celebrities, like ever (no pun intended), but her family is just as cool. Her brother just graduated from Notre Dame, and she’s one of the biggest singer/songwriters of our generation, but they still know how to have fun and act like kids with each other (just check out Taylor’s Easter Instagram posts). And don’t even get me started on her parents, who can be found at nearly all of her concerts, handing out guitar picks and meet and greets. When it comes to Taylor Swift, it really is a family affair!

## 5. The Beckhams

The Beckham family may go down as one of the most chic families in Hollywood history. Although neither parent began with a career in fashion, they now are both heavily involved in the fashion industry. Victoria has her own clothing line, and David has become fairly well-known for his underwear ads, even starting his own line with H&M. Their kids are just as style-conscious: Brooklyn has recently landed his first fashion campaign, and their youngest, Harper, is one of the most stylish toddlers ever.

## 6. The New Jonas Family

![The New Jonas Family](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k3/aj/xpca5kbs558bdca7944d4900308213_600x800.jpg)

When you think of the Jonas family, you probably think of the family that brought us Kevin, Joe, and Nick. While they will forever be a classic Hollywood family, Kevin’s new family with his wife and daughter are quickly making their way into the hearts of millions. Not only are they a gorgeous couple, but their daughter Alena is so cute that you might accidentally spend an hour or two perusing their Instagram accounts looking for videos. Don’t say I didn’t warn you!

## 7. The Kunis-Kutcher Family

Fans of That 70s Show always hoped that Jackie and Kelso would end up together, and that wish came true off-screen when the pair began dating in 2012. Today, they are proud parents to a baby girl named Wyatt, and although we don’t see much of them anymore, when we do, the way they gush about their daughter is too adorable for words.

Who are your favorite celebrity families? Who did I miss on this list? Let me know your picks in the comments!
