Johnny Depp and Vanessa Paradis have been together for 12 years and are still not married. The couple even have two kids together and yet Johnny tells Extra “I never found myself needing that piece of paper”.

"Marriage is really from soul to soul, heart to heart. You don't need somebody to say, okay you're married."
