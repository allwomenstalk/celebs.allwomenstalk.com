# 7 Celebrities Who Are Married to Non Celebrities ...

Believe it or not, there are so many celebrities who are married to non celebrities. I think it makes things so much easier for them to try and keep a "private" life. Some of these celebrities even met their significant other before their famous lives. How cool is that? Check out some of the celebrities who are married to non celebrities and then let me know if you were shocked by any of these!

## 1. Matt Damon

![Matt Damon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bi/cp/sfwx9ywk_400x560.jpg)

Matt Damon met his future wife, Luciana Barroso (a former bartender), back in 2003. Two years later, the couple got married. In the August 2013 issue of Esquire, Matt talked about how being married to someone who's not in show business allows him to stay private. The couple renewed their vows on the island of St. Lucia in early 2013 with their four daughters in attendance. Talk about a happy ending! He's just one of many celebrities who are married to non celebrities.

## 2. Patrick Dempsey

![Patrick Dempsey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d0/dg/birth2f1_400x560.jpg)

Was it fate when Grey's Anatomy star Patrick Dempsey walked into his future wife's hair salon back in 1994? Jillian Fink Dempsey is a well-known makeup artist and married the hunky actor back in 1999. The two have three children together. They are such a cute (and incredibly good-looking) family!

## 3. Elizabeth Banks

![Elizabeth Banks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/im/rp/ammxfflf_416x594.jpg)

Hunger Games actress Elizabeth Banks and her husband Max Handelman met in college, back in 1992. Yup, college sweethearts! She married the sportswriter/producer in 2003. Their first son, Felix, was born via surrogate in 2011, followed by brother Magnus in 2012.

## 4. Reese Witherspoon

![Reese Witherspoon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/np/tc/y8vb9t9z_600x825.jpg)

Reese Witherspoon made major headlines when she split with first husband Ryan Phillippe after a seven-year marriage back in 2007. The two agreed to joint custody of their daughter Ava and son Deacon. In 2011, Reese got remarried to someone with a much lower public profile. Her new beau is talent agent Jim Toth. They welcomed a baby boy, Tennessee James, in 2012.

## 5. Seth Rogen

![Seth Rogen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vy/lu/qxsooyhr_600x800.jpg)

Funny-man Seth Rogen met his wife Lauren Miller back in 2004. The two started dating back then when they both worked on "Da Ali G Show." They got married in 2011! Fun fact: Miller has acted in several small parts, including minor roles in some of Rogen's films. Whoa! She also co-wrote the comedy "For a Good Time, Call..." and sold it to Focus Features for a whopping $2 million.

## 6. Vince Vaughn

![Vince Vaughn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/cn/sv5zxl94_600x857.jpg)

Vince Vaughn married Kyla Weber, a Canadian realtor, back in 2010 in Illinois. Six months later, they announced Kyla was pregnant with daughter Locklyn. Their son, Vernon, was born in 2013. The family now lives a quiet and happy life in in Southern California.

## 7. Julia Roberts

![Julia Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ol/cw/u7vno9y6_600x969.jpg)

Pretty Woman's Julia Roberts married her second (and current) husband Danny Moder back in 2002 in New Mexico. She met him on the set of her movie, The Mexican, in 2000. Things didn't work out then because he was married and she was dating actor Benjamin Bratt. Eventually though, two pair recognized their connection with one another and became a couple. They now have three children!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many celebrities who have married non celebrities. I think it's fascinating to see how/where all of these relationships started! Of the list, which couple do you love together most?
