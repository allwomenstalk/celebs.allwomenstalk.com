# 7 Celebs Who Drastically Lost Weight for Roles ...

Celebrities who lost weight for movie roles really took their craft to the extreme. With many celebrities already put under the pump when it comes to body image issues, having to lose weight for a role can lead to quite drastic measures. From extreme dieting to intense work out regimes, many celebrities have undergone significant body transformations just to get into character. The following are just a few examples of celebrities losing weight for movie roles.

## 1. Natalie Portman

![Natalie Portman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/08/12_490x767.jpg)

It was reported that Natalie Portman lost 20 pounds for her role as a professional ballet dancer in the movie “Black Swan”. Taking into consideration her already petite frame, no wonder it became a cause of concern for many critics.

## 2. Renee Zellweger

![Renee Zellweger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/08/22_900x609.jpg)

Renee Zellweger is an example of celebrities who lost weight and gained weight for movie roles. After gaining nearly 30 pounds for her role in the “Bridget Jones’s Diary”, Renee Zellweger then had to shed the pounds quite rapidly to play an extremely skinny Roxie Hart in the movie musical “Chicago”.

## 3. Tom Hanks

![Tom Hanks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/08/32_968x1290.jpg)

It’s not always a case of the women celebrities who lost weight for movie roles. For his role in “Castaway” Tom Hanks lost 50 pounds to achieve the starved figure required for the part.

## 4. Beyonce

![Beyonce](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/08/4_2060x3090.jpg)

Beyonce is well known for her curvy, ‘bootylicious’ figure. However, when she took on a role in the movie “Dreamgirls” she shed approximately 20 pounds. It was reported she lost the weight on a two week fast. Thankfully, she put some of the weight back on immediately after filming wrapped.

## 5. Christian Bale

![Christian Bale](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/08/52_440x707.jpg)

For his role as an insomniac in the movie “The Machinist”, Christian Bale reportedly resorted to living on a cup of coffee and an apple a day to lose weight. Celebrities who lost weight often did so through extreme measures and you don’t have to be a nutritionist to realise that this is taking weight loss to unhealthy extremes.

## 6. Anne Hathaway

![Anne Hathaway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/08/62_881x1070.jpg)

One of the most recent examples of celebrities who lost weight for movie roles is Anne Hathaway. She was reportedly asked to drop 16 pounds for her role as Fantine in the movie adaptation of “Les Misérables”. This isn’t the first time either, as she has also dropped weight for “The Devil Wears Prada” and “The Dark Knight Rises”.

## 7. Emily Blunt

![Emily Blunt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2012/08/71_801x1066.jpg)

Emily Blunt was encouraged to lose weight for her role as the weight-obsessed Emily in “The Devil Wears Prada”. She also underwent transformation for “The Adjustment Bureau” where she trained for almost four hours a day prior to filming to get into the shape of a professional dancer.
