# 11 Celebrities Wearing Flared Skirts  Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities wearing flared skirts are on to one of the hottest trends right now. Flared skirts have dominated the catwalks lately and it’s a trend that just as easily translates into your real life wardrobe. These skirts can come in varying designs but the key similarity is that they all flare out towards the hem – just how much depends on what kind of look you’re after! Take a cue from these celebrities wearing flared skirts and incorporate this flirty and feminine style into your wardrobe.

## 1. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/1_kate-bosworth_550x1100.jpg)

First up on our list of celebrities wearing flared skirts is perennial trendsetter Kate Bosworth. Her fit and flare skirt features exaggerated flaring at the hem. It comes in an icy blue hue with a slight metallic sheen. She teams it with a silver and white top, silver pointed pumps, and a bright red lip.

## 2. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/2_olivia-palermo_550x1100.jpg)

Olivia Palermo works a polished daytime look in a white flared mini skirt. She teams it with an on-trend snow leopard blouse, embellished belt, and nude cut-out heels. A bright blue handbag and oversized sunglasses completes the look.

## 3. Hailee Steinfeld

![Hailee Steinfeld](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/3_hailee-steinfeld_550x1100.jpg)

Youngster Hailee Steinfeld was seen sporting this eye-catching ensemble. She mixes and matches prints with a long sleeved top and fit and flare skirt. She completes her outfit with a pair of pale blue ankle strap heels.

## 4. Emmy Rossum

![Emmy Rossum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/4_emmy-rossum_550x1100.jpg)

Emmy Rossum goes for a more preppy look in her Carven get-up. She teams her flippy flared skirt with a collared shirt and matching printed jacket. A light blue handbag and orange heels are the finishing touches to this colourful look.

## 5. Jordana Brewster

![Jordana Brewster](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/5_jordana-brewster_550x1100.jpg)

Jordana Brewster sticks to a basic colour palette of black and white. She teams her spotty mini skirt with a semi-sheer black blouse and pointed black pumps. A boxy clutch and pendant necklace are her accessories of choice.

## 6. Jennifer Hudson

![Jennifer Hudson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/6_jennifer-hudson_550x1100.jpg)

Jennifer Hudson goes all #fashun on us in this Versace flared skirt. She teams the embossed leather skirt with a semi-sheer turtleneck top, bright orange coat, and knee-length boots.

## 7. Gwyneth Paltrow

![Gwyneth Paltrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/7_gwyneth-paltrow_550x1100.jpg)

All white outfits are certainly a big trend this season. Here, Gwyneth Paltrow teams a flared white mini skirt with a white knit top and bright coloured pumps. A compact quilted handbag and a set of stacked bangles completes this crisp, mainly white outfit.

## 8. Arizona Muse

![Arizona Muse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/8_arizona-muse_592x888.jpg)

Model Arizona Muse knows how to work a trend or two. Here she teams a preppy collared top with a flared leather skirt and knee high black boots. The outfit is a bit Blair Waldorf meets model-off-duty.

## 9. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/9_miranda-kerr_592x888.jpg)

Miranda Kerr Works a more demure look in this ankle-length coral skirt. The flare is much more subtle and because of the length and fabric the skirt has a draped effect. Miranda teams her skirt with a two-tone top, black peep-toe heels, and a tiny clutch bag.

## 10. Rooney Mara

![Rooney Mara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/10_rooney-mara_592x888.jpg)

Rooney Mara’s flared skirt has a subtle structured design. It looks like it could be the perfect versatile black skirt! She goes for a contemporary look and teams her skirt with grey crop top and strappy black heels.

## 11. Maria Sharapova

![Maria Sharapova](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/07/11_maria-sharapova_550x1100.jpg)

Tennis ace Maria Sharapova shows off her pins in this flippy print skirt by Zimmermann. The tropical print on the skirt makes it the perfect summertime option. She teams it with a short sleeved blouse and maroon heels.

As these celebrities show us, there are plenty of ways to style a flared skirt this season. Choose a soft flare for a subtle take on the trend or be bold with a more structured, out-there flared design. Which of these celebrity takes on the flared skirt do you like best?
