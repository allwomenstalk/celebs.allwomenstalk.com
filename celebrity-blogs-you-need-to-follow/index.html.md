# 7 Celebrity Blogs You Need to Follow ...

When I say "[celebrity blogs](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-best-celebrity-blogs/)" you may be thinking of the standard gossip sites, but no ladies, I'm talking about blogs made by celebrities themselves. It's really cool to take a glimpse into their lives through their webpages, and even learn a thing or two from their tips and "how to's" in the process. When we read gossip magazines and watch "celebrity news" shows, the content is often times hearsay, but these blogs are the real deal. Take a look at some of the best celebrity blogs out there:

## 1. Beyonce's Tumblr "I Am"

![Beyonce's Tumblr "I Am"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qa/vy/ptdoi9lo_600x800.jpg)

It's inevitable that something created by Queen Bey herself would be one of the greatest celebrity blogs out there. The pictures featured on the blog are cute and candid and give you a glimpse behind the scenes of her fabulous tours, as well as her life in general. She even has a video diary from her Mrs. Carter tour, which is definitely worth watching. See it here at: [iam.beyonce.com](https://iam.beyonce.com/tagged/tour).

## 2. Olivia Palermo's Style Blog

_ This newlywed socialite is a complete style maven. Her look is always polished and pretty, and lucky for us readers, we can learn her ways through her personal website. You can even shop her boutique and get some of her hand-picked pieces. See it here at: oliviapalermo.com_.

## 3. Dianna Agron's Tumblr "You, Me, and Charlie"

![Dianna Agron's Tumblr "You, Me, and Charlie"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cu/g5/bmip6lkg_600x869.jpg)

While travelling in Australia promoting Glee, Dianna created this Tumblr to post pictures of books and music she was listening to. The site is full of obscure pictures and artsy portrayals of ideas and quotes. She came up with the name because she has always liked the name Charlie, and because a psychic once told her that someone named Charlie is looking out for her. See it here at: [youmeandcharlie.tumblr.com](https://youmeandcharlie.tumblr.com/archive).

## 4. Lady Gaga's Website "Little Monsters"

![Lady Gaga's Website "Little Monsters"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hu/lg/ki00apct_600x833.jpg)

Littlemonsters.com is a place where Gaga's faithful fans can share their own original content with each other, and also view Gaga's personal videos and pictures. You need to sign up and become a member to access the site, but don't even think about "trolling" other members since the site is a safe space for her monsters to kindly share and support one another.

## 5. Katy Perry's Tumblr

![Katy Perry's Tumblr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uf/rg/tt3w2z56_600x733.jpg)

Under the headline of "Kitty Purry's Blog", Katy has created a space where she can share her wit and witticisms, pictures, and videos. Don't expect to see anything boring or basic here - the content truly reflects Katy's vivacious personality.

## 6. Jay Z's "Life and Times" Website

![Jay Z's "Life and Times" Website](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/my/nn4jdmxi_600x790.jpg)

Lifeandtimes.com features tour pictures and videos, as well as throwbacks and celebrity cameos. Jordan Dunn has recently been featured on the site with videos of her cooking channel "Well Dunn".

## 7. Zooey Deschanel's Site "Hello Giggles"

![Zooey Deschanel's Site "Hello Giggles"](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w9/wi/b5uu5vvo_600x833.jpg)

Hellogiggles.com is a space for women to write fun, witty, inspirational content. You can actually click the "contribute" tab and sign up to write your own pieces to be featured on the site! You can also purchase Hello Giggles swag on the site, and a new shop is coming soon with even more Zooey-inspired threads. The site is just as cute and quirky as you'd expect it to be.

140 characters on Twitter can only tell so much about a celebrity, but these blogs are truly the portal to a star's life. These featured blogs are really fun and interesting and really allow the creator's personality to shine through. Do you have a favorite blog created by a celebrity?
