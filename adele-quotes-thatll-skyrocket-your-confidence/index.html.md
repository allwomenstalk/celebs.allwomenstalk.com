# 10 Adele Quotes Thatll Skyrocket Your Confidence ...

Adele is so great at speaking her mind and telling it like it is. I always look forward to hearing or reading interviews from her because I know that she’s going to drop some wisdom on me, or at the very least, make me laugh. If you need a confidence boost, a laugh, or someone to talk some sense into you, this list has got you covered. These quotes that she’s said will boost your confidence immensely, and who doesn’t think that Adele inspires them? Don’t we all just want to be inspired by Adele? I know that I do!

## 1. Don’t Be Afraid of Anyone

![Don’t Be Afraid of Anyone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uq/wz/lragx0md569656ba72c9c278946535_492x634.jpg)

You should not be afraid of anyone, especially people who tell you that you can’t do something. This quote from Adele is so great, because sometimes we all just need that little boost of confidence to keep going. This is a great quote for the times when you just need a little boost!

## 2. Learn to Love Again

![Learn to Love Again](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/me/z7x1a7vm569b82fda187b805990926_540x540.jpg)

Isn’t it funny that when we have our hearts broken, we hate love and never want anything to do with it again? But as we start to fall in love we completely forget what all of that heartbreak felt like. Adele sums that up so beautifully in this quote, even if, like she said, it seems so obvious.

## 3. Be Brave and Fearless

![Be Brave and Fearless](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ym/sr/el1s42if569b817ad9a50100303715_600x1001.jpg)

I think this is such a great quote for the people out there who (like myself) worry that they’re constantly making the wrong decision. Sometimes it’s so important to remind ourselves that even if the choice we’re making is the wrong decision, at this current place in our lives, we’re making it for a good reason.

## 4. Stop Chasing People

![Stop Chasing People](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cn/do/vrodvunx569b8152774e0049920739_600x600.jpg)

We spend so much of our youth chasing people. Why? We should be so much more important to ourselves than people who won’t stay. Invest time in yourself, not in those people that you spend so much time chasing. It’s a tough concept to put into practice, but it will make your life so much more carefree.

## 5. You Get What You Put in

![You Get What You Put in](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tx/uc/xby2vvey569656bb65007475163642_500x500.jpg)

A lot of the time, some of us expect for good things to be handed to us. There’s so much more to life and success than that, but you have to put in the time and work to make those things happen for you. Who else would know about putting time and energy into your life than Adele?

## 6. Your Career is a Hobby

![Your Career is a Hobby](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wt/aa/vv3diy2e569656bb63dff655845889_564x662.jpg)

Don’t you think this is so rare for a celebrity to say? We’re used to celebrities being complete workaholics when it comes to their career. I love that she’s able to differentiate between her life and her career, especially now that she’s a mother who wants to be with her child just as much as she wants her career.

## 7. Don’t Hang out with Those People

![Don’t Hang out with Those People](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wh/c5/cst6yspn569b811c8e1ea027312363_600x600.jpg)

This is so, so important. The older you get, the more you realize that you don’t need negative influences in your life. If you’re still hanging out with people who mess with your mental health, get rid of them. Your happiness is so much more important those negative influences!

## 8. Self IMPROVEMENTS

![hair, face, eyebrow, hairstyle, forehead,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j3/x2/kzpfv63k589209025416f354850600_600x429.jpg)

It's always important to want to grow, but like Adele says, be sure it comes from a good place and not a place of negativity or unhappiness.

## 9. Confidence

![hair, red, beauty, hairstyle, brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hj/u2/ct2jcdbo58922d8cddd2b301877221_600x748.jpg)

It's a highly unnoticed trait, however, confidence is huge! When you have confidence no matter the aspect of life it's in-career, relationship, self-you will be able to achieve anything you set your mind to.

## 10. You're a Lady

![hair, face, blond, nose, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fl/ls/x7kaul2w58922da577324204456093_600x450.jpg)

I LOVE this quote! It's so simple, funny, yet so deeply true! Don't ever let a man demean you. Don't let him make you feel less than you are and give you less than you deserve!
