# 7 Great Street Style Looks from Whitney Port ...

There are so many fantastic street style looks from Whitney Port. She's proven time and time again that she's all about class and sophistication. Even when she's sporting a totally casual look, Whitney always looks girly and put-together. Check out some of awesome street style looks from Whitney Port that are totally worthy of recreating this summer!

## 1. Mix and Match

![Mix and Match](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/au/fg/t8pajkfh_500x800.jpg)

Every fashionista knows that mixing and matching patterns can lead to the most adorable outfits! Whether you're mixing animal prints, graphic prints, or just vibrant colors, it can help take your outfit to the next level. Remember, it's all about confidence. Your outfit doesn't necessarily need to 'match'; all the pieces just need to go together. Whitney's a pro at looking fab while mixing fun prints! This is just one of the awesome street style looks from Whitney Port.

## 2. Casual

![Casual](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k8/xu/epk5nq1v_592x888.jpg)

Even while looking super casual, Whitney manages to look super stylish. To recreate this look, pair your black skinnies (or leggings) with some flats and a basic tee. It's the perfect outfit to run errands in while still looking put-together. You can choose to wear your hair down and natural or rock a high pony-tail or bun!

## 3. Crop Top + Patterned Pants

![Crop Top + Patterned Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vp/f0/hynw4qkw_592x888.jpg)

Patterned pants are a summer must have! You can find tons of adorable ones on [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/WG3Pe4). It's great because you can pair them with a black or white crop top! Throw on your oversized shades and you're good to go. What kind of patterned pants do you like?

## 4. Boyfriend Jeans

![Boyfriend Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ay/y1/u4hf03r3_500x750.jpg)

There are tons of cool ways to style boyfriend jeans. You can opt for the whole oversized-everything look or pair your baggy jeans with a form fitting top. That could easily change the vibe of the outfit and turn casual into chic.

## 5. Flannel

![Flannel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sg/zr/rdbbctk3_592x888.jpg)

Turns out whenever someone's NOT wearing a flannel around their waist, they're actually actually wearing it as a shirt. Who would've thought?! LOL! I think a flannel button-up is perfect for the summertime. It's a great addition to a tank top + shorts outfit because it'll keep you feeling 'too exposed' and whatnot. Plus, it adds a bit of color to your outfit, which you can never go wrong with.

## 6. Dress + Converse

![Dress + Converse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k9/nd/x0anl862_592x888.jpg)

A cool way to add some edge to a girly outfit is to pair your dress with some converse or combat boots. I think it gives an outfit an 'effortlessly cool' vibe - which is why Whitney always looks so awesome! You could also swap the dress for a graphic tee and skater skirt.

## 7. Colorful

![Colorful](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j3/my/l9i8nklo_424x650.jpg)

Since summer's all about fun colors, what better way to celebrate the season than a multi-colored dress?! You can find one on [aliexpress.com](http://www.aliexpress.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) that'll be perfect for your summer errands. The colors are really expressive and definitely serve as a statement piece for your outfit. Try and keep the accessories to a minimum though - I think the dress will already call enough attention!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of the many delightful street style looks from Whitney Port. This style guru always looks effortlessly chic and has become a fashion inspiration to us all! Of the list, which look was your favorite? Which look will you be trying to recreate this summer?
