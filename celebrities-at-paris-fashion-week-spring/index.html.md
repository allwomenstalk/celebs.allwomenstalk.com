# Celebrities at Paris Fashion Week Spring 2015 ...

Sigh. If only we could all make our way to Paris for fashion week... right, ladies?! That'd be amazing. But, until then, we're just going to have to live vicariously through these celebrities. Check out some of the big names who were in attendance at Paris Fashion Week Spring 2015:

## 1. MIRANDA KERR, ODEYA RUSH & SELENA GOMEZ

![MIRANDA KERR, ODEYA RUSH & SELENA GOMEZ](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yx/cd/dqlqk8vw_600x969.jpg)

These gorgeous ladies posed together at the Louis Vuitton show.

## 2. Michelle Williams & Jennifer Connelly

![Michelle Williams & Jennifer Connelly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jg/mc/dyf3n1pn_600x969.jpg)

These two actresses were all smiles sitting front row!

## 3. Shailene Woodley

![Shailene Woodley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wq/oj/lda034kv_600x969.jpg)

Before heading to the Miu Miu show, Shailene posed for some photos, debuting some lighter blonde hues.

## 4. Cara Delevingne

![Cara Delevingne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jf/qb/t75rjh1v_600x969.jpg)

## 5. Justin Bieber

![Justin Bieber](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gr/z6/gz5i8vqe_600x969.jpg)

Justin was spotted shopping in between fashion shows!

## 6. Ciara

![Ciara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h3/ti/uua8kttd_600x969.jpg)

Ciara looked stunning in red lace at the Valentino show. I love the draped coat!

## 7. Salma Hayek & Lenny Kravitz

![Salma Hayek & Lenny Kravitz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n3/io/jwzrpm0r_600x969.jpg)

These two A-listers looked super chic at the Saint Laurent show.

## 8. Kendall Jenner

![Kendall Jenner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wq/ux/d7ccyywh_600x969.jpg)
