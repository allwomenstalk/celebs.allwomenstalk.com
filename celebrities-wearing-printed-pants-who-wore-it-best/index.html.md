# 10 Celebrities Wearing Printed Pants  Who Wore It Best?

There are plenty of celebrities wearing printed pants these days. Printed pants have been one of the top trends lately and they can be styled in all sorts of different ways. When paired with a blazer and heels they can look red carpet-worthy, or they can be dressed down with a loose tank top and sandals. Either way, they’re an easy way to add some statement-making prints to an outfit. Take a look at these celebrities wearing printed pants and let us know who you think wore theirs the best.

## 1. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/1_jessica-alba_592x888.jpg)

As far as celebrities wearing printed pants go, Jessica Alba has been seen sporting them on more than one occasion. It’s safe to say that she’s a fan of the trend. Here she works a chic monochrome look in her floral printed pants. She teams them with a white blouse, sleek leather jacket, white pointed pumps, and two-tone handbag.

## 2. Sienna Miller

![Sienna Miller](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/2_sienna-miller_592x888.jpg)

Sienna Miller works a casually tailored look in a pair of black printed pants. She wears her printed pants with a white collared blouse, black cardigan, and aviator-style shades. The red ballet flats add a pop of colour to the overall look.

## 3. Kristen Bell

![Kristen Bell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/3_kristen-bell_592x888.jpg)

Here, Kristen Bell works a completely blue and white outfit. She teams her blue and white printed pants with a spotty blue top and blue peep-toe heels. She keeps the jewellery to a bare minimum and sports just another hint of colour in the form of bright pink nail polish.

## 4. Anne Hathaway

![Anne Hathaway](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/4_anne-hathaway_592x888.jpg)

A pre-haircut Anne Hathaway opts for a wide-legged style when wearing printed pants. She goes for a print on print look by teaming her geometric printed pants with a striped blouse. She accessorises with assorted gold jewellery and bold geometric sunglasses.

## 5. Christina Hendricks

![Christina Hendricks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/5_christina-hendricks_592x888.jpg)

Christina Hendricks works a ladylike look in her rose-print pants. She teams them with a white blouse with an embroidered collar, black pumps, and a black clutch. The printed pants are quite eye-catching and have the potential to seem dowdy but the trick is to keep the look fitted and tailored.

## 6. Kristen Stewart

![Kristen Stewart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/6_kristen-stewart_392x594.jpg)

Here, Kristen Stewart steps out in some bright yellow printed pants. They come in a slim fit with zippered ankles and an intricate green pattern throughout. Since the pants are quite statement making she keeps the rest of her look fairly simple with a white tee, black blazer, and black heels.

## 7. Ashley Greene

![Ashley Greene](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/7_ashley-greene_592x888.jpg)

Ashley Greene stays on trend in a pair of printed pants while running errands. She works a relaxed look in a pair of grey and white printed pants. She teams them with matching two-tone top, black ballet flats, and rounded sunglasses.

## 8. Jordana Brewster

![Jordana Brewster](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/8_jordana-brewster_481x724.jpg)

Here, Jordana Brewster teams her monochrome floral printed pants with a black top and puffer-style coat. The addition of pink printed heels adds an extra pop of colour to the outfit.

## 9. Vanessa Hudgens

![Vanessa Hudgens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/9_vanessa-hudgens_592x888.jpg)

In keeping with her style, Vanessa Hudgens has teamed her printed pants with an assortment of boho-inspired accessories. She keeps the clothing fairly simple with her multicoloured printed pants and white top. The long metallic disc necklace, embellished cardigan, and low-slung bag all add some luxe touches to the outfit.

## 10. Carly Rae Jepsen

![Carly Rae Jepsen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/10_carly-rae-jepsen_592x888.jpg)

Carly Rae Jepsen teams her black and green printed pants with a sheer shirt, strappy platform heels, and a white tasselled clutch. Hints of green are also present throughout the outfit in the form of green piping on the shirt and peeking through on her shoe insoles.

These are just a few celebrities who have been seen sporting printed pants. Geometric and floral prints seem to be the most popular choices, but abstract prints are also a stylish option. Which of these celebrity looks do you like best?
