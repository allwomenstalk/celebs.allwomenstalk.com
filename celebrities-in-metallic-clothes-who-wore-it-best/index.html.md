# 10 Celebrities in Metallic Clothes – Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities in metallics are a pretty common sight. The metallic trend is as popular as ever and plenty of celebrities are embracing metallic looks for the red carpet. It’s no wonder, either, as metallic clothes and the glitz and glamour of the red carpet seem to go hand in hand. Check out these celebrities in metallics and let us know who you think wore it best.

## 1. Jessica Biel

![Jessica Biel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/1_jessica-biel_550x1100.jpg)

While you might expect most celebrities in metallics to be wearing glamorous dresses, Jessica Biel opts for something a bit different with this ensemble. She went for a blinged out look, teaming her metallic Oscar de la Renta tunic and pants with bejewelled sandals, stacked bracelets, and statement earrings.

## 2. Michelle Williams

![Michelle Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/2_michelle-williams_550x1100.jpg)

Michelle Williams rocks a black and gold Jason Wu dress to a premiere of ‘Oz the Great and Powerful.’ Since the dress is quite glitzy, she keeps her accessories fairly simple. The belt worn around the waist is a smart way of breaking up the dress and creating a svelte silhouette.

## 3. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/3_miranda-kerr_550x1100.jpg)

Miranda Kerr shone brightly in this bronze mini dress. She accessorised the structured metallic dress with black ankle strap heels and a slim collar necklace. The high bun hairstyle gives the entire outfit a youthful feel.

## 4. Gabrielle Union

![Gabrielle Union](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/4_gabrielle-union_550x1100.jpg)

Gabrielle Union looks set to party in this metallic mini dress. She teamed her contoured dress with simple peep toe pumps and dangly statement earrings. It’s definitely an outfit fit for dancing the night away.

## 5. Nicole Richie

![Nicole Richie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/5_nicole-richie_550x1100.jpg)

Nicole Richie goes glam in this floor length gown by Lorena Sarbu. The cream gown features an all over triangle metallic beaded pattern. She teams her dress with a compact clutch and mixed jewellery. The relaxed finger waves in her hair give the entire look a 1920s Great Gatsby inspired feel.

## 6. Blake Lively

![Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/6_blake-lively_550x1100.jpg)

As one of the faces of Chanel, it was quite fitting that Blake Lively wore a Chanel dress to a Chanel Event. The silver dress had a plunging neckline and featured bejewelled details. She paired her metallic dress with equally metallic Christian Louboutin pumps.

## 7. Heidi Klum

![Heidi Klum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/7_heidi-klum_550x1100.jpg)

Heidi Klum dazzles in this sequined silver tube dress. She teams her metallic dress with silver strappy sandals – jewellery is hardly required as the dress itself is so sparkly! The red nail polish adds a touch of colour to the entire look.

## 8. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/8_kate-bosworth_550x1100.jpg)

Here, Kate Bosworth wears a shimmery strapless sequin sheath dress by Stella McCartney (try saying that ten times really quickly). The multicoloured effect of the sequins is quite mesmerising, and she teams the dress with a gold clutch and bejewelled sandals.

## 9. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/9_olivia-palermo_550x1100.jpg)

Olivia Palermo shows us how she mixes and matches her metallics with this glitzy ensemble. She wears a fringed gold vest with a metallic lace pencil skirt. She completes the outfit with basic pumps, compact clutch, and metallic bracelet.

## 10. Nikki Reed

![Nikki Reed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/03/10_nikki-reed_550x1100.jpg)

Nikki Reed shines in this gold Michael Kors dress. It features an embellished finish and cutout details to the sides. She pairs her dress with simple strappy heels and a minimal black clutch.

Whether it’s a shimmying mini dress or a floor-length gown, the metallic trend can be worn in a range of different ways. These celebrities show us that a glitzy metallic outfit is the way to go. Which of these celebrities do you think rocks the metallic trend the best?
