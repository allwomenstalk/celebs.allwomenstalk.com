# 5 Brilliant Quotes from Marilyn Monroe  about Life ...

**Quotes from Marilyn Monroe** should be a part of any girl's life. We can all take some useful advice from Marilyn. Being the elegant, sassy, wise and witty woman she was, she definitely had some good quotes in her lifetime.

## 1. “Fear is Stupid. so Are Regrets.”

Here's one of my favorite **quotes from Marilyn Monore**. Have you ever felt like you were missing out on something great because of a certain fear? Or the fear was holding you back from making your dreams come true? Then let that fear go. Realize that the fear is all in your mind. You made it up. Let yourself free from it. You are strong and deserve much more. Speaking of letting go, never regret something that you’ve already done. It is gone. You can't take it back and you can't change it. Take it easy and move on.

## 2. “if It Doesn’t Fit, It’s Ugly Anyway.”

A great reminder for girls out there who have confidence issues. Just remember your body is never bigger, never not enough and never less important. Your body is perfect just the way it is. Every pot has its lid. Messing with the lids doesn’t make the pot ugly. You can find a lot of options that will emphasize your beauty.

## 3. “I Restore Myself when I’m Alone.”

A very important statement. Always take time for yourself. Sometimes it just gets to be a little bit too much and you lose your path. Or, you get really exhausted. Make a cup of tea, sit down and reflect on yourself. Think about your actions, your behavior, the words you use, the actions you choose. Restore yourself. Recharge yourself. Don’t forget who you are.

## 4. “Always Be Yourself. Retain Individuality, Listen to the Truest Part of Yourself.”

We've all lost ourselves once or maybe twice in our lives. Feeding your personality will help you keep it from happening again. A lot of people don’t know who they are and who they want to be. That’s why they get lost a lot. Figure out who you are and work on your best traits.

## 5. “Always, Always, Always Believe in Yourself. Because if You Don’t, then Who Will, Sweetie?''

Be confident in what you want, what you are doing, and what your future holds. No matter what, carry on chasing your dreams, even if everyone's trying to convince you to stop. Be your number one fan.
