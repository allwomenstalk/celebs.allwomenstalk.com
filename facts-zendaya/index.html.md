## 1. Her Full Name

Zendaya’s moniker isn’t just a cool-sounding name; it carries depth and heritage. Zendaya Maree Stoermer Coleman may be a mouthful, but each part has its story. 'Zendaya' means 'to give thanks' in the language of the Shona people, an homage to her African American father’s roots. Her middle name, 'Maree', is a twist on her German-American mother’s middle name, 'Marie'. As for 'Stoermer Coleman'? Well, that represents the union of two family names. It’s like Zendaya herself: eclectic, thoughtful, and captivating-just another colorful thread in the tapestry of her unique personal narrative. And if you think that's interesting, wait until we dive into her [multiracial heritage](#2) and how it’s influenced her journey.
