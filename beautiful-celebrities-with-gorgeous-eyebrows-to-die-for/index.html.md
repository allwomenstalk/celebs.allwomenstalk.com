# 7 Beautiful Celebrities with Gorgeous Eyebrows to Die for ...

The list of celebrities with gorgeous eyebrows is endless. Some of our favorite stars sport impeccably groomed eyebrows with stunning arches, while others take a chance and bring about more of a bold look. One of the most important things that you should prioritize when doing your make up besides achieving flawless skin, is taking care of your eyebrows. Eyebrows are the key to completing your look since they frame your eyes and define the face. If you are looking for some inspiration in the eyebrow department, here are 7 beautiful celebrities with gorgeous eyebrows to die for!

## 1. Cara Delevigne

![Cara Delevigne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30191100_5738_600x806.jpg)

Cara Delevigne without a doubt sets the criteria for some of the best eyebrows in the industry. How lucky is she to be born with some of the most prominent, strong and luscious eyebrows? They are one of the most striking characteristics of her face that give her a sexy and "dangerous to know" appearance. Everybody has been trying to achieve her unique look; in fact one of the most searched things about her are her eyebrows! The girl is a trendsetter and she is definitely at the top of the celebrities with gorgeous eyebrows list.

## 2. Lily Collins

![Lily Collins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30190651_6209_600x900.jpg)

Another celebrity star whose eyebrows are lusted after by millions of people around the world is Lily Collins. Big bold eyebrows are currently a trend in the magazines and on the catwalks, but when Collins was growing up that was not the case. The actress admitted that her mother helped her be confident and embrace her signature features at a very early age. She has nothing to worry about now, because millions would give up anything for eyebrows like hers.

## 3. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30190616_6311_600x900.jpg)

Jessica Alba was blessed with naturally stunning eyebrows - they are long and bold with a slight arch. The shape of her eyebrows allows her to achieve a charming and innocent look, but she knows how to spice them up and transform into more dark and full look by filling them in. Her eyebrows are so versatile, which makes women all over the world even more envious.

## 4. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30190547_4194_600x839.jpg)

There is nothing that’s not attractive on Miranda Kerr’s jaw-dropping face, and that includes her eyebrows. The former Victoria’s Secret model’s eyebrows are perfectly groomed and shaped in respect to her eyes. They are not too full but yet not too light, a perfect balance for a perfect look.

## 5. Ariana Grande

![Ariana Grande](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30190541_4291_599x906.jpg)

This young starlet with an exceptional voice is also an owner of a majestic pair of eyebrows. She keeps her picture [perfect eyebrows](https://beauty.allwomenstalk.com/beginners-eyebrow-shaping-tips-for-girls-wanting-perfect-brows/) a couple of shades lighter than her hair and doesn't seem to have a defined arch. They seem natural and soft, and they perfectly shape her eyes and frame her face.

## 6. Kim Kardashian

![Kim Kardashian](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30190444_3826_383x544.jpg)

Kim Kardashian is known for taking care of herself, and she knows to never forget about her eyebrows. She was so protective of her eyebrows that she never even let anyone come close to them until Anastasia Soare came along. The trick to emulate this reality star’s amazing eyebrows is with waxing and tweezing!

## 7. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30191257_5710_600x885.jpg)

Have you ever wanted to know the secret to Emma Watson’s lovely eyebrows? Well here they are! In an interview with the Huffington Post, Emma Watson revealed that she gets her eyebrows threaded and shaped to get the desired look. Not much of a secret, huh? She is one of the lucky ones blessed with eyebrows to die for!

The number of celebrities with some of the most beautiful eyebrows can go on, and on, and on! Which celebrity’s eyebrows are you obsessing over and which eyebrow style would you love to rock? Share in the comments!
