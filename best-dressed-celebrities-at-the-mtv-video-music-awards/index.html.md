# 5 Best Dressed Celebrities at the MTV Video Music Awards ...

Wondering who the **Best Dressed Celebrities at the MTV Video Music Awards** were? I hate to say this but it was quite an uninspiring year fashion wise at the MTV Video Music Awards. Black seemed to be the color of the evening but very few managed to pull it off and even fewer made it on this list of the **Best Dressed Celebrities at the MTV Video Music Awards**. Even Lady Gaga failed to shock with her look, probably because we've already seen her Jo Calderone avatar. Well I guess you just have to read on to know who the few celebrities who were good enough to be called the **Best Dressed Celebrities at the MTV Video Music Awards **were...

## 5. Demi Lovato

![Demi Lovato](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/08/demi-lovato-red-carpet_350x550.jpg)

Demi Lovato literally sparkled in this silver Mandalay sequin plunging mini with matching Christian Louboutin pumps. She looked sexy, confident and happy and definitely rocked that MTV Video Music Awards carpet!

## 4. Kelly Rowland

![Kelly Rowland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/08/kelly-rowland-379x560_379x560.jpg)

Except for that wig which should have been destroyed a long time ago, I really like Kelly Rowland in this Falguni & Shane Peacock dress. Like I said earlier, there were a lot of black looks this year but Kelly shows you how to do it VMA style!

## 3. Katy Perry

![Katy Perry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/08/katy-perry1-359x560_359x560.jpg)

Katy Perry changed her ensemble quite a few times but I think this Asian inspired look was by far my favorite. I love the Atelier Versace mini (she's got the best legs!), the pink Jimmy Choo’ Beak’ sandals that matched her candy pink hair perfectly and the adorable cherry blossom flower in her hair. All in all, her look was fun, quirky and very very Katy! Perfect candidate for the best dressed celebrities at the MTV Video Music Awards.

## 2. Zoe Saldana

![Zoe Saldana](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2011/08/Zoe+Saldana+2011+MTV+Video+Music+Awards+Arrivals+_UyrhIhh-4Zl-391x560_391x560.jpg)

Sleek, sexy and effortless as always, Zoe Saldana looked fabulous in this Barbara Bui mini dress at the MTV Vidoe Music Awards. This woman rarely makes a fashion mistake and she sure hit the bulls eye yet again. I.want.those.shoes!

## 1. Beyonce

_ Omg! Beyonce was without a doubt THE best dressed celebrity at the MTV Video Music Awards. She used the opportunity to reveal her baby bump in a divine Lanvin gown in a color that looked spectacular on her. Congratulations to the new parents and might I say, she has never looked better!

I searched far and wide but these were the only celebrities who were inspiring enough to be included in the **Best Dressed Celebrities at the MTV Video Music Awards**. Beyonce was definitely my favorite of the night! Who was your pick for **Best Dressed Celebrities at the MTV Video Music Awards?**
