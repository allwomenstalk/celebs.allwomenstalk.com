# 11 Celebrities Wearing Double Denim  Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities wearing double denim are embracing one of most divisive trends around. Despite the Canadian Tuxedo having a chequered past, the denim on denim trend has been enjoying a fashionable revival. When done right, double denim can look quite on-trend. Take a look at these celebrities wearing double denim and be sure to let us know what you think about their outfits.

## 1. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214849_4227_592x888.jpg)

No one can work a trend quite like Kate Bosworth, and here she kicks off our list of celebrities wearing double denim. She teams her dark denim skinny jeans with a dip-dyed denim top. She breaks up the denim on denim look with a white tank top and also wears some flat ankle boots. Rounded sunglasses and layered necklaces complete this cool off-duty look.

## 2. Chloe Sevigny

![Chloe Sevigny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214849_1966_592x888.jpg)

Chloe Sevigny works a summer-friendly look in a denim t-shirt and denim skater skirt. She sticks to basic accessories like a black beanie, wayfarer sunglasses, black handbag, and black sandals. This is a flirty, relaxed take on double denim that doesn’t involve the staple pair of jeans.

## 3. Gwen Stefani

![Gwen Stefani](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214849_3753_592x888.jpg)

Here, Gwen Stefani works a streamlined denim look by sticking to the same denim wash from head to toe. She keeps the indigo tones going with a blue colour blocked handbag. She also adds that extra wow factor to her outfit with some strappy chain embellished heels.

## 4. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214849_2138_592x888.jpg)

Rihanna is quite a fan of the denim on denim look, and here she teams a button-down denim shirt with a ladylike pencil skirt and nude pointed pumps. She also carries a matching denim-toned clutch. She keeps it strictly street, however, by teaming this fitted denim outfit with a shiny black cap and gold hoop earrings.

## 5. Emmy Rossum

![Emmy Rossum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214849_8816_592x888.jpg)

Emmy Rossum looks relaxed in a chambray button-down top and boyfriend jeans. Teaming your jeans with a lighter coloured shirt is a smart way to make your outfit look less denim-y. She also breaks up her denim look with a tan belt and handbag, and sticks to a casual footwear choice of sneakers.

## 6. Ashley Greene

![Ashley Greene](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214849_2113_592x888.jpg)

Ashley Greene sticks to a classic look of a denim shirt, skinny jeans, and black ballet flats. The look is simple and streamlined, and is a great off-duty outfit option. Oversized sunglasses and a tan handbag complete this casual daytime look.

## 7. Alexa Chung

![Alexa Chung](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214850_3623_592x888.jpg)

Alexa Chung works a layered look by wearing a denim jacket and shorts under a girly navy duffel coat. Layered necklaces, lace-up platform boots, and an olive green handbag completes this denim on denim look.

## 8. Christina Hendricks

![Christina Hendricks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214850_0075_592x888.jpg)

As you can see, the denim shirt and jeans combo is a popular one. Here, Christina Hendricks teams her light denim shirt with dark denim jeans and killer patent pumps. She also carries a structured two-tone handbag and wears dark oversized sunglasses.

## 9. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214851_3165_592x888.jpg)

If you don’t want an obvious double denim look, make like Emma Stone and team a classic denim jacket with black denim jeans instead. A classic striped top breaks up the outfit and she also wears black ankle boots, a slouchy beanie, and wayfarer sunglasses.

## 10. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214851_0258_592x888.jpg)

Miranda Kerr shows off her legs in some summery bleached denim shorts. She teams them with a button-down denim shirt for a cool and casual denim themed look. A large handbag, ballet flats, and oversized sunglasses added some polish to an otherwise relaxed denim outfit.

## 11. Kristen Stewart

![Kristen Stewart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01214851_1698_333x500.jpg)

Here, Kristen Stewart sticks to a classic combination of a white t-shirt and denim. She teams her dark denim skinny jeans, which she wears cuffed at the ankle, with a light denim top, partially buttoned up. White Converse sneakers complete this casual look.

These are just a few celebrities we’ve spotted sporting denim on denim looks. What’s your opinion on double denim? Which of these celebrities do you think pulled this tricky trend off the best?
