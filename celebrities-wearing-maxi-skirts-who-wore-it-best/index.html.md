# 10 Celebrities Wearing Maxi Skirts  Who Wore It Best?

Celebrities wearing maxi skirts range from those going about their day to day business to those on the red carpet. The maxi skirt is a pretty versatile piece of clothing and comes in a range of different styles. Seeing how your favourite celebrity rocks their maxi is a great way to get some style inspiration. Check out these celebrities wearing maxi skirts and let us know who you think rocked this floor-sweeping look the best.

## 1. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/1_olivia-palermo_550x1100.jpg)

Always a favourite in the style stakes, Olivia Palermo shows us how to rock a tulip hem maxi skirt. She teams her black skirt with a beige top and jacket, cinched in with a nude belt. She completes the outfit with two-tone sandals and a croc-print clutch. It’s a simple yet sophisticated look and is an excellent example of celebrities wearing maxi skirts.

## 2. Rachel McAdams

![Rachel McAdams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/2_rachel-mcadams_550x1100.jpg)

Rachel McAdams works a more glamorous night time look in her maxi skirt. She wears a blue floral maxi skirt with thigh high split by Peter Som. Since the skirt is quite busy, she teams it with a plain short-sleeved top. The whole look could have been quite dowdy, but the revealing split definitely vamped up the entire look.

## 3. Julianne Hough

![Julianne Hough](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/3_julianne-hough_550x1100.jpg)

Here, Julianne Hough wears a Caroline Herrera ensemble. She teams a red striped top with an abstract printed maxi skirt and bright satin pumps. The skirt is quite full and structured, which gives the entire outfit a polished feel.

## 4. Minka Kelly

![Minka Kelly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/4_minka-kelly_550x1100.jpg)

Minka Kelly opts for a casual look in a strappy singlet and grey maxi skirt. She completes the relaxed look with aviator sunglasses, a straw tote, nameplate necklace, and flip flops. It’s a summer-ready outfit that can take you from the beach to lunch with friends.

## 5. Victoria Beckham

![Victoria Beckham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/5_victoria-beckham_550x1100.jpg)

Victoria Beckham’s black and white Maison Martin Margiela ensemble looks quite corporate. She wears a white short-sleeved shirt tucked into a fairly tailored looking black maxi skirt. She completes the look with dark oversized sunglasses and a structured bag.

## 6. Diane Kruger

![Diane Kruger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/6_diane-kruger_550x1100.jpg)

Diana Kruger is quite fearless when it comes to fashion. Here she wears a sheer maxi skirt and simple black top. The lacy pink skirt is quite eye-catching, and she dresses it up with strappy black heels, gold drop earrings, and a compact satin clutch.

## 7. Beyonce

![Beyonce](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/7_beyonce_290x580.jpg)

Beyonce looks super colourful in her Etro maxi skirt. She teams the patchwork maxi skirt with a hot pink sweater. She accessorises with large hoop earrings, oversized sunglasses, and a black fedora. This outfit is a great example of how you can wear colour during cooler weather.

## 8. Lauren Conrad

![Lauren Conrad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/8_lauren-conrad_290x580.jpg)

Lauren Conrad opts for a neutral look with this outfit. She wears a cream tank top with a buttery beige coloured skirt. She accessorises with an oversized clutch and cuff bracelet.

## 9. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/9_kate-bosworth_290x580.jpg)

Kate Bosworth works a relaxed look with her pastel green pleated maxi skirt. She teams it with a grey sweater and wedge sneakers. A metallic bag, layered jewellery, and oversized sunglasses complete her look.

## 10. Sophia Bush

![Sophia Bush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/10_sophia-bush_290x580.jpg)

Sophia Bush wears a colourful outfit of a tangerine top and printed maxi skirt. The skirt itself features a colourful print and has a soft, draped design. She completes her look with strappy heels, a tan clutch, and a statement necklace.

Whether they’re on or off the red carpet, celebrities do love a maxi skirt. These are just some examples of how celebrities have been styling their maxi skirts. Which of these looks do you like best?
