# 7 Talented Celebrities You Didnt Know Could Sing Really Well ...

We all love celebrities for so many reasons, which could even include celebrities you didn’t know could sing well. It may come as a surprise that some of these celebs are multi-talented. Or you may have heard that they had beautiful voices on your own. Either way, check out the voices of some of them on my list of celebrities you didn’t know could sing well that’ll surely keep you entertained!

## 1. Robert Downey Jr

Here’s a good one for my list of celebrities you didn’t know could sing well: Robert Downey Jr.! He is best known for his role as comedic, genius playboy superhero Iron Man, but did you know he actually has an amazing singing voice? If you saw him sing on Oprah a few years ago or on popular TV show Ally McBeal, then you might know what I’m talking about. And, for those who haven’t, his voice is soulful, sultry, and dreamy. I am a big fan of Robert because of his nonchalant attitude, and hearing him sing so beautifully just made me fall even more in love. Check out his song that he did with Sting called “Driven to Tears.”

## 2. Scarlett Johansson

Here’s an actress with an amazing voice also from the superhero world. I love, love, love Scarlett Johansson. She has luscious lips, a stunning body, and such a beautiful face. And, now that I’ve found out that she has a great voice, I like her even more. Check out the song “Realtor,” which has such a good vibe.

## 3. Jensen Ackles

Jensen Ackles plays the sultry, sexy character Dean on Supernatural, and has had roles on Days of Our Lives. Yet, none of these showcased his beautiful voice! This man has got some pipes. How could you not love him? Listen to him singing “The Weight” to get a feel for his voice!

## 4. Ryan Gosling

Ryan Gosling is the sensitive, in touch with his feelings kind of guy that every woman wants. I love him for the awesome roles he picks. Plus, he’s gorgeous, and super talented. One of those talents is singing. He plays with a band called Dead Man’s Bones who I discovered while researching for this article. Listening to them more has really made an impact on just how much I adore him. You will too, after listening to songs like “Name in Stone.” It's totally swoon-worthy.

## 5. Jada Pinkett-Smith

You might have thought Will and Willow were the only musical ones in the family, but you’d be mistaken! This gorgeous actress is the lead singer for awesome rock band Wicked Wisdom who have actually been around since 2002. They’ve gained a big following and even performed at the 2013 Afropunk Festival! Check out their performance of “Fakeness” this year!

## 6. Robert Pattinson

Okay, okay, so most people who follow Rob’s career know that he’s a great singer. But, I love his music so much that I had to include him. His voice is so unique and full of emotion. His music has even been featured in some of the Twilight series! The soulful song “It’s All On You” is one of my favorites!

## 7. Juliette Lewis

Funny girl Juliette Lewis has let her acting career sit on the back burner for a bit to pursue her other passion: music. And, she’s quite good at it. She’s the lead singer for her band, Juliette and the Licks. The band is an...acquired taste, but she has a rocking voice. Check them out!

Well there ya have it; a list of celebrities you didn’t know could sing well! Were you surprised at some of the choices? I definitely was. Let me know in the comments!
