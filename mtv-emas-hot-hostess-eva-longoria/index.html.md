# MTV EMAs Hot Hostess \- Eva Longoria ...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2010/11/Eva-01_500x800.jpg)

Eva Longoria had a busy busy night as the MTV EMA hostess. Though it wasn't easy taking over from Katy Perry, Eva managed to make the night her own.

To start off the night Eva hit the red carpet in a George Hobeika mini dress, which has garnered a lot of attention (with good and bad reviews), looking absolutely stunning. Through the night, she went through quite a few wardrobe changes ending up with a total of 13 different looks.

Thrown in the entire mix were a few bodysuits, a sexy red number in tribute to the Spanish football team (the World Cup champions) and a chocolate Emilio Pucci dress. The Desperate Housewives star also came out in a gigantic ham costume as an ode to Lady Gaga's infamous MTV VMAs meat dress.

The British tabloid The Mirror claimed that Gaga was upset over the ham outfit, "Lady Gaga seethed: ‘Why can’t she stop stealing my ideas?' Gaga didn’t find it funny and said that Eva should find her own gimmicks.”

However, sources close to the singer have claimed that she thought it was "hilarious".
