# 11 Celebrities with Guinness World Records ...

There are so many celebrities with Guinness World Records. I think it's safe to say that hard work pays off! Nowadays, you can have a world record for just about anything. Seriously, anything. Check out what these celebrities with Guinness World Records are being recognized for:

## 1. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aa/dz/ktkap3k5_600x893.jpg)

Miley holds the record of being the "most searched for pop star on the Internet." I mean, is that even a surprise at this point? She's constantly making the news with her crazy antics. She's just one of many celebrities with Guinness World Records.

## 2. Metallica

![Metallica](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y9/ql/b3bo0yab_600x600.jpg)

Metallica are still making moves in the music scene and I think it's safe to say that they're pretty much the most-traveled band around. After they performed in Antarctica, the band became the first (and only) band to play a concert on all seven continents. How impressive is that?

## 3. Eminem

![Eminem](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ni/mk/sbfif8kn_600x760.jpg)

Eminem's always pushing the limits with his music and when he released his single "Rap God," the rapper talked his way right into the record books. With 1,560 words in the song, Eminem holds the record for having the most words in a hit single. Kudos to you, Eminem! There's no way I'd be able to say that many words in the time that he did.

## 4. Shakira

![Shakira](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/sm/pl7y9ax4_600x899.jpg)

Shakira has Facebook to thank for her new Guinness World Record. She's noted for being the most "liked" person on Facebook with over 100 million "likes"! Rihanna previously held this title.

## 5. Katy Perry

![Katy Perry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qb/lq/uckjbjjn_600x969.jpg)

Move over, Justin Bieber. Katy Perry's now the musician with the most Twitter followers! At the time of taking in all the records, Katy clocked in 52,463,838 devotees. Today, she has over 58 million! Do you follow her on Twitter?

## 6. One Direction

![One Direction](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dc/yr/f8luoohn_600x628.jpg)

One Direction is constantly reinventing themselves through their music and that's why these British lads hold the record of being the first act to debut at No.1 with their first three albums. I wonder if their next album, Four, will break another record!

## 7. Vin Diesel

![Vin Diesel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f1/cs/kuvhjp4j_600x901.jpg)

Fast & Furious' Vin Diesel holds the record for having the most "likes" on a Facebook item. After Vin shared a photo with late actor Paul Walker, his followers showed their love and earned him this new record.

## 8. Beyonce

![Beyonce](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hu/j4/xdy74fn3_600x775.jpg)

Queen Bey holds the record for the fastest-selling iTunes album. Her surprise release sold 828,773 copies worldwide in the first three days it was available. Yup, those numbers are real.

## 9. Ellen Degeneres

![Ellen Degeneres](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tw/u9/u4anbeaa_600x806.jpg)

Remember Ellen's AWESOME selfie from the 2014 Oscars? Well, she now holds the record for the most re-tweeted message on Twitter, beating out President Obama! Were you one of those people who re-tweeted her celebrity-studded selfie? I sure did!

## 10. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hr/gg/wjc4fdgt_600x900.jpg)

It only makes sense that Jennifer Lawrence has a Guinness World Record, right? She currently holds the record for the highest-grossing action movie heroine of all time. You go, Katniss! "The Hunger Games: Catching Fire" also holds the record for being the highest-grossing post-apocalypse movie. No surprise.

## 11. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/mw/j9ohkapz_600x769.jpg)

It seems like Taylor Swift is constantly breaking music records. The talented singer holds the record for being the first solo female act with not one, but TWO million-selling weeks on the U.S. album charts for albums Speak Now and Red. With her release of 1989 this month, I'm sure she'll be breaking another record very soon!

Well, there you have it. Celebrities are breaking records every single day! Were you surprised by any of these Guinness World Records?
