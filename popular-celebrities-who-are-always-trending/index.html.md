# 7 Popular Celebrities Who Are Always Trending ...

It seems like whenever we go on Twitter or turn on our televisions, there are those select few celebrities who are always trending. Sometimes for good, most of the time for bad, but whatever it is that they’re doing, it keeps them in the spotlight. And of course there are the celebrities that are famous for reasons we can’t figure out, so they need to maintain a spot on the trend list to stay remembered. Let’s check out a few celebrities who are always trending.

## 1. Kanye West

![Kanye West](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/02212736_4228_500x666.jpg)

Rapper Kanye West was once considered one of the best rappers, along with Lil Wayne and Jay-Z, whom he collaborates with a lot. After his mother passed away though, Kanye lost a lot of respect from fans because of his carefree attitude. He was always on social media and television because he was constantly wrecking something and many people lost respect for him after he developed a drinking problem. In 2009 at the Video Music Awards, everyone was convinced that Kanye had lost it because Kanye decided to interrupt Taylor Swift after she was called up to receive an award. Kanye basically said that Beyoncé should have won the award, not Swift. In 2014 Kanye is still one of those celebrities who are always trending, because he has gotten himself back on track and is focused on making good music again.

## 2. Kim Kardashian

![Kim Kardashian](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/02213056_4125_456x639.jpg)

If Kanye is everywhere, of course his Kim K. is going to be right along with him, since they have been together for a while, had a daughter about 7 months ago, and are now engaged. Kim K. was in the spotlight way before she got together with Kanye though, dating singer/rapper Ray J, football player Reggie Bush, and marrying and divorcing basketball player Kris Humphries. She is one of those celebrities who are famous, but no one can figure out why. Nevertheless, Kim K. has been around for a while and it doesn’t seem like she’ll be going anywhere soon, since she now has her own reality show and mothers the child of an actual celebrity.

## 3. Nicki Minaj

![Nicki Minaj](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/02213136_2254_570x784.jpg)

When Nicki Minaj first arose, people were surprised that a female could actually rap and keep up with the big boys. People love Nicki because she is a little crazy, and she shows up on social media a lot because of her unusual style, which is Barbie like. For the same reason some dislike her, calling her fake and weird because she doesn’t dress "normal" and changes her hair color on a regular basis. Her fan base, though, which she refers to as Barbs and Barbies, makes sure that Nicki is recognized and always trending on Twitter, whether it be for her music or her latest outfit.

## 4. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/02213449_4188_500x690.jpg)

Ex-Disney star Selena Gomez has been in the spotlight a lot lately, for her music of course, but mainly because she is growing up. The media and parents everywhere have been saying all kinds of negative things about the fact that she has moved on from her Disney star mentality. Dating Justin Bieber and dressing scandalously would be normal for any other celebrity, but people judge her because she was once a star that children looked up to. People have to realize that Selena isn’t going to be young forever, and has moved on from the Disney Channel.

## 5. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/02213524_5628_600x802.jpg)

Also an ex-Disney star, Miley Cyrus is probably being picked on more than any other celebrity right now. Her new look and recent obsession with twerking have gotten her all kinds of negative attention. Recently she has been looked down upon for her performance at the 2013 Video Music Awards because she wasn’t dressed to the audience's satisfaction and she decided to twerk on singer Robin Thicke. There does seem to be a light at the end of the tunnel for Cyrus though, because even though people don’t like her style and dance moves, they love her music.

## 6. Lady Gaga

![Lady Gaga](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/02213954_3520_429x600.jpg)

Lady Gaga and her “monsters” have taken over social media. With over 40 million followers on Twitter it isn’t hard to understand why Gaga is always being talked about. Her fan base is ever increasing despite the fact that she is considered an awkward person. She even dresses crazier than Nicki Minaj, but the media wouldn’t dare judge her for it because they would have to deal with her monsters.

## 7. Drake

![Drake](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/02214034_9849_500x750.jpg)

Actor turned rapper Aubrey “Drake” Graham is all over the place! He had a small fan base before he was a rapper, because he played on a teen show called Degrassi, but as a rapper he has gained a lot more recognition. Of course the media made fun of him for a while, because he did start off as a character in a wheelchair, and it was obvious that he didn’t want it to be known. Now he is being praised for his songs, which always top the charts.

Celebrities will always find ways to end up the center of attention, whether it's intentional or not. Social media helps keep certain ones in and others out, to feed the frenzy that celebrities often create around themselves. Are you a part of the reason the above celebrities are always trending?
