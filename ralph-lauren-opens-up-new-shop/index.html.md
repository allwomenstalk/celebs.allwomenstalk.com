# 16 Photos of Ralph Lauren Shop Opening ...

It has taken three years of painstaking restoration but the new Ralph Lauren boutique and restaurant finally had its grand opening at 173 boulevard Saint Germain in lovely [Paris](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/paris/). This by the way is now the largest store of the brand in all of Europe! Fancy huh?
