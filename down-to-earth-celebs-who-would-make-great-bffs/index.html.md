# 9 down to Earth Celebs Who Would Make Great BFFs ...

While the media is showing us stories of celebs going to rehab and throwing tantrums, there are still some down to earth celebs who I think would make great BFFs. They are laid-back and don’t care that they are famous. They use their media attention to help others and they act just like the rest of us. I love it when celebs don’t let fame get to their head and remain down to earth celebs.

## 1. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sq/kv/w7/c54nksrc_441x800.jpg)

I can’t think of anybody else who should top the list of down to earth celebs who would make a great best friend than Jennifer Lawrence. She is hilarious, loves to eat, and does not care about what others think. She would be great to chill and watch a movie with or be the life of the party for a night out on the town. And can you imagine the great inside jokes we could have.

## 2. Amanda Seyfried

![Amanda Seyfried](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vt/yo/hu/jp98unb0_450x755.jpg)

Amanda Seyfried is like Jennifer Lawrence in that they both seem so grounded. She looks fabulous all of the time, but you never see her pulling crazy stunts or starving for the media’s attention. She always seems so laid back and down to earth.

## 3. Ellen DeGeneres

![Ellen DeGeneres](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r4/bo/0g/sea0cgmn_600x783.jpg)

I love to dance and therefore I love Ellen DeGeneres. She is one of the funniest ladies out there, but she never makes jokes at the expense of others. She is my role model and I believe her and me would be just fantastic friends.

## 4. Shailene Woodley

![Shailene Woodley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r1/zj/fp/x5ry68wv_500x657.jpg)

When you consider the fact that she has been blowing up Hollywood by starring in hit movie after hit movie, she still seems so down to earth. She has not let any of that go to her head. Her parents gave her rules when she started acting that she had to remain the same person no matter how much fame came her way, and she has stayed that way.

## 5. Dakota Fanning

![Dakota Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/df/yy/zd/oes248q7_526x650.jpg)

If there is one value I appreciate in a friend, it is a thirst for knowledge. Dakota Fanning is not only an incredible actress, but she is brilliant. She attended NYU which proves that she puts education above her fame. She is the kind of friend where you can have intelligent conversations with and talk about topics like politics, current events, and philosophy.

## 6. Mila Kunis

![Mila Kunis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wc/mk/fm/bjooc1xg_500x697.jpg)

Ever since she was Jackie in “That 70’s Show” I fell in love with Mila Kunis. She is a tomboy who doesn’t care about girly drama and is not afraid to admit she loves computer games. She also has a huge heart. She attended the Marine Corps Ball after a soldier serving in Afghanistan invited her via YouTube.

## 7. Khloe Kardashian

![Khloe Kardashian](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zv/t8/ns/ywqv7y88_500x663.jpg)

When you hear Kardashian the first thing you think of isn’t exactly “down to earth.” But out of her entire family, Khloe has always been my favorite. She knows that her family is famous but doesn’t let it get to her head. She puts her friends and family before any amount of fame. Not to mention, she is absolutely hilarious and makes the funniest comments.

## 8. Zooey Deschanel

![Zooey Deschanel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o9/yz/gc/forccdlj_600x900.jpg)

While I love being able to “bro” out with my friends. I am completely obsessed with Zooey Deschanel and her quirkiness. She loves glitter and cute dresses and all things girly. I can tell she would be the type of friend to bake you cupcakes when you’re feeling sad and listen to you cry about your problems.

## 9. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rm/sh/rv/052nz7xz_500x700.jpg)

If Zooey is the friend who listens to you while you’re upset, than Emma Stone is the friend who makes you laugh to forget whatever it is you were sad about. I don’t think she has ever had a role that didn’t cause me to fall on the floor laughing. She is an A-list celebrity but would much rather spend a night at home with her friends or boyfriend then get crazy out on the town.

Don’t get me wrong, I love my friends, but these celebrities just seem so down to earth and awesome to hang out with. Not every celebrity is obsessed with partying and getting their picture in the news. Some celebs are just as laid back and relaxed as the rest of us. What did you think of these down to earth celebs who would be great BFFs? Who are some other celebs you think would be great friends? Do you appreciate it when celebs seem more down to earth?
