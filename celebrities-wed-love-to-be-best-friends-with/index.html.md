# 9 Celebrities We'd Love to Be Best Friends with ...

We all have celebrities we’d love to be best friends with. It’s easy to forget that celebrities are regular people just like us, especially when they don’t always act like real people. However, we rarely come across celebrities who do act just like us, and when they come along we all decide they’re celebrities we’d love to be best friends with.

## 1. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/24012509_1019_600x800.jpg)

Taylor Swift is the girl everyone wants to be best friends with. Even celebrities are vying to be her best friend. She’s the girl you want to spend Friday night baking with, as many celebrities, such as Kelly Osborne, have. Even people who once hated her are now best friends with her. Take Lorde, for example. A few months ago, she made a few not-so-nice comments about her and today they’re great friends. Even people who don’t like her end up liking her. She’s one of the nicest celebrities we’d love to be best friends with.

## 2. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/24012553_3037_600x969.jpg)

Who doesn’t want to be best friends with Jennifer Lawrence? I think everyone loves her at least a little bit. She trips, she falls, she’s not afraid to eat or speak her mind. She’s what we all wish Hollywood would be like.

## 3. Mindy Kaling

![Mindy Kaling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/24012614_9338_600x845.jpg)

Mindy Kaling never fails to make people laugh, and isn’t that what we all want in a best friend? Just looking at her real-life best friendship with B.J. Novak, I think it’s safe to say that we all want to be the third best friend in their best friendship. There are no two funnier friends than Mindy and B.J.

## 4. Mila Kunis

![Mila Kunis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/24012632_7842_600x847.jpg)

Mila Kunis is perfect because she doesn’t try to be perfect. Obviously she looks effortlessly flawless in her films, but when she’s not working she’s just laid-back. She’s not afraid to be irreverent or skip the makeup. She’s every girl’s dream best friend, someone you’re not afraid to be yourself around.

## 5. Ellen DeGeneres

![Ellen DeGeneres](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/24012649_9524_600x900.jpg)

I’m pretty sure that everyone on the entire planet wants to be best friends with Ellen DeGeneres. She’s quite possibly the nicest celebrity to ever exist, as well as the funniest. Plus, you could go to her show whenever you wanted, or more specifically during the 12 Days of Giveaways.

## 6. Lauren Conrad

![Lauren Conrad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/24012713_8553_600x899.jpg)

Lauren Conrad is the inspirational best friend you need in your life. She also has a plethora of great beauty and fashion tips, as well as an awesome wardrobe to steal from. She’s every fashion lover’s dream best friend.

## 7. Meryl Streep

![Meryl Streep](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/24012742_4699_600x748.jpg)

Meryl Streep needs no explanation. She’s as perfect as perfect can be, there’s no other way to say it. She’s Meryl Streep. No matter your age, whether 16 or 60, you most likely want to be best friends with Meryl Streep.

## 8. Anna Kendrick

![Anna Kendrick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/24013022_9813_600x873.jpg)

Anna Kendrick is every teenager’s dream celebrity best friend. Take a few seconds out of your day to read her Twitter feed and that is blatantly obvious. She’s witty and sarcastic, so she’s basically what every teenager aspires to be, at least in their Twitter presence.

## 9. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/24013044_9475_600x1069.jpg)

Emma Stone is another classic example of the celebrity we all want to be best friends with. She’s hilarious, irreverent, and not afraid to speak her mind. We all aspire to be her, and we all wish we knew her. She’s also best friends with Taylor Swift, which brings us right back to the beginning of this list. Even celebrities want to be Emma Stone’s best friend.

Which celebrity would you love to be your best friend? Let me know in the comments!
