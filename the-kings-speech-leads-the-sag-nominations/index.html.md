# The King's Speech Leads the SAG Nominations...

Today the acting community woke up excited as the nominations that they care about the most - those made by themselves - were announced.

The 17th Screen Actors Guild Awards' nominations were announced and it looks like this year's Avatar is continuing its strong run. The King's Speech tied with The Fighter, ending up with four nominations each.

In the TV categories, 30 Rock leads the nomination race with four nominations, followed closely by a host shows. Mad Men, Dexter, Boardwalk Empire, Temple Grandin, You Don't Know Jack and Glee each earned three nominations.
