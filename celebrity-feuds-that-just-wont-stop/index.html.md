# 7 Celebrity Feuds That Just Wont Stop ...

Here are various celebrity feuds that are hard to watch but incredibly hard to turn away from. Whose side are you on? We just can’t make up our mind! These are some celebrity feuds that may never end.

## 1. Sinead O'Connor Vs. Miley Cyrus

![Sinead O'Connor Vs. Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rl/z9/i5/sn3iz95q_560x415.jpg)

This is one of the more recent celebrity feuds to come to the surface and for some reason you would think these two would get along. Oddly enough, this feud was started by Cyrus stating that O’Connor’s “Nothing Compares 2 U” inspired some of her more recent creative decisions. Innocently enough right? Well O’Connor was not so pleased with this comparison and felt the need to write an open letter. Of course, Cyrus stung back with some rude tweets.

## 2. Eminem VS Mariah Carey

![Eminem VS Mariah Carey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0j/co/ht/hc8kia1g_550x404.jpg)

These singing starlets should be helping each other at the top rather than pushing each other back down. According to Em, this war started over some broken hearts. After the two supposedly parted ways, each singer swapped diss songs like ''Obsessed'' (Eminem) and ''The Warning'' (Mariah). Carey denies the two were ever an item.

## 3. Drake VS Chris Brown

![Drake VS Chris Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dl/kx/dl/eok9o5e6_600x400.jpg)

Everyone knows this the feud that probably will never end. Not only are they fighting over their lady love, Rihanna, there has been a fight recorded in a club that left both parties bruised and bloody. Pictures surfaced on the web of the broken glass and drinks spilt all over the bar. It was last reported that they were both being sued by the club owner. Really not sure this one will ever end.

## 4. The Wanted VS One Direction

![The Wanted VS One Direction](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rr/my/kg/0rgqykz6_600x338.jpg)

Here’s another boy band feud for you. Just like N’Sync and the Backstreet Boys in the past, these two bands have been fighting for that number one spot. It all started over some nasty words said at a One Direction concert. After the words got back to The Wanted, member Tom Parker tweeted at Louis Tomlinson, ''You even talk about us at your own gigs. Are you that upset you didn't get in this band?'' Tomlinson shot back with a tweet of his own: ''Pal, we both know I wouldn't waste my time auditioning for your band.”

## 5. Donald Trump VS Rosie O’Donnell

![Donald Trump VS Rosie O’Donnell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fz/ol/ni/fyo235vg_525x445.jpg)

Can you ever win a feud if it involved Mr. Trump? Well Rosie is going to try. This feud has been going on for many many years now, and looks to have no end in sight. While still on The View in 2006, Rosie threw the first punch by saying that Trump is a “snake-oil salesman” and making fun of his hair, his Miss USA pageants and even his finances. Of course Trump said something back, and he didn’t hold back. Trump called O’Donnell fat, a loser and a slob. Ouch.

## 6. Nicki Minaj VS Mariah Carey

![Nicki Minaj VS Mariah Carey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0e/bx/uk/pvw92kd9_600x450.jpg)

Carey pops onto this list one more time. This feud all started while the two starlets were filming American Idol both as judges. If you ever watched the show, it was very noticeably awkward for the two who exchanged rude looks multiple times. We are thinking the number one reason this started was due to the sheer fact that they had two divas on the panel.

## 7. Angelina Jolie VS Chelsea Handler

![Angelina Jolie VS Chelsea Handler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rv/sn/tj/vh6ul3wc_467x400.jpg)

Although they may not be screaming at each other and appearing on the front-page every week, there is some serious tension here. This all started when Jolie reportedly stole Jennifer Aniston’s husband, Brad Pitt. Handler, being Aniston’s bff, made some major jabs at Jolie on her late night talk show. I wouldn’t want to run between this fight!

Wow. I feel stressed out after reading this! These celebs have been fighting these fights for quite some time now, with no end in sight. Do you think these are fights that should continue, or should they all just give up on it already?
