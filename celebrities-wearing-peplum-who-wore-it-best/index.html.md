# 10 Celebrities Wearing Peplum – Who Wore It Best?

The peplum trend was embraced in a really big way and there are plenty of examples of celebrities wearing peplum. From formal red carpet outfits to everyday street-style looks, celebrities have worn the peplum trend in so many different ways. If you’re after some pointers on how to rock the peplum trend then look to your favourite celebrities for style inspiration. Check out these celebrities wearing peplum and be sure to let us know who you think wore it best.

## 1. Zoe Saldana

![Zoe Saldana](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/1_zoe-saldana_550x1100.jpg)

This is one of the funnest examples of celebrities wearing peplum. Zoe Saldana wears a red and white peplum top with printed shorts by Prabal Gurung. The top is quite voluminous but is cinched in with a cute bow waist belt. Since the outfit is quite busy, she keeps the rest of her look fairly simple with neutral shoes and minimal jewellery.

## 2. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/2_olivia-palermo_550x1100.jpg)

Olivia Palermo’s outfits always make a splash. Here she is at Paris Fashion Week in a Dior look. Her shapely jacket features a peplum hem, which is matched by a flared skirt. She teams her outfit with basic black pumps, a chain strap handbag, and a matching chain link bracelet.

## 3. Ashley Greene

![Ashley Greene](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/3_ashley-greene_550x1100.jpg)

Ashley Greene embraces the leather trend in this all black outfit. She teams a leather peplum top with black shorts and patent pumps. A slim layered necklace and compact clutch complete the look.

## 4. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/4_emma-stone_550x1100.jpg)

Emma Stone has sported a few peplum outfits in recent times. Here she wears a navy Burberry peplum top with an emerald green sequin skirt. She completes the classy, polished look with some pointed black pumps.

## 5. Naya Rivera

![Naya Rivera](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/5_naya-rivera_550x1100.jpg)

Naya Rivera wears her black peplum top with a bold printed pencil skirt by Monique Lhullier. The combination of a peplum top and pencil skirt really plays up a curvy silhouette. She accessorises with strappy heels, a structured clutch, and a bright red lip.

## 6. Blake Lively

![Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/6_blake-lively_290x580.jpg)

Blake Lively works a black and white look in a Jason Wu for Target dress. The skirt and peplum detail feature gem embellishments, which add some interesting texture to the dress. She accessorises with drop earrings, a statement cocktail ring, and rounded pumps. The side swept hair is a nice touch, too.

## 7. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/7_emma-watson_592x888.jpg)

Emma Watson wears her peplum by Jason Wu. She teams a dressy peplum top with a simple black skirt and strappy heels. The feathery embellishment on the top is a fun touch, as is the leopard print clutch she’s sporting.

## 8. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/8_kate-bosworth_592x888.jpg)

This is a quirky combination of clothes from Kate Bosworth. She wears an owl motif sweatshirt tucked into a tweed pencil skirt with slight peplum details at the waist. A simple black clutch and suede booties complete the look.

## 9. Cara Delevingne

![Cara Delevingne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/9_cara-delevingne_592x888.jpg)

Cara Delevingne works her peplum in a model-off-duty fashion. She wears a Burberry striped peplum jacket with skinny black pants and peep-toe ankle booties. She accessorises with a simple charm bracelet, statement ring, and rectangular clutch.

## 10. Cat Deeley

![Cat Deeley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/05/10_cat-deeley_592x888.jpg)

Cat Deeley shows us a more casual, street-style approach to wearing peplum. The subtle peplum detail on this plain white top is subtle enough that it doesn’t overpower the entire outfit. She teams the top with brown leather pants and nude strappy heels to create a neutral looking outfit.

These are just a few celebrities showing off their peplum power. From peplum tops to skirts to dresses, there’s lots of different ways to style your peplum look. So when it comes to celebrities and peplum, who do you think wore it best?
