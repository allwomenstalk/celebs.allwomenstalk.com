# 7 Celebrities at Fashion Week Who Looked Amazing ...

There were SO many celebrities at fashion week who looked absolutely incredible. Then again, what else can you expect from New York Fashion Week? These celebrities looked like they could have been walking the runway themselves! They got to sit front row at the best shows and check out the new collections. Check out some of my favorite looks from these celebrities at fashion week. Let me know who you think looked the best!

## 1. Adrienne Bailon

![Adrienne Bailon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/q6/0z/v56h4ucv_600x900.jpg)

Where do I even begin? Adrienne Bailon was definitely one of the best dressed celebrities at Fashion Week! My favorite look from her had to be this Michael Costello Custom Strapless Jumpsuit. It looks absolutely amazing on her! She paired it with an Elie Tahiri Shelby Coat that completed the look. I love how she wears statement outfits but always keeps the accessories at a minimum. That way, she's daring without being over the top. I think she rocked this look!

## 2. Zendaya

![Zendaya](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vj/e8/gp/kkx3e85p_359x600.jpg)

Zendaya was definitely on her A game during Fashion Week! My favorite look was the one she wore to the Pamella Roland show. I honestly love this girl's sense of fashion. It's a bit quirky sometimes, but she carries herself with such confidence that she can rock anything! I love the color on her and how she paired it with a bold lip color. The cropped top looks great on her.

## 3. Joe Jonas & Blanda Eggenschwiler

![Joe Jonas & Blanda Eggenschwiler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/kf/d0/rq7elfqv_399x594.jpg)

Joe always looks so stylish, so, NYFW was no exception! Joe and his girlfriend, Blanda, sat front row and looked great while doing it. Joe was even named the "Style king of New York Fashion Week" by British GQ! That's pretty impressive, huh?

## 4. Bella Thorne

![Bella Thorne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nc/dc/q5/tquc11h9_592x888.jpg)

Bella rocked NYFW just like her BFF, Zendaya! She looked awesome in a cropped cable knit sweater and flowy floral skirt. Bella rocked this look to the Alice + Olivia show. Her gorgeous red hair was worn in a side braid, with a ribbon woven throughout. It's a super cute look! She even got to walk the runway during Fashion Week's "Go Red For Women" showing!

## 5. Blake Lively

![Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/n5/si/dl751swx_486x730.jpg)

This woman can do no wrong. I mean, seriously, how is she so beautiful? Blake rocked this look to the Michael Kors show and looked stunning. I love how she always keeps her fashion really simple. I'm loving the nude and navy combo! Doesn't she look fantastic?

## 6. Kat Graham

![Kat Graham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/si/pp/ub/oiy7xbjs_423x600.jpg)

Kat looked gorgeous throughout Fashion Week! I love her style. She even walked the runway for the "Go Red For Women" show alongside Bella Thorne, AnnaSophia Robb, and more! I love this look because even though it looks like something you could wear on a casual NYC day, Kat makes it look so fierce and runway-ready. How does she do that?!

## 7. Dakota Fanning

![Dakota Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/m1/ey/yjl701ok_300x450.jpg)

I think Dakota is the cutest thing! I loved her look at the Rodarte show. She always keeps it classy and elegant. Dakota's been becoming quite the fashion icon on the red carpet and I can definitely see why! Her sister, Elle, has just as good a fashion sense! Fanning sisters 1, world 0.

Well, there you have it! These were just seven of my personal favorite looks from NYFW. Everyone there looked incredible. After all, isn't that point of attending these shows?! Who had your favorite look?
