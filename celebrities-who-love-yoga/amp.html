9 Celebrities Who Love Yoga ...

Jennifer Aniston • Gisele Bündchen • Adam Levine • Jessica Biel • Robert Downey Jr • More ...

By Leeann

There are tons of celebrities who love yoga, tons. I don’t blame them. Yoga is one of the best exercises you can do for your body. It works out every muscle, accelerates your heart rate, calms your mind, and it makes you look damn good! It’s no wonder so many celebrities swear by it! So without further ado, here are 9 celebrities who love yoga!

1. Jennifer Aniston

She must start off the list of celebrities who love yoga because she’s pretty much become synonymous with it. Jen does yoga religiously. She’s in her 40s and look at her - still gorgeous and as toned as ever. Did you see the woman in ‘We Are The Millers’? Yoga isn’t just good for the body though; it’s good for your mind as well. Jen has said in the past that yoga calms her down, and with a hectic schedule like hers, she could use a little calm in her life!

2. Gisele Bündchen

Gisele is tall, gorgeous, long-legged, some kind of a supermom, and one of our celebrities who love yoga. Of course her figure isn’t just due to her being an avid Yogi, but the fact that she practices yoga does help I’m sure! She practices yoga to keep her balanced: “I keep myself in good shape, eating healthily, meditating and doing exercises such as kung fu and yoga." The great thing about yoga is that it’s accessible for anyone, and it’s easy to learn. For those who don’t like going to the gym and lifting weights or running on the treadmill, yoga might just be the thing for you!

3. Adam Levine

Yes, that’s right. The sexy Maroon 5 front man is one of our 9 celebrities who love yoga and practices it religiously. As he told Details magazine in 2011, he starts every morning with a strict yoga routine. In fact, he swears by it. He was quick to point out too that it yoga isn’t just a vanity thing: "I don't like how people bullshit about how yoga is not about vanity. Playing a show before thousands of people is a highly unnatural state," he says, "and when I get on the mat to do an hour of yoga before the show, I come out physically relaxed." So there you have it, if it’s good enough for a rock star, it’s good enough for us!

4. Jessica Biel

It’s no secret in Hollywood that Jessica Biel has one of the best bodies in the biz. It shouldn’t strike you as a surprise therefore that she’s one of those celebrities who love yoga. In fact if you’re looking to meeting Mrs. Justin Timberlake, yoga class would be a good bet. She told Fox news that she barely has a life: ”I have no life… I spend a lot time in yoga class.” While I find that incredibly hard to believe, if she comes out of yoga class with a body like that, I’ll gladly have no life for a while!

5. Robert Downey Jr

I never knew he did yoga. But now that I know he does I think I like him a little bit more. The fact that RDJ is one of the celebrities who love yoga just makes yoga that much cooler. There’s not much else I can say about how awesome this is. He came back from a pretty incredible low and he credits yoga as an exercise that helped bring him back.

6. Lady Gaga

Mother Monster is said to have started practicing yoga since before she was famous. She’s said to have started Bikram Yoga (that’s the super, super hot sweaty one) in 2004, and is now an avid devotee. I’ve tried Bikram yoga before, and trust me it’s not easy at all. It pushes your body to the limits, and while Lady Gaga is one of the celebrities who love yoga, the fact that she does Bikram yoga tells me one thing: this woman is hardcore!

7. Lea Michele

I love Lea. And Lea is one of the celebrities who love yoga. I don’t know her history with this practice, but the 27-year-old triple threat started really getting into yoga after her boyfriend Cory passed away this past year. On the advice of a friend, she started practicing and went to a spiritual retreat, which opened her eyes to yoga and meditation. That’s the beautiful thing about yoga, it’s good for the mind and the body!

8. Michelle Williams

Of all the celebrities who love yoga, Michelle Williams has taken it upon herself to give back to an exercise that’s done so much for her. In 2011 she gave back to Yoga For Single Moms Project. What did she do? Read on: “Yoga gave me relief like nothing else; it made me a better person and a better mother... And then I started to think about how expensive it is to get a babysitter to take a yoga class. The idea behind the program is that if you can clear the time, we do the rest. It provides childcare while the mum is in the yoga class, and it's all free." Namaste.

9. Rachel McAdams

Rachel is another one of those celebrities who love yoga that practices for the spiritual side of it. She practices Kundalini yoga, which falls into the spiritual realm of this exercise. Reportedly she gets up at 4:30 every morning to practice, which is what I call commitment! Every breath is done in conjunction with a certain pose, which is said to release some the body’s untapped energy. No matter what, you can’t deny that yoga calms you and relaxes you, all while working out your body. How awesome is that?

There are plenty of other celebrities out there who practice yoga, and with all the different types of yoga out there to choose from, you can find the one that fits you best. I’ve tried several different types of yoga, and so far Moksha is my favourite, but everyone has something different that works for them. Yoga is beneficial to both the mind and the body, and therefore one of the best ways to get active! So tell me, does yoga fall into your daily fitness routine?
