# 7 Female Watch Brand Ambassadors ...

Celebrity endorsements are unavoidable, be it television ads, billboards, or even movies! Join Harleen as she brings to you 7 fabulous female watch brand ambassadors!

The world of watch brands comes with a line of celebrity brand ambassadors. They are key for marketing existing watch brands as well as launching new collections. Check out these amazing watch brand ambassadors. All of the following brands are available from TheWatchhut.([thewatchhut.co.uk](https://www.thewatchhut.co.uk/))

## 1. The Saturdays for Ice-Watch

![The Saturdays for Ice-Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/1_the-saturdays-for-ice-watch_600x398.jpg)

The girls were first spotted wearing Ice-watches in the video of their single «Missing You» which led on to be a successful song. Frankie Sandford, Una Healy, Vanessa White, Mollie Kind and Rochelle Wiseman all claimed to have favourite colours from this beautiful collection!

## 2. Cameron Diaz for Tag Heuer

![Cameron Diaz for Tag Heuer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/2_cameron-diaz-for-tag-heuer_600x385.jpg)

The Charlie’s Angels star is very much an A list celebrity. Her collaboration with Tag Heuer has been successful for the brand. The brand refers to Diaz as ‘joining forces with a beautiful rebel’. Cameron Diaz is sure to be set to be a long standing Tag Heuer ambassador.

## 3. Deepika Padukone for Tissot

![Deepika Padukone for Tissot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/3_deepika-padukone-for-tissot_509x600.jpg)

Padukone is known for playing the crazy Veronica in the 2012 film «Cocktail». The young and up coming actress has been in partnership with Tissot for six years, and has recently launched a new collection of the Tissot watches.

## 4. Kelly Rowland for TW Steel

![Kelly Rowland for TW Steel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/4_kelly-rowland-for-tw-steel_600x400.jpg)

The famous singer best known for being in the girl group Destiny’s Child as well as her solo career has been in partnership with TW Stella since December 2011. The «Commander» singer has had the great opportunity to work with the brand to create two special edition TW Steel timepieces.

## 5. Ke$ha for Casio Baby-G

![Ke$ha for Casio Baby-G](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/5_ke-ha-for-casio-baby-g_500x750.jpg)

The singer is very well known for her song «Tik-Tok» and has collaborated with the popular brand to become a global ambassador as well as creating two unique design styles for watches. The first design was released her first Baby-G collection in 2011 and the second in 2012, both proving to be very successful.

## 6. Victoria Azarenka for Citizen

![Victoria Azarenka for Citizen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/6_victoria-azarenka-for-citizen_600x429.jpg)

The tennis star has been a Citizen Ambassador for nearly one year. She has competed in US opens as well as achieving two Olympic medals for her native Belarus. The champion was selected by the brand because of her determination, acumen and style as well as her on-court accomplishments.

## 7. Amy Adams for Lacoste

![Amy Adams for Lacoste](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/7_amy-adams-for-lacoste_600x445.jpg)

The Enchanted star became an ambassador for Lacoste back in 2012. She has appeared in the brands perfume advert. The celebrity is only to set become even more famous with her role as Lois Lane in the upcoming Man of Steel.
