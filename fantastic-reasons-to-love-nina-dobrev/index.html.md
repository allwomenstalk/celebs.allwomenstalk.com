# 7 Fantastic Reasons to Love Nina Dobrev ...

There are so many awesome reasons to love Nina Dobrev. She's captured the hearts of so many with her incredible acting. She's beautiful and genuinely nice, a rare find if you ask me! Check out some of the reasons to love Nina Dobrev and then feel free to add your own reasons in the comments section!

## 1. She's Gorgeous

When Nina gets dressed up for red carpet events, it's not even fair how gorgeous she is. She even posts tons of cute selfies on her Instagram account (@ninadobrev) and a lot of them feature her BFF, Julianne Hough! Nina's one of those people who just radiates positivity, so her inner beauty comes shining right through. This is just one of the cool reasons to love Nina Dobrev.

## 2. The Vampire Diaries

To state the obvious, Nina's amazing in The Vampire Diaries. She's constantly tweeting behind the scenes photos from the set, sharing her feelings on the episodes, and more. She's clearly a big fan of the show herself and I think that's really cool!

## 3. She's Super Athletic

A lot of people might not know this, but Nina's quite the athlete. She just so happens to be a very talented gymnast who could also swim laps around you in the pool. She's also a fan of yoga, volleyball, soccer, rock climbing, wake boarding, snowboarding, horse riding, and more! Do you think you'd be able to keep up with her?

## 4. Degrassi Alumni

You'd be surprised at how many people don't know that Nina got her start on Degrassi! She even showed off her gymnastic skills on the show. She quickly became a fan favorite in the show even though her storyline was a bit sad. Did you use to watch her on Degrassi?

## 5. She's Genuine

Despite all of her success, Nina hasn't let it get to her head. She seems like one of the sweetest and most down-to-earth people in the movie biz. If she ever runs into fans, she always takes the time to pose for a picture with them, which is awesome.

## 6. She Loves Her Fans

Nina's constantly showing love to her fans on social media. "If the fans weren’t watching and supporting and loving what we’re doing then we wouldn’t be here doing it for them," she said. "We wouldn’t be making the show. So thank you. They rock. Their enthusiasm got us renewed, and their enthusiasm keeps us going.” I love that she takes her time to acknowledge the loyalty of her fan base.

## 7. She's Single

Even though we all prayed she'd get back together with co-star Ian Somerhalder, Nina's perfectly fine being by herself. “I don’t need a boyfriend to be happy," she said. "If I meet someone and I want to be with him, then I will be. I’m very confident in being single until I find someone who I’m extremely crazy about and who I want to devote my time and love to. Until then, I will just be on my own and I am totally fine with that." I think that's awesome! I love that she holds off on love until she finds someone worthy of it.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many awesome reasons to love Nina Dobrev. She's become quite the success story and I'm very excited to see her in all of her upcoming projects. If you're a fan of Nina, what's your favorite thing about her?
