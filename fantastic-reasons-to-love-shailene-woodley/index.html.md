# 7 Fantastic Reasons to Love Shailene Woodley ...

There are so many fantastic reasons to love Shailene Woodley. She's Hollywood's 'it' girl at the moment, appearing in two huge movies this year. I'm a huge fan of her acting, ever since her ABC Family days in 'The Secret Life of the American Teenager.' She's come a long way since then and it's such a treat to watch her evolve and come into her own with all her acting projects. Check out some of the cool reasons to love Shailene Woodley.

## 1. She's Gorgeous

Shailene is beautiful, inside and out. She just radiates positivity and love, and it's so refreshing to see that in Hollywood. She used to have long, gorgeous locks of hair but she chopped it all off for her latest movie role. Of course, Shailene makes anything look good. Her new 'do definitely inspired others to take the plunge and go for a cool shorter look! When she gets glammed up for press or red carpet events, it's not even fair how amazing she looks. This is just one of the reasons to love Shailene Woodley!

## 2. Divergent

Divergent was released a couple of months ago and it's still on everyone's radar. It was a huge success and it's only going to get bigger with the other movie installments. I think this was definitely Shai's break-out role onto the big screen. Sure, she's done a lot of successful projects, but Divergent is sure to get her the attention she deserves. Have you read the series or watched the movie?

## 3. The Fault in Our Stars

Shailene's the leading lady in of the most-anticipated summer movies this year. Her role as Hazel Grace is one that I cannot wait to see. At the New York fan screening of the movie, Shailene spoke of her character and said, "After reading The Fault in Our Stars, a fictional character became one of my greatest role models." I love that she's so in love with the movie, because her passion will definitely show on screen. Her co-stars have nothing but nice things to say about her!

## 4. She's Humble

Shailene's getting more and more successful each day but she doesn't let it go to her head. She's still really humble and down-to-earth. She's one of those celebrities whom you can totally picture being your best friend. How awesome would it be to get to hang out with her all the time?

## 5. She's Green

Shailene's all about being environmentally-friendly. She even revealed that she makes her own toothpaste, body lotion, and face oils. She also gets her food straight from the farm!

## 6. She Doesn't Have a Cell Phone

Most of us can't picture a day without our smartphones, but that's not the case with Shailene; she doesn't even have a cell phone! "I'm talking to people more than I've ever talked to [them] in my life because I no longer have that crutch," she said. To be honest, that makes total sense. Do you think you'd be able to give up your phone - completely - like Shai did?

## 7. Love is Love

To Shailene, romantically, it makes no difference if you're a male or female. If you've got a good heart, that's enough. The talented actress said, "I fall in love with human beings based on who they are, not based on what they do or what sex they are." I think that's such a great mentality.

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of many reasons to love Shailene Woodley. She's slowly but surely taking over the Hollywood scene and I can't wait to watch her do it. What do you love about Shailene?
