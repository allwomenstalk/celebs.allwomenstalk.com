# 10 Celebrities Wearing Leather Pants – Who Wore It Best?

Leather-look clothing is a big trend right now and celebrities wearing leather pants are a pretty common sight these days. Trading your ordinary jeans for a pair of leather or faux leather pants is a stylish way to give a basic outfit a more on-trend twist. You can wear your leather pants with anything from an oversized sweater to a chic tuxedo jacket. Take a look at these celebrities wearing leather pants and let us know who you think wore it best.

## 1. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/1_olivia-palermo_550x1100.jpg)

First up on the list of celebrities wearing leather pants is Olivia Palermo. She teams her sleek leather pants with a chunky sweater, dark sunglasses, and an oversized croc print bag. She livened up her mainly dark outfit with some brightly decorated ankle strap heels.

## 2. Kate Bosworth

![Kate Bosworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/2_kate-bosworth_550x1100.jpg)

Kate Bosworth rocked her leather pants as she breezed through LAX airport. She worked a monochrome look in an oversized blazer, studded pumps, and light grey handbag. The must-have celebrity accessory of oversized dark sunglasses topped off the entire look.

## 3. Zoe Saldana

![Zoe Saldana](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/3_zoe-saldana_550x1100.jpg)

Zoe Saldana worked a more classic look by teaming her leather pants with a camel coloured coat and dark blue blouse. She accessorised her outfit with pointed pumps, a neutral clutch, and bright red lipstick.

## 4. Katharine McPhee

![Katharine McPhee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/4_katharine-mcphee_550x1100.jpg)

Here, Katharine McPhee works a slightly layered look in leather. She teams her leather pants with a black grey sweater layered over a black button down tunic. She accessorised her outfit with black ankle boots, a black clutch, and a jewelled cuff bracelet.

## 5. Chloe Moretz

![Chloe Moretz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/5_chloe-moretz_550x1100.jpg)

Youngster Chloe Moretz teams her leather pants with a sculpted sweater and T-bar heels. The outfit is pretty simple but she also wears a mix of accessories in the form of a spiked bracelet, chunky watch, and multiple rings.

## 6. Miranda Kerr

![Miranda Kerr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/6_miranda-kerr_550x1100.jpg)

Miranda Kerr is pretty much the go-to girl for model off duty looks. Here she wears her leather pants with a denim shirt, cropped blazer, and wedge ankle boots. Her accessories include an oversized black Givenchy bag and quirky sunglasses.

## 7. Gwyneth Paltrow

![Gwyneth Paltrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/7_gwyneth-paltrow_550x1100.jpg)

Gwyneth Paltrow cuts a sleek figure in this outfit. She keeps things simple by teaming her black leather pants with a black blouse and black booties. The addition of a spiked cuff bracelet and black nail polish are edgy touches to this all black outfit.

## 8. Sophia Bush

![Sophia Bush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/8_sophia-bush_550x1100.jpg)

Here, Sophia Bush works a sleek tuxedo inspired look. She teams her leather pants with a white tuxedo jacket, pointed stiletto heels, and a compact clutch. A slicked back ponytail completes the polished evening look.

## 9. January Jones

![January Jones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/9_january-jones_550x1100.jpg)

January Jones steps out in New York in a retro style outfit. She teams her leather pants with a sky blue military coat with fur trims. She accessorises her look with a simple black clutch, aviator sunglasses, and black pumps.

## 10. Nicole Richie

![Nicole Richie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/04/10_nicole-richie_550x1100.jpg)

Here, Nicole Richie works a head to toe leather outfit. She teams her leather pants with an edgy looking laser-cut top. She accessorises with stacked bangles, gold rings, and black platform pumps.

These are just a few examples of how celebrities have been wearing their leather pants. Dressed up with a blazer or dressed down with a chunky knit, leather pants can look all sorts of cool, no matter what the setting. Which celebrity do you think wore their leather pants the best?
