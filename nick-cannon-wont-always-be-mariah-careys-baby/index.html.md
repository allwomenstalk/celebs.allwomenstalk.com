# Nick Cannon Won't Always Be Mariah Carey's Baby ...

It seemed like a strange match to many of us, but as they showcased their love through themed photographs, first of themselves and then of their adorable twins, it started to look like the odd marriage of Mariah Carey and Nick Cannon was going to stand the test of time. The Diva and the Comedian, a modern-day fairy tale!

For the last week or so, however, various gossip rags and tabloids have been reporting that, although very much in love, the two have been living separately and are maybe possibly preparing for divorce. According to TMZ, meaning you may or may not take this tidbit seriously, Nick's infamous radio interview with Big Boy all the way back March may have been the catalyst for the couple's problems. Nick's revelation of five celebrities he'd had sex with apparently didn't sit well with Mimi. Rumor has it that their lawyers are working out all the custody and financial details.
