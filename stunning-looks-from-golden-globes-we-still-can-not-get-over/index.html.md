## 5. Eva Green

![Eva Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o0/fw/t8lz635l5704726cc7202711997355_564x928.jpg)

## 6. Jenna Dewan Tatum

## 7. Emmy Rossum

## 8. Joanne Froggatt

![Joanne Froggatt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cb/ek/un1s7y6t5704724797e55354838133_600x1014.jpg)
