# 9 Celebrity BFFs Who Are the Real Deal ...

I love seeing celebrity BFFs make it past all the drama that comes with fame. Being in the limelight 24/7 must be tough so I’m glad these superstars found someone to go through it all with! A lot of these celebrity BFFs do the same thing we do with our own besties! That means they love and support each other over the years. Awesome, huh?

## 1. Bella Thorne & Zendaya

![Bella Thorne & Zendaya](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/19094958_6682_403x594.jpg)

These besties met on the set of Disney Channel’s “Shake It Up.” The two are extremely talented (both with a record deal, book deal, etc.). It’s obvious that hours of dance rehearsal made these two inseparable. They refer to each other as sisters and even though “Shake It Up” is now over, the girls still support each other on whatever project they’re working on. I love that these Disney starlets and celebrity BFFs are growing up to be such empowering women!

## 2. Demi Lovato & Selena Gomez

![Demi Lovato & Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/19095114_5536_600x966.jpg)

These girls might just have one of the longest lasting friendships in Hollywood! The two Texas natives met as toddlers on the set of Barney. Recently, Selena performed on X Factor and the two caught up backstage. Selena posted an adorable photo on her Instagram with the caption: “It’s just the evidence of forever.” Her BFF replied by saying that they’re 14 years strong!

## 3. Jennette McCurdy & Ariana Grande

![Jennette McCurdy & Ariana Grande](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/19095213_6019_600x868.jpg)

These two might be my fave because they’re not only co-stars but real life besties. They lean on each other for support and always make each other smile! When Ariana went through a breakup, Jennette sent her texts letting her know what a wonderful person she was. When Jennette’s mom passed away, Ariana was quick to share her comforting words. I have a feeling these two will always be BFFs!

## 4. Taylor Swift & Selena Gomez

![Taylor Swift & Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/19095239_5678_600x974.jpg)

If you keep up with award shows, you’ll notice that these two are always sitting together. The truth is they actually request to be seated next to each other! These two are super supportive of each other and rely on each other for everything. They make appearances at each other's concerts and Sel even takes tips for songwriting! I love how they don’t let the pressure of being the “best” in Hollywood ruin their friendship!

## 5. Miley Cyrus & Kelly Osbourne

![Miley Cyrus & Kelly Osbourne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/19095327_0953_600x918.jpg)

These two starlets have definitely had their fair share of “crazy.” They’re not afraid to be who they are and I love them for that! These girls defend each other via Twitter against any harsh and negative rumors. They’ve both been in the limelight since they were kids and I think that was a big factor in them bonding so well. I don’t think these two will call it quits anytime soon!

## 6. Justin Bieber & Jaden Smith

![Justin Bieber & Jaden Smith](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/19095409_9803_600x900.jpg)

These cuties have a bromance that rivals all others! They’ve been inseparable ever since Justin sang on the soundtrack of Jaden’s movie, “Karate Kid.” Jaden even made a cameo in Justin’s “Never Say Never” movie, where you can see how much fun they have together! The boys are totally supportive of each other and always find time to hang out. Here’s hoping that Jaden makes another cameo in Justin’s upcoming “Believe” movie.

## 7. Katy Perry & Rihanna

![Katy Perry & Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/19095440_4855_600x800.jpg)

These two divas became BFFs on their ride to fame. They both have evolved in their music careers and have supported each other the whole way through! They vacay together and even rock out at big music fests like Coachella together. One thing's for sure, they definitely give each other crazy fashion advice! I love how they found each other and can be themselves.

## 8. Vanessa Hudgens & Ashley Tisdale

![Vanessa Hudgens & Ashley Tisdale](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/19095613_6663_600x898.jpg)

They played frenemies in High School Musical but they’ve been best friends in real life ever since they finished filming! The two are always spotted hanging out, doing the usual BFF things: a Starbucks run, a gym date, shopping spree, etc. They’ve supported each other publicly for many years now. When Vanessa split from handsome stud Zac Efron, Ash was there to help her feel better! When Ashley announced her engagement, Vanessa was there ready to celebrate. These two always have each other’s backs.

## 9. Snooki & JWoww

![Snooki & JWoww](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/19095705_5912_600x900.jpg)

Who doesn’t need a fist-pumping partner in crime, huh? These two have been inseparable since the start of Jersey Shore! Whether you liked the show or not, it spiraled their lives into super stardom. The two have been very successful business wise, and are making really smart choices. They love and support each other in everything they do and still share funny moments on their show, Snooki & JWoww.

I love how these celebs manage to stay BFFs even through all the craziness in Hollywood. In a cutthroat industry, having love and support is a huge deal! Who do you think are the cutest besties?
