# 7 Celebrities Who Are Great Friends with Taylor Swift ...

There are so many celebrities who are friends with Taylor Swift. I mean, who wouldn't want to be friends with one of the biggest names in music who just so happens to be, in my opinion, the sweetest person in the industry?! I love that Taylor befriends people with similar interests and passions as her. I think that's how you can tell that these friendships are genuine. I also love that she documents all her cool adventures with her friends on her social media accounts! Check out some of the celebrities who are friends with Taylor Swift.

## 1. Karlie Kloss

![Karlie Kloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/mr/gpchmtkn_597x800.jpg)

Nowadays, it seems like you can always find Taylor and Karlie together. Taylor became best friends with Karlie earlier this year and they've pretty much been inseparable since then. Their Instagram profiles are full of adorable photos together! She's just one of the celebrities who are friends with Taylor Swift.

## 2. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v9/yy/gso3rqsy_353x530.jpg)

Selena and Taylor have been best friends for quite some time now. They even request seats next to each other at every awards show - how sweet is that? I love that they're always supportive of each other and always encourage the other to be the best version of herself. Selena's 22nd birthday recently passed and Taylor posted an adorable photo of herself wearing an "S" necklace in celebration of Selena!

## 3. Lena Dunham

![Lena Dunham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jc/ch/kgefrtpa_544x761.jpg)

Taylor's a self-proclaimed 'Girls' fanatic so I think it's amazing how close she's become with Lena! The two seem like they have a blast together. Over the Fourth of July weekend, they even lit up sparklers together! Lena's got nothing but nice things to say about the superstar. That also goes for her boyfriend, who's called Taylor the 'closest thing we have to Michael Jackson today'. Do you agree with that?

## 4. Jaime King

![Jaime King](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/l7/wjlad0ps_578x855.jpg)

'Hart of Dixie' star, Jaime King, is close pals with Taylor! If you go on Taylor's instagram page (@taylorswift), you'll see tons of adorable photos of the two. This includes pictures of them baking a pie (yum), vacationing together, etc.

## 5. Jessica Szohr

![Jessica Szohr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mj/zn/nasmpi06_392x549.jpg)

Gossip Girl alum, Jessica Szohr, is super close with Taylor. She even starred in her "22" music video! The two spent the Fourth of July weekend (along with half of the names on this list) together.

## 6. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y9/t4/l9ls22iv_600x803.jpg)

Wouldn't it be awesome to have Emma Stone as one of your best friends? Well, Taylor Swift can tell you all about it. The two hang out very often and I can totally see how they would get along so great. Emma even joined Taylor on a yacht for the holiday weekend back in July!

## 7. Ed Sheeran

![Ed Sheeran](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gn/hh/q62g6rzb_533x800.jpg)

Taylor and Ed are incredibly close. They've written songs together, performed together, and even make awesome videos together. When Ed's "X" album was released, Taylor shared her excitement and encouraged all her fans to go on and buy it. I love that the two are able to inspire each other and challenge each other in the music field because I think they're two of the most talented people in the industry at the moment.

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of many awesome celebrities who have become super close with popstar, Taylor Swift. As you can tell, Taylor's pretty popular and has some A-list friends! I don't know about you, but I'd love to hang out with them all! Were you surprised by any of the names on this list?
