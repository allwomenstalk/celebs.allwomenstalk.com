# 8 Stunning Celebrities in Emerald Green: Red Carpet Inspiration

Emerald green is undeniably one of the hottest colors of the season, radiating a sense of luxury and timeless elegance. If you are looking for a little fashion inspiration to brighten up your wardrobe, look no further. Check out these stunning celebrities rocking it in emerald green on the red carpet and beyond. This jewel-toned hue is a showstopper for any occasion!

A huge number of celebrities have walked the red carpet looking elegant and luxurious in emerald green. It is like seeing a great parade of sophisticated beauty, featuring the biggest names and the prettiest faces in Hollywood. Because emerald green was famously declared the color of the year by Pantone, we continue to see more stunning dresses and fashion items in this lively hue. Let us see how our favorite actresses donned their emerald green dresses and who shines the brightest in these jewel-toned outfits.

## 2. Elegant Emily Blunt

_ Another red carpet head-turner was The Devil Wears Prada_ actress Emily Blunt. When she attended the SAG Awards, she chose a shimmering emerald gown that perfectly complemented her complexion. Paired with shimmering platforms and a metallic clutch, she looked like an angel. It was impossible to stop staring at her classy, refined beauty! This look remains a top reference for anyone searching for an **emily blunt green dress** online.

## 3. Quirky Katy Perry

![Katy Perry in a quirky emerald green outfit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/celebs/2013/06/3_quirky-katy-perry_500x668.jpg)

Singer Katy Perry was also seen wearing a pretty emerald green gown when she attended "You’ve Got a Friend: A Celebration of Carole King and Her Music." Although she is well-known for her experimental clothes and funky hair colors, it was refreshing to see Katy in such a sophisticated shade. She kept her accessories minimal, using just a beautiful necklace and a statement ring to adorn her outfit, letting the color do the talking. Many fans of **celebs in green dresses** cited this as one of her most memorable and polished appearances.

## 4. Cutie Amanda Seyfried Ladylike Chic Short Dresses

_ Long before emerald green became a massive mainstream trend, actress Amanda Seyfried wowed the crowd in her emerald green short dress at the premiere night of her movie Mamma Mia!_. Amanda proved that the best accessory to any dress is a sincere smile and a confident spirit. Her choice showed that this color works just as well for "ladylike chic" short dresses as it does for floor-length gowns.

## 5. Sparkling Katherine Heigl

_ Famous celebrity designer [Naeem Khan](https://www.naeemkhan.com/) created a dress that made Katherine Heigl a true stunner. Katherine donned a sparkly one-shoulder dress in emerald green at the premiere night of her movie The Ugly Truth_. The sequins added an extra layer of depth to the jewel tone, making her glow under the camera flashes. This is a perfect example for those looking for **green dress celebrity** inspiration that leans into high glamour.

## 6. Gorgeous Jennifer Lawrence

_ Everybody is always talking about Jennifer Lawrence and her versatile looks on the red carpet. In a special screening of The Hunger Games_, Jennifer showcased her style in a sleek emerald green [Calvin Klein](https://www.calvinklein.us/) dress. She kept the overall look simple and modern by wearing her hair in glossy curls with flawless, natural makeup. Her appearance is a frequent favorite for those searching for an **emerald green actress** who knows how to wear color with ease.

## 8. Stylish Emma Stone
