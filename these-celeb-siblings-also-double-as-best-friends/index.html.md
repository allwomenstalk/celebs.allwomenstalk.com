# These Celeb Siblings Also Double as Best Friends!

There are so many celeb siblings that call themselves best friends! I've always thought, if you have a sister or brother around, you've got a friend for life. There's an unbreakable bond between sisters and brothers - you can count on them no matter what. These celeb siblings recognize that bond because they're always having fun with each other and supporting each other through it all!

## 1. Kendall & Kylie Jenner

![Kendall & Kylie Jenner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/di/tz/skxy4ejf_600x600.jpg)

The Jenner sisters lean on each other for love and support whenever things get crazy! They're constantly posting photos together on their social media accounts and it's obvious that the two are pretty much inseparable! They're not the only celeb siblings in that family - the girls are, of course, BFFs with their sisters, Kim, Khloe, and Kourtney, and their brother Rob Kardashian!

## 2. Vanessa & Stella Hudgens

![Vanessa & Stella Hudgens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pm/jl/m793zdqk_600x600.jpg)

Vanessa is always bringing her little sis Stella as her date to red carpet events. The two are constantly hitting up music festivals together and being fashionistas together. Stella even trusted her big sis to dye pink strands in her hair!

## 3. Gigi and Bella Hadid

![Gigi and Bella Hadid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/go/jl/pi8nrzgc_600x600.jpg)

Gigi and Bella are both hard workers in the modeling industry! They're always showing each other support and hanging out with each other. In that industry, it's definitely important to have a best friend you can trust!

## 4. Cassie and Alexa Steele

![Cassie and Alexa Steele](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f8/bz/n068v8h4_600x600.jpg)

Cassie and Alexa both got their starts on Degrassi (crazy, right?!) and are the best of friends. Alexa is very supportive of Cassie and her music career - she's always letting her fans know about how badass her sister's songs are!

## 5. Lennon and Maisy Stella

![Lennon and Maisy Stella](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/to/kb/anx50khb_600x600.jpg)

Lennon and Maisy are probably my favorite celeb siblings. I think they're SO adorable! The two also make up an amazing musical duo. They're both actresses on ABC's hit drama series, Nashville. If you haven't seen their YouTube videos, do yourself a favor and check them out right away!

## 6. Aly and Aj Michalka

![Aly and Aj Michalka](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fc/eb/pyih3tap_600x600.jpg)

How could anyone not love Aly & AJ?! This sister duo captured our hearts with their music and are still doing so under their new band name, 78Violet. They're always posting photos together on Instagram and AJ is beyond excited for Aly's engagement!

## 7. Nicole and Sofia Richie

![Nicole and Sofia Richie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qh/ln/jtlikjw6_600x600.jpg)

Nicole and Sofia Richie are the funniest sibling duo! If you watch Nicole's VH1 series, Candidly Nicole, you know that Sofia is constantly making funny cameos!

## 8. Julianne and Derek Hough

![Julianne and Derek Hough](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v8/kn/yyeec1rl_600x600.jpg)

This sibling duo is inseparable! Julianne is always taking her brother as her date to events and is beyond supportive of him on DWTS. Julianne has just signed on as a new judge for the show so they'll be spending even more time together!

## 9. Ariana and Frankie Grande

![Ariana and Frankie Grande](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/ml/j8ds4cjc_434x386.jpg)

It's no surprise that Ariana's favorite person in the world is her big bro, Frankie. She's always with him and defends him when anyone has anything bad to say. Frankie does the exact same for her!

## 10. Nick, Joe, and Kevin Jonas

![Nick, Joe, and Kevin Jonas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hn/qm/toqgq3py_500x500.jpg)

The Jonas Brothers. Good times, good times. (Sorry, I got nostalgic for a minute!) These brothers have been through many ups and downs together but they continue to have unconditional love and support for one another. They've also made some awesome music together!

## 11. Tia and Tamera Mowry

![Tia and Tamera Mowry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sv/xx/l68bixr6_600x451.jpg)

## 12. Dakota and Elle Fanning

![Dakota and Elle Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pw/jj/a5kux61g_600x609.jpg)

## 13. Hilary and Haylie Duff

![Hilary and Haylie Duff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hk/rj/yn9hg2t8_600x599.jpg)

## 14. Miley and Brandi Cyrus
