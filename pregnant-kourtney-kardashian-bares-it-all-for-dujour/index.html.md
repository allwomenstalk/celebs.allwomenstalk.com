# Pregnant Kourtney Kardashian Bares It All for Dujour ...

Kourtney Kardashian bared it all in three nude shots for DuJour magazine! Her barely-there ensembles show off her third baby bump and the mom-to-be looks absolutely radiant. She loves the skin she's in and it shows! She told the magazine, "I really feel so comfortable, and almost at my best, when I'm pregnant...I don't know, I just feel just like it's such an amazing feeling, the transformation that your body goes through and knowing that this is what my body is made to do, there's something about that that's so empowering and beautiful and I just really embrace it." If you're wondering if Kourtney cares about any backlash, she doesn't. "To me, nudity is not something to be ashamed of," she said. "I'm not embarrassed of my body, so I think it was something that initially I did for myself. I also, in general, don't really care that much about what people think of me. It doesn't rule my world, or affect my decisions, or anything about what other people think, as long as I did something because I wanted to." Amen to that!

She also added, "I'm not trying to impress anybody, or be something that I'm not. It's just a beautiful thing, just being able to take those photographs and have them, and just be able to show my children."
