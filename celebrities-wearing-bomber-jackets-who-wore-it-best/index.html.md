# 11 Celebrities Wearing Bomber Jackets  Who Wore It Best?

The bomber jacket has been a popular trend lately and we’ve seen a number of celebrities wearing bomber jackets. It has been the must-have jacket shape of the season and is perfect for dressing down a polished outfit or for working a casual sporty look. The bomber jacket comes in a variety of designs and can be worn with anything from flirty dresses to skinny jeans. Check out these celebrities wearing bomber jackets and make sure you let us know who you think wore theirs the best.

## 1. Olivia Palermo

![Olivia Palermo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/09/hs/n5vm5qqz_350x700.jpg)

When it comes to celebrities wearing bomber jackets, trust Olivia Palermo to take a high fashion approach to things. She wore this butterfly print bomber to a fashion show and teamed it with satin shorts, a lace blouse, black tights, and studded booties. It’s an interesting mix of texture and print and a look that you could argue only a celebrity could pull off.

## 2. Gwen Stefani

![Gwen Stefani](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lf/cq/ppxj4kg9_350x700.jpg)

You can’t get more classic and versatile than a black bomber jacket. Gwen Stefani wore her sporty jacket with floral graphic printed pants and blue pointed heels. Finished off with come cat eye sunglasses, this outfit is polished and on-trend.

## 3. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0c/04/hgxdk6pj_592x888.jpg)

Taylor Swift worked a preppy look in this college-inspired bomber jacket. She teamed her two-tone jacket with a white t-shirt, mustard yellow shorts, and tan lace-up shoes. This is a casual look that you could easily recreate, guitar case optional.

## 4. Chloe Grace Moretz

![Chloe Grace Moretz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a0/na/nayma35n_592x888.jpg)

Chloe Grace Moretz worked an edgier angle in this all black outfit. She teamed a studded bomber jacket with a black top and zippered skinny jeans. Embellishments are always great for adding interest to an outfit and this is a chic example of how to do so.

## 5. Ellie Goulding

![Ellie Goulding](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/hu/bampv54o_592x888.jpg)

Showing that bomber jackets can be teamed with just about anything this season, Ellie Goulding wore hers with cuffed tailored pants and strappy heels. Teamed with a printed t-shirt, the jackets gives the remainder of the outfit a sportswear inspired vibe.

## 6. Lena Dunham

![Lena Dunham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ch/t6/q1df24hz_592x888.jpg)

Lena Dunham made sure her blue bomber jacket matched her printed dress. She completed her outfit with black patent lace-up shoes and a bold red lip. Throwing on a bomber jacket over a summer dress is a great way to toughen it up and is an on-trend alternative to a leather jacket.

## 7. Kim Kardashian

![Kim Kardashian](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c9/zt/sdtclt5q_592x888.jpg)

Kim Kardashian worked a polished vibe in this neutral outfit. She wore a neutral bomber jacket with a figure hugging white dress and nude heels. It’s a more elegant take on the bomber trend and shows that it can be worn with dressier outfits.

## 8. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y9/cb/omr9d5so_592x888.jpg)

Always onto trends, Rihanna teamed an oversized white bomber jacket with a cropped striped top and fitted white pants. Oversized sunglasses (yes, even at night), a zippered clutch, and strappy metallic heels completed her all-white outfit.

## 9. Laura Carmichael

![Laura Carmichael](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/au/r6/t7pw5utm_592x888.jpg)

Laura Carmichael shakes off the ‘Downton Abbey’ vibes in this on-trend outfit. She teamed a textured bomber jacket with a leather look mini skirt, patent heels, and a structured side bag. It’s a contemporary and stylish outfit that can take you from day to night.

## 10. Ciara

![Ciara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cg/f3/qzwchaqo_592x888.jpg)

Ciara worked a very sports luxe look by teaming her bomber jacket with skinny leather-look leggings. A slouchy beanie, aviator sunglasses, oversized bag, and platform combat boots completed this sporty looking outfit.

## 11. Karlie Kloss

![Karlie Kloss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bn/f1/kq6azaxp_600x1200.jpg)

Karlie Kloss teamed her bomber jacket, which is emblazoned with her own name, with a timeless outfit of a grey sweater, skinny jeans, and ballet flats. A ladylike handbag in one hand is enough to carry the essential items, while a larger tote is perfect for storing bulkier items.

These are just some celebrities who have embraced the bomber jacket. Are you a fan of the bomber jacket? Which of these celebrity looks do you like best?
